2 MINUTE TIPS SERIES

 

When we all were stationed to work from home, we were told to be creative and brainstorm ways to connect with our viewers. I thought it was the perfect time to go back to basics and do some fun informative videos. Some of the topics may seem obvious or old information but we should not forget that at one point we all had to learn how to properly fit a helmet. I remember learning the basics about helmets and ever since then I couldn’t help but notice how many people are riding around with helmets that were purchased 20 years ago and look like they came out of a dusty box in the garage. I think to myself, they must just not know that helmets expire.

I want to cover the basics, the topics that sometimes get overlooked, or people aren’t aware of all the options available. In the end, these discussions might help someone learn something new, get an interest in reading more about cycling shoes, or the different kinds of cycling shorts.

I’m no cycling professional but I have learned the hard way in many of the topics of the videos. I think sometimes that’s the best way to understand how things work. Stay tuned for new videos as they come. If you have any topics you want me to discuss, then just shoot me a note in the comments below. What do you guys want to learn more about? Stay well and happy cycling!
