Belgium: National Park Hoge Venen

We have three days of, so what better to do than spend these days cycling? A good training for our upcoming world trip in the Belgium hills. We pack our new Hilleberg Nammatj 3GT tent, new Cumulus sleeping bags and our new Optimus stove. Weather forecast: cold and dry!

We park the car in Epen en soon we find ourselves in the woods and hills of National Park Hoge Venen. We cycle to Robertsville and continue to Prum and Monchau and two days later crossing the woods close to Aken and the three-countries-point we have pedalled 230 kilometers and gained a few altitude meters!
