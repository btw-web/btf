Tierra del Fuego National Park

We’ve reached the end of the world! And what better place can you pick to celebrate New Years? Right! So, we set out to Parque Nacional Tierra del Fuego to celebrate a new year full of cycling adventure!

It’s a short unpaved ride to the National Park, but a nice ride! We find a great camp spot next to Lago Roca, with an amazing view on the lake and the mountains behind the lake. We have our own fireplace and Elmar starts gathering wood for a nice fire. That will keep the nosy foxes away! We bought ourselves real ‘Beagle’ beer and with a nice fire, some good meat and a dark beer we say goodbye to 2013, the year we made the best decision in our lives! May 2014 be just as good of a bicycle adventure year!
