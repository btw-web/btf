Uruguay

The best things about Uruguay:
1. The people.
2. The ice cream.
3. Chivitos. A steak sandwich with fried egg, ham, tomato, lettuce and pickle. Delicious.
4. Paved hard shoulders, and little traffic.
5. Campsites by the Rio Uruguay.
