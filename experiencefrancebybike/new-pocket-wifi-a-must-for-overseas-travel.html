New Pocket WiFi A Must For Overseas Travel!

One of my favorite travel accessories for cycling in France this year is the “Pocket Wifi” developed by Travel Wifi based in Paris, France.

Not only will this personal hotspot enable me to send Instagram updates on the go, it will also allow me to access details on attractions along the route throughout the day, as well as information on lodging and eating options.  Most important, I’ll be able to access route information and maps from my favorite apps while I am out cycling, when I need this information the most. Finding my hotel or B&B at the end of the day should be a breeze with this new tool!  And with the unlimited France plan, I no longer need to be concerned about using too much data running my phone and i-Pad!

The Travel Wifi personal hotspot is affordable, simple to order, easy to use, easy to return and is a hit with users around the world.  If you use a cell phone, i-Pad, computer, notebook or any other kind of device that requires Wifi, don’t leave home this summer without reserving your own Travel Wifi hotspot!

Here are some of the key benefits of the Pocket Wifi:

The Pocket Wifi is affordable and, you can connect up to 10 devices

  • The France “starter” is 6.4€/day for 500 Mb/day, 4 G speed
  • The France “premium”, unlimited plan is 8.4€/day, 4 G speed
  • The Europe “premium” plan is 9.4€/day for 5 Gb, 3 and 4 G speed
  • The World “starter” is 9.4€/day for 500 Mb/day, 3 and 4 G speed
  • The World “premium” is 12.4€/day for 1 Gb/day, 3 and 4 G speed

The Pocket Wifi is easy to order

Starting with a very easy to navigate website, ordering your personal hotspot is as easy as 1,2,3!  Travel Wifi guides you step by step through the process, including choosing delivery or pick-up of your personal hotspot. Your Travel Wifi device can be delivered to your hotel or rental house or apartment, it can be picked up at Charles de Gaulle or Orly airport, as well Tourism Offices in Cannes, Nice and Marseille and the device can also be picked up at the Travel Wifi offices in Paris.  These and other options can all be reviewed here.

The Pocket Wifi is easy to use

You’ll receive the hotspot ready to use.  You’ll receive a wall charger, a USB cable to charge the device, instructions and a pre-paid envelope to return the unit before you leave.

Your personal hotspot is preconfigured, no installation is needed.  You turn the unit on, activate Wifi with the name and password written on the back of the device and you’re good to go.  After the first connection, your devices automatically connect to the Travel Wifi network. There’s a very thorough FAQ section that will answer any question you could possibly have.

The Pocket Wifi is easy to return at the end of your trip

That’s it…easy as can be!

There’s also a referral program where you can earn credits for future rentals by referring friends:  10€ credit when a friend creates an account and 10€ credit when they book a package for the first time.

I can’t wait to use a Travel Wifi hotspot on my upcoming cycling trip in France.  Watch for updates from the road and a final report on my first experience cycling with my own personal hotspot!
