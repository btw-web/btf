The EV5 in Pictures (or part of it!)

My commute home continues to be faster and faster! I’m down to 21 minutes and 30 seconds. Amazing. The journey in the other direction is proving more difficult to improve upon: it hasn’t budged since the 18th August.

Anyway, more news from Jean-Marie in Belgium. He’s sent me a link to a bit of his cycling club’s website with details of the EV5 from Belgium to Aosta. The best things about what you can find there are the pictures: it has really brought the route alive and has enabled me to visualise it for the first time from ground level.
