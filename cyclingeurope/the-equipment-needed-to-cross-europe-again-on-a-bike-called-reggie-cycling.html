The Equipment Needed To Cross Europe (Again) On A Bike Called Reggie

Here is a picture of most of the equipment I’ll be taking with me when I set off from the south-eastern corner of Greece in exactly two-weeks’ time. There is a full list of everything below the image. If you think I’ve missed anything, please do let me know. Please note that apart from the cycling sandals, I haven’t included my clothes. This doesn’t mean I’ll be cycling across Europe buck naked…
