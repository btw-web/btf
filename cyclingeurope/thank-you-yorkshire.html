Thank-you Yorkshire!

My short speaking tour of Yorkshire ended last night here in Ripon. Appearing on the radio and making presentations is far more tiring that riding a bicycle long distances. Thanks to Russell of Russell’s Bike Shed, the people at Amici and Bici and Iain Pearce in Sheffield, to Ian Street, Andrew Edwards and the Cross Keys Pub in Leeds and to Robert Thorpe and everyone at Le Tour Ripon here in Ripon! Thanks also to the people at BBC Radios Sheffield, Leeds and York.
Now I just need to get that second book finished for the launch event at the Yorkshire Bicycle Show in Leeds Town Hall on Saturday 5th July – the day of Le Grand Départ; I’ll be there at 3pm!
