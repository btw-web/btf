The Cycling Europe Photo Competition 2020

It’s back! As we are confined to our houses with plans for long cycle tours temporarily on hold, what better time to dig out your cycle touring photos from 2019 and reflect upon past glories? And if you fancy being in with a chance of winning some great prizes courtesy of Cicerone Travel Guides, why not enter them in the 2020 Cycling Europe Cycle Touring Photograph Competition. The rules are simple; your photo(s) need to have been taken while out on a cycle tour in 2019 – that might be just one day – and they must include your bicycle. For full details of the competition, please visit this page of CyclingEurope.org and good luck!
