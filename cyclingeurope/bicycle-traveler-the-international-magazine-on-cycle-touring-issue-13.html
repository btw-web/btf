Bicycle Traveler: The International Magazine On Cycle Touring, Issue 13

Every six months or so an email arrives from The Netherlands announcing the publication of a new edition of Bicycle Traveler. We’ve had to wait a little longer for the 13th issue but it’s now available, and best of all, it’s free! There are some familiar names in the list of contributors including Tim and Laura Moss, founders of the Cycle Touring Festival and Tom Allen. Even if you don’t have the time or inclination to read the articles (which you should), the photography is stunning.
