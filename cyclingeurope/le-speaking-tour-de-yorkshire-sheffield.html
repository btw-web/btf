Le (Speaking) Tour de Yorkshire: Sheffield

Here is Russell of Russell’s Cycle Shed in Sheffield. You’ll normally find him in his workshop (and he’s about to move into new premises at Sheffield train station) but tonight to was at the cycling café called Amici and Bici hosting my talk. The crowd was friendly and it was good to have a trial run of the talk before the big one in Ripon on Friday… Tomorrow I’m Leeds bound. I’ll be on BBC Radio Leeds at 10am and then with Tom Bruce at Ian Street’s (sold out) ‘Night of the Long Rides’ event at the Cross Keys pub.
