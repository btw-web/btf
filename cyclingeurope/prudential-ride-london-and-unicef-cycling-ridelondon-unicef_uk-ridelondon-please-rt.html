Prudential Ride London And UNICEF

By Imogen Walker, UNICEF UK

Are you looking for a cycle ride with a difference? Or maybe you want to challenge yourself but aren’t quite sure how. Then look no further as Unicef UK are delighted to be able to offer places in the Prudential Ride London 100. Now in its 3rd year, the event is going from strength to strength with over 85,000 keen riders applying for a place in the ballot this year.

RideLondon is a unique 100-mile cycle ride takes you through central London before cycling through the stunning Surrey countryside and ending with a spectacular finish on The Mall. What’s more, it’s all on closed roads – so no need to worry about competing with traffic.

The ride itself takes place on 2 Aug 2015, so you’ve got plenty of time to get on your bike and get training.

You can secure a highly sought-after place with Unicef UK for just £30 by completing our online registration form on our website. Registrations close on 6 May 2015 so get cracking if you’re interested.

As a Unicef UK challenge event participant you will receive an electronic fundraising pack, further fundraising materials upon request and a Unicef UK cycling jersey.  You will also be supported by our Challenge Events team to help you get the most out of your event.

As well as the personal challenge of completing the route, your involvement will also benefit good causes and RideLondon aims to become the largest charity fundraising cycling event in the world. As a Unicef UK rider, you will be asked to fundraise a minimum of £600 in sponsorship, which will help us to continue to help children in danger around the world.

Got any questions? Visit the Unicef UK website or contact Liam Flynn in Unicef’s Challenge Events team on 0207 375 6109 or email challenge@unicef.org.uk.
