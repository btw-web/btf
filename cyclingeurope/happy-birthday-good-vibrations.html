Happy Birthday Good Vibrations!

It’s now a year since Good Vibrations: Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie was first published. The literary journey since August 2011 has been almost as eventful as the one by bike to Brindisi back in summer 2010; here’s hoping that Year 2 will be just as adventurous! Thanks to everyone who has advised me & supported me in the last twelve months and equal thanks to the thousands of you who have gone out of your way to read the book. The feedback has been much appreciated and to know that some of you have been inspired to attempt something similar has made the whole project more than worthwhile. Thank-you one and all.

Andrew (& Reggie!)
