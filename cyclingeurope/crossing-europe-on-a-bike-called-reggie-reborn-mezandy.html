Crossing Europe On A Bike Called Reggie… Reborn!

Here it is in all its glory; the new cover for Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie as designed by Andy Mitchell of Custard4Gravy. It’s wonderful and better than I ever imagined. I’m now in the process of updating the various editions of the book that are out there but fear not! If you have an old version of the book, click here and then press ‘print’ (OK, it’s not a perfect solution…). Andy is now hard at work on the cover of ‘Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie‘. I wonder which will be the first to be completed; my manuscript or Andy’s cover (my money is on the latter!)? If you would like to contact Andy with your own project, here is his email. He also produced the fantastic poster for the (sold out) event at which I am speaking in May in Leeds. He is a talented artist!
