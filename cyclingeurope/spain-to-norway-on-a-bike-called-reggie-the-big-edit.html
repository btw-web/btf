Spain To Norway On A Bike Called Reggie: The Big Edit

It’s been a quiet few days here on CyclingEurope.org as I have been busy editing down the draft manuscript of ‘Spain to Norway on a Bike Called Reggie’ ready for submission to the publishers on October 3rd. I’m already on my second read through and I suspect there will be at least one more before the end of September. My red pen skills, honed during 15 years of teaching, are being put to good use. As is my knowledge of the cafés of West Yorkshire. If those of Edinburgh were good enough for J.K. Rowling, those of God’s own county (western section) are certainly good enough for me. Can you identify the ones in the pictures below? Publication by Summersdale is scheduled for spring 2017.
