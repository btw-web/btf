The Cycle Touring Festival: To Fly Or Not To Fly…

One of my talks here at the Cycle Touring Festival was subtitled ’15 Top Tips for Long-Distance Cycle Touring’. I’ll post the list to CyclingEurope.org in the coming days. However, at number three in the list was ‘avoid flying with a bicycle’. Neil Wheadon, vet and cycle tour leader (not at the same time) wouldn’t agree. Here he is with his bespoke bike box and bicycle with dismantle-able frame. I still haven’t changed my mind…
