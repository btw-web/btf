Bad News… Good News!

Whenever I post something about cycling – the previous post being a good example – I invariably receive at least one email or message or comment saying something along the lines of “All this cycling stuff is great Andrew but, quite frankly, I’d rather know more about the current pricing strategy of your books…”. I aim to please…

The bad news… The attentive amongst you will have noticed that the Kindle versions of Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie and Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie have recently been selling at just 99 pence. I know, it’s incredible value but, alas, all good things must come to an end and tomorrow at midnight (or thereabouts), the price will revert to the extortionate amount of £1.99. Life, my friends, can be cruel…

The good news… But! Yes, but exclamation mark! As the eBook price more than doubles, I have good news regarding the paperback price of both books. As of earlier today you can buy a paperback copy of Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie for just £7.99 and Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie for just £8.99. Never have the books been so cheap to buy and what’s more, the price change is… (wait for it)… permanent!
