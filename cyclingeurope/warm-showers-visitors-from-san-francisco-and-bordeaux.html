Warm Showers: Visitors From San Francisco And Bordeaux

Not quite like waiting for a bus, but nearly… I received an email from Martin a couple of nights ago: could he stay overnight as a Warm Showers guest? No problem. No sooner had he arrived last night than I received a call from a woman in cycling distress: Jenna, also a Warm Showers member had arrived in Reading – the town where I live – and had a problem with her wheel. Could I help? Of course… So, after probably six months of no Warm Showers visitors, I had two on the same evening. Nice people!
