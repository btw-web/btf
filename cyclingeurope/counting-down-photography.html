Counting Down…

It’s not a good thing to wish away your time but sometimes it’s all but inevitable. My Instagram countdown of the days that I have to work before finishing my current job and heading off for my adventures elsewhere has now reached number 12 and has created its first block of nine numbers as shown here. Each of the numbers – some physically large, some very small indeed – has been photographed in the 24 hours preceding me posting the image. Some might be familiar (can you guess where number 13 is from, for example?) others less so but all images are original and not culled from the Internet. At the end of the countdown – number 1 will be posted on the morning of Friday 19th December – I will reveal all the locations with a larger photograph. I bet you can’t wait…
