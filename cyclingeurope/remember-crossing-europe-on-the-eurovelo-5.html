Remember Crossing Europe On The EuroVelo 5?

I’m currently reminiscing, putting together a short presentation* for my visit to Brussels next week where I’ve been asked to reflect upon my 2010 cycle from the UK to southern Italy along the EuroVelo 5. Browsing through the photos and website posts from the time (I came up with the idea in 2008 and completed the cycle in 2010) brings back fond memories. I suppose the one thing I should really do before next Friday is re-read the book: Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie…
