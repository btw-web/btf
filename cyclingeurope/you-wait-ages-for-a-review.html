You Wait Ages For A Review…

…then three – one for each book – come along all at once!

Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie:

“Five stars as it kept me smiling throughout, written simply but in a compelling style that made me want to get on my bike, forget Strava and just ride!” (‘Damo’, 9.10.17)

Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie:

“Brilliant! I couldn’t put this book down! Despite not being a cyclist myself I thoroughly enjoyed this eloquent man’s account of his travels. I highly recommend the “Reggie” series of books!” (Mrs R. Coleman, 9.10.17)

Spain to Norway on a Bike Called Reggie:

“It’s a good read; definitely worth the price. Makes you want to travel to that part of the world, even if not by bike.” (T. Fellowes, 9.10.17)
