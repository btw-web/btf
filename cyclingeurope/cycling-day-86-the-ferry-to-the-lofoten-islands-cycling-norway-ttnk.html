Cycling Day 86: The Ferry To The Lofoten Islands

There will be a little bit of cycling once I arrive at Moskenes (although to where I’m not quite sure) but for the time being it’s a case of watching the world go by (as long as they are in a ship) from the front of this streamlined boat. I can see Islands in the distance which I presume are the Lofotens but it is nice to be making progress without having to invest any physical effort whatsoever…
