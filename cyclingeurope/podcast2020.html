The Cycling Europe Podcast: The Story So Far In 2020

It’s been a busy year so far for The Cycling Europe Podcast and with episode 18 just published, it’s perhaps a good moment to look back over the six podcasts that have been published since January. All of the podcasts can be listened to directly from The Cycling Europe Podcast homepage or via your favourite purveyor of podcasts. Here are the links that will take you directly to The Cycling Europe Podcast on some of the most popular platforms:

  • iTunes
  • Spotify
  • Google Podcasts
  • acast
  • Overcast
  • PodBean
  • Player FM
  • YouTube

Below is a summary of what’s happened so far in 2020 and there’s more to come including interviews with round-the-World cyclist Ian Finley and cyclists and authors Abigail Melton and Lilith Cooper who will be chatting all about their new book Gears For Queers.

If you have an interesting cycling story to tell (it doesn’t need to be connected to Europe!), please get in touch by emailing podcast@CyclingEurope.org. It could be you headlining a future episode of The Cycling Europe Podcast…
