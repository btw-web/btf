“Good Vibrations Was Excellent – A Must Read”

50 reviews on Amazon.co.uk & still going strong with an average star rating of 4.7. Here is number 50;

“This book was just great. From the first page to the last I was never bored and was always desperately wondering what Andrew (the author) was going to do next. Everywhere he goes is described in amazing detail and the book is written in such a unique and truthful way which I don’t think I’ve ever come across before. It is both a travel journal and an adventure book filled with those random (but incredibly interesting) facts about the places he passed through which you would never know otherwise. I would thoroughly recommend Good Vibrations to everyone: cyclists, holiday makers and people just want a fun, different read for a change. This book is sure to make any reader want to plan their own spectacular European trip. That’s exactly what happened to me…”

JohnDAdamson, Amazon.co.uk
