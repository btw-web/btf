The Cycle Touring Festival: 4th-7th May 2018

Now in its 4th year, the Cycle Touring Festival returns to Clitheroe in Lancashire over the May Bank Holiday weekend. It’s a wonderful few days of chat, food, films and beer… What’s not to like? Whether you’ve cycled around the world or just down to the shops, you’ll find like-minded people who are simply interested in travelling on a bicycle. Once again I’ll be making my own contribution, reflecting upon 10 years of ‘Cycling Europe’ and also discussing podcasting (which is slightly disconcerting as I’m still climbing the learning curve myself…).
