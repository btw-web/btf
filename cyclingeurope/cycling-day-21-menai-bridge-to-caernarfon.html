Cycling Day 21: Menai Bridge To Caernarfon…

…via the eastern part of Anglesey.

After yesterday’s storm and my slightly truncated journey that stopped at the Victoria Hotel close to the Menai Suspension Bridge, I decided against a long ride to Holyhead and back for the sake of starting the cycle along the Lon alas Cymru where, err… it finishes. More of that tomorrow.

Instead, I spent most of the day exploring the eastern end of Anglesey and rather than read about it, you can watch the video. Lights? Camera? Action!
