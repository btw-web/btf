Cycling The EuroVelo 8 Mediterranean Route: The Brochure!

Every EuroVelo could do with one of these… Hopefully sometime soon they will. A glossy 90-page brochure giving an excellent overview of the EuroVelo 8 Mediterranean Route that I cycled back in 2013. The route is broken down into 15 sections starting in Cadiz and finishing in Cyprus and contains not just fabulous inspirational photography, but also plenty of practical advice about cycling in each of the countries. The only thing that’s missing is mention of the only book ever written about cycling the EuroVelo 8: the ‘inspirational’ Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie by Andrew P. Sykes.

You can download the brochure here.

More details on the European Cyclists’ Federation EuroVelo website.
