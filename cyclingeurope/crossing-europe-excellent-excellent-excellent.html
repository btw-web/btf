Crossing Europe: “Excellent, Excellent, Excellent”

“Absolutely adored this book. I’ve recently finished the trio of books and thoroughly enjoyed each of them in their own right, but, this was my favourite. What I loved most about this book is that it made me feel like I could do the same thing, I’ve not read many travel journals that have had the same effect. Thank you Mr Sykes!”
