Crossing Europe: ‘Worthing 2 Sicily’

“On the 6th July 2019 at 1.30pm we will be setting off to cycle over 1700 miles for Chestnut Tree House, unsupported. From The Rose and Crown, Worthing the journey will include riding through France  over the Alps into Switzerland, then into Italy and all the way to Catania, Sicily. The journey will take us through some amazing cities along the way; Paris, Milan, Rome and Naples. 24 Days of cycling covering 60-100 miles a day will take its toll, climbing over 20,000 metres during the journey. This second epic adventure will punish us relentlessly, stressing our bodies, camping in all weather conditions and environments with temperatures (potentially) as high as 40 degrees celsius. This will not be easy but all worth it for such an important charity in Sussex. The Aim is to raise £10,000. Can it be done?“

Spencer and Dan

You can follow Spencer and Dan via their Facebook page and support them on their Just Giving page. Good luck to them!
