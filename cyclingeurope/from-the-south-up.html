“From The South Up”

I had the opportunity of watching this film on the moderately-big screen of The Reliance in Leeds where local cycling entrepreneur Ian Street hosts his monthly ‘Leeds Bicycle Film Club‘. I challenge anyone to watch it and not want to jump onto the next plane to a far off place and start cycling… (If you are a fan of Ken Burns, you’re in for a treat.)

The filmmaker is Brendon Tyree from Sheffield. His website is here.
