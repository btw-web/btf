“A Great Read Which Will Inspire Any Cyclist!” *****

Another 5* rating for Good Vibrations: Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie:

“This book would tempt any cyclist (and non cyclist!) who has not been away on a tour to pack up the bike and pedal off. Andrew Sykes sums up the beauty and freedom of having two wheels and a tent! He manages to capture the spirit of adventure that the food tastes better, the beer tastes better, the views and scenery are better – all because he is travelling under his own power and at a slower pace. The author manages to capture the chance encounters with people he meets on the way. A great easy read which will inspire any cyclist! Only negative is one of envy – I want to do a trip like that!”

‘alasdair7’, Amazon.co.uk
