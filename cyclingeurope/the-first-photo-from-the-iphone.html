The First Photo From The iPhone

As predicted, much stress was involved in sending this picture. The instructions from the teenage technophile were far too brief to be of use and after a call to an Indian call centre and far too much alcohol, I have managed to start to get to grips with the machine. It is sleek, flat and hard, like a piece of slate. It is very cool and will, along with the SPOT (which has a far less trendy name) make me the most technologically switched on cyclist along the Eurovelo 5 this summer…
