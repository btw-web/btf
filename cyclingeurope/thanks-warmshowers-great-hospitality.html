Thanks WarmShowers – Great Hospitality!

I only stayed with four WarmShowers people during the summer’s trip along the Mediterranean coast but they were all great hosts. I’ve just been giving some feedback on the website and found this picture of Diego who hosted me at the end of June at his home near Cremona, Italy (it was cycling day 25). I can’t remember taking this photo; he must have sneaked into the garage to pose with the famous Reggie! Thank-you Diego (& Carla , Lucia  & Eddy  – my other WarmShowers hosts) for your much appreciated hospitality! All recommended. My own profile on WarmShowers is here by the way and I’m always willing to host so don’t hesitate to get in touch should you be passing through Reading along the Thames Valley in the UK.
