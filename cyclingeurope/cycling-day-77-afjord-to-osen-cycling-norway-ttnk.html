Cycling Day 77: Åfjord To Osen

Click here to see the detailed statistics of today’s cycle.

A much better day today. Weather just a grim as yesterday but everything else improved! I tried to focus the lens of the camera on the smaller things today (although I couldn’t stop myself from including a couple of big vistas). Great campsite by the coast with a warm welcome. Just the kind of place I’ve been dreaming of finding in northern Norway. And no midges!
