A Wonderful West Yorkshire Weekend

My final weekend of under-employment (I don’t feel that I have ever been really unemployed as I had plenty to do during my short career break) has been spent with friends, showing them the wonders of this corner of West Yorkshire. Wish me luck for tomorrow!
