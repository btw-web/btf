The Charity!

I’ve decided that, as an extra motivation to keep cycling (especially when I get to The Alps!) that it makes sense for my efforts to help support a charity. Over the last month I have been avidly watching Springwatch on the TV and then caught the BBC “Wild Night In” programme last Sunday. It was a mini-telethon raising money for the BBC Wildlife Fund and it struck me that it would be a great charity to support; working across the World (including the UK) to support small projects that help protect the diversity of the animal kingdom. And in doing so, supporting the local communities who live and share their environments with the animals in danger. On my daily commute to work I myself benefit from getting up close to the nature of South East Oxfordshire and it makes my cycle a fantastic little journey.
