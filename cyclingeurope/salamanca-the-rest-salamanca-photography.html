Salamanca: The Rest…

Call it ‘part 2’ or ‘the photos that weren’t taken from or of the Universidad Pontificia’. Look out for… what looks like a frequently egged former head of state in the Plaza Mayor, modern buildings that fit in seamlessly with the older ones, a yet to be carved coat of arms, some seashells, a peep into an abandoned plot of land in the heart of the city, every battery you might ever wish for, a curious brass plaque in the pavement and quite a few frogs (although not the one that you are supposed to find to bring you good luck).
