Good Vibrations: “I’d give it more stars if I could…” 5*

Another 5* rating on Amazon for Good Vibrations: Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie;

“I’m sure I heard Andrew on The Bike Show podcast giving a rough outline of his trip. It was such a good podcast I noted the title down & bought the book on the strength of the interview.Â Was not disappointed in the slightest; great read from start to finish. You feel like your on the journey with him (Reggie too..!!!). He met some great people on his way, made friends & Reggie sounds like a true star in the making. Some of the campsites almost sound like they came from a Carry On film! The drivers with a tendency to hit the horns will make you smile; if you’ve ever been to Italy you’ll know all about it. All in all a real easy read on cycle travel. Even if a cycling book may not be your thing it’s such an entertaining read its worth it. I’d give it more stars if I could..”
