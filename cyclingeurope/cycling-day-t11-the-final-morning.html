Cycling Day T+11: The Final Morning

Another one of those nights of ‘rest’ rather than ‘sleep’ on the Pride of Rotterdam. Having completely forgotten that the clocks change upon arrival in the UK I was up and out of the cabin by 6:30am continental time, 5:30am British summer time. Two and a half hours to spend watching the land next to the Humber estuary slowly drift by…  Short cycle into the centre of Hull and my train for Huddersfield – see below – departs at 9:39… ETA Huddersfield 11am? Followed by a 6km cycle to Blackley, journey’s end.
