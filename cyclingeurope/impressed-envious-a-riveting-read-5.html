“Impressed & Envious! A Riveting Read!” 5*

Another 5* review for the collection on Amazon.co.uk;

I honestly didn’t know what to expect when I decided to embark on this cycling trip from my armchair, but found almost immediately that I was absorbed in tales of highs, lows, comedy and adventure that kept me engrossed for hours! The author describes his travels with an attention to detail that allows you to feel as if you were cycling alongside him and as well as amazing scenery to enjoy we are also treated to laugh out loud moments at Andrew’s witty observations along the way. The book is an inspirational and riveting read that left me feeling both impressed and envious of the author’s achievement. I would thoroughly recommend this book and look forward to reading about further adventures by this author.

Thanks, ‘SEC’.
