The Ultimate Road Trip: Route 66…

…well, the one in Calderdale. Route 66 of Britain’s National Cycle Network.

“National Cycle Route 66 runs from central Manchester to Spurn Head via Bradford, Leeds, York, Beverley, and Kingston upon Hull.” according to those in charge. More details here. This afternoon part of route 66 was the final leg of a 46 km jaunt along the Calder Valley and back home again. Full route on Strava or Relive.cc (very cool!) Steep Lane is just that.
