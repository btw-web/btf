Tour De Yorkshire: Day 0 (Part 1)

Not sure how many of days 1 to 4 upon which there will be comment on CyclingEurope.org but here I am on the eve of ‘Le Tour Yorkshire’ in Leeds. The (non-cycling) action kicks off at 6pm in Millennium Square with the usual razzmatazz associated with any big sporting event. More of that later. In the meantime, the big news is the blue thing at the top of these photos; the sky is blue and the sun is shining. What’s more, the forecast for the weekend is looking good for some camping in Clitheroe at the Cycle Touring Festival as well as for the somewhat speedier action on this side of the Pennines. Watch this space…
