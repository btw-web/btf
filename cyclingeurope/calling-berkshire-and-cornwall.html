Calling Berkshire And Cornwall…

If you are near Reading on Monday 12th December or Portreath on Tuesday 13th December and would like to hear me speak about travelling from Tarifa to Nordkapp, this might be for you:

Both events are FREE(!) and open to the public and both have Facebook ‘event pages’: here’s the page for the Reading event, and here’s the page for the Portreath event. If you aren’t on Facebook, it shouldn’t be a problem turning up on the evening in Reading but for the event in Cornwall, please email me beforehand so that the organisers have an idea of numbers. (For non-members of ‘One and All Cycling‘, a club based in Truro, a small donation to club funds will be requested.) I’ll hopefully see some of you in December.
