Cycling Europe 2019: Spain And Portugal – The Movie

It’s time to cast your mind back to the summer of 2019 and my cycle from Santander in Spain to Coimbra in Portugal (via a train along the Douro Valley and Porto). A welcome return to continental long-distance cycling for the cyclist and a shakedown for Wanda the Koga WorldTraveller. She surpassed all expectations, munching her way through the kilometres with ease. So sit back and, for 90 minutes, let the film take the strain as Cycling Europe presents Spain And Portugal 2019: The Movie…
