In Praise Of… The Leeds Bicycle Film Club

The end of the month approaches…. which can only mean that The Cycling Europe Podcast is just around the corner. November’s podcast – episode 3 – will be film themed. Here’s a short extract from the part of the podcast where I visit The Leeds Bicycle Film Club. Hear the full report in the podcast on November 1st.

Aside from the film club, I’ll be chatting to film maker extraordinaire, Barry Godin, cycling adventurer Jimmy Hyland and the man who wrote the ‘definitive’ list of cycling films for the BFI, Harry Bunnell.
