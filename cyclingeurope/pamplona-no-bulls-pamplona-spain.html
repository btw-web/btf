Pamplona: No Bull(s)

Pamplona without the bulls this time. Look out for lots of independence-minded balconies in the city centre sporting flags along those lines (as well as one door), an English mother (or is it a stepmother or a mother-in-law?), a closed down Hemingway, some of the many pilgrims en route to Santiago, a sign that I’ve seen before but have yet to work out what it means and some hills that will be the focus of tomorrow’s attention…
