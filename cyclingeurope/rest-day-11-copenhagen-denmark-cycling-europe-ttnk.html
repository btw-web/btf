Rest Day 11: Copenhagen, Denmark

The first day of prolonged rain so probably better off the bike than on it. That said, my visit to the centre did involve a 7 km ride from the campsite (see previous post) and a very wet ride back in the late afternoon. I joined a three hour ‘free’ guided tour which was excellent and then wandered for a while. I have bought a new camping mat to replace the Thermarest and its ever-increasing bulge and a sleeping bag liner to make the cold nights ahead just a little warmer.
