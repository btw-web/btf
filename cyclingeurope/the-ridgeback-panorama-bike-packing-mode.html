The Ridgeback Panorama: Bike-Packing Mode

One of the nicest things about the Cycle Touring Festival here in Clitheroe is the conversations that take place between the more formal sit-down-and-listen sessions. Earlier this evening I was approached by a chap called Robert, an academic at Reading University. We got chatting and it turns out that he is also the proud own of a Ridgeback Panorama. However, he’s removed the pannier racks and fitted bike-packing bags. He’s sent through some pictures and the Panorama seems to adapt itself well. What do you think?
