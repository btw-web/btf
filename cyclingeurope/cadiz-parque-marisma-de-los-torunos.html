Cádiz: Parque Marisma De Los Torunos

The original plan, as noted in last night’s post, was to go kayaking. Alas it was too windy and all such activities had been cancelled. Instead I went with one of my teachers José (with the beard), his girlfriend and my fellow student Glen to the Parque Metropolitano Marisma de los Torunos y Pinar de la Algaida. There’s a mouthful. Interestingly (and not before time I hear some of you shout) it makes a great place to go for a cycle as you can see from the pictures. You’ve been waiting for some practical cycling suggestions ever since I arrived in Spain, no? Well I hope the five week wait was worth it. A siesta beckons, then a bit of packing and then a final wander around the city that I have grown to like very much indeed. Cádiz will be missed.
