“Brilliant” 5*

“Sometimes when you read the endorsements on a book you wonder if they can possibly be true. Have a good look at the back cover of Good Vibrations if you get chance & I guarantee they’re 100% accurate. The book is a very easy, compelling read. The author has a wonderfully easy way of bringing to life his experiences as he cycled across Europe on Reggie, his trusty bike, from Reading to Brindisi in southern Italy, bringing two years of planning to reality. Perhaps most importantly he makes the reader believe that whilst it may have taken considerable effort to plan and execute, it is something that anyone with an interest in cycling and travel could achieve. If you enjoy cycling or know someone who does, buy it and you won’t be disappointed – I wasn’t.”

Steph Pandit, Amazon.co.uk
