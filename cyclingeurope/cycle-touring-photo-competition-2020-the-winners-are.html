Cycle Touring Photo Competition 2020: The Winners Are…

…not yet decided. Give me a chance! The deadline has only just passed. The way it will work is as follows. The judges will now deliberate and come up with a shortlist of ten photographs. Those ten shortlisted winners will all win a brand new EuroVelo map. There will then be a public vote that will start on Tuesday 2nd June here on CyclingEurope.org to determine the first, second and third placed photographs. Those people who submitted more than one photograph will only have one of their photographs judged. If you submitted more than one photo, you can find out which one made the cut by browsing the entries below.
