Crossing Europe… v. Along The Med… (…On A Bike Called Reggie)

The 218th review for Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie was posted on Amazon.co.uk yesterday. That’s exactly twice as many for Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie which now has 109 reviews. When it comes to the ratings, however, Along The Med is still in front with an average review of 4.6 compared to Crossing Europe‘s 4.5.

Crossing Europe was published over five years ago in August 2011 whereas Along The Med has only been out for just over two years so in terms of reviews, it has time to catch up. If you are one of the many people to have taken time to write down your thoughts about the books either on Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.com, iBooks or Kobo, it is very much appreciated. Thanks.

And don’t forget… Spain to Norway on a Bike Called Reggie is out in spring 2017, published by Summersdale and available, as they say, in all good bookshops.
