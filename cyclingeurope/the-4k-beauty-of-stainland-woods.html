The 4K Beauty Of Stainland Woods

One of those posts for the ‘nothing-to-do-with-cycling’ category but I have no hesitation in sharing with you this video that I made earlier today during my lockdown walk through the local woods. I am so grateful to live in a place that, within a few minutes of my front door, offers me such wonderful countryside.

This video was made in 4K; one of the first that I have dared to put together in the larger format but even on my non-4K screen and TV, you can see the the image is wonderfully clear. My plan is to use the 4K format on future cycles using a combination of footage taken on the iPhone (used for this video) and on my Canon XA-40 camcorder which I bought in anticipation of the planned trip to Japan that has now been put on hold. I fear, however, that I may need a more powerful computer to cope with the data. If anyone has any 4K advice, it would be interesting to hear what it is. In the meantime, sit back and enjoy the wander through the woods and across the golf course…
