Cycle Touring Photo Competition 2020: The Winners!

The voting has now closed and the votes have been counted. Many thanks to everyone who submitted their photos and the hundreds of people who have taken the time to vote over the course of the past week. Thanks also to Cicerone Press and ECF EuroVelo for offering the prizes. But, without further ado, the winners are…

1st Place: Emma Silversides – winner of three Cicerone cycling guides of her choice and a EuroVelo overview map. Congratulations Emma!

2nd Place: Paul Berney – winner of two Cicerone cycling guides of his choice and a EuroVelo overview map. Congratulations Paul (& son…)!

3rd Place: Craig Dodson – winner of one Cicerone cycling guide of his choice and a EuroVelo overview map. Congratulations Craig!

Congratulations to everyone who made the shortlist. You have also won a EuroVelo overview map that will be on its way to you very soon. Well done!
