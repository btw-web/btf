Cycle Touring Photo Competition 2020: Update

Over 100 entries have been submitted to the Cycle Touring Photography Competition 2020! And there’s still three weeks to go before the deadline of the 31st May. All you need to do is send your best photos from 2019 (that must include a picture of your bike on a cycle tour of some kind) to office@CyclingEurope.org. Great prizes from Cicerone Press and the European Cyclists’ Federation. Full competition details at CyclingEurope.org/Compeition. Below are the entries that have so far been entered into the competition. Hopefully they will inspire you to send in your own photos. Good luck!

All of the top 10 shortlisted photos will receive a EuroVelo map courtesy of the European Cyclists’ Federation.
