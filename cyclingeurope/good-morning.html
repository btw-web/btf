Good Morning?

Last night we had an almighty storm; it’s a pity that I only heard it rather than watched it because there were more flashes of light than at a celebrity wedding.
The downside is that it is continuing to rain. Perhaps rain in these parts is sponsored by Carling…
My plan for the day (only just hatched so it may change!) is to stay put and get myself ready for the final country on my trip; Italy, both in terms of planning and the more practical things such as doing my washing. Expect pictures of washing machines rather than mountain vistas in today’s entries on the blog. However for the timebeing, I’m staying in the tent!
