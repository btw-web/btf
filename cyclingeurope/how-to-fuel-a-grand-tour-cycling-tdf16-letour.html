How To Fuel A Grand Tour

The Tour de France 2016 is almost upon us and SIS have created an infographic that gives an insight into how the riders in Team Sky stayed fuelled during those long days in the saddle. I have to say my own approach as I cycled across Europe was somewhat less scientific but then again it did take me nearly four months to cycle from Tarifa to Nordkapp. Perhaps with some SIS energy inside me, I might have cycled somewhat quicker. The Tour de France kicks off this weekend in Normandy and I’m delighted to see that Mont Ventoux – my own mountain success back in 2013 – has been included once again. Image courtesy of SIS…
