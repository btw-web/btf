CyclingEurope.org: Social Spring Clean, In, Err… Winter

Perhaps it was the anticipation of watching tonight’s episode of The Apprentice. Actually, probably not… But anyway, I spent a little time this morning sprucing up the ‘brand’ of CyclingEurope.org. It’s something I do on an irregular basis, usually when I’m a bit bored. This time, however, I do like the results.

Not much of an impact here in the website (in fact, none at all), but things have been freshened up on Facebook, Twitter and, more significantly, YouTube where the front page is much stronger with the videos grouped according to their topic. I’ve also uploaded the latest episode of the podcast to the YouTube page. What are you waiting for? Go have a browse!
