Huddersfield Town 2, Yorkshire Air Ambulance 1,000 (£)

Well, actually it was Huddersfield Town 2, Yeovil 0. But fear not; I’m not about to start giving a classified check of the League 1 results every Sunday morning,  As mentioned a few posts ago, my brother (and several hundred other people) cycled from Yeovil to Huddersfield in aid of the Yorkshire Air Ambulance ‘Keep It Up’ campaign. He successfully arrived in time for kick-off and raised over £1,000 in the process. Well done to him! Here is a picture of him (on the far right, crouching) with some of the other cyclists. Should you wish to read about the match itself, please follow this link. And it’s not too late to sponsor him…
