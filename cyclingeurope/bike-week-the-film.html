Bike Week: The Film

There is a theme developing here… The second film of the day, this time from Cycling UK (yes, the CTC as was) promoting Bike Week:

Bike Week is an annual opportunity to get out and enjoy yourselves cycling. Run by the world’s oldest transport organisation and membership charity, Cycling UK, Bike Week promotes the fun and freedom to be found in the saddle, and shows how cycling can easily become a part of everyday life – by encouraging ‘everyday cycling for everyone’.

With the 2017 event taking place 10-18th June, Bike Week is set to bring over half a million people together to join in events right across the UK. With family-friendly off-road rides, free Bike Checks and 20% off cycle servicing in Halfords stores, longer distance challenge rides, and even naked bike rides, Bike Week really does have something for everyone!
