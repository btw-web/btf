Thank-you!

You did it! Just after midnight last night I sent £202 to BBC Children In Need thanks to all the people who downloaded the Kindle version of ‘Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie‘ in the first two weeks of November. Thanks if you were one of them. Enjoy the read! As you make your way through the book, remember that you can see all the pictures that I took as I cycled from southern England to southern Italy on the Facebook page. If e-reading is not your thing, it’s worth pointing out that the updated version of ‘Good Vibrations: Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie‘ (new cover, that’s all!) is available on Amazon.co.uk. However you choose to read the book, feedback is always welcome either via email, via the Facebook page or via a review on one of the sites where it is available for purchase. And don’t forget that you can follow either myself or Reggie on Twitter. Yes, I have a bicycle that tweets. You can even catch up on plans for the next cycle from Athens to Cadiz, Spain along the Eurovelo 8 at CyclingEurope.org. There is no escape… Enjoy the weekend!
