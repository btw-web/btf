Goodbyes… And Some Cycling Artwork

In a few days I will leave Reading after just over fifteen years studying, working and living in the Berkshire town. One nice thing about doing so is using my departure as an excuse to meet up with friends that I will leave behind. This morning I said goodbye to Sian, Mike, their two children Owen and Cerys and Alexa. I used to work with Sian and Alexa and they have remained good friends ever since. As we chatted over coffee, Owen drew and below is the result of his artistic efforts, a picture of me and the bike… I like the hat!
