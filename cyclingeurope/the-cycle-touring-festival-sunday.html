The Cycle Touring Festival: Sunday

What a good day… It’s been the fourth edition of Laura and Tim Moss’ Cycle Touring Festival in Clitheroe; I’ve been to three of them and it has been the best I’ve experienced so far. Great people, interesting stories and with my podcast hat on today was spent listening, questioning and recording. Episode 006 of The Cycling Europe Podcast should be a good one…
