Cádiz: The Last Post

Once this is uploaded I’m off back to the flat to pack (listening to the leaders debate as I do so), have a shower and then hit the sack in preparation for a 6am rise and a coach to Tarifa at 7am. My time in Cádiz is done. I have learnt much, I have met many interesting people and especially today, I have taken lots of photographs. Many of things you see below I have been walking past for weeks and when I look back upon my time here, they are the things that will come to mind first. It is a city I would whole-heartedly recommend.
