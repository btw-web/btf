Czech Out The Latest Review

Lee from the Czech Republic has read and reviewed Good Vibrations: Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie on Amazon. Here is what he has to say;

All I can say this is a wonderful book to read and you really sense you are sharing the journey from Reading to Brindisi with Andrew and Reggie… If, like me, you like your cycle tour books with a good sense of humour about life on the road then you cannot go wrong reading Andrew’s tale. One thing I’ve taken from this book is that there is a European-wide cycling network out there though the bureaucrats in Brussels don’t appear to have fully funded it! It reminds me of the scene from Blackadder where is handed a blank scroll claiming it to be a map of the area he will visit and can he fill it in as he goes along. Well, this is what Andrew has done by writing his book. He’s filled in all the detail on the Eurovelo 5 route for us all to enjoy.
