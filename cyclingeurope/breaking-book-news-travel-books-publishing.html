Breaking Book News…

I am delighted to announce that ‘Crossing Europe on a Bike Called Reggie‘ has now broken into the highly competitive Indian book market. It is currently the number 1 cycling eBook available on Amazon.in! 

(OK, I admit it, it’s sold one copy but it’s a start…)

Meanwhile back in the UK, ‘Along The Med on a Bike Called Reggie’ continues to gain more fans and more accolades from the reading public. Ian Southwood is the latest reader to post a five star review for the book;

“Outstanding: I have now read both of Reggie and Andrew’s rides.  writing brings home… the efforts, humour and immense satisfaction of completing an epic ride. I look forward to the next adventure.”

It’s good to note that the second book – Along The Med… – now has an average Amazon rating which matches the first book – Crossing Europe… – of 4.6/5.0.

Meanwhile over on Apple iBooks it was equally great to see both books in the travel Top 10 this morning.

If you are one of the people who has chosen to read either the paperbacks (also available from Waterstone’s) or the eBooks (also available on Kobo where Along The Med… is also the number 1 cycling book), your support is very much appreciated!
