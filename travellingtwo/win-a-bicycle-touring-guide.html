Wondering where to go on your next bike tour? Enter our newest contest, and you might find yourself inspired.

That’s because we have 5 copies of a Guide to Bicycle Tours Around The World to give away. It’s worth $28 U.S. if you buy it online, but going free to our lucky winners!

The guide is produced by Biciklo – a search engine that only lists bike tours. The magazine itself isn’t just a catalogue of tours. It has in-depth articles about interesting destinations.

Mountain biking in Maine, riding in British Columbia’s Gulf Islands and the inaugural journey of the Vuelta Sudamericana are just some of the areas the current guide covers. You’ll also find equipment lists for touring, and general bike touring tips.

Both the magazine and the Biciklo website were founded by two keen bike tourists: David & Hannah. Together they’ve cycled in North America, South America, Africa, Asia, Europe and the Caribbean.

“We discovered that finding the right tour was a time-consuming process,” says David. “For example, a Google search for ‘cycling tour France’ turns up 13 million results!”

Biciklo aims to make that search easier. You can sort tours by country, length or type and they list over 8,000 tours from 500 operators.
