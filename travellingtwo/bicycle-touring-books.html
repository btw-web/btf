Bike Touring Basics

In 2011, we created Bike Touring Basics as an introduction to the essentials of bike touring.

The information is now somewhat dated, but we invite you to download a copy. The book covers topics such as which touring bike to buy, budgeting, equipment we like and what to expect from a day on the road.

Recently we took the information in Bike Touring Basics, fully updated it and incorporated it into our Bike Touring Survival Guide. Download a free preview or get the whole book.

20 Tips For Cycling The Netherlands

We’ve lived in the Netherlands since 2009, and we believe it’s one of the best places in the world to bike tour.

There are bicycle paths everywhere, a moderate climate and a surprisingly diverse landscape for such a small country. Over the space of a few days you can explore lakes, forests, beaches, sand dunes and so much more.

To celebrate the Netherlands, and to encourage you to come cycling here, we created this book of Dutch cycling tips.
