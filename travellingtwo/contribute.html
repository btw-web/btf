Contribute To TravellingTwo

Have something to say to other cyclists or just love the site?

You can help us keep this site looking its best and expanding all the time to add new content for cyclists. Here are the ways you can contribute.

Share The Love:

If we write something you enjoy, tell someone. We really appreciate it. You can also tell us what you like about TravellingTwo.

Shop through our site:

We always give our honest opinions about bike touring equipment. If we don’t like something, we’ll tell you. On the other hand, when we love something, we’ll also point you to a shop where you can buy it. Mainly we recommend Wiggle andÂ REI. They are two retailers that we’ve shopped from repeatedly and always been happy with.

If you click from our site through to these retailers and buy something, we will get a small commission (between 2-7% of the purchase price). This does not raise the price that you pay but the money helps us to keep the site running and write more articles about bike touring. Thanks! We appreciate your support.

Be a part of our:

  • Podcast – Let us interview you over the phone about your latest tour or any aspect of bicycle touring and we’ll include the interview in an upcoming podcast.
  • A Cyclist’s Life Series – Tell us about a week in your life on tour. We want your highlights, challenges and reactions. It can be a current week or one from a past trip. For this we need:
    -7 days written in a diary format, outlining the daily routines and events in a bike tourist’s life (these can be short; just one line if the day was uneventful, up to 300-400 words if it was a big day)
    -A photo or two to go along with the diary
    -An introduction paragraph about you
    -Any relevant links you want included
  • Q&A Interview – We pose 10 questions by email about a destination you’ve been to or a trip you’ve taken.

For all of these, or if you just have an idea of an article you’d like to see us write, Contact Us and we’ll go from there.
