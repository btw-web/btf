Getting Inspired With The Do Lectures

If you’re looking for inspiration to get out and do something, you could do a lot worse than watch some of the Do Lectures.

The mission of the Do Lectures is, in the group’s own words, “to give you the tools and the desire to change the things you care about”.

There are plenty of fine talks to watch but why not start with our favourite talks: one from bike tourist Al Humphreys and a second from mountaineer Paul Deegan. If this doesn’t make you want to get out there and tour, nothing will…
