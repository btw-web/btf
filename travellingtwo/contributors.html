The image on the front cover of Bike Touring Basics was taken at 4,500m on the Puna de Atacama in northern Chile.

It was part of a multi-month bike tour in South America, by Harriet and Neil Pike (http://www.andesbybike.com).


Photo by Neil & Harriet Pike (http://www.andesbybike.com)

“We were doing a loop which took a couple of weeks with no shops and not much water, so we had heavy bikes. You can see that Harriet’s 10 liter Ortlieb water bag, on the back of the bicycle, is reasonably full! In the photo we’re above the Salar de Maricunga and are descending a rarely used mine road, heading to the stunning Laguna Negro Francisco in Tres Cruces National Park,” says Neil.
