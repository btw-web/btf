Roberts Roughstuff

Price: Frames start from about $1,500. Complete, bespoke bikes start around $3,200.
Specs: Steel frame with 26″ wheels. Designed for a mix of on and off-road riding. Low centre of gravity for a more stable ride. Rohloff is an option.
Who has used it: Josie Dew, Myra & Simon, Wayne Maurer
Upsides: A beauty of a bike from one of the best known touring bike builders in the UK.
Downsides: The price tag can break the bank.
