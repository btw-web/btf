Bruce Gordon Rock ‘n Road Tourer

Price: $3,349 including racks.
Specs: Steel frame, welded in California. Deore XT components. Either 26″ or 28″ hand-built wheels. Bruce Gordon’s own racks. Space for 3 water bottles. Comes in metallic red, blue and black. Drop or flat bars.
Who has used it: Neil Gunton
Upsides: Handcrafted excellence from a one-man operation with great pride in the product.
Downsides: Budget for shipping and assembly costs on top of the list price.
