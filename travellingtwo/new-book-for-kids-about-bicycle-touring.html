New Children’s Picture Book Features Bicycle-Touring Dogs

Bicycle-touring authors Fin & Zoa are at it again. They’ve just published Lost in London; the latest story in their ‘dog detectives’ series of children’s picture books.

The books centre around the adventures of two dogs who cycle around the world, solving mysteries along the way.

It’s a tale inspired by real life, because Fin & Zoa have been cycling around the world with their two dogs – Jack and Paco – since 2008.

Now they’re on a book tour around the UK to celebrate the latest addition to the series, so keep an eye out for these 2 cyclists and 2 dogs on the roads of Britain. They’ll be in London around mid September.

You can also follow along on their blog, and see a map of exactly where they’re heading.
