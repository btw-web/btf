Winner of the Vaude Road I Handlebar Bag

Celebrating the start of the week by announcing a contest winner is always fun. Keep reading to discover who the lucky person was, in our contest to win a fantastic red-and-black Road I Handlebar Bag from Vaude…

Between Twitter and the comments on the post, we had around 200 entries!

We like to give everyone an equal chance, so we used Random.org twice to pick the winner: once to decide if the winner should come from Twitter or the comments, and once to zero in on a single person.

And the winner is…..

Lucy was actually the last person to enter the contest, which just shows that it’s never too late to stick your name in the draw! We know she’ll give it a good test run, because she’s cycling 1,100 kilometers in France and Spain this coming summer. Enjoy, Lucy. We’ll be in touch shortly.

Thanks to everyone else who entered, and to Vaude for donating the prize. Keep watching for the next giveaway. We already have one planned for January!
