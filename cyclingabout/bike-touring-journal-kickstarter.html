Check Out This Beautifully Illustrated Bike Touring Journal on Kickstarter

If you fancy getting back to the old ways, take a look at the Bike Touring Journal on Kickstarter.

Claire from Punctures and Panniers has teamed up with Polish artist Ania to create a fun, illustrated travel journal for those who love travelling by bike. This isn’t an empty book, there’s lots of illustrations, travel tips, maps, quotes, recipes and envelopes inside. The journal is printed in the UK on 100% recycled paper, it uses biodegradable inks and employs a 100% natural rubber band to keep it together.

The price is £20 for one, and £35 if you’d like two, and they will be shipping before Christmas this year.
