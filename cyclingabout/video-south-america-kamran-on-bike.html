Video: Cycling South America With Kamran On Bike

A recap from Kamran On Bike of his 13,000km (8000mi) bicycle ride across the entire length of South America from January 2016 to March 2017. This video is nothing short of stunning!

Born and raised in Pakistan, Kamran holds a PhD in computer science. Other than being a computer wizz, Kamran takes some of the most stunning photographs of any bike tourer I follow.

Start: Ushuaia, Argentina (54°S latitude)
End: Necocli, Colombia (8°N latitude)
Total distance: 12,994km (8074mi)
Total days on tour: 458
Countries Cycled: Argentina, Chile, Bolivia, Peru, Ecuador, Colombia
