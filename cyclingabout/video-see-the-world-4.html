Video: See The World 4 – USA’s National Parks by Iohan Gueorguiev

After completing the Great Divide Route, Iohan makes the journey from Colorado to California, visiting many of USA’s national parks including; the Canyonlands, Arches, Grand Canyon, Death Valley, Yosemite, and everything in between. You will need 62 minutes of your time to see what all of these places look like in winter.

Iohan Gueorguiev from Bike Wanderer creates some of the most popular bicycle touring videos on YouTube. You can read his eight-part blog series about his trip on his website HERE.
