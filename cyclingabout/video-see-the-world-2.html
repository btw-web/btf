Video: See The World 2 – Alaska

Iohan heads out on a 37-day bicycle trip across Alaska, checking out the Denali Highway, Dalton Highway, Denali National Park, then rolls through Palmer, Glenallen, Alcan and across to Haines in Canada. This 54 minute film beautifully captures his adventure through some of the most picturesque parts of the world.

Iohan Gueorguiev from Bike Wanderer makes some of the most popular bicycle touring films on YouTube. To read more about his trip in Alaska head to THIS page. There’s six blogs with photos, a bit about the music and his sponsors.
