Video: Paul VDP x Falls Creek

I just got back from a three-week bicycle tour, shooting a documentary with a group of friends who’ve never been bicycle touring before. One of those friends was Paul van der Ploeg; a world champion mountain biker!

Falls Creek Resort, which is located in the Victorian alpine region in Australia, invited us to do some mountain biking on their freshly groomed trails. I couldn’t resist pulling my Panasonic GH4 out to get some action shots of Paul doing what he does best!

If you’d like to get to know Paul, you can read an interview we conducted with him HERE.

Thanks again to Falls Creek for looking after us.
