Video: The Man Who Wanted To See It All – Heinz Stücke

Heinz Stücke (born 11 January 1940) is a German bicycle tourer who has covered more ground than anyone else. Between 1962 and 2010, he cycled more than 609,000 kilometres (378,000 mi) and visited 195 countries and 78 territories. As the most travelled cyclist ever, we have a lot to learn from him.

I found the way of life I enjoy, and what is better than to follow a life which is fulfillment. The journey is my fulfillment.

Heinz Stücke
