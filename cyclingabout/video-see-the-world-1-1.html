Video: See The World 1 – The North (1/2)

Iohan goes for an adventure across the northernmost regions of Canada, starting at the frozen Arctic Ocean. He then uses the Ice Highways connecting the remote communities in the north to make his way south to the warmer regions of the Yukon and British Columbia. The trip took 30 days and covered around 3000km.

Part one is 28 minutes – make sure you click through to part two for the rest of the story.

Iohan Gueorguiev from Bike Wanderer makes some of the most popular bicycle touring films on YouTube. There’s a little more information about this trip on his website.
