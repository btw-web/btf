Video: Volcano Hunting in Central America // By Bike, Board & Foot [EP.16]

Follow along as I bike, hike and slide the Central American Volcanic Arc – from Nicaragua to Guatemala.

It was so much fun climbin’ into craters, slidin’ down muddy slopes, chillin’ next to iridescent green lakes and watchin’ freaking lava explode into the sky. This short-chain of volcanoes has the full gamut of geo activity – it’s a must-do if you’re ready to witness the sheer power of mother nature!
