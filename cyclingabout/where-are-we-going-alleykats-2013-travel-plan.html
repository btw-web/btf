Where Are We Going? Alleykat’s 2013 Travel Plan

Our plans are always changing. But this will give you an idea or what is happening over the next year or so! The following will hopefully allow us to ride through many countries in their best seasons.

December: We will be riding across Northern Greece from Albania to Turkey.

January: We will ride to Southern Turkey from Gallipoli, where we will spend two to four weeks working on an organic farm in the mountains.

February: We will be flying to Germany to pick up our new tandem frame. This visit will be very brief! We will then get back to Istanbul for a while, and commence our 4-6 week tandem ride across Turkey.

March: We will finish riding through Turkey and enter Iran late in March.

April: We will spend all of this month getting from one side of Iran to the other.

May: Iran/Turkmenistan

June: Uzbekistan

July: Kazakhstan or Tajikistan/Krygyzstan depending on political situation or direction we choose.

August: China

September: Mongolia/China

October: We will arrive in Beijing, China and shortly after fly to Kathmandu, Nepal later in October for a month of hiking the Annapurna Circuit and Everest Base Camp.

November: Later in November we will be riding through Nepal and into Northern India.

December: India

January: India

February: Fly out of India and into either Southern China or Vietnam.

If you have any tips and tricks, or places to stay in the above countries, please shoot us an email or drop us a message.
