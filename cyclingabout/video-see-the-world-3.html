Video: See The World 3 – Great Divide in Winter

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route is the longest off-pavement route in the world. It follows the continental divide (Rocky Mountains) all the way from Canada to Mexico. Most people ride it in the summer, but Iohan could only swing it in winter. Despite having previously ridden the Arctic ice roads of Canada, the conditions were even colder on the divide. You will need 55 minutes of your time to complete this beautiful film.
