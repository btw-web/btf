Video: See the World 11 – Panama

Iohan makes some of the rawest bike travel videos out there. They’re long, but showcase a lot of scenery and conversations he has throughout his trips. The POV-style filming makes you really feel like you’re along on the ride too.

Episode 11 has Iohan riding into Panama through the Rio Sereno border. Despite hearing that Panama wasn’t interesting, Iohan found the whole ride to be absolutely amazing, the camping spectacular and the dirt roads and horse trail unbelievable. You can read Iohan’s blog series about his trips HERE.
