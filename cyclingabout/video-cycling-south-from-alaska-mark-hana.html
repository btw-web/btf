Video: Cycling South From Alaska With Mark and Hana

Mark and Hana have been cycling south from Alaska since 2016. Their trip is very well documented using big photo stories on the website Highlux Photography. If you’re into the smaller backroads and off-road routes, their resources and photos will give you a great taste of where a bike can take you in the Americas.

To be able to go out and explore continents on a bicycle enables you to get a layer deeper into those societies. You’re always aware of what’s happening; the subtle changes in the landscape as they take place, from the mountains to the desert and the jungle… I believe it’s the ultimate way to travel.

Mark Watson
