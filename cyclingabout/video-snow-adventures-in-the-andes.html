Video: Snow Adventures In The Andes On My Touring Bike [EP.7]

This month has been a pretty wild ride thanks to some unseasonal snowfall here in Peru. A cold spell hit hard recently, killing hundreds of mountain people who didn’t have the resources to keep warm. I was one of the lucky ones up around 5000M in a remote part of the country.

This video is the documentation of my ride from La Paz, Bolivia to somewhere deep in the mountains of Peru. Enjoy!
