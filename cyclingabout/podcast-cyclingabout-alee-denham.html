Podcast: CyclingAbout With Alee Denham

https://soundcloud.com/vegan_athletic/cycling-about-with-alee-denham

Vegan Athletic Podcast: “I can’t think of anyone better to talk to when it comes to bike touring, bikepacking and adventure cycling! Alee Denham has a long history with the bicycle, so I did my best to pick apart all the great tips and tricks that he has accumulated over the years. From how to let people from other countries know you are vegan, what to pack for short and long trips and what is the best choice of equipment for everything in between. 

This guy is a very good human with very good things to talk about indeed.”

Skip through to 9 minutes and 30 seconds to get to the bike touring bits. The podcast is also available to download on iTunes or you can find it in podcast-land under “Vegan Athletic Podcast”.
