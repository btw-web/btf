Video: The Bicycle Touring Family That Travelled From Quebec To Central America

Meet the Conks. This family of four sold their home, bought bikes and went bicycle touring from Quebec to Guatemala!

Chris and Julie Conk took this 11 month trip with their two children (8 & 11 years old) to give them some perspective, to allow them to daydream and to grow together as a family.

This uplifting video was put together by Bryan from LiveHigh, who is just about to ride bikes across the USA with his brother. You can check out his other videos on his YouTube channel, or follow him on Facebook.
