Video: Cycling Central Asia with Pete and Mary

Pete and Mary of TwoOnFourWheels made some cracking videos on their trip through Europe, Central Asia, China and parts of South East Asia. This one is probably my favourite, perhaps as it brings back so many amazing memories from a special part of the world for me.

Tragically, Pete and Mary were hit by a truck in Thailand and both passed away. RIP guys and thanks for being such an inspiration to us all!
