Video: WWOOFing Isn’t Necessary For Cats Who Farmstay Volunteer

Our first time volunteering at a farm stay was a very rewarding experience. We stayed at the Dikencik farm in southern Turkey through the Helpx program. In this documentary, we discuss volunteer farm stays, talk about our experience, interview our hosts and get the low down from the other colourful volunteers! If you’ve never farm-stayed before, sign yourself up and give it a go.

Music on guitar by the talented volunteers at Dikencik, Mahna and Ansel. Edited on iPad with Pinnacle Studio and shot on a Panasonic LX5 compact camera.
