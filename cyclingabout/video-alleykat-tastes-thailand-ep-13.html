Video: Alleykat Tastes Thailand (EP.13)

Thailand is a paradise for all things food! It’s home to the best street food in the world, without doubt. The countryside is often stunning to ride through and the drivers are always kind and courteous to cycle tourists. There are western comforts in every small town in the form of 7Eleven convenience stores and clean budget hotels. We love Thailand!

Edited in Pinnacle Studio for iPad, filmed on a Panasonic V700 camcorder.
