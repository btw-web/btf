Video: Bolivia – The Easy Way

Bolivia – The easy way from Florian Schmale on Vimeo.

In 2018 I crossed Bolivia on a bicycle. I choose an easy route – but really easy it was not.
I got stuck in mud, stuck in the world biggest salt desert, Salar de Uyuni and got annoyed by a lot of flat tires. An Adventure it was for sure!
