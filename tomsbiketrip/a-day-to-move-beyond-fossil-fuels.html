A Day To Move Beyond Fossil Fuels

Cycling legend Kim Nguyen, the tenacious chap behind the Ride Planet Earth bicycle journey from Australia to Copenhagen for the COP15 conference, has been hard at work since finishing his epic ride. He’s asked me to help him promote Moving Planet, a day of action on September 24th (that’s this weekend!) in support of transitioning as quickly as possible to a global low-carbon society. It looks to be gaining a lot of momentum — well done Kim and co!

Moving Planet is a worldwide rally to demand solutions to the climate crisis. Come on bike, on skates, on a board, or just on foot. Come with your neighbors and your friends, your family and your co-workers. Get ready to be a part of something HUGE.

Pop over to the Moving Planet website to find out if there’s an event planned near you — or there’s always the Facebook group…
