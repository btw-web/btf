Cycling the Carolinas

We are in quarantine!!  A long story made short:  we were once again asked to help with Samaritan’s Purse, a ministry that does relief work.  We were headed to Honduras to help after the hurricane.  We met the team in Greensboro N.C. to fly out the next day.  However, one of the team members became sick with COVID and that put our whole team into quarantine.  We both feel great and have not been sick…but we are isolated and following the rules.

We miss our bikes and are really excited to get back to cycling but since we had some free time (about 14 days) we decided to put a video together.  This is a small glimpse of our time in the Carolinas on bicycles.  We were enamored with the trees, loved the grand old homes, and once again, all the people we met as we rode were truly the best part of all!  Thank you Carolinas for your rich heritage, beautiful views, and amazing people.
