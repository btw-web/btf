The 23 Best Online Stores for Bicycle Touring and Bikepacking

A list of the best online bike shops for road, MTB, touring, commuting, bikepacking.
Parts, camping gear, cycling clothes, panniers, bicycles and more

We all agree that buying from a physical bicycle shop is the best way to go, but when it comes to bicycle touring gear not all of us are so lucky to have a good bike store nearby.

Shopping online ain’t easy either, there’s a ton of great bicycle stores on the web, but only a few of them have touring or bikepacking gear.

So what are the best online shops for bicycle touring and bikepacking? We did this time-consuming research for you and found out some great ones, that we decided to list in this article.

All of them have a great range of components, cycling gear, clothes, and such. But only a few have a good choice of touring bicycles and even less sell lightweight camping gear.

We hope this list can help those of you who are looking for a great online bicycle store where to purchase your gear for your next big tour.

The online bike stores in the list below are separated among those based in UK and Europe, and the American ones
of course shopping closer is environmentally better and usually cheaper.
Anyway many of these bicycle shops do deliver worldwide

The best Europe and UK Based Bicycle Shops

The United Kingdom has a long tradition of bicycles. Many historical manufacturers are from here and bike culture in the UK is huge in all disciplines, bike commuting is also very widespread. Here’s a list of bicycle shops that are based in the United Kingdom, most of them ship all over Europe.

Evans Cycles.com

The UK-based shop Evans Cycles is one of the few bicycle stores to have a specific Touring Bicycles category, with 26 different models from Koga, Cinelli, Treck, Ridgeback, Genesis, Specialized, Fuji, Jamis and Dawes. They also have Altura, Ortlieb, FWE, Topeak and Carradice panniers, Tubus, Topeak and other brands racks.

Among the accessories: Map Holder, Garmin (and other brands) GPSs, GoPro cam and mounts, Phone cases, etc. A vast choice of cycling clothes and components.

They ship worldwide, with a 90-days return policy and a 0% finance for UK users.

Visit Evans Cycles.com

Chain Reaction Cycles

Chain Reaction Bicycle Store has a very well organized website, with categories for clothing, components and everything else, dived under each discipline.

Their top touring products are Cube Creme Ghost, Topeak Jurney single wheel trailer, Avenir Entrepid Child Trailer.

Plenty of casual shoes with clips and clothes. Lynskey titanium rack, Garmin, and GoPro cams. They are Irish but they literally ship all over the globe (almost, sorry Mozambique).

Visit Chain Reaction Bicycle Store

Cyclestore

Another UK based shop. Classic Kona and Cannondale touring bikes. Some trailers, a good range of panniers (Thule, Ortlieb, Altura, and some other brands), GoPro clones, and other action cams.

The website has a Camping and Hiking category, but there’s almost nothing in it. Ships almost worldwide.

Visit Cyclestore

Tredz Bicycles

A great choice of touring bikes, seriously one of the best online bicycle shops out there. Cube, Ridgeback, Cinelli, Surly, Dawes, Kona, Bobbin, and more. A wide range of components, cycling clothes, accessories like panniers and bike cases.

Tredz is a UK based company, they ship for free for orders over 20 Pounds in the UK while a surcharge is applied to shipments within Europe. They do not deliver to other continents, unfortunately. Check here their shipment policy.

Visit Tredz Bicycles

Bikester

A specific category for bike touring gear with 1286 products. 10 pages of bicycle touring lightweight tents, 610 models of sleeping bags, 190 sleeping mats, 140 camping stoves. 

A choice of panniers that will make you dizzy, no Ortlieb or Crosso though, so browse here if you’re looking for al alternatives, such as the classy (but expensive) Brooks panniers, or the reliable Vaude ones. If bikepacking is your thing look no further, plenty of large saddlebags and cool framebags. Also interesting is the choice of shoes.

Surprisingly though, there’s a poor choice of Touring Bicycles. They have a specific Expedition Bikes category, but few of the items available there are proper touring bicycles.

100 days return policy, free shipping over 99 pounds, here’s the downside: they only ship within Europe.

Visit Bikester

Slane Cycles

Ships for free worldwide and this is almost the only reason to chose this shop. It has a Touring Bikes category but it’s empty at the moment. The Slanes are based in Belfast, Northern Ireland.

Visit Slane Cycles

Scotby Cycles

All the Ortlieb bags you can dream of, but basically, nothing else touring specific. Ships only in Europe, free delivery over 20Â£.

Visit Scotby Cycles

Wiggle

Wiggle is not only about cycling, but it is also instead more of an outdoor shop with a special eye on bicycle touring. It features Vaude, Brooks and Altura panniers, various racks, Garmin not-bicycle-specific GPS devices, sleeping bags and mats, cooking gear, Nordisk, Vaude, and Terranova tents.

The choice of touring bikes though is not vast. A few GT and Bobbin bikes and that’s it. Ships worldwide.

Visit Wiggle
Wiggle the Uk and Europe

All4Cycling.com

This Italian shop only has three touring specific bicycles under the category adventure. Here you could find the amazing Garmin Virb 360 cameras, a vast choice of shoes, a ton of cycle computer, and GPS devices. Ships worldwide.

Visit All4Cycling.com

Biketart

On Biketart you can find Ridgeback, Genesis, Saracen, Marlin, and Pashley Touring Bicycles. Not much more touring-specific gear. UK based, ships worldwide.

Visit Biketart

 

Merlin Cycles

Sensa touring bikes plus the usual stuff. Ships worldwide.

Visit Merlin Cycles

Westbrook Cycles

Ships worldwide. Cannondale touring bikes, Topeak, and Brooks Panniers.

Visit Westbrook Cycles

Leisure Lakes Bikes

Enormous choice of panniers, also Ortlieb here. Brunton Chargers (like the interesting Hydrogen Reactor Charger).

Some camping gear a few Specialized and Trek touring bicycles (go under the category road and then touring). Leisure Lakes has 11 shops in the UK, they ship only in Europe.

Visit Leisure Lakes Bikes

Tweeks Cycles

Genesis, Ridgeback and Cube bikes. Altura panniers, Kryptonite locks, Lezyne, and Garmin GPSs and even Karma drones. Based in Wales and ships worldwide.

Visit Tweeks Cycles

The Top USA-based Online Bicycle Stores

If you are based in the United States of America or in Canada, you might want to shop locally to save on shipping.

Of course, there are no problems, loads of great online bike stores in the US. Cycle touring, road biking, bike camping, MTB… whatever you want.

Many of these companies also have physical stores where you can go and see with your eyes – old style.

If you’re based in Central or South America you will get cheaper shipping rates from these shops compared to the European ones.

Cyclo Camping

CycloCamping.com was created by Stephane and Sheri Marchiori, a Franco-American couple who found a true passion in bicycle touring. At the creation of CycloCamping.com, they had a decade of experience in bicycle touring, including a 5-year journey around the world by bicycle.

The best tyres in all sizes, plenty of choice in panniers and bags, tents and camping gear. They sell all kinds of accessories but not touring bikes, for that you’ll have to go elsewhere. This is the place to go if you want bike touring gear, in the US or worldwide.

Visit Cyclo Camping

Adventuron

Adventuron is a family-run outdoor shop that covers a lot of disciplines in the outdoor world – from fishing to cycling, from camping to hiking and hunting, cycle touring, bikepacking, and even car Overlanding.

They really have a lot of stuff in stock, you could easily buy all the gear you need for a long bike tour exclusively from them. Unfortunately, they only ship in the USA (free shipping on all items if the total is above 100$).

Visit Adventuron

Janson USA

Janson USA has a strong point in Mountain Biking and some original custom builds exclusively made by them on order, tailor-made on the customer’s need. They use frames and components by famous brands to deliver an originally made bike. 

Janeson USA also stocks several panniers and bike bag brands and also a lot of great camping gear. Jenson USA ships worldwide.

Visit Janson USA

Competitive Cyclist

Competitive Cyclists is an American online bike store specialized in cyclocross, road cycling, and mountain biking.

At CC you can find a good choice of panniers and bike bags for touring and commuting, also some brands that are not easily found online. They ship worldwide.

Visit Competitive Cyclist

Ribble Bicycles

Ribble is a bicycle manufacturer based in the USA. Besides their own products though, they stock in the shop a good variety of components, clothes, and accessories. Their bike are very interesting, they also have a few touring models.

Ribble ships its items worldwide, of course, charges depend upon volume and weight of the package. Have a look at their delivery policy.

Visit Ribble Bicycles

Gear Trade

Here’s something different, Gear Trade is a US-based marketplace where you can buy and sell both second-and and new outdoor gear, including cycling stuff. Some great deals here! Only for USA citizens.

Visit Gear Trade

Treefort Bikes

Treefort bikes is the best online shop for bikepacking we found. It also features a good choice of classic panniers. Unfortunately, they only ship to the United States. The website is pretty old-school.

Visit Treefort Bikes

Bikemania

Bikemania is a good online shop for bicycle touring. It sells Bombtrack and Soma touring bicycles and the amazing (and expensive) Sabbath Titanium Touring Bikes.

Nice Skinz Frame Bags (good for bikepacking), the Lone Peak Olympus Panniers (that turns into a comfortable backpack) and the cheap Vincita. Bikemania ships internationally.

Visit Bikemania

Performancebike

Marin and Fuji. A very small selection of camping gear, Ships worldwide.

Visit Performancebike
