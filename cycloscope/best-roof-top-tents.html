9 Best Roof Top Tents on the Market [2022]

Looking for a roof top tent for your car? In this article, we will review 9 of the Best Overland Roof Top Tents currently on the market for 2022

Roof top tents are a must-have if you go on a lot of Overlanding trips. Don’t get me wrong, You can still use ground tents for Overlanding but most people prefer RTTs as they are way more comfortable than ground tents.

Over the past few years — Roof top tents have been gaining a lot of popularity by overlanders and car campers. As a result, Today, there are tons of brands that make awesome roof top tents.

In fact, there are so many brands out there that it is almost overwhelming if you are new to Overlanding with RTTs. That’s why to help you pick the right roof top tent, we put together this article on the 9 Best Roof Top Tents of 2022.

But before we jump into the list, let’s quickly look at what a roof top tent is and all the amazing benefits it comes with.

What is a Roof Top Tent and What are its benefits?

To put it simply, Roof top tent is a tent that you can set up on the roof of your car. Here are all the benefits of owning a roof top tent.

  • You don’t have to sleep on the ground.
  • You can camp anywhere. You don’t have to spend time looking for level ground.
  • They are very easy to setup. You can set up most RTTs in under 1 minute all on your own.
  • RTTs come with built-in mattresses and they are way more comfortable to sleep in when compared to ground tents.
  • You get to enjoy some great views in the morning and at night.
  • Since they don’t touch the ground — they are way cleaner than ground tents.

Different types of RTTs?

There are over 4 different types of roof top tents –However, soft shell and hard shell RTTs are the most popular ones.

Soft Shell Roof Top Tent

Soft Shell RTTs are the most popular type of roof top tent on the market today. They are generally way more affordable when compared to hard shell RTTs.

The body of soft shell roof top tents are mainly made of canvas materials and its takes around 1 minute to set them up. Also, soft shells are generally way lighter than hard shells.

Hard Shell Roof Top Tent

Hard Shell RTTs are perfect for people who are looking for a luxury option. They are generally twice the price of soft shell RTTs but way more comfortable.

They are way more durable when compared to soft shell roof top tents. Plus, hard shell is extremely easy to setup. You can set it up in 30 seconds or less. 

Now that you know the basics of roof top tents — Let’s jump into our list of 9 best roof top tents.
