Bombtrack Beyond – Review! Non-Plus Ultra Bikepacking Machine

A review of the Bombtrack Beyond bikes
Bombrack 1, Bombrack 2, Bombrack+, Bombrack+ ADV

Weight, tire clearance, wheel size, frame material, size, and geometry

This expedition machine has been one of our favorite since it was introduced in 2016, finally here is our review.

The Bombtrack Beyond comes into 4 different models, which we’ll compare in this article:

  • Bombtrack Beyond 1: price about $2,400
  • Bombtrack Beyond 2: price about $2,600
  • Bombtrack Beyond+: price about $2,200
  • Bombtrack Beyond+ ADV: price about $2,900

The Bombtrack Beyond+ ADV is the top of gamma and our favourite. It features a single-speed chainring (30T) and a 12 speed 10-52T as the drive train of choice, a great set of gears capable of bringing you up the steepest and roughest hills.

The carbon fork reduces the weight but still offers triple fork cage mounts.

Although Bombtrack still offers different handlebar options, the Jones Loop Bar will be the standard now. They might seem a little weird, but they offer more hand positions, helping the shoulders and upper body stay relaxed for hours in the saddle.

The Beyond 1 and Beyond 2 anyway are great machines for bikepacking and cycle touring expeditions

Designed as a bike packers dream, the Beyond features mounting options for front and rear racks as well as up to five water bottle cages.

The Bombtrack Beyond is just what I would use for extreme off-road bikepacking expeditions

Bombtrack Beyond Models Comparison Table

Bombtrack 1 VS 2 VS + VS +ADV in a nutshell

A 2×10 drive train for the Beyond 1 and 1×11 for the Beyond 2 the Bombtrack 1 has the widest set of gear and it’s the fastest, but is also the only one with a front derailleur and with relatively budget components.

The Beyond+ ADV is the only one featuring a carbon fork, while both the + and +ADV are upgraded to Hydraulic disk brakes. Same gear sets for these two but a 11-50T (SRAM PG-1210 EAGLE) vs a 10-52T XG-1275 for the Beyond+ ADV – both have 12 speeds.

Bombtrack Beyond Geometries Compared

As we can see from the table here, the Bombtrack 1 and 2 have the exact same geometry, they only differ slightly in components.

The Beyond+ and the +ADV are similar to each other, the difference staying on the wheel size thus affecting the trail and overall wheelbase that is longer in the +ADV by only about 8mm.

The difference in the overall length though is huge between the + series and the Beyond 1 and 2, about 70mm. The plus also have a more sloping geometry and the +ADV a much wider tire clearance.
