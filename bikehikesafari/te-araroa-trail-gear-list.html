Te Araroa Trail Gear List

This is my Te Araroa Trail Gear list. The Te Araroa Trail has a reputation for being hard on gear with lots of rain and mud. The base weight of my gear list is around 6kg (13lb) which will put less stress on my body compared with the hiker that is carrying heavy loads.

This is not my first thru hike so I have the advantage of dialling in my gear. All the items I am carrying have been used on other trails and I find no need just yet to upgrade any items just yet.

But this might change in the future. Read the Complete guide to Thru Hiking the Te Araroa Trail if you want to read a daily journal of the trail, hints and tips about hiking the trail and how to guides.

Footwear
