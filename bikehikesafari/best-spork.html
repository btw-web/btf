Buyers Guide

Here is some information you might like to know before purchasing a backpacking spork.

Spork Material

There are several types of material that work well for sporks.

Titanium

Titanium sporks are lightweight and very strong. Although they cost a little bit more it is usually money well spent on an item that should last a lifetime.

Plastic

Plastic sporks are ultralight and reasonably priced. Because they are so cheap they make a great option to buy as your first spork to give it a try and see if it is for you. Unless you really do a good job at looking after them, you may break them a lot easier than any of the other materials. Another great thing about plastic sporks is the choice of colors. It is very easy to get a spork that will stand out when you are in a group which is great to prevent people being confused on who owns what spork.

Aluminum

Aircraft aluminum Sporks are not as common as plastic and titanium but they are lightweight, reasonably priced and quite strong. But Titanium Sporks will be stronger in the long term.

Durability

Sporks are generally quite strong and should last a long time. The material will usually determine the durability of the spork. Titanium is the most durable material and plastic is the least durable material.

Conclusion

So, there you have it, the best sporks for camping on the market. As to which one to get, this depends on whether you want the spork to double as a knife, and what is your preferred material.

Frequently Asked Question

What is a Spork?

A spork is a combination of a fork and spoon. Sporks are very popular with campers, backpackers and hikers who want an ultralight cutlery item that can be used for everything.

Is a Titanium Spork better than Plastic?

Titanium sporks are much stronger than plastic sporks but they cost more and despite the fact that titanium is an ultralight material it is often heavier than plastic. Plastic sporks are lighter and cheaper but will break a lot easier. Overall, titanium is better.
