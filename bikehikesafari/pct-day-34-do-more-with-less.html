PCT Day 34 Do More With Less

20th May 2015

Mileage 0

Mojave (566.5)

I sat in my hotel room resting. I watched a documentary about hiking the Pacific Crest Trail called, ‘Do More With Less’. Inspiring. They nailed it.

Everything about this documentary I have or will experience. Any person in this video could be me, well all except the guy with nail polish! Definitely not the guy with nail polish.

It is the best representation of me and what it is to do the trail. It’s a long video but for those with the means and the time. Enjoy. Oh and I should be back on trail tomorrow. It is slightly better than my attempt a making a video.
