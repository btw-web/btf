Buyers Guide

When conducting your research, there are certain factors that you need to consider before making a purchase to prevent disappointment and allow you to know what to expect when the product arrives.

Always think about the material that the tarp is made from, the size of the tarp, any warranty period, and your budget. Once you have weighed these aspects against your own needs and requirements, you’ll be able to make a fully informed decision with confidence.

Material

The first thing you need to think about is the material used to make the tarp as this will determine the overall quality and longevity of the product.

The best material to look out for is nylon as it has the durability needed to go camping, especially during harsh weather conditions.

Nylon is also extremely lightweight and doesn’t take up much space meaning that you don’t have to worry about it weighing you down if you are going hiking. 

Size

When you research ultralight tarps, it’s important to know what size you are looking for so you can be assured that you have full coverage.

If you purchase a tarp that is too small then it isn’t going to cover the whole surface and thus not give you the protection needed while a tarp that is too large is going to overwhelm and not be as easy to control when setting it up.

Making sure that you measure your tent will mean that you can get a tarp that covers it properly. 

Warranty

Another important factor to think about is whether the product comes with a warranty period or returns policy which will give you extra peace of mind.

Purchasing a product that is backed by a warranty will ensure that you make the most of the product. You can also be assured that the customer service teams are specially trained to assist you with any technical issues and queries you may have.

Budget

Lastly, always consider your budget.

As you can see from the recommendations in this article, there are options available at varying price points which mean that whether you have the money to spend or are working with a stricter budget, you’ll be able to find an option that is perfect for you that doesn’t break the bank.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is The Lightest Tarp Material?

The lightest tarp material Dyneema Composite Fiber because it barely weighed anything whilst also remaining sturdy, lightweight, and resistant to abrasions.
Nylon is another lightweight tarp material but it is not as waterproof so if you are camping in places that are susceptible to strong winds and rain then you will need to get another protective layer. 

What Is The Lightest Waterproof Material?

If you need a lightweight waterproof tarp then the best materials are Dyneem Composite Fiber and silnylon which both boasts strong resistance to heavy rain and are leakproof while still maintaining its lightweight design.
Silnylon is nylon that has been injected with silicone to make it sturdier and stronger against water. 

What Makes A Tent Ultralight?

Tents are ultralight when they are made from light materials such as Silnylon, Dyneema Composite Fiber and Nylon which are easy to store away when not being used while still being incredibly durable and reliable.
Dyneema Composite Fiber is the lightest and certain nylon materials are heavier than others but this is determined by whether the fabric has been treated.
