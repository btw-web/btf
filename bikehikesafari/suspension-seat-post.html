I’ve gone through the best suspension seat posts that you can get your hands on right now. A suspension seat post makes cycling short and long distances much more comfortable. If you enjoy riding on uneven surfaces, then a suspension seat post makes the ride a lot more enjoyable.

As your body isn’t feeling every bump along the way and you no longer feel every impact either. Overall, a suspension seat post will make you love cycling even more than you thought you did and remove the possibility of injuries from riding on difficult terrain.

Below, we have collected a few points that we think you should know and consider when buying suspension seat post.

Types of suspension seat posts

At the moment, you will find that there are three different types of suspension seat posts that you will constantly come across. Each one has its own advantages and disadvantages.

Elastomer Suspension Seat Posts

A elastomer suspension seat post uses a solid rubber bumper that cushions you against any lumps and bumps that you come across in the roads.

There is normally some sort of hinged linkage system, which reduces any chance of rebound, which essentially then gives you a more comfortable and stable ride.

The rubber cushion can wear out over time, but it is very easy to replace. Also each rubber cushion has a different amount of give, so you need to make sure that the rubber cushion you have will give you enough give for your weight.

They can make a squeaky sound when dirt and dust get into the moving parts. Adding some lubrication or even soap can reduce or stop this annoying sound.

Air Dropper Suspension Seat Posts

This is the newest kind and design of suspension seat posts on the market right now. This type offers you the chance of dropping your seat post when you need to. As these are still very new, they can seem scary or complicated.

However, they are a great alternative if you are experienced on riding on gravel roads and want to be able to change your seat’s height quickly, without having to stop and get any tools out. However a downside is that they don’t always provide the smoothest ride.

Coil Spring Suspension Seat Posts

The coil spring suspension seat posts are also quite new to the market. Yet they are very similar to an elastomer suspension seat post.

This type of seat post uses a hinged linkage, but instead of rubber they use a coiled metal spring which helps with absorbing and dampening impact and bumps while cycling. With this option, you can increase and decrease the tension depending on the terrain and surfaces you are facing.

However, you don’t want to decrease the tension too much otherwise you will have to put more effort into the pedals. Also as the spring is metal, there is a chance of squeaking so you might have to grease the spring now and then to avoid this.

Rider’s Weight

All suspension seat posts offer different levels of support and resistance. You want to choose a suspension seat post that can handle your weight, otherwise you will buy a seat post that doesn’t provide you with any real benefits.

Most suspension seat posts can handle a wide range of weights, but it is important to check this. You want to feel the benefits from the seat post, otherwise what is the point of buying one that doesn’t give you a better riding experience.

Fit

You need to make sure that the suspension seat post will fit your frame. You want the perfect fit, as you will be sitting on this seat post, so you need to have faith and trust in it.

Normally, most suspension seat posts are really easy to install, but if you can’t find one to fit perfectly you can buy seat post shims which will fill any gap that you have, so you can ensure a tight fit.

You want to make sure that the seat post fits well, otherwise this could lead to issues while you are out cycling.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is A Suspension Seat Post For?

A suspension seat post features a suspension system between the top part of the seat that connects the post to the saddle.

The suspension is ideal for any rider who cycles on rough and uneven surfaces. The suspension gives you comfort, which then allows you to have a more enjoyable cycling experience.

Is It Ok To Cut The Seatpost?

As long as the minimum insertion length has been met, then you can cut the seatpost. However, you must always have the minimum insertion length factored in.

Once you have cut the seat post you must file down the end, to make it safe handling and to make sure it doesn’t cause any injury to you while out cycling.

Is A Suspension Seat Post Worth It?

A suspension seat post can help you conserve energy, as it allows you to sit down for longer while on rocky paths. The suspension seat post will absorb all of the bumps which makes sitting down a lot more enjoyable, so you don’t have to work as hard to pedal.

What Are Suspension Seat Posts Made From?

You will find that there are a few materials that a suspension seat post can be made from. All of the materials you find are very durable and strong. They can be made from aluminium, steel, titanium and carbon fibre.

Sometimes you may notice a mixture of a couple of these materials used in one seat post. All of these materials are reliable for a suspension seat post.
