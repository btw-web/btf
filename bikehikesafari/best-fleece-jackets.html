Buyers Guide

A fleece jacket is a great item of clothing to own. Not only are they comfortable to wear, but they provide warmth to the wearer in colder climates. They are ideal to either wear by themselves on those crisp mornings or as another layer while up on the slopes.

This is a casual piece of clothing, and if you are known for your outdoor activities, you most likely have a fleece jacket that is your go-to when the weather starts to get colder.  And most of them are made from recycled plastics. Ever wonder where your recycled plastic water bottles might end up!

In this buyer’s guide, we have mentioned a couple of things to look out for when you are buying your fleece jacket. 

Full Zip or Pullover

In this article, we have given you examples of both full zip and pullovers. When it comes to a full zip fleece jacket, you do have the option to have more pockets on your jacket. Which you don’t commonly see many on a pullover.

However, pullovers are popular as they are normally more lightweight than a full zip and pack down a lot smaller for carrying. When it comes to a full zip or a pullover, this is down to personal preference.

Both have their pros and cons, but it depends more on your personal style which one you choose. Both styles will help you keep you warm. In my opinion full zip is better and gives better options to vent heat on warmer days or when hiking up a hill.

Wind Proof

A fleece jacket is usually worn when layered with a waterproof jacket or coat. As when they are worn by themselves, most of them aren’t very windproof. Very few use a windproof membrane to keep the wind from taking away the heat.

Fleeces are considered very breathable, yet a lot of them let the wind in, which causes the wearer to lose any heat they had created to keep themselves warm. Best to use a windbreaker or ultralight rain jacket to stop the wind.

Therefore, it isn’t the end of the world if you buy a fleece jacket that isn’t windproof, as you will most likely be pairing it with another jacket.

However, you do need to remember that it isn’t windproof when you decide to wear it by itself. You don’t want to wear it by itself on days when it is cold and there is a strong breeze in the air, as the jacket will give you no benefits. 

Fit

When it comes to any item of clothing, the fit is really important. When it comes to fleece jackets there are different fits that you can buy, therefore, it is critical to see what fit your jacket is that you are buying and make sure that the fit will match up with its intended use.

If the jacket has a more casual use, then you may want to look into a roomier fit, that isn’t too tight. While if the jacket is meant for bike touring or skiing you may want to look for a better fitting jacket.

That will help keep in your heat and is easier to move in.

As a result, when buying your fleece jacket, think about what use it will get. If you aren’t sure, then try and find a middle ground where the jacket is still well-fitting, but also a tad loose.

You may find in this instance that a pullover is much more preferred in this scenario.

A fleece jacket is a key item of clothing when taking part in outdoor activities, as it will help keep you warm and comfortable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Should I Look For In A Fleece Jacket?

There are a lot of things to look out for when buying a fleece jacket. However, the main ones we would suggest you look out for are the following:

– Warmth
– Weather Resistance
– Weight

A lot of jackets will have added extras like thumbholes, pockets, and hoods. These are also great things to look out for, but you don’t want the extras to be more important once the main thing this jacket should be doing is keeping you warm.

Therefore, once you have found some fleece jackets that will keep you warm, then you can look into the added features the jackets pose. 

Should A Fleece Be Tight Or Lose?

A fleece jacket is normally worn over items of clothing, so it should be easy to wear over them. However, the jacket shouldn’t restrict any movement.

Usually, a fleece jacket will be worn as a mid-layer, therefore it should be a snug fit, but not too tight to restrict movement and to be uncomfortable. 

Why Do People Wear Fleece?

Typically, people wear fleece as it is a really soft material, which is very comfortable to wear. Not only is it soft to wear, but it helps retain heat, but also is moisture resistant.

Therefore, fleece is comfortable to wear and should keep you warm, but not overheat you.
