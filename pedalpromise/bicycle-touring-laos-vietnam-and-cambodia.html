Bicycle Touring Laos, Vietnam and Cambodia

Rolling jungle mountains of Laos, Coastal villages of Vietnam and remote and rural Cambodian towns. What a magical three to bicycle tour!

We fell in love with the friendly locals of Laos, such an amazing country, in places so dependent on the waters of the Mekong!

In Cambodia we found ourselves bewildered by the incredibly flat landscape. Now the Mekong that connected jungle communities in Laos was separating villages in Cambodia with the rising floodwaters!

Vietnam, was the most chaotic of South East Asian countries we cycled. We loved the food, but the highway madness left us feeling that we were lucky to rest alive in bed every night. Beautiful chaos.
