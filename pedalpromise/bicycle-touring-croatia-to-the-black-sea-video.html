Video snippet compilation from Croatia to the Black Sea

Our adventure continues from the Croatian coast to the Black Sea. A flicker of clips detailing the road from Croatia through Serbia, Romania and ending at the Black Sea in Bulgaria.

From the crystal clear waters of Croatia, along the mighty Danube, past hundreds of friendly faces and over rolling hills of corn. Finishing finally aboard the giant cargo ship that will take across the Black Sea to Georgia.

The views, landscapes and sights from the road are often breathtaking, but with these films I try and capture the little bits in-between all this. The smiles, a meal cooked from the bounty of the days riding, just the simple things of travelling by bike.

The soundtrack “Alive” by Tess Creasy is an inspirational song for all to take a step to adventure or travel, to live a great life. I first heard this track live at Tess’s album release party 1 month before the start of our adventure. I knew instantly it was a perfect track to capture this journey and the emotions of what I hoped this trip would stir, the feeling of being alive.
