Clothing

A world tour requires durable, quality and technical clothing. Our list of bicycle tour clothing is designed for a world tour that will encounter moderate fluctuations in climate.

Our clothing is not designed to encounter tempretures less than -5 degrees by day or less than -15 degrees by night.
