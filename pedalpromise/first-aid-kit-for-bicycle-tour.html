First Aid Kit Bicycle Touring Medical kit is broken into two distinct soft shell Cases/Bags

  1. Medication Case (tablets, general sickness and all non-emergency/trauma illnesses)
  2. Trauma Case (for all abrasions, cuts, bruises and emergency situations)

TIP – Know the exact location of where your Trauma bag is within your panniers and clearly identify it. The soft shell case that contains all Trauma medical supplies is labelled with a blue tag. “Trauma with a Tag” is what we call it!
