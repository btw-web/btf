From City to Sea

From City to Sea is a unique video by Janosch Klugeon an unsupported bike journey from Innsbruck, Austria to Lido di Jesolo by the Adriatic Sea. This bikepacking adventure took 5 days and covered 375km.  It is great to see so many people creating their own routes! Stay tuned for this and many more routes in the coming days.
