Jamis Renegade: Starring Thomas Turner


Meet Thomas Turner, Jamis Factory Pilot and Renegade owner. A regular guy who simply loves riding his bike—anywhere and everywhere. He’s been around the world and seen a thing or two. He knows about getting from Point A to Point B on two wheels, no matter what the distance or terrain. He also knows that adventure is everywhere, and is just as easily found in the next county as it is the next country.
