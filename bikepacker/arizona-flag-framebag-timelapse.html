Arizona Flag Framebag Timelapse

Nick From Rogue Panda Design has been making frame bags for about a year now. His attention to detail in the bag below is pretty special and uncommon. This video shows the detail and time that Nick put in to his second “Arizona” frame bag, a bag that was built around the Arizona State flag. Rogue Panda Designs is doing some pretty neat things along with custom frame bags, such as making panniers, seat bags and handle bar rolls. Check out more on their website or stop over to their Etsy site. For better quality, click on the settings wheel and switch to 1080p.
