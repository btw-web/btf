Length Of Sweden


LENGTH OF SWEDEN captures the journey of Rita Jett, Kristian Hallberg and Erik Nohlin as they pursue one of the world’s longest, most vulgar, and arguably most beautiful randonées. Atop Specialized Sequoias, fully loaded with Burra Burra packs, the team endured 2,177k of Swedish tarmac as they traversed the entire length of Sweden… in 156 hrs.  


You have until Sunday at 8PM to watch the full length feature HERE. If you miss it now, Specialized will be releasing the documentary in segments in the coming months.
