#bikepacking at Interbike

While they are still few and far between, more companies are entering the realm of adventure cycling. And large brands at that – who are influencing their competitors to also delve into this small cycling sub culture. Below is an interesting showcase of the bikepacking products and displays that were on the floor at Interbike this year by way of Instagram.
