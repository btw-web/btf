Inspired To Ride Video Contest

Today marks the first day where you can purchase and stream INSPIRED TO RIDE, the new cycling documentary that covers the first annual Trans Am Bike Race. The race travels from Oregon to Virginia, covering 4,233 miles over 10 states, all on two wheels with no support. To celebrate the worldwide launch of INSPIRED TO RIDE we’ve created a contest.

The Contest:

Download or stream INSPIRED TO RIDE then snap a picture of the screen where you find the Bikepackers Magazine logo making a cameo appearance somewhere in the film. You might have to look very closely but the logo is on the screen for a full 13.2 seconds. Then email your picture to mike@inspiredtoride.it, subject line: Bikepackers Magazine with a short comment or review about the film. Winners will be announced on Thanksgiving Day, November 26th.

Download or stream the film through these outlets: iTunes http://apple.co/1j9dLe6 VHX http://inspiredtoride.vhx.tv Vimeo https://vimeo.com/ondemand/inspiredtoride

Prizes:

There will be 10 overall winners of the contest. 1 grand prize winner, and 3 of each tiered winners noted below.

  • (1) Grand Prize Package: Signed Official Movie Poster (Mike Hall, Juliana Buhring, Billy Rice, Nathan Jones, Jesse Stauffer, Hunter Weeks & Mike Dion), Signed DVD (Mike Hall, Juliana Buhring, Billy Rice, Nathan Jones, Jesse Stauffer, Hunter Weeks & Mike Dion), Custom Laser Engraved DVD Case and a Trans Am Tribe T-shirt
  • (3) DVD or Blu-ray Discs
  • (3) Launch Tour Posters
  • (3) Trans Am Tribe T-shirts

To learn more about the project visit: http://inspiredtoride.it
