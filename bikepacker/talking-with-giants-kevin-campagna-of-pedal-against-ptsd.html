Talking with Giants: Kevin Campagna of Pedal Against PTSD

Shoulders of Giants interviews Kevin Campagna, a veteran from the war in Afghanistan, who suffers from post traumatic stress disorder (PTSD). Kevin speaks about the importance of cycling in his journey of living with PTSD. This mental illness can strike in life for many reasons, and it is important to know how to cope. Pedal Against PTSD is an organization that is raising money to give bicycles to individuals that are suffering from the disorder in order to help improve their well being. You can donate to the cause directly on their Facebook page by following this link \\ https://www.facebook.com/PedalAgainstPTSD
