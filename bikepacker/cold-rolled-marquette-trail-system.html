Cold Rolled – A way of life

At the very least this will get you stoked for the upcoming winter season. Many of you have seen “Cold Rolled” presented by Clear & Cold Cinema, a collaboration with Salsa Cycles and TravelMarquetteMichigan.com. The short film explores the progression of fat biking, specifically in Marquette, Michigan – a small harbor city off of Lake Superior in Michigan’s upper peninsula. It is a way of life up there, and the movement has made its way across the country. Clear & Cold Cinema
