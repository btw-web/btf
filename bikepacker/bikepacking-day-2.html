Bikepacking Day 2

After Yari Ghidone discovered his first bikepacking trip, he knew he needed to head back for some more. So he loaded his MadFat bike, strapped on his loaded bags, put the bike in the car and climbed up to Col Lazzara above Perrero. With his dog Cherie by his side, he went up into the mountains to a beautiful overlook with stunning views. A great place for camp, he decided to set up the tent, collect some fire wood and call it a day. The photos and video do a fantastic job depicting Italian bikepacking.
