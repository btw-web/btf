Wander Two Yonder: Bikepacking France Episode 2

If you missed episode one, CHECK IT OUT HERE. 

In this episode, we head back to the coast of Southern France and lap up some sun with our new found friend Cedric, exploring everything great Marseille has to offer. Checking out the famous Norte Dame Le Gard, Des Calanques National Park and wining & dining in the tiny fishing village of Les Goudes. We then pick up a camper van and drive north to the spectacular French Alps to watch the 2017 Tour De France. Climbing a few of the famous mountain peaks ourselves. Join us and explore this amazing part of the world, meeting some of our incredibly humble local hosts along the way.
