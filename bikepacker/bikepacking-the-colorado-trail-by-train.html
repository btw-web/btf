Bikepacking the Colorado Trail by Train

Erik Porter, Mason Mashon, Jess Pederson and Ben Gavelda took on the last 4 segments of the Colorado Trail, arguably the most stunning length of singletrack in Colorado. If you missed the feature article on Bike Magazine, they took on the route with some beefy Diamondback Bikes starting from Silverton, and making their way to Durango over the course of a few days. Both the feature on Bike Magazine and this video show the true beauty of this region in Southwest Colorado, no wonder why so many bikepackers are drawn to the Colorado Trail.

Riders: Eric Porter @portermtb Jess Pedersen @jesspedersenmtb
Photographer: Ben Gavelda @bengavelda
