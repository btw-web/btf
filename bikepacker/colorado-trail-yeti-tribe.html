Colorado Trail – Yeti Tribe

Colorado Trail. I’m Part of The Tribe. Joey Schusler is at it again, this time on the Colorado Trail where he and some buddies from the Yeti Cycles Tribe toured the route this summer. This beautiful film shows the remote beauty that the Colorado Trail has to offer. Joey release the move Huayhuash early this summer, a beautiful documentation of an adventure through the Huayhuash Range in Peru. Below the video are a few photos that Joey shot on route, hope you enjoy! Filmed & Edited by: Joey Schusler Sound Design by: Keith White Riders: Justin Reiter, Craig Jones, Sam Seward, and Joey Schusler. Photos: Yeti Cycles / Joey Schusler
