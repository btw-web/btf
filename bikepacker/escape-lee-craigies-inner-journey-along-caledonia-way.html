Escape – Lee Craigie’s Inner Journey Along the Caledonia Way

Lee Craigie of the Adventure Syndicate shares her short film about cycling the Caledonia Way Scotland. This video was filmed along NCN78, the Caledonia Way. The Caledonia Way is a long distance cycling route, linking Campbeltown in the west, with the Highland capital of Inverness in the east.

Sustrans makes smarter travel choices possible, desirable and inevitable.
