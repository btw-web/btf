NAHBS 2016 is in the bag. And here’s a recap of bags!

NAHBS 2016 was a blast. There were a lot of familiar faces and a lot of what you’d expect to see at a show full of handmade bikes. There was a healthy mixture of disciplines represented, although I expected to see more off-road touring bikes than I did. Bikepacking is growing, but road bikes are still very popular. That being said, there were a solid handful of bikes designed to explore far off places. I grabbed some photos of bags mostly and a couple bikes while I walked the floor. Revelate and Porcelain Rocket were well represented although I saw a couple offerings from Randi Jo Fabrications, Carsick Designs, Bedrock, PDW, and Andrew The Maker. Enjoy!
