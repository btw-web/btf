2014 Arizona Trail Race – Video

Check out this amazing short film by Aaron Johnson. It captures the true essence of the Arizona Trail Race, a 750 mile race from the Mexico border to the Utah border. The race is the first of the bikepacking triple crown, before the Tour Divide and Colorado Trail Race. Aaron set up this video adding the unique call in service of MTBCast, A service that shares the racers high and low moments that by calling in, we are so grateful to have Joe Polk and his hours of audio recordings.
