Fat Pursuit: A Short Film


For the last 3 years, Jay Petervary and crew have hosted this fantastic race in the greater Yellowstone ecosystem. This wonderful short film by Aaron Johnson Photo depicts the nature of winter ultras, specifically the Fat Pursuit. The Fat Pursuit is one of the most difficult winter ultras because of the dry snow and mountainous terrain. It’s a wonderful experience that brings together awesome people while showcasing the beautiful area around Yellowstone.

The 60K race will take place on December 17th
The 200M race will take place on January 6th
The 200K will take place on January 7th
