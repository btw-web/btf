Faces of the Tour Divide – 2014

Below are the faces of the 2014 tour divide, in order of finishing times. Included in the results are individual time trials (ITT). We think each rider deserves a face to their name. This year Southbound racers endured extremely cold and wintery conditions. Also included was the new Basin to Butte route, instead of taking I-15 racers took to forest roads into Butte. For more on the rules and the race check out TourDivide.Org All Photos by Kathy Schoendoerfer unless otherwise noted.
