Jefe Branham Talks Bikepacking

Before Jefe Branham set off on his 6th Colorado Trail Race, we caught up with the most decorated veteran and asked him a few questions about preparing for bikepacking events, essential items and his sewing history. He shared with us details about his loaded Ibis Ripley, and unpacked his homemade bikepacking bags for us. Whether or not you are a racer, Jefe shares some fantastic advice to get you and your bike ready for your next bikepacking ride.
