Whisky Parts Introduces Carbon Fat Rim

Whisky Parts Co. is again pushing the limits of what fat bikes can do with their No. 9 100w Trail Rim. Far from just another wide, light rim, the 100w rim sets a new standard for durability and reliability through the use of hookless sidewalls and their new TBLS bead profile. These design features create a stronger and stiffer sidewall with more impact resistance, while making tubeless set up simple. Our proprietary double wall rim shape and lay up results in a much more stiff rim as compared to single wall carbon offerings. Backed up by their stellar 5-year warranty, these will have you rolling for years to come. Get fat and fast with the No. 9 Trail 100W rim.
  • Matte unidirectional carbon finish
  • 94.5mm inner / 100mm outer rim width
  • 4.8” max recommended tire width
  • 735 g – rim weight
  • 30psi max tire pressure
  • 526mm Effective Rim Diameter
  • 559mm ETRTO Includes tubeless tape, valve stem, spoke nipple washers
  • Rider weight limit – 300lbs
  • 5 year Whisky warranty
  • ASTM Condition 3 – For rough off-road riding and jumps less than 24” QBP# RM2610 UPC# 708752123458 MSRP is $649
Available now through Quality Bicycle Products. About Whisky Parts Co. Based in Bloomington, MN, Whisky Parts Co. makes parts for riders determined to make the most of their riding. Offering carbon forks, rims and cockpit parts, Whisky provides a high-quality option for riders and builders who value performance, safety and reliability. More information about the No. 9 100w Trail Rim and the entire range of Whisky parts can be found at the company’s website: www.whiskyparts.com
