Video: Welsh Ride Thing

Another bikepacking flick by Ian Barrington filmed on the 7th annual Welsh Ride Thing, an informal bikepacking ride in Wales that lasts 3 days, with no set route but rather reference points. Individuals can take their own route to each reference point, some take the short route, while others create epic days. The event starts with a ‘weigh-in’ contest to showcase the lightest (and heaviest) bikes. Check out Beth and Ian’s wet and windy but well document trip of the Welsh Ride Thing.

For more videos and to trip reports from Ian, head over to his website – Ianbarrington.com
