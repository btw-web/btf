Instruments of Adventure

Transcending our natural environment is rewarding enough on a bike, but once you add one and maybe two different modes of transportation, there is a sense of something bigger and bolder. Salsa sponsored riders Bjorn Olson and Kim McNett have been doing these adventures for a while up in Alaska, and they invited some good friends to join them aboard different instruments for a memorable adventure.
