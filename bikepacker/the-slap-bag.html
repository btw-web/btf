The Slap Bag: A One Handed Open/Close Bike Handlebar Bag

This looks like an interesting option for your bikepacking kit, Check out the video below for more details.
Once you get one you’ll wonder what you did with your phone, food, or keys before you had the Slap Bag. The bag mounts to your handlebar and stem and uses a material much like a slap bracelet to enable it to open and close with ease. No zippers, Velcro or drawstrings will come between you and your food on that next pack ride. Image how many more pictures you will take because you have your phone easily accessible. Think of the extra miles you’ll get in each day knowing you have food that you can eat while you ride, straight out of your Slap Bag.
