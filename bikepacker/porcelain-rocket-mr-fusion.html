Porcelain Rocket Mr. Fusion

In this video we take a first look at the Porcelain Rocket Mr. Fusion V2. This is Porcelain Rocket’s harness saddle system. After testing the original version all of last winter and summer, Porcelain Rocket came out with their V2 version. The V2 has much thought and design behind the it with a more durable holster, waterproof dry bag and upgraded seat post clamp.

MR. Fusion V2 – Price $185

Features:
  • 16 oz. complete (including waterproof drybag, rack, seatpost clamp, and all hardware)
  • 4130 Chromoly mini-rack (built by Hunter Cycles in Santa Cruz ,CA) with built-in gear ‘saddle’
  • Proprietary RF-welded drybag included (made in the USA)
  • 500D & 330D CORDURA® main panel construction
  • HDPE Stiffened side panels to avoid thigh rub
  • Bartacked at critical stress points
  • Duraflex™ acetyl buckles throughout
  • CNC’d seatpost clamps available for 27.2, 30.9, and 31.6mm ( please note at CHECKOUT! )
  • Requires 8″ between seat rails and tire, and about 6″ of exposed seatpost
  • NOT recommended for carbon seatposts
