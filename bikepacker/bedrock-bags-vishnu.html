Video First Look: Bedrock Bags Vishnu

The Bedrock Bags Vishnu Handlebar Bag was designed to be small for a number of reasons, which makes it useful for many applications. As you will see in this video, the simple lightweight bag comes with two dry bag closures on each end. It has three attachment points including two one wrap straps around the handlebar, and another one wrap strap to secure the bag to the fork crown or head tube. The bag is great for shorter trips, long day rides, racing, and it works really well when you have a lot going on as far as brake and shifter housing. The bag weighs 137 grams / 4.85oz, and retails for $80.

For more details and to purchase head over to VelorutionCycles.com. Stay tuned for a full review in the near future.
