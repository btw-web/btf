SEE THE WORLD 5: Where the Mountains go (trailer)

What if you had a second chance to ride the GDMRB? What would you do differently? In the summer of 2015, I spent 90 days cycling from Banff, AB to the Antelope Wells, NM mixing the popular bike route with sections of the CDT, Colorado Trail, Kokopelli trail and all those crazy jeep roads that connect them all. “Where the Mountains Go” is a short video/trailer for an upcoming full feature travel documentary of the ride.
