First Look Video: Alpamayo Designs Front Harness


The Alpamayo Designs Front Harness is a harness handlebar bag that comes with large spacers which allow room for your brake housing, shifter cables and any other accessory cables you have on your bike. The three buckle system comes with a quality X-Pac bag, but can certainly be used with any other aftermarket dry bag. It is clear that Alpamayo Designs makes quality bikepacking bags, we are excited to put these bags through the ringer in the desert canyons this fall and early winter.

Alpamayo Designs Front Harness: 60£ ($77)
