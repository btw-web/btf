A Bikepacking adventure with WHY Cycles aboard their R+ and S7 Titanium bikes


This fall we had the privilege of joining the Why Cycles crew in Utah for a little bikepacking expedition. It was an eye opening experience on the R+, their drop bar off road machine. The video does a good job of capturing what this bike can handle as well as how much fun the owner of the company likes to have. A big shout out to Pete Basinger and San Juan Productions for putting together the edit. Utah is one special place.
