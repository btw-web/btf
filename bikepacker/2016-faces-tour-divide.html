2016 Faces of the Tour Divide

It’s that time of the year again, when almost all Tour Divide racers have returned home, reveling in their once-in-a-lifetime experiences on this epic route. There are certain towns and individuals along the route that embrace the Tour Divide more than most. While racers must take care of and support themselves, trail angles such as Kathy from Ovando and Russ from the High Country Lodge in Montana get bombarded with hundreds of racers. Says Kathy after a busy day on the divide… “Been a crazy day with scratches, illnesses and counseling.” Both, however, take the time to not only tend to racers but capture the true spirit of the Tour Divide with their raw photos of the racers on route. Also found along the route near Breckenridge is the talented Linda Guerrette, from Linda Guerrette Photography. It’s always a treat to run into Linda, because Breckenridge is beyond the halfway point. Below is a list of finishers in order from Trackleaders.com.
