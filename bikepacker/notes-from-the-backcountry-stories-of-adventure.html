Notes from the Backcountry: Stories of Adventure

Although the bikepacking community spans the globe it still remains a tight knit group of people who inspire each other daily. When we run into like-minded people who share our passion for traveling by bike, we tell stories of huge adventures in small sound bites. Each experience is unique and paints a picture of these encounters we have with nature that would never have occurred by any other means than bikepacking.

We have partnered with Apidura to allow riders a space to share their stories with the possibility of being rewarded by doing so. The “Notes from the Backcountry” contest will take place on a quarterly basis. We will take constant submissions and each quarter we will choose the most inspirational piece and publish it on the site. The author of the chosen submission will also be rewarded with a 100 GBP credit toward any purchase on www.apidura.com. 

Submission Guidelines + How To Submit: 

  • 200 – 300 words
  • Two high resolution images provided capturing the experience
  • Send submission via email to bikepackersmagazine@gmail.com with the subject line of “Notes from the Backcountry” and your full name
  • Submissions will be reviewed quarterly and a stand out piece will be rewarded by receiving a 100GBP credit to www.apidura.com and will be published on Bikepackers Magazine. (First award date of June 15)
  • Runner-up submissions will be evaluated with the possibility of being published, sans prize.
