Oregon Outback Videos

The first annual Oregon Outback is in the books, and we learned a lot. The route crosses Central Oregon from south to north meanders through the Oregon landscape mostly on gravel roads. Some decided to rock bikepacking bags, others touring bags. Some were on 29+ bikes, others riding drop bar gravel rigs. Either way it is a great excuse to get out with your friends and have the journey of a lifetime.  Here is a two part series of Britten and David’s journey through the Oregon Outback on Traitor Slots.
