GIVEAWAY: Bogg’s Trail Butter 6-Pack

Bogg’s Trail Butter Triple Nut Blend is a bikepackers dream when it comes to trail food. Bogg’s Trail Butter giveaway 6-pack includes six 4.5 ounce packs, two of each of their three flavors. The three flavors are Ozark Original, Mountaineer Maple and Expedition Espresso. These are not just your classic nut butters, but they blend flavors including but not limited to seeds, fruit, and coffee grounds.
