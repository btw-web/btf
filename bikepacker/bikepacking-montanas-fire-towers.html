Bikepacking Montana’s Fire Towers

Salsa Cycles teamed up with Mountain Flyer to produce another awesome storysite. The storysite shares the story of Trina Ortega, Kelly MacWilliams, and Laura Haraldson as they bikepack to the Montana fire lookouts. Following them was Scott Haraldson to take some fantastic photography. Enjoy some of these photos below, and if you like what you see, visit the storysite, which has more photos and a great narrative. Additionally, if you have yet to pick up a new Mountain Flyer, head to your local bike shop or news stand. There is a great story from the same trip in their latest edition.
