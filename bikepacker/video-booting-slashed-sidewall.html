Booting a Slashed Sidewall

Our creative friends at Western Bikeworks are here to show you how to fix a slashed sidewall while in the backcountry. A slashed sidewall, whether you are running a tube or tubeless set up, is one of the most common issues for bikepackers on the trail.  Watch the video for a quick and efficient tutorial on how to patch it up and get back out there.    For more videos subscribe to Western Bikeworks YouTube channel and check them out at westernbikeworks.com
