What’s On Kyiv – Bicycle Touring In Ukraine

The largest English magazine in Ukraine, What’s On Kyiv, interviewed me about my plans for cycling through Ukraine later this year.

In the interview, you will learn:

  • Where I am planning to go in Ukraine and how I’ve planned out my bicycle touring route.
  • What I’ve been told (both good and bad) about traveling in Ukraine.
  • Why I’ve continued to bicycle tour year after year since the age of 17.
  • What I like most about long-distance bicycle touring.
  • And what I recommend for those whoa are thinking about traveling by bike for themselves.

Visit the website at What’s On Kyiv to read the full interview for yourself.
