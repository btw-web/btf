Bike Switzerland Challenge Tour – Day 6

On day six of the Bike Switzerland Challenge Tour we cycled from Luzern to Rapperswill, Switzerland. This was possibly my favorite day of cycling on the entire tour. One of the best parts of the day was the long descent into Rapperswill and the ride across the bridge into the city. We ate dinner outside the hotel and Barry and I strolled around the center of the city one night fell.
