Sleeping At Jigo’s Mom’s House In Targu Mures, Romania

While participating in the paragliding competition this weekend in Targu Mures, Romania I spent two nights sleeping at Jigo’s mother’s home. Jigo (I’m sure I’m spelling his name incorrectly) was the organizer of the paragliding competition and is also my paragliding instructor’s business partner.

At the house with me was Robert (my instructor) and Jigo, Jigo’s 4-year-old son, Jigo’s mom (who slept on the floor in the living room), Jigo’s brother Mihai, and Mihai’s wife/girlfriend Claudia. Jigo’s mother’s Hungarian neighbors/friends were also there during the day to help with the food preparation for the competition.
