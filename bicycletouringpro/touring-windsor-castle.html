Touring Windsor Castle

After spending the night with family friends, Jill and Shaun, I jumped on a train in Henley, England (without my bicycle) and traveled just a short distance to Windsor Castle. Once there, I spent the next several hours roaming around the large interior courtyard (my favorite part) and exploring the available showrooms.
