Have Fun Riding Your Bicycle This Winter

Riding a bicycle during the cold, wet months of winter can be intimidating and scary, but it can also be incredibly fun and rewarding.

In this new book, I set out to answer some of the most common questions about winter cycling. Questions such as:

  • How do I stay warm while riding my bike?
  • Do I need to purchase a special bicycle for winter cycling?
  • What kind of tires should I put on my bicycle to ride on ice and snow?
  • Which fabrics are best for winter bicycle clothing?
  • How do I protect my bicycle from corrosive salt, gravel, sand and water?
  • What’s the best way to be seen by passing vehicles during the day and night?
  • How can I prevent myself from crashing?
  • And a whole lot more!

Whatever questions you might have about riding your bicycle in the wintertime, the information in this book will not only help you stay warm, arrive home safely and weather even the worst possible conditions, but will keep you smiling all winter long.
