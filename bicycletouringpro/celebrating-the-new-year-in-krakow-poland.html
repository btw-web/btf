Celebrating The New Year In Krakow, Poland

To ring in the New Year (2013), thousands of people crowded into the center of Krakow, Poland… and I was one of them. A woman on stage began the countdown: Ten, nine, eight, seven, six, five, four, three, two, one… and then the crowd went wild. Liquor was thrown into the air, bottles were smashed against the ground (glass was everywhere), men and women kissed all around me, and friends embraced. Watch the video above to see some of the festivities during last night’s New Year celebration in Krakow, Poland.

Last year was fantastic, but I have a feeling that 2013 is going to be even better! Happy New Year everyone!!!
