Has Bicycle Touring Pro Helped Or Inspired You?

Has Bicycle Touring Pro helped or inspired you in any way?

If so, I’d love to hear from you… and maybe even feature you on the Bicycle Touring Pro website. Just fill out this short form: http://biketour.tumblr.com/submit

Thousands of people come to BicycleTouringPro.com each year and discover the wonderful world of bicycle travel. Thousands of those people then take the information they find here and go off on their own guided, self-guided or self-supported bicycle touring adventures.

Even though Bicycle Touring Pro‘s information has helped thousands of people execute the bicycle tour of their dreams, it’s quite common for me to never hear from the many of the people who go off on these amazing cycling trips each year.

That’s why I’m reaching out and asking for you to please get in touch with me!

If you are a Bicycle Touring Pro reader or someone who has purchased any of Bicycle Touring Pro‘s many how-to resources, I would appreciate it is you would please take a moment to fill out this short form and tell me about your recent bicycle tour or anything you’ve learned from my books, website, videos or emails.

Tell Your Story – Submit A Tour Report Now!

I’m interested in finding out just how many people are going on bicycle touring adventures each year… and I’d like to feature a few select individuals on the website here at Bicycle Touring Pro.

So, if you have been helped or inspired in any way by Bicycle Touring Pro and you’ve actually gone off on a bicycle tour of your own, I’d love to hear from you. Just fill out this short form and upload a photo or two from your adventure.

I look forward to hearing from you!
