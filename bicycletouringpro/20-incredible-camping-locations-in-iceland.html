20 Incredible Camping Locations In Iceland

One of the things that makes Iceland such an incredible location for a long-distance bicycle touring is its endless array of camping spots. Finding an area to pitch your tent each night isn’t difficult. The most difficult part about camping in Iceland is figuring out which direction to set up your tent so as to maximize your view!

Below you will see twenty photos of twenty different camping locations in Iceland. These are all spots that I camped at during my recent bicycle tour across Southern and Western Iceland. Enjoy!
