Istanbul, Turkey From The Galata Tower

I paid 12 Turkish Lira to climb to the top of Istanbul’s Galata Tower earlier today and snap these photos of the city’s impressive skyline. It’s my second to last day in Istanbul and the city has never looked more beautiful. I’m going to miss this place and I hope I have a chance to come back sometime soon.
