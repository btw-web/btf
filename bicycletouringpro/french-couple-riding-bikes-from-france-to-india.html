French Couple Riding Bikes From France To India

While cycling back from one of my daily rides in Orebic, Croatia, I arrived at the harbor just in time to see a couple riding a pair of fully-loaded touring bikes take a seat along the stone wall that lines the beach.

Pierre and Anne-Marie are from France and they’re riding their bicycles to India. Once there, they will cycle back to France through Africa.

The said they expect the trip to take an entire year, and we chatted for a good 20 minutes before taking pictures with one another and then going our seperate ways.

For more information on Pierre and Anne-Marie’s bicycle adventures, you can check out there website at: www.duventdanslesboucles.net
