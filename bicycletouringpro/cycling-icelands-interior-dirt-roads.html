Cycling Iceland’s Interior Dirt Roads

The thing I was looking forward to most in Iceland was cycling on some of the rocky interior roads that the country has to offer. The thing Brandon was dreading most about his time in Iceland was cycling on those same dirt roads. In the end, I think both of our expectations came true. I loved the off-road riding in Iceland and the isolated landscapes we were able to access while on these bumpy paths… and I’m pretty sure Brandon hated every minute of his time in the interior.
