Join the 3rd Annual “Bikes & Burritos” Overnight Bicycle Tour

Join me (Darren Alff – the Bicycle Touring Pro) on the weekend of February 29 – March 1, 2019 for the 3rd annual “Bikes & Burritos” Overnight Bicycle Tour in Camarillo, California. We’ll be cycling from my parents’ home in Camarillo to the beach and then spending the night at Sycamore Canyon Campground on the shores of Point Mugu/Malibu.

This is your opportunity to cycle with me, meet other Bicycle Touring Pro readers, and enjoy a scenic overnight bike tour along the coast of Southern California. Click here to sign up for the tour!

The 2018 and 2019 “Bikes & Burritos” Overnight Bicycle Tours were such a success… we’ve decided to do it again!

Here’s The Plan:

February 29 – 12:00 PM: We’ll meet in Camarillo, prepare our bicycles and then take off on a flat 20-mile bike ride from the farmlands of Camarillo to the scenic seascapes of Point Mugu. We will be cycling slowly, traveling at a pace that everyone can enjoy and making multiple stops along the way.

Then we’ll make camp for the night in Sycamore Canyon Campground, which is situated just a short walk away from the Pacific Ocean. After setting up camp, we’ll walk to the beach before returning to our campsite for some Mexican burritos from my favorite local restaurant (Chipotle). In the evening, we’ll sit around the campfire, talk and tell stories.

March 1: After a good night’s rest in our tents, we’ll pack up our bicycles, eat a quick breakfast, and then cycle 20 miles back to Camarillo.

When & Where?

Start Time: February 29, 2020 (Arrive in Camarillo between 12:00-12:45 PM. We’ll be leaving on our bicycles at 1:00 PM)

Location: The tour will start and end at my parents’ house in Camarillo, California. (The exact address will be sent to you via email after you’ve registered for the tour.)

End Time: March 1, 2020 (at approximately 12:00 PM)

What’s Included:

  • Two days of cycling
  • Mexican burrito/dinner from Chipotle
  • Campground Fee
  • Firewood
  • Marshmallows/smores
  • Continental breakfast
  • Drinking water
  • A collectible “Bikes & Burritos” T-shirt
  • Support vehicle – We will transport your food, clothing, camp chairs, and any other items you don’t want to carry on your bicycle from Camarillo to the campground. (Don’t forget to bring a camp chair for the campfire in the evening!)

Join The Tour!

If this sounds like a bike tour you’d like to participate in, please reserve your spot today! There are only 15 spots available.

REGISTER FOR THE TOUR

The cost to participate is $147 USD per person. Sign up here. This fee covers the cost of the campground, firewood, one Mexican burrito from Chipotle, a continental breakfast, marshmallows/smores, a collectible “Bikes & Burritos” T-shirt and the transport of camp chairs/personal items between Camarillo and the campground. You are responsible for getting to the tour’s starting location in Camarillo, California and you must bring your own bicycle, helmet, clothing, camping equipment, camp chair, snacks, water and/or anything else that you want to bring.

Feel free to come alone, with a friend, family member, co-worker or loved one.



IMPORTANT: If you sign up for the tour, please note that there are NO REFUNDS if you decide to cancel. If you sign up for the tour and then cancel at the last minute, you are preventing someone else from participating in the tour. Therefore, no refunds will be given. We will be doing the tour no matter what the weather.

I look forward to meeting you and I can’t wait to share my passion for bike travel with you and the others on the 3rd annual “Bikes & Burritos” overnight bicycle tour!
