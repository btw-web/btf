Winter Cycle Touring In The Czech Republic

I arrived in Cadca, Slovakia on my bicycle and left with my bicycle on a train. I took the train a few hours to the west and jumped off in Czeska, Trebova, Czech Republic. I spent the night in a small hotel in the city and the following day cycled a short distance up the road to the town of Usti nad Orlici, which is where I stayed for two nights before jumping on yet another train and returning to Poland. The photos you see here are some of the shots I produced from my time in the Czech Republic. As you can see – lots of train rides and lots of GoPro camera shots.
