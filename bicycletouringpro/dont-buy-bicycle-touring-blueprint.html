Here’s what I think…

  • Don’t buy The Bicycle Touring Blueprint if you can afford to waste days, weeks and months of your life trying to find similar information for free on the Internet – most it from inexperienced bicycle travelers who don’t know what they’re talking about, have only spent a very short amount of time on the road, or only have experience with bike touring in a very small part of the world.
  • Don’t buy The Bicycle Touring Blueprint if you have money to kill and don’t mind wasting your hard-earned cash buying the wrong bicycle, gear and clothing for your first bike tour.
  • Don’t buy The Bicycle Touring Blueprint if you want to make all the mistakes so many first-time bicycle tourists make on their initial bike tours – mistakes that ultimately compromise their travels and force them to quit and go home early.
  • Don’t buy The Bicycle Touring Blueprint if you think you already know everything there is to know about bicycle touring… or if you just want to make it up as you go along, and you’re okay with suffering unnecessarily.
  • Finally, don’t buy The Bicycle Touring Blueprint is you aren’t serious about making your bike touring dreams come true – because it’s the people who put in the work, do the research, and receive the best education that go on to achieve personal bike touring success.
