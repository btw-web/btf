The 12 Best Cool Cycling Jerseys On The Internet

They say that when you look good, you feel good. Well, you are going to feel downright awesome in any one of these cool cycling jerseys. There are so many amazing options available. There’s classic cool, name-brand cool, superhero cool, don’t-run-me-over cool, snug-in-all-the-right-places cool, and functional cool. And all of these pieces will keep you cool… literally! Each of these cool cycling jerseys is made to be breathable and well-ventilated with moisture-wicking fabric. Even the long-sleeve options will shield you from the elements but keep you from being drenched in sweat. Read on for twelve of the best cool cool cycling jerseys on the Internet:

Superhero Cycling Jerseys

Channel your inner superhero while looking awesome and staying dry in these cool superhero cycling jerseys. This fabric combination of polyester and Lycra feels soft as it hugs your skins but wicks away sweat and moisture. The durable, full-length zipper adds to the comfort and breathability. To top it off, that batman logo won’t fade with wash or wear.

Skeleton Bike Jersey

This skeleton print cycling jersey is way too cool and a great twist on the classic black and white. The 100% polyester fabric is a mesh designed to keep you cool and dry. The half-zip makes for a classic, comfortable fit. As an added bonus, there are three handy pockets on the back of the jersey. This cool cycling jersey is sold as a set with padded cycling shorts.

Orange Zig-Zag Cycling Jersey

The zigzag stripe is the perfect complement to the jersey without being over the top. This jersey’s coolmax fabric (polyester and lycra) will hug your body and move along with you, keeping you dry as you go. A full-length zipper in front and a hem that is longer in the back add to the cool comfort of this top. The back of the jersey offers three pockets, including a zipped one. This cool cycling jersey is sold as a set with gel-padded cycling shorts.

Casual Loose-Fit Cycling Jersey

This comfy jersey is available in four color options. The relaxed fit is a nice change to the usual form-fitting jerseys and a great option for rides when being aerodynamic isn’t your first priority. You’ll look great in any color you choose and you’ll be comfortable, given the moisture wicking fabric, half-zip front and rubber waist grips. Store your things in any or all of the three rear pockets.

Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon Bike Jersey

Love the understated design plus strategic stripes and color-blocking that are sure to make your body look amazing. This whole jersey is made of a breathable fabric with added ventilation through mesh panels in the armpits. The full-length zipper is another option for great ventilation. The silicone band in the bottom hem means this jersey will stay put and move with you instead of flap with the breeze or creep up your back. Three back pockets are available to conveniently store your things. This cool cycling jersey is sold as a set with padded cycling shorts.

Silver Spiderman Bike Jersey

This cool Spiderman inspired bike jersey is designed to make you look cool and feel cool. The coolmax fabric is moisture-wicking and lightweight. The gorgeous spider design almost hides a durable, full-length zipper for another ventilation option and a great fit.

Cool Blue 9-Color Bike Jersey

There actually isn’t a man on this earth that doesn’t look great in this color blue; but it gets better because this jersey comes in nine different color options—all of which are in perfectly classy styles that will make you look your best. You’ll definitely want to step in front of the camera wearing this one, but you’ll want to hit the road too because it is perfectly functional. Stretchy, cool-max fabric is lightweight and moisture wicking and sure to keep up with you without getting sticky. The cleverly-designed, no-slip bottom hem is longer in the back than the front. Added bonuses include the full-length front zipper and three rear pockets (including one with zipper). This jersey is sold as a set with gel-padded cycling shorts.

Don’t Run Me Over Cool Bike Jerseys

Because why not bring your sense of humor along for the ride? This bright orange cycling jersey is breathable with a full-length front zipper that is sure to fit different shapes and sizes thanks to its t-shirt like cut. Along with the funny (and safe!) message on the back are three convenient pockets to store your things.

Relaxed-Fit Superhero Bike Jerseys

Here’s another chance to channel your inner superhero with these relaxed-fit cycling jerseys available in a number of different styles: Captain America, Batman, Spiderman, Superman, The Green Lantern. This pullover jersey is no-nonsense with no zippers pockets or frills. The breathable fabric is a mixture of cotton and polyester, designed to be a snug fit.

Striped Cycling Jersey

This PianPian jersey comes in a variety of different designs and colors, sure to bring out your personality and style. Each jersey is a breathable mesh fabric (100% polyester) dyed in quality ink that won’t fade. Three rear pockets make storing your things easy. The bottom hem is longer in back than in front to keep you covered. Each jersey is sold as a set with matching, cushioned shorts.

Cool Green Crocodile Cycling Jersey

Add some bite to your ride, but not from the chill with this cool green crocodile cycling jersey. This long sleeve jersey is cleverly designed with bright colors, a back pocket and strategically placed reflective tape. It is cut to stay with you so it won’t creep up as you ride along. This long sleeve bike jersey is sold as a set with matching, padded cycling pants (or trousers, for those of you in the UK).

Long-Sleeved Giant Bike Jersey

This long-sleeve, polyester jersey by GIANT is a classic; but innovatively designed with strategically placed logos, and a close- fit cut that’s sure to stay with you and keep you covered. It is made of moisture-wicking fabric that fits smoothly and keeps you dry. Wear it on its own or use it to layer up.
