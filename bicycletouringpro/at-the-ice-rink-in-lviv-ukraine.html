At The Ice Rink In Lviv, Ukraine

The outdoor ice rink in Lviv, Ukraine was my favorite part of the city. I would go there at least once a day and watch the people skate. I enjoyed watching not only the people on the ice, but watching and listening to the people on the outside of the rink as well. I made it a habit while in Lviv to walk to the rink in the evening when the most people were on the ice and just watch. It was my favorite part of the city and now that I’m gone, it is the one thing I miss most.
