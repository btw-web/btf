Cycling In The Snow – Video

Myself and Bicycle Touring Pro reader, Nathan Ross, recently got together and went on a long bike ride…. in the snow. The video above was shot near the border of Summit County and Morgan County, Utah, USA, and was created to document our day together. Both Nathan and myself were riding regular mountain bikes with rubber mountain bike tires. We were not using studded snow tires or any type of special gear.
