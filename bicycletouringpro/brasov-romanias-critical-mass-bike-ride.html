Brasov, Romania’s Critical Mass Bike Ride

I was exploring Brasov, Romania on foot when I stumbled upon a ton of people on bikes. I knew in an instant that it was a critical mass bike ride and I desperately wanted to join the fun. Unfortunately, my bike was in my apartment about a kilometer away and by the time I would have run back for the bike, the riders would have been long gone. So I snapped these pictures instead and after they all pedaled away I continued my solo walk. I was told that the bike ride happens at the end of every month in Brasov, but I’m not exactly sure which day it takes place on.
