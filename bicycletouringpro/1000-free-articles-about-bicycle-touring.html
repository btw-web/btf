1,000 Free Articles About Bicycle Touring

From the very beginning, my goal with Bicycle Touring Pro has been to help people like you plan, prepare for, and execute your own bicycle touring adventures – whether that means participating in a guided bicycle tour or conducting a self-supported bicycle touring adventure.

Since the launch of the Bicycle Touring Pro website in late 2007, I have passionately produced more than 1,000 free articles about bicycle touring.

In that time I’ve also written four popular books about bicycle touring, stretching, and winter cycling… and these products have helped thousands of people from all around the world conduct their own bicycle tours.

To celebrate the recent publication of Bicycle Touring Pro’s 1000th free article, I thought it would be a good time to share with you some of my favorite (and most useful) bicycle touring articles from the past 6 years.
