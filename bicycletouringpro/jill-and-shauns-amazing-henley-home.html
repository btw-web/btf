Jill And Shaun’s Amazingly Beautiful Home In Henley-On-Thames

Friends of the family, Jill and Shaun, were nice enough to host me for two nights during my bike ride through London, England. Their amazingly beautiful home in Henley-on-Thames was once a horse stable, but has recently been restored by Jill and Shaun themselves (Jill used to work as an interior designer) into an incredibly beautiful home. Thank you Jill and Shaun for two wonderful days in your home, some amazing food, and of course, your company.
