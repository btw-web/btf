Alejandro Stores Our Bike Boxes Near The Airport

After assembling our bicycles at the airport, Brandon and I were met by Alejandro, who had contacted me before our arrival in Iceland and offered to store our bicycle boxes for us while we were in the country. After arriving at Alejandro’s apartment in Keflavik, he fed us breakfast and took the time to talk to us about some of the things we should see and do during our time in Iceland.
