Shaun Takes Me To An English Garden

I don’t recall the name of the property, but during my stay in Henley-on-Thames with Jill and Shaun, Shaun took me to a nearby house/garden where he walked around the property with me and showed me what an English garden looks like. I was impressed and inspired! Touring the gardens made me want to run home and work on creating a garden of my own. And Shaun was an excellent tour guide! He knew the names of the plants and he was able to casually interact with the people. I could tell that he was right at home and I thoroughly enjoyed my time in this massive English garden.
