Meeting The “London Cyclist” – Andreas Kambanis

After leaving Jill and Shaun and their beautiful home in Henley-on-Thames, I jumped on a train to downtown London, where I was met at the station by Andreas Kambanis (aka the “London Cyclist“). I’ve spoken to Andreas several times before via Skype and email, but this was my first time ever seeing him in person. We cycled a short distance across town to the Look Mum No Hands cafe, where we downed some refreshing lemonade drinks and spoke about our similar yet differing lives/business ventures. Then we cycled back to Andreas’ apartment before shaking hands and saying goodbye. Be sure to read Andreas’ blog at www.londoncyclist.co.uk.
