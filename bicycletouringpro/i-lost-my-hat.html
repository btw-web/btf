I Lost My Hat In London, England

While cycling to the train station in London, England with John Muldoon, I placed my black Fox Racing baseball hat on the back of my bike and forgot to secure it before cycling off. The hat likely blew into the street somewhere north of London’s center, so I had to go for a few days with some terrible helmet hair. While in Brugge, Belgium, however, I made it my mission to find a new hat. I had wanted to find a black one with little to no writing on it, but was unsuccessful in the endeavor. Instead, I found this bright red hat with a massive Vans logo on the front of it. I stopped in a small park outside of Brugge to take this photo of me wearing my new hat.
