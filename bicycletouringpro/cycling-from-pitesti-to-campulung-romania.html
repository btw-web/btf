Cycling from Pitesti To Campulung, Romania

I thought that the ride from Pitesti to Campulung, Romania would be nothing but steep uphill climbing, but in reality it turned out to be fairly flat. It was one of those roads that was indeed going uphill, but the incline was so gradual that you barely noticed you were going uphill most of the time.
