You’d think that as a famous world traveler, I’d have tons of juicy love stories from my travels all around the world. In fact, I’m often asked if, because I travel so much, I happen to have a different girlfriend in each and every city.

Watch this video and listen to the very end to hear three funny love stories from my travels in Peru, Albania and Ukraine.
