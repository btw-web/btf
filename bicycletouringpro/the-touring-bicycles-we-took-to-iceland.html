The Touring Bicycles We Took To Iceland

We started our first day in Iceland with about 20 miles of light riding. We didn’t want to push ourselves too much, so 20 miles on our now fully-loaded touring bicycles was more than enough. Brandon used a Trek 520 touring bicycle with a set of Lone Peak panniers for his time in Iceland (left). I rode a Co-Motion Pangea Co-Pilot with a set of Ortlieb Bike-Packer and Sport-Packer Plus panniers (right).
