The Sprocket Podcast Interviews Me About My Year-Long Bicycle Tour Across Europe

The Sprocket Podcast recently interviewed me about my current year-long bicycle tour in Europe… and you can listen to the interview right now by visiting this link: www.thepodcasthost.com

In the interview I talk about:

  • The positive and negative aspects of bicycle touring in Iceland
  • Guided bike touring in Switzerland
  • Cycling across Bulgaria and Romania… and making new friends along the way
  • The difficulty in maintaining relationships when you travel for extended periods of time
  • The importance of trying new things, challenging yourself and being open to new ideas
  • My thoughts on cycling infrastructure in America vs. Europe
  • And a whole lot more!

Head on over to the Sprocket Podcast website to listen to the full interview.
