Romania’s $5 USD Pizza

One of the things I like so much about Romania is that a large pizza like this (covered in vegetables, for example) costs anywhere from $3 – $5.00 USD. In the United States (where I am from) and many other places in the world, a pizza like this would cost $20 USD or more.
