The Incredibell Disco Bell – A Bike Bell With Lights

What you are seeing  in the video above is the Incredibell Disco Bell – created by the Mirrycle Corporation in Boulder, Colorado, USA. It is a bike bell with a traditional bike bell sound, but with 6 LED lights on the top of the bell that light up in a circular pattern when you give it a good ring.

The Disco Bell has a dome dimameter of 45 mm, fits around handlebars with a 22.5 mm outside diameter and retails for just $12.50 USD.

Installing the bell is a total snap and both the bell and the LED lights function exactly as they should. I’ve been using the bell for several months now on my commuter bike and I can certainly say that the Disco Bell is a fun, attention-getting bell with a wonderful ring.

My Rating: 10 out of 10
