Share Your Story With Bicycle Touring Pro

Here’s the deal: I want to find a few people who would be willing to share their amazing bicycle travel stories here on BicycleTouringPro.com.

I’m looking for people like you to contribute articles of the following type:

  • Stories from the road – Example
  • Product reviews – Example
  • Lessons you’ve learned from your travels – Example
  • Photo essays – Example
  • How-To articles – Example
  • Packing lists – Example
  • And more!

If you are interested in writing an article of your own for BicycleTouringPro.com or sharing a story from one of your own bicycle touring adventures, please go to this page and fill out the form that you find there.

The reason I’m looking for new people to write for BicycleTouringPro.com is not because I’m all out of ideas and need help coming up with new content. No way! In fact, I have hundreds of ideas for new and educational articles already mapped out.

The reason I want people like you to contribute to BicycleTouringPro.com is because 1) I’m only one person and can therefore only share the experiences I have had when traveling with my bike. And 2) secondly, I have not been everywhere in the world. I’d like to feature some stories, articles and essays from people who have been to parts of the world that I have not.

I believe that by having more people contribute to the site here at BicycleTouringPro.com, we can all learn that much more and create a more global community by featuring stories from bicycle travelers all around the world.

So, if you think you have an idea for an article worth sharing here at Bicycle Touring Pro, head on over to the Contribute page and fill out the short form that you find there. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me.

I look forward to hearing your ideas for new, exciting and educational bike touring articles!
