50 Places To Bike Before You Die – The Book

If you’re looking for some new, safe and stunning places to ride your bike this year, I encourage you to check out Chris Santella’s new book, 50 Places To Bike Before You Die.

This 224-page hardcover book is the 9th in the series of “Before You Die” books by Chris Santella. Like all the books in the series, 50 Places To Bike Before You Die takes you to beautiful locations all around the world (the Dalmatian Coast in Croatia, the Indochina Trail in Vietnam, and the urban jungle of New York City) With a combination of stunning words and photos, Santella’s text transports you to some of the best cycling destinations on the planet and motivates you to get on your bike and ride!

One of the locations mentioned in the book is a route you might be familiar with. It’s the route I cycled earlier this year with Bike Switzerland on their Challenge Tour through the Swiss countryside.

Click here to purchase a copy of 50 Places To Bike Before You Die. Enjoy!
