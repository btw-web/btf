The Roman Baths In Varna, Bulgaria

I spent about an hour walking around the Roman baths in Varna, Bulgaria. The baths are located just a couple blocks away from the apartment I rented, so the ease of access was certainly a plus. The fact that the entrance fee was a measly 4 Lev (about $2.50 USD) helped as well. In the end, there really wasn’t a whole lot to see at the baths. Just a bunch of crumbly old wall segments and what I imagine are some prop Roman pilars thrown about the property. It was a fun little thing to do, but definitely not something I would ever recommend.
