Bike Switzerland Challenge Tour – Day 7

On the second to last day of the Bike Switzerland Challenge Tour, we woke in the city of Rapperswill, cycled across Liechtenstein, and then stopped for the night in the tiny village of Sax. I was riding with Rob and Barry as we made our way through Liechtenstein, and together we decided to make a slight detour into the country’s capital city of Vaduz.
