Movie Night In Bulgaria

While camped out in what was probably the best campsite I’ve ever had in all of Europe, I spent the evening in my tent watching a movie (The Artist) on my laptop computer.

When traveling by bike for months on end, it’s nice to have a small slice of modern living every once and a while.
