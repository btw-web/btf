My 4th Floor Apartment In Krakow, Poland

This is my new home in Krakow, Poland. It’s a studio apartment with a lofted sleeping area that I rented on AirBNB.com for just over $900 USD (for a one month period). I love the apartment thus far – which is great, because I’ll be living here until January 5th, 2013. The apartment is located on the 4th floor of a 100+ year-old building just outside the center of Krakow. It’s in a great area with shops and restaurants nearby and the center of the city within walking distance.
