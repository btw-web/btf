On Top Of The World – Romania Edition

Getting this photo wasn’t easy. I first had to find a place to prop up my camera so that it would stay in place and point in the right direction. Then I had to set the 10-second timer, run over to the tree stump, climb to the top of it and stand in position before the flash went off. I just barely made it in this photo… and after climbing the stump nearly a dozen times, decided to call it a day.
