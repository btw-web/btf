Writing My “Winter Cycling” Book In Cadca, Slovakia

It has been a pretty uneventful week here in Cadca, Slovakia. I went on a few short hikes in the mountains around the city, but I spent most of my time locked away inside my hotel room, putting the finishing touches on the rough draft of my new book about WINTER CYCLING. I promised myself that I would not leave Slovakia until the book was done, so while it feels like I hardly did a thing this past week, I actually accomplished something really big. I finished writing my new book!
