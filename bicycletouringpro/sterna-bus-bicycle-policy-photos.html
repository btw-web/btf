Traveling By Bike With Iceland’s Sterna Bus Company

Instead of cycling back to Selfoss from Jökulsárlón, Brandon and I decided to take a 6-hour bus ride back to Iceland’s second largest city with the Sterna bus company. For a small extra fee, our bicycles were loaded into the back of the large passenger van. For six short hours we enjoyed the company of others and, more than anything, enjoyed the warmth that the small bus provided.
