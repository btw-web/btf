Triathlon Shoes For Men – Cycling Shoes For Your Next Triathlon Or Ironman Event

Triathlon shoes for the cycling stage of a triathlon event are a unique type of shoe. Similar to road shoes worn by road-racing cyclists (like Lance Armstrong and other famous Tour de France riders), triathlon shoes feature a lightweight, tight, narrow design, with lace straps (to prevent any part of your shoe from flapping in the wind), pedal clips (to provide a strong, secure motion on the bike as you ride), and an over-sized heel strap to ensure a smooth and speedy transition between a triathlon’s sequential endurance disciplines (usually swimming and running).

What Makes Triathlon Shoes So Special?

As you can see, triathlon shoes have a number a number of features in common. They are lightweight, aerodynamic, breathable, with tongue straps that tie everything down, heel straps that make it easy to get the shoes on and off, and many of the shoes feature a comfortable design that allows the shoes to be worn with or without socks – thereby speeding up your time in the transitions. Many triathlon shoes can even be mounted to the bike before you arrive, so you can simply run out of the water, jump on your bike, start pedaling, and slip into your triathlon shoes as you begin to ride. This allows you to greatly reduce your time in the transitions and quickly zip ahead of your competition.
