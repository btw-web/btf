Rodrigo Welcomes Me Into The Family

BicycleTouringPro.com reader, Rodrigo Mattioli, invited me to spend the night with him and his family during my week of bike travel in London, England. Rodrigo, his wife, and their young daughter were all so kind and welcoming. We spent the evening in the young couple’s kitchen where we spoke about photography, dance, and their lives in Brazil/London. The next day, we cycled into downtown London where had lunch at an Asian restaurant, explored the stalls at Stables Market, and then got lost while biking around London’s center.
