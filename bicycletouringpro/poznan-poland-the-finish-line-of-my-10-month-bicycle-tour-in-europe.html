Poznan, Poland – The Last Stop On My 10-Month Bicycle Tour Of Europe

The final stop on my 10-month bicycle tour across Europe was the city of Poznan, Poland. I rented a small studio apartment on the west side of the city (Ulica Polna 50) and spent approximately three weeks working and playing in the city. During my time in Poznan I launched my new Winter Cycling book, went to a few museums, took table-tennis classes (with an instructor that didn’t speak a word of English), won first place in an amateur table-tennis tournament, and met up with Aridana from Help! I Have Nothing To Wear, Pawel from Poznan, I Love You, and Aleksandra – a young woman I met in Iceland in May of 2012. The photos here are shown in no particular order.
