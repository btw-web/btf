One Week In Varna, Bulgaria

For the last eight days I’ve been living and working in Bulgaria’s third largest city – Varna.

Varna has really surprised me. The people are nice, there are bike paths all over the place, the food is good, and I’ve seen more naked people in this one city than I have in any other city in the world (although some of the places I visited in Croatia and Montenegro come awfully close.

While the city is a little rough around the edges at times, I could easily spend another two to three weeks here. I will honestly be sad to leave when I cycle out of the city tomorrow afternoon.
