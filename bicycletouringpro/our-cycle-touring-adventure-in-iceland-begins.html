Our Cycle Touring Adventure In Iceland Begins!

The first thing Brandon and I had to do upon arriving in Iceland was to set up our touring bicycles. We removed the bikes from their cardboard boxes, realigned the handlebars, mounted the front wheel, screwed in the pedals, attached the front and rear racks, blew some air into our tires, strapped on our panniers full of stuff, and then we were off!
