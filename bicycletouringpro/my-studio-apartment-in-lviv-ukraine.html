My Studio Apartment In Lviv, Ukraine

The first-floor studio apartment I rented Lviv, Ukraine from AirBNB.com for $27 USD per night (220 Ukrainian Hryvnia) turned out to be a real gem. The place is small, but modern, clean and well-designed. I spent most of my time sitting at the table in the living room or working on the bar stool at the desk near the kitchen. This map of Lviv shows where the apartment is located. The center of town is just a short walk away and there is both a pizzeria and a supermarket just around the corner. Further details here: http://www.airbnb.com/rooms/496553
