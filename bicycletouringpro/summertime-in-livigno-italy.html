Summertime In Livigno, Italy

Adam, Gabi, Raphael and Nicole spent four days and three nights in the beautiful mountain resort town of Livigno, Italy. We ate pizza and ice cream almost every day. The best part of the trip, however, was our hike up to the top of the east side of the valley. The views from the top were spectacular and it felt good simply to get out and stretch my legs in the wilderness.
