Women’s Triathlon Shoes – A Collection Of The World’s Best Triathlon Shoes For Women

Women’s triathlon shoes are a unique breed of cycling shoe. They are lightweight, stiff (in order to provide a powerful stroke when you’re riding your bicycle), aerodynamic, breathable (so your feet stay cool and dry), and easy to get on and off – which is important during the fast transitions between triathlon stages.

  • Click here to see the list of men’s TRIATHLON SHOES.

What makes women’s triathlon shoes different from men’s triathlon shoes? Well, the colors and styles are obviously different. Women’s triathlon shoes tend to feature softer, lighter colors – such as white, light blue, silver, pink and yellow… while men’s shoes are often times black, red, gray, green, etc.

Because triathlon shoes are meant to sometimes be worn without socks (allowing you to slip into the shoes quickly as soon as you jump out of the swimming stage of the triathlon or Ironman competition), many of the women’s triathlon shoes you see on this page are tighter and more narrow than their regular road racing shoe counterparts. For this reason, we recommend you consider buying a pair of women’s triathlon shoes that are a half-size larger than you might normally wear – especially if you plan to wear the shoes with socks! This will ensure that the shoes no only fit, but that they don’t rub or aggravate the skin on your foot in areas that might cause a blister or pain of any kind.
