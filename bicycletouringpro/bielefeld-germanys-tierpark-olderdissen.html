Bielefeld, Germany’s Tierpark Olderdissen

Fee has been living in Bielefeld for years and yet she had never even heard of the city’s free animal park known as Tierpark Olderdissen. I discovered the park (practically a small zoo) while performing a Google search for “What to do in Bielefeld, Germany” and our visit to the park turned out to be my favorite thing we did while in the Bielefeld area. The park is filled with animals that are either now or used to be living in the forests of western Germany. At the top of this article is a gray fox and below is a brown bear.
