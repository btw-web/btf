Video From My Bike Tour Across South Africa

In 2013, I spent 3 whole months cycling across South Africa, Lesotho and Swaziland. I created a video about the two-week-long mountain bike tour I participated in while I was there, but I shot some additional footage during my self-supported bicycle tour across these three countries, and I never did anything with that footage… until now. Watch the video above and you’ll get an idea of what it’s like to bike tour across South Africa.
