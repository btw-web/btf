7 Bicycle Touring Mistakes You Should Avoid

When it comes to planning, preparing for, and executing a long-distance bicycle tour, there are 7 major mistakes I see bicycle tourists making over and over again.

In the video above you will hear me discuss these 7 major bicycle touring mistakes… and I will give you some alternative ideas, strategies, and approaches that you can use to make sure you don’t make these same mistakes on your own bicycle touring adventures.

In the first 45 minutes of the video I discuss the 7 major mistakes made by bicycle tourists… and in the later half of the video I take questions from readers and answer those questions live! So watch… and enjoy! And if you have any questions about any of the 7 major points I make in the video, just leave a comment below and I will do my best to get back to you with an answer as soon as I possibly can.

This video was recorded during a live event that took place on May 24th, 2011.
