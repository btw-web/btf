Ultimate List Of The Best Gravel Bikes 2021

Read Time:8 Minute, 6 Second

So I guess we can right 2020 off as a glitch in the video game of life. For me personally when it comes to bikepacking adventures not much was done, as I sit here in lockdown still in Victoria, Australia.

But the future’s looking brighter as bike companies begin to release their 2021 bikes. And the gravel bikes are looking so damn nice! These are my favorite type of bikes. And have I got some good ones to share with you!

So here is an ultimate list of 2021 gravel bikes. Some bikes have many different iterations, for these bikes I just included the higher end option.

  • Canyon Grail:ON CF 8
  • Wilier JENA HYBRID SHIMANO GRX
  • Specialized Diverge Pro Carbon
  • CUBE NUROAD C:62 SL
  • Trek Checkpoint SL 7
  • BMC URS 01 TWO
  • Giant Revolt Advanced Pro 0
  • Cannondale Topstone Carbon Force eTap AXS
  • Orbea TERRA M21e 1X
  • Berria GRAVA GRX
  • Kona Rove LTD
  • Whyte GISBURN v3
  • Wilier Jaroon SHIMANO GRX
  • Rocky Mountain Solo 50
  • Kona Libre

Canyon Grail:ON CF 8

What better way to start off a gravel bike list then with an electric gravel bike? The Canyon Grail:ON comes in the CF 8 and CF 8 eTap options. For the eTap your are looking at an extra $1,200. We are going to take a look at the CF 8 model.

It is a carbon frame gravel e-bike with high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. With the GRX 800 groupset, Bosch Performance Line CX (Gen4) motor and Bosch PowerTube 500Wh battery. Total weight for this bike is 36.8 lbs / 16.7 kg. It can fit either 650b / 700c wheels. And comes with the Schwalbe G-One Bite 50mm. Priced at $5,799

Wilier JENA HYBRID SHIMANO GRX

Another high-end carbon frame gravel ebike, featuring with high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. Available in the, GRX Di2 2X11 which costs €7,200, this GRX 1X11 at €5,800 or the Shimano 105 R7020 at €4,300 which are all e-bike options.

The mid choice model out of the three, Shimano GRX comes with GRX 800, GRX 600﻿ groupset, the Mahle EBIKEMOTION X35 motor. Rolling on some CST PIKA 700X38 tires. A bit fan of Wilier’s angles and small attention to detail on the frame build.

Specialized Diverge Pro Carbon

The new Specialized Diverge comes in a few different options, by a few I mean 11 or so. The highest end Specialized S-Works Diverge comes in at $10,000 if you want to go all out. Otherwise save a few thousand and get the Specialized Diverge Pro Carbon.

The Pro Carbon features a 1 × 12 Electronic drivetrain with X01 Eagle AXS, Force eTAP groupset. It is a carbon frame gravel bike with ultra high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. With the SRAM Force 1, 40T, 42T crank and SRAM XG-1295 Eagle, 12-speed, 10-50t cassette. Handlebars have a 16 degree flare and the Pathfinder Pro, 2Bliss Ready, transparent sidewall, 700x38mm tires. You do get 47mm of tire clearance with a 700c wheel and 2.1” with a 650b wheel. Priced at $6,700

Also to add to this bikes bikepacking adaptability it features a very unique, internal storage solution in the down tube. The SWAT door lets you utilize space lower down on the bike for stability. Giving you the option to stow away; tools, a flat kit, nutrition and even Specialized SWAT Jacket!

CUBE NUROAD C:62 SL

Cube have about three choices for the all new 2021 Nuroad gravel bike. However, this 2021 CUBE NUROAD C:62 SL is a carbon frame gravel bike with ultra high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes.

With a 1 × 12 Electronic drivetrain and XX1 Eagle AXS, Force eTAP﻿ groupset. That means a SRAM Force, 42T crank and SRAM XG-1299 Eagle™ Rainbow, 10-50T cassette which looks so nice on this bike. Super light build coming in at only 17.2 lbs / 7.8 kg. Comes with the WTB Riddler TCS 700x45C tires. Priced at €3,898

Trek Checkpoint SL 7

The 2021 Trek Checkpoint SL 7 is another carbon frame gravel bike with ultra high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. Featuring the 1 × 12 Electronic drivetrain and XX1 Eagle AXS, Force eTAP﻿. SRAM Force, 40T crank and SRAM XG-1295 Eagle, 10-50, 12 speed cassette. Weight of the bike is 19.1 lbs / 8.7 kg and is priced at $5,999.

BMC URS 01 TWO

If the X01 Eagle AXS, Force eTAP bike is too much bling there is also the Apex 1 and Rival 1 options which are still great bikes. With the URS 01 TWO you get a SRAM FORCE 1 AXS, 38T crank and SRAM XG 1295 EAGLE, 10-50T cassette. With 700c WTB Raddler, 40mm tires. Overall weight is 19.0 lbs / 8.6 kg and price come sin at $6,499. While Apex 1 is $2,999 and Rival 1 is $3,999. And 300 to 500 grams in extra overall weight. Also these bikes are available in some bright and fun colors, yellow, green and purple!

Giant Revolt Advanced Pro 0

The new 2021 Giant Revolt come sin a variety of options to suit all budgets. But if you want to go all out, this Advanced Pro 0 features a 2 × 12 Electronic drivetrain and Force eTAP groupset. With a SRAM Force D1 DUB, 30/43 crank and SRAM Force, 12-speed, 10×36 cassette. And Maxxis Velocita, 700x40c tires. Priced at $5,500

Cannondale Topstone Carbon Force eTap AXS

If you want variety the Cannondale Topstone has so many options to choose from, electric bikes, womens gravel bikes, to beginner and entry level options. All the way up to the Topstone Carbon Force eTap AXS. Which you guessed it. Has Force eTAP﻿ groupset with the 2 × 12 Electronic drivetrain. This sees a SRAM Force eTap AXS, 46/33 crank and SRAM XG-1270, 10-33, 12-speed cassette. Weight is 19.2 lbs / 8.7 kg. And I gotta say as a bomber, you gotta love the red and black tones!

Orbea TERRA M21e 1X

Again with the Orbea TERRA there is a few options to choose for all budget ranges. But the best as to be the M21e 1X. Which is one of those carbon frame gravel bikes with ultra high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes.

With 1 × 12 Electronic drivetrain and Force eTAP, Force 1﻿ groupset. That means a SRAM Force 1 Dub 40t crank and SRAM XG-1270 10-36t 12-Speed cassette. Rolling on some Vittoria Terreno Dry Gravel G2.0 TNT 700x38c tires. Priced at $4,599

Berria GRAVA GRX

The Berria GRAVA GRX is a carbon frame gravel bike with high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. The weight of the frame is 4.3 lbs / 1.9 kg. Which is powered by a 2 × 11 GRX 800, GRX 600 groupset. With Shimano GRX FC-RX600-11 46x30T crank and Shimano CS-HG700 11-34 cassette. And the 700c Vittoria Terreno Dry 38C tires. Priced at €2,699

Kona Rove LTD

The 2021 Kona Rove LTD is a steel frame gravel bike with high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. It has been a popular gravel bike choice, it is the first I believe that we have seen it with a 2 x 11 gravel drivetrain. With the GRX 800 groupset, Shimano GRX 810, 30/46t crank and Shimano 105 11-34t 11spd cassette. it does feature the Kona Rove Verso Full Carbon Flat Mount Disc fork for that extra suppleness. And the WTB Venture TCS DUAL 650x47c tires. Priced at $2,399

Whyte GISBURN v3

If you have not noticed some of the bikes in this list are getting a little more budget friendly. But don’t forget that some of those really high end bikes I mentioned earlier, have cheaper option with different groupset combinations.

The Whyte GISBURN v3 is a little cheaper as it is a aluminum frame gravel bike with carbon forks, but still has high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. You get the 1 x 11 GRX 800﻿ groupset, with the Shimano FC-RX812, Hollowtech II 40T crank and Shimano CS-M7000, 11-42 cassette. Along with this you also get a dropper post, many mounting points and the WTB Resolute TCS System 700c x 42c tires. Priced at £2,050

Wilier Jaroon SHIMANO GRX

The 2021 Wilier Jaroon SHIMANO GRX 1X11 is a steel frame gravel bike with carbon forks, high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. A 1 x 11 GRX 800, GRX 600 groupset. With the SHIMANO GRX FC-RX600 40T crank and SHIMANO CS-M700 11-42T cassette. KENDA HAPPY MEDIUM 700X40 tires, weighs 22.3 lbs / 10.1 kg and is priced at €2,250

Rocky Mountain Solo 50

The 2021 Rocky Mountain Solo 50 is a aluminum frame gravel bike with carbon forks, high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. The components included are 1 x 11 GRX 800, GRX 600﻿ groupset. Has a triple mount on either side of the fork. Powered by a Shimano GRX RX600 40T crank and Shimano SLX 11-42T 11spd cassette. It comes with the Rocky Mountain Gravel handlebars with 18 degrees flare. Rolling on the WTB Venture TCS Light Fast Rolling 700 x 40c tires. Priced at $1,899

Kona Libre

The 2021 Kona Libre is a aluminum frame gravel bike with a carbon fork, mid-range components and mechanical disc brakes. Making a affordable and great entry level 2021 gravel bike choice.

Powered by the 1 x 11 Apex groupset which features a SRAM Apex, 40t X-Sync crank and SRAM PG1130 11-42t cassette. And some WTB Riddler TCS 700x45c tires, priced at $1,799
