What to look for when selecting gravel pedals?

First we need to quickly cover the selection process when looking for the best gravel bike pedals.

Okay so you have discovered that you love shredding the gravel on your bike adventures more so then just traditional touring on busy roads with traffic. You are searching for those quiet wilderness environments to explore.

When looking for pedals that are preferred for this style of exploration you will need to look for pedals that are suited best to MTB shoes. With such off-road bike adventures you are going to be walking around. And you need shoes that are both comfortable on bike and off. Making them best for hike a bike sections.

There are three kinds of pedals options that are best for gravel bikes. Flat pedals, clipless MTB pedals and double sided clipless and flat pedals. So why no road clipless pedals you ask? Well maybe you didn’t! But anyway, it is recommended not to use them as they are suited for road shoes. Which are very uncomfortable when walking around on rough surfaces, not providing any grip and are not good options for using a multiple purpose shoes, for at camp, etc.

If you prefer flat pedals like me, then there is one major factor for the best flat pedals for gravel biking. That being that they should have large pins around the pedals, helping your shoes grip to the pedal. Also when riding up steep hills this will help produce better efficiency and stop your feet from slipping.
