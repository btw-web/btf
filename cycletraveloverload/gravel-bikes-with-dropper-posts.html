Gravel Bikes With Dropper Posts

Read Time:10 Minute, 33 Second

Dropper Post are often an ideal addition to a gravel bike for some people. Obviously you could buy any gravel bike, and then get a dropper post and upgrade it. Which I honestly would recommend the most.

Then you are not restricted from choosing from only these bikes listed below. You can find that perfect gravel bike for whatever style of riding you plan.

Do gravel bikes need dropper posts?

It depends on what spectrum you lean towards when it comes the gravel grinding discipline. If you are more into gravel racing. Then most likely not, this would just add unnecessary weight to your bike that could slow you down.

But if you plan to bikepack on your gravel bike and go on fun adventures. A dropper post could be perfect. Do you plan to ride some steep and technical terrain? having the ability to lower your seatpost will help to position yourself more comfortably on bike and what better way than with a dropper? who wants to manual change the height of their seat.

Kona Sutra

The all new 2022 Kona Sutra LTD comes with a TranzX Dropper +RAD Internal 31.6mm seatpost.

The new Kona Sutra LTD takes on the DNA of the 2021 ULTD which was an upgrade or “unlimited” option compared to the 2021 LTD. They are different bikes when it comes to geometry. Check out this comparison of the 2021 Kona Rove vs LTD. Which compares the ULTD and LTD.

So Kona have gotten rid of the ULT title and now the 2022 LTD is their ultimate adventure rig! Comparing the specs of the 2022 LTD to the 2021 ULTD everything geometry wise is identical. The only difference I could find was slightly bigger disk rotors on the 2022 model. Using the SRAM Centerline 180mm compared to 160mm. Also they got rid of the guard around the front crank which adds to its cool points.

Looking for a review of the Kona Sutra LTD? This is the bike I personally ride. See what I have to say!

Canyon GRIZL

The 2022 Canyon Grizl CF SL 7 is the only model which comes with the Iridium SP0058 Dropper Post.

Made specifically for gravel bike bikepacking! All Grizl models come equipped with wide 45 mm rubber with clearance to take up to 50 mm. Canyon have done a great job only including a balance with the right number of practical frame and fork mounts you’ll actually use. Apidura offer collaboration bags for your gear, that are fully waterproof highly durable packs tailored to the Grizl.

Marin Gestalt

The 2022 Marin Gestalt X11 comes with the TranzX YSP38J Dropper Post which offers 105mm/85mm Travel.

The Gestalt X is the mountain bikers drop bar bike, featuring the all reliable and simple 1x drivetrain. The reason for Marin to create the Gestalt X was to allow riders to expand their horizons beyond the all-to-familiar paved road. A few things that they though about when creating this bike was adding generous tire clearance, lowering the stand over, using solely one-by drivetrains, adding dropper post routing, and tuning the geometry for more adventurous riding.

Evil Chamois Hagar

A true Mountain Bikers gravel bike. The Evil Chamois Hagar comes with the OneUp V2 30.9mm dropper post which offers 125mm travel for small frames and 150mm for M/L/XL.

Learn more about the Hagar here.

Genesis Fugio

The 2022 Genesis Fugio 30 features the X-Fusion Manic Gravel dropper post with 50 mm of travel.

Looking for the best steel gravel bikes? The build of this bike has been upgraded with 725 tubing, so that thinner walls can be used compared to non-heat-treated steels. Up front, the fork is now full carbon to save a little weight and further enhance the ride quality. The bikes weighs in at 24.2 lbs or 11.0 kg.

Co-op ADV

With the Co-op ADV 2.3 bike you get the X-Fusion Manic Gravel Dropper Post.

A bike for the roads less travelled, with the aluminum frame and carbon fork combination you get a bike that is light. Making for fast adventures! The dropper post lever is integrated into the left brake so you can drop the seat without taking your hands off the bars.

Kellys SOOT 70

This bike comes with the KS E20-I Remote Telescopic 30.9mm dropper with 75mm travel.

Specialized Diverge

The Specialized S-Works Diverge bike comes with the X-Fusion Manic Dropper with 50mm travel.

Specialized have innovated again and made some more changes to their new Diverge line up. With many options to choose from for each budget level. The ultimate all rounder gravel bike.

“We tossed gravel geometry ‘status-quo’ out the window. The new Diverge has the most progressive geometry we’ve ever created for a drop bar bike, giving riders a capable and stable bike in the dirt, that still feels responsive and nimble on the road. We’ve increased the frame’s reach, introduced a slacker head tube and a longer offset fork. All this creates incredible confidence with a planted feeling in the dirt, while spec’ing shorter stems keeps the overall cockpit length the same and the steering lively. We’ve kept the bike’s BB low to increase stability, but high enough to utilize with 650b wheels if that’s your jam.”

The new Diverge has space for up to 47mm tires with a 700c wheel and 2.1” with a 650b wheel.

GT Grade Carbon Pro

The GT Grade Carbon Pro bike comes with a TransX YSPI01 27.2 mm Dropper Post with 50mm travel.

The GT Grade is a good option for adventure, with triple fork mounts and top tube mounts. It has a specific feature with the Flip Chip on the fork so you can go from 55mm to 70mm of fork offset.

Nukeproof Digger

The Nukeproof Digger Factory bike is the one with the Brand X Ascend internal 31.6mm dropper with 125mm travel. The dropper lever is integrated into the GRX RX-810 brake lever.

With versatility, strength and comfort at a low-weight, this Digger-model is an exceptional roller on the tarmac, provides the utmost confidence and control on the trail while ensuring you have the greatest comfort for long-distance touring ventures

Niner MCR

Looking for a full suspension gravel bike? Look no further. This is a very unique gravel bike that offers vibration dampening galore. It comes with the KS LEV SI 27.2mm dropper with 100mm travel.

This bike is designed to be a much more capable gravel bike. Suited for more aggressive trail riding. With the Niner MCR 9 RDO, you’ll be able to ride faster, go farther, and stay longer. The MCR 9 has 50mm of Constantly Varying Arc suspension that is gravel-tuned for more confidence, comfort, and control on rougher terrain. The carbon fiber frame has clearance for up to 700x50c or 650b x 2.0″ tires.

The frame also has an integrated rear fender that protects the suspension. And you get 11 fixed mounting points for frame bags, bottles. Allowing you to go on epic bikepacking adventures on some of the toughest trails!

Marin Headlands

The Marin Headlands 2 comes with the TranzX YSP11 27.2mm Dropper Post with 105mm travel.

Marin full carbon gravel bike option is best for those looking to ride fast and take all their gear with them. Ready for adventures on and off road. Ideal for long distance commuting and gravel riding.

Including modern geometry, generous tire clearance, and sublime ride quality, all combine to make an incredibly capable, lightweight bike. Taking you beyond the road.

Cervélo Áspero

The Cervélo Áspero GRX 1 Disc bike comes with the TranzX YSI01 50mm travel dropper post.

A gravel bike that has been designed to “haul ass, not cargo”. The Aspero by Cervelo is a bike made for the rider who wants to push their physical limits. Someone who loves the freedom and exploration that gravel culture was founded upon but who has a hunger to ride hard and to go all out. To ride fast! It features a Classics-racing stiff build for speed and only speed. Performing like you would want a road bike to perform off-road.

Devinci Hatchet

The all new 2022 Devinci Hatchet Carbon GRX RX810 11S bike comes with the KS LEV-SI 27.2mm dropper with 65mm Travel.

A bike that merges off-road riding with fast gravel roads and even road riding if you wish. With 700c x 45mm / 650b x 2.1’’ tire clearance. When it comes to more technical terrain, the Hatchet has MTB-inspired geometry including a longer top-tube and shorter stem for enhanced handling.

Norco Search XR

A few of the Norco Search bike models come with dropper posts. The Norco Search XR C1, XR S1, XR C2 and XR A Suspension all come with the X-Fusion Manic Gravel 27.2mm Dropper with 50mm travel and a drop-bar remote.

All City Gorilla Monsoon

Both the All City Gorilla Monsoon Apex and GRX comes with a dropper post. The TranzX JD-TSI01 27.2 mm with 50mm drop and 350mm in length.

This has to be one of the better choices when it comes to monstercross gravel bikes. It is their dirt do-it all solution. A sweet beast designed to handle on- or off-road shredding, bikecamping, short-duration dirt touring, and the pursuit of good times!

Kona Libre CR/DL

The Kona Libre CR/DL comes with the TranzX Dropper +RAD Internal 31.6mm.

The Libre CR DL leaves no stone…er piece of gravel unturned. This gorgeous Race Light Carbon frame provides both performance and comfort across varying terrain.

Liv Devote Advanced 1

You get the Giant Contact Switch dropper on the Liv Devote Advanced 1.

A lightweight women’s gravel bike suited for bikepacking to gravel grinding to adventure rides. A clean and contoured finish with internal cable routing. The bike comes with clearance for up to 45c tires or 50mm, 650b tires.

Whyte GISBURN v3

The Gisburn comes with the Trans-X YSP18 30.9mm dropper post which gives you 80mm travel for 50/52/54 sizes and 100mm travel for 56/58 sizes, engaged by the Shimano GRX left lever.

A little more affordable gravel bike when compared to some other high end options which also offer dropper posts. As it is an aluminium frame gravel bike with carbon forks, but still has high-end components and hydraulic disc brakes. Comes with many mounting points for all the gear and the WTB Resolute TCS System 700c x 42c tires.

Similarly the Friston also comes with a dropper, which is exactly the same as the Gisburn model.

Bombtrack Hook ADV

A steel frame gravel bike with suspension fork and not too mention a dropper post. The KS E30i 27.2 mm dropper with 65mm travel and 390mm length for Small and Medium frame sizes. And 100mm travel and 400 mm length for size large.

Masi CXGR Supremo

The Masi CXGR Supremo comes with the KS E-TEN 272 Remote, 27.2mm dropper with 65mm travel.

Further evolving from its debut in 2016, the Masi CXGR Supremo brings a wealth of modern technology to the dedicated all road rider. A combination of flat mount TRP Spyre dual piston mechanical disc brakes and 12mm thru-axles delivers confidence and control in the most demanding moments.

Rocky Mountain Solo 70

The Rocky Mountain Solo 70 comes with the KS Lev Integra 30.9mm dropper seatpost.

A bike ready to ride whatever terrain you through it ways. Built with 650b compatibility and hyper-versatile geometry in mind. You get the option to mount gear with contact points for bikepacking gear, racks and fenders.

A more affordable bikepacking gravel bike option if you are on the budget. The strikes a good balance for price and quality. Made from an alloy frame and carbon fork.

Marin Nicasio Ridge
