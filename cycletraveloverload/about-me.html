About CTO

Ever since a young kid bikes have been a source of inspiration, expression of creativity and identity for myself. A way to simply have fun and an abundant source of freedom! Honestly it tears me up but I genuinely believe that I was destined to ride bikes for the rest of my life!

Since my earlier years riding bmx and having fun outdoors with mates. And after stumbling across this fantastic way of travelling around the world, by bicycle. (Which was one of the most craziest instances. It was almost like a spontaneous event, a long story!) Ever since attempting that first adventure by bike, I haven’t looked back.

The idea for this website started as a YouTube channel where I shared my everyday adventures travelling across multiple countries all over the world and since then it’s grown from just a platform to share my adventures. To something that inspires and informs people all about bikepacking and bicycle touring. 

It is my goal that within the next couple years this site will be inspiring and informing hundreds of thousands of people monthly, helping them get out there and explore new places by bike!Â 

I want to share everything that I have learnt. I find this way of adventure has really looked after me and I want to provide the utmost value in return to those who are just looking to get into this type of travel and really just help them get going.
