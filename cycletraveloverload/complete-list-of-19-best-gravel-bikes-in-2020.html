Complete List of 19 Best Gravel Bikes in 2020

Read Time:9 Minute, 43 Second

Before writing this article I was looking all over the web for the best gravel bikes that have been newly introduced for 2020. And you know what? Nothing? So I started researching to find the best options and below I have compiled a list of the best 19 gravel bikes for 2020, in no particular order. Without further ado here they are.

  • Devinci Hatchet Carbon GRX LTD 2020
  • Cannondale Topstone 2020
  • GT Grade 2020
  • BMC URS 2020
  • Cervélo Aspero 2020
  • Giant Revolt Advanced 2020
  • Canyon Grail CF 2020
  • Trek Checkpoint 2020
  • 2020 Orbea Terra M20-D 1x
  • 2020 Specialized Diverge Comp
  • Felt Breed 30 Gravel 2020
  • Rondo Ruut ST 1 Gravel Bike 2020
  • Ibis Hakka MX 2020
  • 3T Exploro Pro Rival Gravel Bike 2020
  • Cube Nuroad Pro 2020
  • Norco Search XR S2 2020
  • Niner RLT 9 Steel 2020
  • Bombtrack Hook EXT-C 2020
  • 2020 Santa Cruz Stigmata

Devinci Hatchet Carbon GRX LTD 2020

The Hatchet is a weapon of a gravel bike, that is more then capable at keeping up with whatever terrain you through its way. Hatchet has 700c x 45mm / 650b x 2.1’’ tire clearance.

With its mountain bike inspired geometry it includes a longer top-tube and shorter stem for enhanced handling on loose, technical terrain. Comes with the standard three bottle mounts and an additional mounting on the top-tube for added storage.

Discrete rack/fender mounts and a capped-thru axle fork for an ultra-clean look. The bike features a state-of-the art intake ports that make the bike look clean with internal cables. The bike comes with a X1 drivetrain and out of the box it has a front derailleur cover for nice aesthetics when there is not front derailleur installed.

The well thought out BB Interchange, provides easy access to frame internals for cable routing. This is a carbon option comes in at $3,229

2020 Cannondale Topstone 105

The new Topstone looks stunning! It is a truely comfortable bike on and off-road all thanks to the Kingpin suspension. Making you ride with better control, better traction and better comfort. A thru-axle pivot in the seat tube gives up to 30mm of suspension.

It features, big clearance for big tires with 700 x 37c WTB Riddler TCS Light tires. Tons of gear and storage mounts. Speed Release thru-axles. DirectLine cable routing. Internal dropper post compatibility. Costing around $2,629.00. Cannondale has some other budget friendly Topstone gravel models that are worth checking out.

GT Grade 2020

Designed with Gt’s triple triangle frame design, this bike is more then capable at grinding all types of gravel conditions. Made from a all-new grade carbon, being stiff and responsive where you need it while also soaking up impacts and vibration on those rough roads.

Featuring the Shimano 105 2×11 speed crankset and matching hydraulic disc brakes. With some WTB ST i23 wheels, wrapped in WTB Riddler TCS 700 x 37c tyres. You can get a clearance of up to 42c tires, and the bike has mounting points galore. MSRP: $3900

BMC URS 2020

BMC have gone above and beyond with this gravel bike. Before making this bike they wanted to know that they would be at the forefront of gravel bikes. And with this URS it is safe to say they are not too far from it. Just look at this bike!

Super-light, race-ready frame and powerful setup combine with the famous BMC precision, state-of-the-art integration and carefully developed Gravel+ geometry. This thing is ready for what ever adventure you through its way. From $3,091

Cervélo Aspero 2020

It seems Cervélo have gone in a different direction with the Aspero. They claim the have changed the gravel design from hauling gear slowly to empowering athletes quickly. This may not sound appealing if you are looking for a gravel bike to go on epic bikepacking adventures with.

A lot of experience from road racing design has gone into this bike. The Áspero frame is designed with world tour stiffness for greater pedal power transfer, using less and lighter material to aid climbing and accelerating, while maximizing speeds with aerodynamic shapes critical to efficiency at speed.

The bike has been engineered to fit most 700×42 650×49 tire and wheel combinations. From $2,800

Giant Revolt Advanced 2020

Design for gravel racing and riding this Giant is smooth, it’s efficient. This bike has landed podium finishes in big-time gravel events. Designed around a lightweight Advanced composite frameset with gravel-riding geometry, allowing you to ride long and hard with less fatigue and greater control.

The composite fork eats up bumps, and the D-Fuse seatpost and Contact XR D-Fuse handlebar absorb shocks and vibrations. The frame features integrated for disc brakes and has clearance for larger tyres up to 45c. From $3,299

Canyon Grail CF 2020

When Canyon first introduced their Grail it got the riding world buzzing, like no other gravel bike did. And they are back at it again with a epic design and those handlebars!

When I first noticed the handlebars from the side view I was like how are they floating like that? The Canyon Ergospeed Gel handlebars offer a dual flat bar design. The bike also comes with Shimano’s new GRX600 groupset which provides gravel-optimised shifting and braking performance. From $2,376

Trek Checkpoint 2020

Treks new adventure road bike comes in a variety of models for your price range. From the AL 3 starting at $1,499.99 to the SL 7 at round $6,208. Offer both carbon and aluminum frame options.

2020 Orbea Terra M20-D 1x

Such a cool and colourful frame, Orbea give you the option when purchasing this bike to customize one to your preferred liking. Starting at $3,299. Of course the bike is available in other colours too.

Orbea makes world class bikes in both road and dirt categories, so it makes sense that they have come out with muti-purpose build machine. The Lightweight carbon frame with fully internal cables the Terra is designed to give the rider the perfect balance between speed and control on demanding gravel roads and trails. 

2020 Specialized Diverge Comp

The Diverge is built to be that bike that can pretty mich keep up with your varied demands of biking sub titles. Whether it’s gravel racing, bikepacking, or weekend shredding. The Premium E5 Aluminum frameset features their comfortable and confident Open Road Geometry.

They have made the geometrey of this new Diverge a bit different then the previous. With a bottom bracket that’s over a half-centimeter lower than the previous Diverge, Specialized also slacked-out the head tube angle, shortened the chainstays, and made a shorter wheelbase. Resulting in a bike that preforms on both dirt and road. From $2,067

Felt Breed 30 Gravel 2020

This bike is perfect for someone looking for a do-it-all, drop-bar bike that is at home on any type of road, be it paved, gravel, or otherwise. Also if you are looking for the newest gravel drivetrain tech from Shimano, then this bike has it. The bike features, Dual-wheel compatibility (650b and 700c), Massive tire clearance for all road and trail surface types, and Sleek storage solutions, and rack- and fender-ready. From $1,699.15

Rondo Ruut ST 1 Gravel Bike 2020

This new Rondo Ruut is light and comfortable, thanks to the new steel frame built with Tange Prestige tubes. Lightweight s-bend seat-stays combined with a thinner, oval top tube increase the bikes bump compliance. The bike is also equipped with the TwinTip 2.0 fork made with FLY technology. From $2,845

Ibis Hakka MX 2020

Ibis call this steed their “answer to the Swiss Army Knife”. being that bike that can tackle, road, cross and grav­el. With the option of running both 700c wheels, or if you are into burli­er back­coun­try explo­ration, 27.5/650b wheels.

Built from a lightweight (1000 grams) carbon fibre Monocoque frameset, with a ultra versatile geometry. It is available in the colors (bone white, teal, coal or fireball). Featuring a 155 mm (depending on the size of bike you get, this is for a size 55), IS 41/28.6 upper, IS 52/40 lower, tapered head tube. At a 71.5 degree head tube angle and a 430 mm dropped design chain-stay. Which has been lowered to increase chainring clearance. And still keep the rear end short for snappy handling. From $1,999 (frameset)

3T Exploro Pro Rival Gravel Bike 2020

This bike is another that is advertised as a go anywhere to anything kind of bike. The bike features the Exploro Pro frame outfitted with the latest products from leading companies like SRAM, Fulcrum and of course 3T components. From $4,298

Cube Nuroad Pro 2020

If you are looking for a budget friendly gravel bike option that is still capable this might be worth looking into. Named a road bike, it still has capability to take you off-road. As it comes with Schwalbe G-One Allround tyres. Ideal for gravel routes and cyclocross exertions, it features a Superlite aluminium frame and a set of Cube RA 0.8 CX wheels. From only $1,136

Norco Search XR S2 2020

Depending on what you are looking for Norco have some great gravel bike options. The thing I like about this bike is it is tough as it is made from steel. The Search XR Steel, includes vibration-damping properties that steel provides are un-matched. When combined with thoughtful frame design, the Search XR is a pleasure to ride. Big tire clearance for 650b x 2.1” or 700c x 28c-45c tires (up to 42c with full fenders). Designed around a adventure geometry, to excel on rougher surfaces. Search XR geometry is a little slacker and longer than conventional road bikes, creating a stable ride. From $2,203.50

Niner RLT 9 Steel 2020

Built from steel this bike is truely made to explore! This bike craves long adventures. Featuring the Reynolds 853 steel frame. And the all-new RDO carbon fiber fork. The combination of the two, is sure to offer a supple ride, regardless of how long you are out on the road. The bike can accommodate tires as wide as 700x50c. This dream build is designed for the bike adventurer. It has 26 mounting points that give you the option to outfit your bike with as many bags, bottles, and racks as you need. I am truely in love! From $5,000

Bombtrack Hook EXT-C 2020

The perfect bike when you want to go to wild places fast! This Bombtrack Hook gives you the ability to carry as much load as you need, with triple mounts on the forks and three seperate bottle cage mounts. Made from a ultralight carbon construction, with 650B Hunt Adventure Sport wheels and easy rolling WTB Ranger tyres. The bike is powered by Sram’s Rival gearing and brakes. From $3,335

2020 Santa Cruz Stigmata

No list is complete with a Santa Cruz bike! It is a bike that has continued to contend at elite cyclocross races around the world. Santa Cruz have looked to provide more advanced bikes with cool new features that only some bikes have but make them available for bikes today.

The bike features their famous threaded bottom brackets, which are a favourite across their entire line thanks to their proven robust, cost-effective and compatible design. The frame now clears up to a 45mm 700c or a 2.1” 650b tire. From $3,599 (MSRP)
