Gravel Bikepacking Bikes

Just a quick note for understanding bike specs listed underneath each bike. I have included specific details about each bike making it easier for you to see which might be better for your personal bikepacking needs. Most bikes come with a few different model options which means different prices and weights which is why I have given weight and price ranges. Low and high gear inches stats have been take from the base model for each bike. Meaning that the higher end option may offer better climbing gears than what is listed. And stack/reach ratio and chainstay numbers have been taken from the geometry info for medium sized frames only. (Also important to note if there is multiple stack/reach ratio numbers for example that means they are slightly different within the bike model options. Comparing all bikes in the same size I was hoping it would be easier to see which one would be more suited to bikepacking.
