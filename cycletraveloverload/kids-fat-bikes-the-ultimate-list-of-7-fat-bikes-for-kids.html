Kids Fat Bikes – The Ultimate List Of 7 Fat Bikes For Kids

Read Time:3 Minute, 15 Second

Wanna get the kids out and about on some fat bike rides? Believe it or not bike companies actually make fat bikes for kids, who would have known? Now you can get one of these bikes listed below for your little one. What better way to introduce your child to the wonders of bikes. Maybe even plan an overnighter bikepacking trip! I cannot wait to get kids just to take them bikepacking!

The bikes listed below are some of the most popular fat bikes for kids. In no particular order, let’s get into it!

S’Cool Kids XXFat 20″

The XXfat 20″ is the first 20 inch production fat bike in the world for which it won the Eurobike Award 2014 in the mountain bike category. Ideal for children from the age of 6 or a height of 125cm. It makes a great MTB without the need for suspension. The fat tires replace the function of the suspension and add extra grip to avoid slipping and sinking in on soft surfaces. It is good for a ride on the beach, in mud or in the snow. Just like most normal ‘adult’ fat bikes. Priced at $499.00 (availability is very limited).

Specialized Fatboy 24”

Now the whole family can go on rad fat biking rides and adventures. Much like Specialized adult version of this fat bike. The Fatboy is built on 24-inch wheels for an ultra-low standover height and kids’ size. The bike is made from a A1 Premium Aluminum and has a geometry with a roomy top tube and short chainstays for easy climbing and confident descending. Also including the SRAM X4 9-speed rear derailleur which provides plenty of gears for your little ones to make it up and down almost anything. Priced at $800.00 They also have the Fatboy 20″ from $770.00

Trek Farley 24

The 24″ model is ideal for kids 7+ years old. Trek have been making some of the best kids’ bikes for years. This Farley 24 is a sharp handling, steam-rolling monster truck of a bike. Featuring a 1×9 drivetrain that powers the monstrous 4.0-inch tires and a host of Bontrager components. Price $989.99

Norco Bigfoot 4.3

Made from a aluminum frame and fork the Bigfoot 4.3 rolls on 80mm-wide rims wrapped in 24 x 4.0-inch tires to keep you on top of soft surfaces. Also including a Shimano’s 18-speed drivetrain and the Tektro mechanical disc brakes. MSRP $829

On-One Fun-Fatty

The On One in the United Kingdom but also ships to the US.  The Fun Fatty features 7005-T6 aluminum frameset and 1 x 10 drivetrain with SRAM X5 components.  The Fun Fatty comes in one tire size (24 inch).

Mongoose Logan

If you are after a budget friendly kids fat bike option, the Mongoose Logan might be up your alley. When in stock it is available at Walmart. Not the best quality, I am going to be honest and say that. But good for kids to ride around on as a introduction to fat biking. Made from steel you know this is going to be a bit of a heavier fat tire mountain bike with 24″ wheels. With 7-speed shifter with Shimano rear derailleur and 4″ wide tires. Priced at $251.77

Framed Wolftrax Alloy Compact

Offering the same as the full size Wolftrax in a more compact package. You could get this one and the adult version and have a day out with your kid. Twins? Maybe? Perfect sizes fat bike for anyone with a smaller body type between 4’6″ and 5’4″. Made from a hydroformed alloy frame design with a central rider position for greater performance and comfort.

Framed MiniSota 2.0

Another cheaper alloy frame and fork build fat bike ideal for the kids. Weighing in at 34 lbs 4oz.
