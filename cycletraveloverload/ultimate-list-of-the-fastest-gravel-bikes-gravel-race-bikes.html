Ultimate List Of The Fastest Gravel Bikes & Gravel Race Bikes

Read Time:8 Minute, 25 Second

If speed is most important to you when it comes to choosing a gravel bike. All the bikes in this article will have you covered.

Gravel bikes are becoming more and more popular these days. Allowing roadies to travel where they didn’t previously dare on their road bike with 32mm tires. The gravel bike has opened up a new world of bike adventure possibilities, while also not sacrificing speed.

All these bikes had to meet our criteria to be classified as some of the fastest gravel bikes on the market. What is that criteria you ask?

An aerodynamic gravel bike is super important to cut through the wind, so these bikes have developed aero tubing and overall design. Allowing you to get the most out of the power you put down.

You could call these categories of bikes, gravel racers, the roadies gravel bike or even some all-rounder gravel bikes. A few of the bikes mentioned have received awards and recognition by winning some famous gravel races. They can also give you the speed you need on those wet road rides when you might need the extra traction. Some all-rounder gravel bikes provide speed and also the ability to carry all your gear, making the perfect light-touring bikepacking rig. The bikes we chose had to fit either one of these gravel sub-categories.

The weight of the bike obviously has to be light enough so you can go as fast as possible. Gravel bikes can be as heavy as 13kg but these bikes are mostly carbon builds and weigh around 9.5kg or lighter!

Frame stiffness is rather important when it comes to riding fast, making the most of the energy put into the pedals. Some comfort can be sacrificed for that extra speed. However some bikes have ways around this, with intuitive suspension designs.

Performance focused riding is the main goal for a gravel race bike. The make a bike ride fast these bikes have taken advice from good road bikes, when it comes to riding position and design. The stack to reach ratio might be slightly more relaxed compared to a road bike but not as upright as some more adventure focused gravel bikes.

When it comes to geometry these bikes can vary from each other. They still give an agile and responsive ride feeling. around 54 to 66 mm in trail lengths. While the chainstay length run from short to mid length or 415 to 435mm. When compared to road bikes they slightly differ in geometry, with larger wheelbase, lower bottom brackets and slackened head angles.

Know that you have some idea as to how we have defined fast gravel bikes. Lets take a look at some of your options to ride fast on all-road conditions.

Ridley Kanzo Fast

View at Competitive Cyclist

Combining gravel specific geometry, aerodynamic tubing and wide tire clearance. The Kanzo fast claims to be “the fastest gravel bike in the world.” We are unable to test for sure if it really is the fastest. But it definitely made the list for a reason. They are claimed to ride fast on any terrain and even outperform some road bikes at their own game.

Ridley really focused on designing the bike to make the most of all the effective power transfer, helping you convert every single watt into maximum speed. The bike has also been designed to cut through the wind while also keeping you comfortable for long rides. Each tube has an optimised NACA profile that was extensively tested in Ridley’s wind tunnel. The bike also features a lowered seat stay for vertical compliance.

Open U.P.P.E.R

View at The Pro’s Closet

The third generation U.P. & U.P.P.E.R bikes offer standard performance gravel bike features. Such as dual wheel size compatibility and a dropped chainstay. These bikes find that balance between road bike speed plus go-anywhere capabilities. Making them fast but also able to go on rad adventures. The idea behind the U.P.P.E.R bikes is the option to run multiple tire setups. Road tires to make it a very fast road bike, turn it into a go-anywhere bike with gravel tires 32-40mm or run 650b mountain bike tires allowing it to be pretty much unstoppable. It does feature a road riding position for that extra speed even if you run MTB tires. Meaning you can get to the trails faster. Weighing just under 8 kg!

Santa Cruz Stigmata

View at Competitive Cyclist

Dedicated to making the best mountain bikes, Santa Cruz have taken what they have learnt from mtb’s and implemented that in their new gravel bike. The Stigmata was released in 2007 and at the time it was categorised as a cyclocross bike. This is before gravel bikes were a thing. it is a great do-it all drop bar bike machine. That is also able to ride fast if you need. Made from a carbon fiber construction, weighing around 8.57 kg / 18.89 lb and clearance for 700×45 mm or 650×53 mm tires.

Cervelo Aspero

View at Competitive Cyclist

Designed to “haul ass, not cargo”. The Aspero by Cervelo is a bike made for the rider who wants to push their physical limits. Someone who loves the freedom and exploration that gravel culture was founded upon but who has a hunger to ride hard and to go all out. To ride fast! It features a Classics-racing stiff build for speed and only speed. Performing like you would want a road bike to perform off-road.

Salsa Warbird

Made for gravel racing, fire-road riding, and road riding the Salsa Warbird geometry focuses on stability and comfort. Offering that extra comfort on long rides. It features many mounts to carry gear for bikepacking adventures. This is a great bike for those looking to ride fast, bikepacking adventures on road or gravel. The Warbird is an agile, light, nimble, very refined, and entirely capable gravel bike. Perfect if you want to ride fast!

3T Exploro

The world’s first full-aero gravel adventure bike! Changing gravel racing and riding forever. The bike combines road speed with mountain toughness. Winning Dirty Kanza, Jeroboam, RAT1000 to name a few events. Having the capability to ride fast on any surface, paved, gravel or singletrack! With a clearance for up to 54mm gravel tires. Available in a number of bike build options.

Pinarello Grevil+

Distinctive and aggressive lines with aerodynamic details make the Grevil+ a versatile and powerful bicycle. I must say it is a very unique looking gravel bike. With an emphasis on aerodynamics you know it is going to ride fast. It has the versatility to run 700c x 25c tires or up to 650b x 2.1 “or 700 x 42mm. Designed to absorb vibrations to add extra comfort. Attention to detail, the front fork has increased flap to protect the disc and aerodynamic optimisation.

BMC URS

A gravel bike ready for high-intensity gravel racing and limitless backroad exploration. Featuring its gravel+ geometry, the URS likes to roam UnReStricted territory. The URS is built around a super lightweight, race-ready frame.

Wilier Jena

The Jena is a gravel bike that fits the bill for most styles of gravel riding. Allowing you to ride fast for performance on dirt roads and single track, or adventure, exploration, and bikepacking. A light, reactive, and easy-to-handle bike with racing-comfort geometries.

Basso Palta

Why consider the Basso Palta? If you are one for quality craftsmanship, long lasting performance and ride quality that begs for speed! Allowing you to ride fast on the road, while maintaining that speed over vary terrains. Tire clearance for up to 42mm tires. It has that unique shape which makes it stand out but the build quality also offer ample amounts of comfort for that rough stuff.

Specialized S-Works Diverge

Fast, capable and fun might be some words used to describe this new Diverge by Specialized. It is the perfect bike if you want to get away of the main roads and far from cars to gravel grind. It is also the steed for riding fast at your favourite gravel race! The bike is well balanced, quick and lively under power but also confidence inspiring when the terrain gets rough. Feature future shock 2.0 with 20mm of axial compliance and a hydraulic damper, good for the rough stuff and efficient on smooth pavement. With its all new gravel geometry, internal storage and tire clearance for 47mm x 700c wheels or up to 2.1” x 650b. It is the ultimate getaway vehicle.

Lauf True Grit

The Lauf True Grit is a versatile gravel and endurance race machine. Featuring that very unique looking Lauf Grit SL gravel suspension fork. With the stiffness of the fork matched to the size of the frame. With 30mm of suspension, with superb small bump performance. The fork require no maintenance. The bike can take up to 45 x 700c tires. The geometry is designed for speed! Offering an aerodynamic position with great stability at high speeds and in rough terrain. Option to run 1x or 2x, and if you go for the 1x option you also get a bottle opener where a front derailleur would otherwise be.

Scott Addict

The all new 2022 Scott Addict Gravel Bike is for those gravel riders who want to go faster and farther on rougher terrain. With aero, lightweight, and integration features. It is a very versatile machine, thanks to the longer and lower design, more generous tire clearance, additional carrying capacity, and plentiful mounts.

Canyon Grail

Cover huge distances at speed and in comfort. That is the promise by Canyon with their Grail gravel bike. It is the ultimate all-rounder, offering road bike speed, all-terrain control and grip with stable but also agile handling. The CF SLX model is designed to win races, while they also offer other more accessible models if you just want to ride fast and save some coin.
