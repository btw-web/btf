Ultimate List Of Gravel Bike Clothing & Apparel For Allroad Riding

Read Time:8 Minute, 38 Second

Are you looking to get off the tarmac and hit the gravel and trails? Road cycling clothing might still technically do the job. But there is gravel specific clothing on the market. Such adventure apparel is designed for off-road riding with durable materials.

When it comes to clothing for bikepacking I like to keep it cazh. Some gravel clothing is almost the same looking as road clothing for the efficiency and speed. But it seems the design is more comfortable for all-day riding.

Maybe you are looking to up your hipster style on the trail? Wanting something that is casual but has the function and on bike comfort of proper cycling clothing? These items have you covered and are pretty trendy, if style is most important!

Gravel Jersey and Shirts

Depending on your style and preference when gravel grinding. There are two different types of gravel tops and shirts. Being the more aero gravel jerseys, much like a normal cycling jersey. And then you have casual, often buttoned up, fun designed, short or long sleeve shirts.

Men’s Breton SE Jersey

So all these next few jerseys are ideal if you want to keep that roadie look going on the gravel. Our good friends at Velocio have a number of different gravel jersey options to choose from. Made from 90% recycled polyester. I love supporting companies who look out for the environment. They also have womens gravel jersey options. With a huge range of features, like, excellent wicking and breathability. UPF30 sun protection and anti-sag pocket design which keeps cargo in place. Priced from $169.00

Cafe Du Cycliste Betty Gravel Jersey

A short sleeve gravel jersey styled for roads less ridden. Made for a lightweight and fully breathable construction from 100% polyester with three rear pockets that feature reinforced pocket structure. Priced at $96.00

Trip Overland Mountain Jersey

Okay so you want a more casual jersey but still have breathability and is fast wicking? These next jerseys are pretty cool! So this Trip Overland Jersey by Morvelo is a combination between a hoodie and a cycling jersey. Made from 100% polyester they also donate 1% of sales to environmental charities. Features a series of pockets, like the rear zipped pocket, positioned so it’s still accessible with a backpack. An interesting function of the micro-fibre inner hood that doubles as a towel and glasses wipe. Priced at £90.00

Barmara Gravel Jersey

Cafe Du Cycliste have a great collection of gravel clothing. With some nice longer sleeve jersey options with a splash of style to them. Made from post consumer recycled 100% polyester (recycled bottles). With a lightweight and fully breathable construction, Barbara is built for adventure in warmer weather. Priced at $108.00

Laureline Blue Cat & Kingfisher Gravel Cycling Jersey

Really am a big fan of Cafe Du Cycliste’s gravel collection. Especially their cool designed short sleeve zip front 90% polyamide and 10% elastane jersey. A lightweight & breathable, three cargo pocket, stechnical woven fabric, classic fit, jersey that is made in Europe. Priced from $156.00

Rapha Explore Technical T-Shirt

Rapha have made some gravel specific clothing. This Explore Technical Shirt available in a long sleeve and women’s options. It is inspired by their performance roadwear, made with technical fabrics to support exploration on and off the bike. Using the same lightweight micro-mesh fabric as the Rapha Flyweight Jersey. Priced at $90.00

Morvelo Gradient Overland LS Shirt

Are flannel shirts more your style? They are for me. Morvelo make some flannel shirts that have technical performance and fit to make it a great all-round cycling top. The water-repellent, windproof, four-way stretch fabric handles high tempo rides. Priced at £95.00

Morvelo Foliage Overland SS Shirt

A adventure sport top disguised as a button up shirt! Blend in and stand out! Perfect for bikepacking adventures when you want to look more casual but also express some style. The stretchy, quick-drying fabric gives complete freedom of movement with the clever side pockets carrying all the cargo you’ll need. Priced at £75.00

Pedaled Kyoto Gravel T-shirts

Love wearing t-shirts and want that casual look and feel but still need it to function like a active shirt? The Kyoto shirts are perfect from gravel adventures to bikepacking weekends. Designed in Japan and made from mostly Merino Wool. Priced at $96.00

Base Layer Gravel Cycling

Base layers are important for adding that little extra warmth on those cold gravel grinding mornings. Typically super lightweight and even with back pockets for storage. They make a great addition to your gravel gear list!

Rapha Brevet Base Layer

Made to withstand day after day on the road the Brevet Base Layer is definitely lightweight. Made in a women’s option, it is designed with fabric with a permanent antibacterial finish. Constructed with silver coated ion yarn, the fabric inhibits the growth of bacteria to keep you fresh and odour free. Priced at $80.00

Cafe Du Cycliste Cecilia Base Layer

Velocio Radiator SL Base Layer

I have personally use Velocio’s trail base layer, and it is amazing! This Radiator SL Base Layer is gravel specific. This one is a new take on the summer base layer, using a unique mesh fabric developed by Polartec that regulates body temp. The two fibers enhance the evaporative cooling effect, by absorbing moisture (Tencel) and drying (polyester) over the surface. Priced at $69.00

Gravel Bibs

Gravel bibs shorts with chamois are ideal for that extra comfort. The best combination if you are looking for a casual let comfortable look is to wear these underneath some shorts which we will discuss later.

Rapha Cargo Bib Shorts

Made from lightweight and extremely water-resistant material, these Cargo bib shorts feature pockets on both legs and upper back. To carry everything you would keep in a jersey. Priced at $270.00

Velocio Signature Bib Short

These Signature bibs are a Velocio cornerstone out of their entire bib short collection. I am a big fan of the thick shoulder straps offering excellent comfort for long rides. They don’t dig into your skin like some road bibs. Offering balanced compression, excellent breathability and all-day comfort. Priced from $229.00

Cafe Du Cycliste Helene Gravel Bib Shorts

Built for adventure and designed to be worn underneath gravel shorts. These Helene Gravel Bibs are made with a mesh design for ultimate airflow, three cargo pockets and leg grippers. Priced at $180.00

Gravel Shorts

More casual looking shorts that are designed to be worn over bibs and offer comfort while gravel grinding.

Vulpine Men’s Gravel Shorts

Designed for use when needing a tough pair of shorts, MTB or gravel or just riding down to the pub. They are made from breathable and stretchy fabric, with a wider cut for a more casual look. Priced from £70.00

Showers Pass Men’s Gravel 10″ Shorts

Made to be great for on and off bike activities, Showers Pass most versatile and lightest shorts to date. With a women’s gravel shorts option. The shorts to do it all! With the combination of performance and casual.  The shorts feature a 4-way stretch articulation, an adjustable waist strap and a more comfortable yet tailored fit. Priced at $89.00

Pedaled Kyoto Gravel Shorts

Can be worn with or without a chamois on all kinds of off-road cycling adventures. Delivering all-day comfort and all-road performance for MTB riders, gravel grinders and bikepackers. The shorts are cut looser than traditional cycling bibs, yet they maintain an athletic silhouette for a comfortable ride and the right style. Priced at $156.00

Cafe Du Cycliste Renée Gravel Shorts

Cutting edge fabric technology is used for comfort and performance while gravel grinding. Slim cut, the innovative woven weave combines stretch and breathability in a lightweight form while the hems are bonded to avoid friction. Also including two, popper shut, side pockets ideally positioned for on the bike as well as a rear zipped valuable pocket. Priced at $174.00

Adapt Overland Waterproof Shorts

A pair of waterproof shorts that are made from a soft and stretchy fully-taped fabric that moves with your body. Also windproof and quick drying, made from 80% Polyester and 20% PU. Priced at £85.00

Gravel Cycling Jacket

It is always handy to carry a rain jacket on the bike, especially for those multi-day or month trips.

Rapha Explore Hooded Gore-Tex Pullover

This is a very awesome waterproof hooded rain jacket featuring a very handy chest pocket at the front for storage. With women’s gravel jacket options. Made with a Gore-Tex, lightweight three-layer construction that’s waterproof and breathable. You know it is going to keep you dry and warm. Designed with exploration rather then speed in mind. Priced at $345.00

Barricade Overland Rain Jacket

This has to be one of my favourite looking gravel rain jackets. A trendy style that also functionals well as a Gravel, Road and Mountain Biking jacket. The soft-feel fabric feels exactly like a fully waterproof should, with huge amounts of storage potential, side ventilation and a hood that sits perfectly under any helmet. Priced at £100.00

Velocio Ultralight Jacket

Rapha Explore Down Jacket

Raphas lightweight, packable down jacket for exploring on and off the bike is made with woven nylon outer fabric and filled with high loft 850-fill-power down to create an insulating layer for exceptional warmth in the coldest of conditions, weighing just 238 grams. Priced at $295.00
