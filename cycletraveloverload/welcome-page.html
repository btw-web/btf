Welcome to Cycle Travel Overload

What is “Cycle Travel Overload”?

It all started with a idea. An Idea to inspire people to get out there on their bike and explore the world. Help give a introduction of “cycle travel’ to the beginner bicycle traveller. Help make the transition into a more adventurous lifestyle. With resources regarding this whole world of bike travel.

If you would like to support my mission to create amazing bicycle travel films and content, it would be greatly appreciated.

My goal here at Cycle Travel Overload is to inspire and help as many people as I can to travel and explore this amazing planet by bicycle. I came to the realisation early on in my bicycle touring experience that it is one of the best, if not the best ways to travel and see a new place. I believe my purpose is to share these experiences and encourage others to get out there and experience that for themselves.

Who am I?

My name is Codey Orgill, I have been bicycle touring for over 3 years now and it is a passion, something I want to share with the world and help as many people as I can in the process. You may know me from my youtube channel “Codey ORGILL” where I share my adventures travelling the; USA, Japan, Australia and many more locations all over the globe. Riding my bicycle one pedal stroke at a time. I have always wanted to start a blog about a specific niche that I am super passionate about and here I am. My dream is to give back to the community and help as many travellers as I can.
