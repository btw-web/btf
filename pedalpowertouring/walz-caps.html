Walz Caps

So you have been on the road for a few days without a shower and your hair is scraggy looking. Maybe you would like to stop for a quick snack after this morning’s group ride, but you are experiencing a bad case of “helmet” hair. Perhaps you are on a long term bicycle tour and are on your way to becoming a “long hair” or maybe you just want to look cool and wear something different on your noggin.

Walz Cap on the beach in Florida

May I suggest a really cool looking cycling cap from Walz Caps? They are handmade in the USA. Come in wool, cotton, or moisture wicking material and in many styles. Add a personalized text embroidery to your Walz cap for a few more dollars.

Walz Cap camping in Florida

 

I happen to own two Walz cycling caps from their “Unique/Limited Wool” product line and both sport a personalized embroidering.  If you have been following us for some time you will have noticed me wearing one on many bicycle tours. The one featured in many of the photos from us cycling, camping, and/or walking around has been with me for over 4 years. Needless to say, it has been put to the test and is holding up very nice.

Walz Cap cycling around in Germany

It also fits nicely under the helmet and provides a nice little visor on those sunny days. My cap weighs near nothing and folds up so small that I carry it in my handlebar bag or in my shorts’ cargo pocket. When it comes to washing out the dirt, sweat, and skin oils, I normally just hand wash my Walz cap. However, there have been a couple times when I threw it into the washing machine with all my other wool clothing, washing it on gentle and then hanging it up to dry.

Walz Cap under the helmet

If you have something special that you would like on your cap, but not sure if it can be done. Give the good folks at Walz Caps a call and see what they can do for you. I did this for my second cap and they were able to embroider what I wanted and it looks great.

Walz Cap in Washington DC

If you are in the market for a new cycling cap and want something different, then give Walz Caps a try. For the record, we are not sponsored, nor have we received anything in return for our little write up. I paid full price for both my caps and we get nothing in return if you purchase one, two, or ten.  Hope you enjoy your Walz Cap and happy touring.
