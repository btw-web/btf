Verona Italy

A little down time in Verona, Italy

The long riding days and the cold, rainy weather have worn on us, so we decided to take a little down time in Verona. After arriving late at the only youth hostel in town, getting settled into our room, and after a good night sleep, we went to sight see Verona. Even the sun peeked out from behind the clouds every once in a while to brighten up the day. Hopefully this means, the worst of the cold weather is behind us now.
