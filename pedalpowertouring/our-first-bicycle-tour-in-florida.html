Our first bicycle tour in Florida

Shortly after receiving our new Co-Motion Nor`Wester Co-Pilot Touring bikes (circa 2008) we decided to take off for 4 days around our “neighborhood” in Florida! At this point we had already been waiting nearly 3 months for the delivery of our bikes, so the trip was long planned. Our plan was to bicycle tour to Ft. Cooper State Park near Inverness, Florida. Like most newbies we packed way, way too much crap, but nevertheless, we we had a great time. The 75 miles between our house and Ft. Cooper consisted of mostly paved trails (Starkey Park, Suncoast, and Withlacoochee) and the occasional connecting county road. To view the pictures from this trip, please click here PHOTOS
