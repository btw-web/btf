Downtime in Neustadt/Aisch

After arriving in Germany and while waiting on some parts from Full Speed Ahead, we spent the next 10 days taking hikes, visiting family, the dentist, the eye doctor, and of course we played around with the camera.

Giving night painting a try. When it is dark and while keeping the shutter open on the camera, we run around with a flash light, lighting up the areas that we want exposed.
