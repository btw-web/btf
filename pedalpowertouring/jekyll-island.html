Jekyll Island

After a late morning start, getting our webpage fixed, eating some gigantic Belgium waffles, and riding against headwind all day we have arrived at Jekyll Island!

On the way there we had the pleasure of saving a little box turtle from getting squashed on a four-lane highway and seeing the scenery change from pine-woods along our route to Georgia marshland along the coast. Most of our route went along actual bike routes–Route 95 going north and Route 10 going east all the way to our destination.

As we finally rode over the bridge to get onto beautiful Jekyll Island, we realized how beat we really are and decided we need a break, or possible a down day.

Jekyll Island seems to be just the place to do that–the island is gorgeous, the campground very nice with warm showers, and there is a nice little community room to relax and charge all the gadgets.
