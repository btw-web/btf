Packing the Bikes

 

This past weekend we disassembled and packed our bikes in preparation for our next big tour. If you’re just tuning in to our BLOG we are headed to Europe next week and will spend a month touring through Romania, Hungary, Slovakia, Austria, and Germany. Anyway… back to packing our bikes.  Hopefully we’ll be able to answer one of the biggest questions we get every time we travel: “How do you pack your bikes?”.

Before getting to the pictures below, I’ve already removed the following items: wheels, fenders, racks, handlebar, lighting system, GPS, pedals, bottle cages, air pump, seat & seat post, kickstands, and rear derailleur.
