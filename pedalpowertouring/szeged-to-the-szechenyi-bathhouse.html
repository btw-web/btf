Szeged to the Szechenyi Bathhouse

We spent the morning sightseeing Szeged before jumping on a train for Budapest. The plan is to get our tour somewhat back on track by skipping parts of the Hungarian Great Plains and this is the reason for another train ride. After arriving in Budapest and getting our spot for the night, we spend the evening giving our aching legs and bodies a nice break by spending a few hours in the Szechenyi bathhouse before having dinner.
