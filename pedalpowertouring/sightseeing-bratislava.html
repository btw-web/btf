Sightseeing Bratislava

First stop today, the ruin of Devin Castle, so we made our way to the main bus station for the 09:09 #29 bus to Devin for the short bus ride out to Devin.  After sightseeing Devin Castle we catch the bus back to Bratislava.  On our way back to the apartment we hit a local grocery store and buy some food for lunch. Following our short lunch break we go over the to the New/Old City Hall and take a tour of the City Museum and learn some history of Bratislava.  From here we wander the city streets and take in the sights.  Bratislava is a very nice city and well worth the visit.
