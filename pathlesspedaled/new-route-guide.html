New Route Guide!

We often get asked where our favorite place to ride is. To this end, we are creating a series of digital route guides for those that hate planning and just want to ride. Our first one is the California Weekender that goes from SLO to Morro Bay to Paso Robles and back to SLO.

The guide is a downloadable PDF you can have on your phone or print out and includes beta on where to stay, eat and day rides you can do in the region. Included with the PDF is a password to a protected page on the site where you can see the individual GPS tracks with POIs and download them directly to your Garmin or phone!
