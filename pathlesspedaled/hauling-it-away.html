Hauling it away…

I loaded up the cargo bike this afternoon AND our Burley flatbed trailer (thank goodness for that!) It took about three trips to get rid of what we had in boxes in the living room and that’s just a fraction of what we’ve got.

The mountains of belongings we have to get rid of (especially me, I’m guilty of hoarding), is really really daunting.


This is really proving a lot harder than we thought, especially if you’re trying to be conscious of NOT just dumping things in the trash.

I’m not quite sure what to do with all my boxes of negatives and prints. I may have a sale of my old black and white work. Might be a good way to raise the money for the trip. What do you think?
