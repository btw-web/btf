Review: Blackburn Outpost Top Tube Bag

In this video I review the Blackburn Outpost Top Tube Bag, a great option if you want organization with your bikepacking bags. For those that would rather read than watch, here is the executive summary:

Pros:
-Organization. 2 side pockets, external mesh pocket and a compartment divider keeps you organized.
-Bright interior lining for finding things easily in low light.
-Top tube straps are rubberized providing good grip and adjustability.

Cons:
-Relatively low volume bag. Not much room compared to some other top tube bags.
-Weather resistance? Lots of seams for water to leach into bag.

Overall, I have really enjoyed this bag over the months I have used it. Bonus points for coming in camo. If you want to get your own and support the site, click on the affiliate link below!
