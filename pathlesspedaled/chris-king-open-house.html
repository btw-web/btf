Chris King Open House!

We just got back from the Chris King Open House where we filmed some truly bike nerdy videos. In this video we chat with a few of the builders and learn about their bikes. There was an obvious trend at this years Open House (beyond the purple and matte bourbon components) and that was the gravel bike. It was interesting to see different interpretations of the gravel bike from those that were more slimmed down mountain bikes to those that were monstercrossed road bikes and everything in between!
