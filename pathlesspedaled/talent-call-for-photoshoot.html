TALENT CALL for Photoshoot!

Are you a cyclist in the SLO / central California coast? We need your help. We are organizing a photoshoot for Adventure Cycling in early April and are looking for cyclists age 20-40 to be part of the shoot. WTF and POC are strongly encouraged to apply. Our goal is to have a roster of 8 cyclists to rotate through for 3 days of shooting. The potential shoot dates are April 9-13. Depending on availability of talent, we may shoot during the week or over the weekend.

COMPENSATION
We know your time is valuable and we want to respect that. While we don’t have a huge budget, we can compensate you $100 per day with meals included for the day.

WHAT TO EXPECT
You don’t have to be a professional model. We want real people that ride bikes. The shoots themselves are pretty laid back. Shoot days will start early to catch some morning light and will end by late afternoon. The imagery will be a mix of riding, camping and interacting at businesses. We will be driving to selected locations to session short stretches of road. You won’t be riding huge miles, just small segments.

Talent (that’s you!) are expected to have your own bike. Because it is a bike touring photoshoot, ideally you will have the necessary racks and bags or bike packing bags to strap on your bike. We can provide SOME bags, but ideally you will have your own.

If you’re interested, please fill out the form below and submit clear photos so that we know what you look like.
