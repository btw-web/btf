Slideshow Presentation at BICAS! on 01/16 at 6:30pm

We are giving a slideshow presentation at BICAS (Bicycle Inter-community Action and Salvage) this Saturday! If you are in, near or around Tucson you should come out! The space itself is vibrant and alive with lots of bike art and bicycle lovers. Their mission is: “Through advocacy and recycling of the bicycle, the BICAS mission is to promote education, art, and a healthy environment while providing service and opportunity for those in need.”

FB Event Link: http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=275480257518&ref=mf
When: Saturday, January 16 at 6:30pm
Where: BICAS – 44W 6th Street, Tucson Arizona, 85706

View Larger Map

We will be showing a few videos from our trip and give a slideshow talking about our journey so far and touring in general. If you do come, bring a jacket since it will be outside projected on a giant wall! Also bring food and drink to munch on.

There will also be lots of bikey things going on like giant photo murals being pasted up and a showing of Africatrek a documentary of a 1992 expedition through Africa by bicycle. Dr. Chip Thomas who was on the expedition will be present as well.

Suggested donation $3, or bring a can of food or an article of clothing to be donated to Casa Maria.

And… if you’ve always wanted to get your very own Bike Mustache, Laura will have a handful available for purchase!

Hope to see you there!
