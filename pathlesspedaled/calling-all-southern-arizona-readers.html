Calling all Southern Arizona readers…

Hey everyone! We are still very much enjoying our time at the Salton Sea (our two-day stay turned into four days, thanks to meeting some wonderful folks who live right near here). Tomorrow, we push off for Slab City, before making our way to Arizona.

Our loose plan is to cross into Arizona through Yuma and head along the southern part of the state, through Tucson and beyond. If you’re along this rough route, or if you know of something along the way that we should be sure to experience, let us know!

And, Tucson peoples… we’re hoping to stay and explore the city for a few days and organize a presentation about our journey. If you’d like to host us or help us set up an event, send us an email.

Thanks!!
