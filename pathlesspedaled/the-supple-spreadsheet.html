The Supple Spreadsheet


I don’t usually get excited about spreadsheets, but this is pretty cool. We reviewed 25 gravel bikes in 2018. Lots of Youtube viewers requested a spreadsheet to organize all the data in one place so people could compare and contrast between perspective bikes. It took a while, but we finally did it.

I present to you the Supple Spreadsheet. In the spreadsheet you’ll find all the bikes we reviewed, links to the actual review videos, how the bikes handle on a spectrum, miscellaneous notes about the bikes and an opinion based “suppleness” score.

THE SUPPLE SPREADSHEET: https://bit.ly/2UYAhKa
