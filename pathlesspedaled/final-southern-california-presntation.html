Final Southern California Presentation!

We’ve been having a great time talking about bike travel and meeting long-time readers during the last couple weeks. It’s really fulfilling for us to get people excited about taking their bikes on adventures. We gave a presentation in San Diego at VeloCult last week and one at Open in Long Beach last night and are scheduled to do one final presentation in El Segundo, CA before we head for ports unknown. The lovely folks at Electric Bikes LA have helped us set up the presentation and are also Brompton dealers who have a wide selection of Bromptons in the store.
