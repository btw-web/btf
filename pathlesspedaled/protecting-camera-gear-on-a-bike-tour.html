Protecting Camera Gear on a Bike Tour

We are packing up and headed out to take Amtrak to Whitefish, MT to ride the iconic Going-to-the-Sun Road this weekend. It has been a bucket list ride for us for some time, especially to get there by train. So I’m packing a lot of video gear in a small handlebar bag. In this vid, I show a simple piece of kit that helps protect cameras, lenses and hard drives when they are in your handlebar bag or pannier.
