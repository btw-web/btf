The Big Hairy Goal: 100k Subscribers on Youtube

I’ve been cranking away on the Youtube and we recently reached 30k subscribers. We’ve set the big hairy goal of reaching 100k within the year AND making this work financially sustainable. In our thinking, there isn’t enough good bike video content that isn’t focused on the extremes (racing / bikepacking / gravel) etc., and we want to provide that voice. Can we make it to 100k within the year? We’ll see.

If you want to support the channel and what we do, we’ve also recently started a Patreon page with a few benefits. Check it out and consider subscribing if you like vids we create!
