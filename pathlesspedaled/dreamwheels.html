It’s 2020 and there is no better way to kick off the year than with some new wheels for new bike adventures! We are excited to announce that we’ve partnered with Chris King to give away an amazing wheelset – a pair of Chris King R45D hubs laced to the super light and strong ENVE G27 rims!  These are the wheels I reviewed in our Expensive Wheels on Cheap Bikes video and were my first introduction to what a really nice wheel set could do you for your riding.  Spoiler:  THEY ARE AMAZING.  They are a little dusty from a few months of testing but are ready to take you on your next adventure.
