Images from the Road

The holidays are fast approaching (can you believe its November already?!) and we’ve been getting emails asking if we can make some prints available to purchase. I’ve started by putting up some of our 2011 and 2012 Calendar images and will be going through our huge image library and making more available as prints in the next few weeks.

I’m using Zenfolio as our shopping cart and print fulfillment. I’ve been pretty happy with their image quality (we’ve got a huge poster version of the Traveler’s Creed hanging in Laura’s mom’s house and it looks fantastic!). You can order 5x7s, 8x10s, 10x15s and even 3×4.5 magnets to put on the fridge. In addition to our calendar images, I’ve been putting up some general landscape and cycling images. One particular series, I’m pretty fond of is called Views from the Train which made is made up of real moody images shot from the Amtrak Empire Builder or Coast Starlight. If there is a print size or image you would like to see but isn’t available, email us and let us know.

I’ve also gotten a few requests for the cyclocross images I’ve been shooting as well, so those are available here.

Again, all orders help us keep going on the road. If you’re planning to give them as gifts, please order with enough time so they arrive by the holidays.


(Keep our adventures going and the site growing! If you’ve enjoyed our stories, videos and photos over the years, consider buying our ebook Panniers and Peanut Butter, or our new 2012 calendar or some of the fun zombie apocalypse shirts we’re designing.)
