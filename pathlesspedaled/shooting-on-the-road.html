Shooting on the Road!

One of my goals is to keep working as a photographer while we’re traveling – making our trip a little more financially sustainable. So far, I’ve been really fortunate and have managed to book some shoots the last few days (making me feel better about sweating all this photo gear up hills!). I’ve done a portrait for vocal artist, Kitzie Stern and an ad shoot for Klean Kanteen.

If you need some photography and see we’re traveling in your area, don’t feel shy about asking : ) I primarily shoot portraits, lifestyle and food photography.
