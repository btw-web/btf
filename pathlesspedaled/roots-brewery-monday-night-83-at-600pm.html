Roots Brewery – Monday Night (8/3) at 6:00pm

Join us for an event that BikePortland is helping put on at Root’s Organic Brewing! We’re super excited to kick off our trip in the bike Mecca of the US.

Where: Root’s Organic Brewing (1520 SE 7th Ave)
When: Monday (8/3) at 6:00pm

I’ll be talking about photography while on a bike tour, show some photos from our latest photo book, talk about all the wacky gizmos we’re bringing, the big green stripe sharrows in Long Beach and of course, our latest endeavor – PathLessPedaled.com.

Hope to see you there!
