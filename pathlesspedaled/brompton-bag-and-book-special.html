Brompton Bag and Book Special!

To help kick off the launch of The Unauthorized Brompton Touring Guide, we’re excited to announce a special limited-time collaboration with Swift Industries!

Until August 24, buyers of The Unauthorized Brompton Touring Guide can receive $20 off their purchase of a new Swift Industries Gilman bag!

The Gilman is a color-customizable bag that integrates with Brompton’s front carrier block. It features five external pockets and a vinyl-lined interior compartment, and is compatible with M, H, P, and S Type Brompton Models.

Many of you know that we have long been fans of Swift Industries, so we’re thrilled to be partnering on this special offer. When you purchase your copy of the e-book, you’ll receive a special code that can be used for checkout on the Swift Industries online order form. (Note: This deal is retro-active! If you’ve already purchased the book, email us and we will send you the code.)
