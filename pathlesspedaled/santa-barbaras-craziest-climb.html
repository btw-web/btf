Santa Barbara’s Craziest Climb!

Santa Barbara has some amazing cycling climbs for a “beach town”. In this video we ride up Old San Marcos Road and Painted Cave Road all the way to Camino Cielo. It is breathtaking in more ways than one. With grades exceeding 14% and roads no wider than single vehicle, the route was entertaining to say the least!
