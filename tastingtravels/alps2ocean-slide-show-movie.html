Alps2Ocean Slide Show Movie

Alps To Ocean New Zealand from Tasting Travels on Vimeo.

One of the things that drives us crazy as cycle tourists is the impossibility to be able to share in full the experiences we are lucky to have through our blog. Particularly those experiences that makes us fall in love with cycle touring, like the fulfilling sense of awe when you cycle through majestic landscapes or the constant encounters with strangers that become our friends.

And so it was that here in New Zealand that we were able to experience these and many other things yet again in a single ride of 301 Km, from Oamaru to Mount Cook Village. The name of the trail is Alps2Ocean, because in theory you would start your bike ride from the alps and you would finish it in the ocean so most of the ride is downhill, and it has been till this date one of the most beautiful trails we have ever ridden in our 4 year bicycle tour.

So in order to make justice to this experience we decided to make our first slideshow video in the hopes that this works better than just pictures. Also, to complement this experience, Annika wrote a very substantial account of our experiences that you can read by clicking here.
