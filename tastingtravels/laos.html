Our friend Andee from Savannakhet has helped us to answer some of the questions. Thans a lot Andee!

What is Laos’ National Dish?

A lot of food and every sauce you can imagine, with a special oyster flavor.

There are several national dishes in Laos and they include: Lab (mixed beef meat with fish sauce, chilli andlemon), bamboo soup (bamboo mixed with fish sauce), beef soup (boiled beef mixed with fish sauce, chilli, and local vegetables), papaya salad and grilled chicken. Papaya salad is made from young papaya and a fish sauce called Pa Dak. It has got a strong smell, but Laotians really seem to like it.

Which do you consider the National Favorite Sport?

Kator for Lao or Sepak takraw for Thai is the name of the National Sport of Lao. The game is very similar to volleyball with the main difference being that the players must pass the ball over the net using their feet, chest, head and knees. Also the ball is essencially made of rattan. It is a very popular sport in South East Asia and a very spectacular one as well. You’ll understand better with the video.

Which would you say is the favorite soft drink in Laos?

Coconut juice but we also tasted many juices and syrups mixed with condensed milk and ice – a real treat.

Who do you consider to be the National Heroe?

According to our friend Andee: His excellency Kaysone Phomvihan is Laos’ national hero. Among the many things he did he fought for Laos against the colonial French and the US army at the time of the secret war. He was born in Savannakhet and his father was Vietnamese. His image is all over Laos, but the most common is in the Lao KIP currency.

Music group loved by young and old?

We have our own song. It is very old and became well known for young and old. It is named: Oh Duang Champa. Champa is Laos’ national flower and this song explains how beautiful the Champa flower is.

Favorite T.V. Show in Laos?

Andee said that Laos T.V. shows have just recently began, but I have seen that people like to watch a lot Thai Soap Operas.

Most popular Brand of car here in Laos?

According to Andee, Toyota and Honda are the most common and come from Japan. Hyundai is becoming popular too because they offer finance plan to acquire one of them. But there are also the famous Tuk Tuk´s, motorbikes or pick up trucks adapted to have a carrier to transport anything! They all come personalized and are a indeed a great ride.

“Sabaidee Luangpabang” is a Thai/ Lao production (the first privately funded Lao movie in 33 years), codirected by the Laotian moviemaker Anousone Sirisackda and Thailand’s Sakchai Deenan, “Good Morning Luang Prabang” is a romantic road movie about a young journalist who grows up in Australia and falls in love with a beautiful Lao girl during a visit to his father’s hometown, Luang Prabang.” – Sonia Kolesnikov-Jessop | NEWSWEEK March 2008

Where would you consider to be the number one touristic destiny for Laotians in Laos?

The city of Luang Prabang is perhaps the favorite destination for Lao people. Once the capital of the power of the powerful kingdom of Lan Xang, it is now the World Heritage Site jewel of the country. Luang Prabang is famous for it’s night market, the nearby waterfalls and nature wonders as the Kuang Si Falls and the famous Haw Kham Royal Palace Museum.

Most common name for Male and Women in Laos?

According to our friend Andee the most popular names in Laos are: Bounmee, Bounma, Airnoy, Somsee for male and E lar, E Loy for female.
