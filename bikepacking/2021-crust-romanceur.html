The Crust Romanceur is Back!

The Romanceür is what Crust describes as “their take on the perfect bike,” combining lugs, discs, a 1″ threaded, and 26″ wheels into a randonneur-style bike that should hold its own on all kinds of different terrain. Crust just announced an incoming batch of Romanceür frames, slated to arrive in November, with a few updates to keep things fresh. The frame and fork are still made from Reynolds 853 steel tubing, but will now be offered in a disc brake and canti brake specific version. The disc model now features front and rear thru-axles, and the fork crown has braze-ons on top for added mounting options. The Crust Romanceür is offered in five sizes (50-62cm) and come in a blue (disc model) or pistachio (canti model) paint job. See additional specs below.

  • Bottom Bracket: 68mm standard threaded BB
  • Seatpost Diameter: 27.2mm
  • Rear Hub: 142×12 (disc) / 130mm QR (canti)
  • Front Hub: 100×12 (disc) / 100mm QR (canti)
  • Tire Clearance (with fenders)*: 26 x 2.3″ / 650B x 48mm / 700c x 45mm
  • Place of Manufacture: Taiwan
  • Price: $1,275 USD

*Canti version is restricted to 650b wheels / tires.

The new Romanceür is scheduled to arrive next month, and framesets retail for $1,275 USD. Head over to CrustBikes.com to ensure you’re notified when they become available. Also, be sure to check out our Rider and Rig featuring Ultrarmonance’s Romanceür.
