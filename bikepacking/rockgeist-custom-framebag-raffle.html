Rockgeist Custom Mudlust Series Framebag Raffle

Our friend Greg Hardy of Rockgeist recently kicked off a monthly raffle to help raise funds to support organizations that uplift, encourage, and support the introduction of our sport to underrepresented and marginalized groups, including Black, Indigenous, People of Color (BIPOC), women, and LGBTQ. Minimizing barriers to entry and growing bikepacking are core parts of Rockgeist’s business mission, and we commend Greg for the good he’s consistently done on both fronts over the years.

This month, Greg is raffling off a made-to-measure Rockgeist Mudlust series framebag with no lead time. Tickets are just $5 and 100% of proceeds will go to our Bikepacking Collective Access Fund, through which we’re working to elevate BIPOC voices through inclusivity in route creation, storytelling, and knowledge sharing within the website, The Bikepacking Journal, and our social community.

Visit Rockgeist.com to purchase raffle tickets before the end of September and you’ll be entered to win a custom Rockgeist Mudlust series framebag, built immediately. By doing so, you’ll help us support aspiring BIPOC writers and photographers and will have a chance to win a great bag from one of the most innovative small brands in the world of bikepacking.
