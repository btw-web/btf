Voile Straps Now Come in 7 New Colors Including Olive and Tan

We were excited when this press release hit our inbox. If you’ve spent any time on this website, you’ve probably seen orange straps holding bags in place or strapping sandals, poles, bottles, peanut butter, or just about anything to a bike. Voilé straps are perhaps the must useful and durable bikepacking accessory you can buy, and a must-have for almost any kit. Originally designed to be an easy-to-use ski strap, the first Voilé strap was made 36 years ago and has since become a staple for adventurers and tradesman, alike. And now, with the addition of three new earth tone colors and three new bright colors, Voilé straps come in 90 different size/color variations.

The new earth tone variations include Olive/Tan, Tan/Grey, Grey/Grey, and Black/Grey. These are available in the Nylon Buckle strap in 12, 15, 20, and 25 inch lengths. Find more info at Voile.com.

There are also three new color options in the aluminum buckle strap: Yellow/Black, Magenta/White, and Cyan/White. These are available in 15, 20, and 25 inch lengths. More at Voile.com.
