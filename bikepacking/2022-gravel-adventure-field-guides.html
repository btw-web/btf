2022 Gravel Adventure Field Guides

Building on the Trinidad-Las Animas County Gravel Adventure Field Guide they released last year, Gravel Adventure Field Guides is now a media company with a mission to “highlight and reinforce grassroots gravel cycling across the U.S.”

They are kickstarting 2022 with news that Patagonia, Arizona; Bend, Oregon; and Pueblo, Colorado will be featured in their own art-inspired pocket cycling guidebooks in 2022. Each guide book is small enough to fit in a jersey pocket and includes route maps, original illustrations and stories, historical vignettes and profiles of local attractions, and exclusive offers from local businesses and regional partners.

As part of the launch, Gravel Adventure Field Guide is excited to present the Explore Trinidad Gravel travel sweepstakes contest. It awards one lucky contestant a $1,500 travel reward for a four-day and three-night trip to visit and ride in Trinidad, Colorado. To enter, fill out a short form on their website and follow them on Instagram @graveladventurefieldguide. The winner will be announced on March 1, 2022.

Head over to GravelAdventureFieldGuide.com to learn more.
