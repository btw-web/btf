Collective Reward #006: Pedal Further Voile Straps!

You can pretty much do anything with Voilé Straps: lash a bag to your handlebars, strap a water bottle to your frame, tie a pair of flip flops to your seat pack, make a pair of camp sandals out of tree bark, and secure your luggage or bike parts for a flight. The list goes on. They’re bombproof, flexible, and infinitely versatile. What’s more, the 25″ straps are perhaps the most versatile, while still being very lightweight. Voilé gave us a box of custom straps with our name and the new tagline for the Bikepacking Journal, PEDAL FURTHER, printed on the side. We’re giving away 30 pairs to 30 lucky Bikepacking Collective members. To enter to win, make sure you’re signed up. And if you’re already a member, you’re already entered. Here are a few photos of the straps in action, and further down you’ll find the winners of the first five Collective Rewards.
