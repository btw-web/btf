Cane Creek Podcast: An Interview with our Founding Editor

For those unfamiliar, Cane Creek is responsible for some of the highest quality bicycle components on the market, including forks, bottom brackets, seat posts, and more, the majority of which are hand assembled in Asheville, North Carolina.

Launched earlier this year, the Cane Creek Cycling Podcast, hosted by marketing director Luke Bukowski, is now five episodes deep. It covers an assortment of cycling-related topics and key players within the industry. For their latest episode, Luke set up a Skype conversation with BIKPEACKING.com founder, Logan Watts, to learn more about the website and Logan’s thoughts on the state of bikepacking. Listen to their full conversation below.

You can hear the rest of the episodes at CaneCreek.com or through Apple Podcasts.
