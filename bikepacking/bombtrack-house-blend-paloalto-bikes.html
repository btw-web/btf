Bombtrack House Blend: PaloAlto Bikes

Following their visits to Loose Cycles in Slovenia and The Woods Cyclery in England (find those linked at the bottom of this post), the third installment in the Bombtrack House Blend shop tour series transports viewers to the northeast of Italy for a peek at PaloAlto Bikes. Located in the small town of Rivignano, PaloAlto is a tiny boutique shop that focuses on small, design-forward brands that share their values.

In this five-minute video, the PaloAlto team offers an overview of their space, some thoughts on why they’re a Bombtrack dealer, and what caught their attention about the Audax in particular. They then go on to build one up into a lightweight all-roader using an interesting blend of components and some custom mods. Watch the video below, then scroll on for a few photos of the bike all built up.
