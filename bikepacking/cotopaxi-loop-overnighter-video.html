Cotopaxi Loop Overnighter (Video)

In September of last year, after months of confinement in Ecuador, Nacho and Simón from 260 Litros (@260litros) seized an opportunity to escape the city for a quick getaway to pedal along our Cotopaxi 360 route for a 140-kilometer bikepacking trip. They put together a short video that sets the stage for their ride and documents their time out riding in the stunning Ecuadorian countryside.
