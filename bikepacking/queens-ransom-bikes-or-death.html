The Queen’s Ransom Route on the Bikes or Death Podcast

In December, Patrick Farnsworth of the Bikes or Death podcast and I took a trip to Phoenix, Arizona, to ride and document the Queen’s Ransom Route. Designed by John Schilling, the Queen’s Ransom is a 227-mile bikepacking route around the greater East Valley of metro Phoenix, Arizona. It weaves together many of the area’s popular singletrack trail systems, regional parks, and the Arizona National Scenic Trail amid stunning topography, rolling dirt roads, and challenging terrain.

Patrick brought his recording equipment along for the ride and we took some time to chat before, during, and after the trip to provide a unique perspective on the route. It’s a great additional resource to our published route guide, and only the second time Patrick has recorded an episode during a bikepacking trip like this. Listen to the full episode below.
