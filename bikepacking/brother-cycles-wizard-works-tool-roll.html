Brother Cycles x Wizard Works Collab Tool Roll

Words by Brother Cycles, Photos by Ollie Harrop (@ollieharrop)

“We’re excited to announce this new collaboration with the guys at Wizard Works, the London based bikepacking bag makers. Wizard Works specialise in making “technical and well performing products while maintaining an aesthetic that lies somewhere between timeless classic and 80s rave” – their products have always caught our eye and we couldn’t be happier to now be working with them.

The collaboration is based around our custom black on tan splatter pattern—developed with Wizard Works and exclusive to Brother Cycles—printed onto an ultra-tough waterproof fabric. Looks awesome, lasts forever. To start, we’ve used the fabric to make a limited run of these super useful tool rolls which are now available to buy online here; however we’ve also made a prototype snack bag which you can see in the photos. If demand is high enough we plan to make these, as well as a whole range of bags in the collaboration fabric.”

The new Brother Cycles Stroma is modeling the tool roll in the photos above. It’s based around a Reynolds 725 steel frame, 700 x 30mm tire clearance, and eyelets for full coverage fenders. The Stroma is their take on an all-season road bike, named after a remote island off the rough north east coast of Scotland, battered by ferocious weather and near impenetrable tides.

Head over to BrotherCycles.com to learn more.
