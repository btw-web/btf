Long Story Short: What’s With all the Holes?

It was only a decade or so ago that most bikes came with no more than four holes in the frame for mounting a couple of water bottle cages. Nowadays, a lot of bikes have quite a few more. Most Salsas have 20+ mounting holes in the frame and fork. But why? Where did these holes come from, and what are they for?
