Adidas Launches New Velosamba Cycling Shoes

In an unexpected reinterpretation of one of their classic sneaker models, Adidas just announced the all-new Velosamba, a clipless-compatible version of the iconic Samba football shoe that they say is geared toward commuters and indoor training. Nearly indistinguishable from classic Sambas upon first glance—a shoe that was a staple in my closet for the better part of my youth—the new Velosamba insole incorporates a full-length reinforced plate for increased stiffness and added pedaling efficiency. Underneath, the rubber outsole, designed for long days of walking around the city, integrates a two-bolt cleat mount for clipping in once you’re on the bike. It has a classic lace-up design and reflective stripes for added visibility on the road.

Adidas Velosamba Specifications

  • Lace closure
  • Coated leather upper with reflective details
  • Textile lining
  • Two-bolt cleat compatibility
  • Full-length internal plate
  • Rubber outsole

The new Adidas Velosamba is available in men’s sizes 4-14 (women’s 5-15) and retails for $120. The US site shows only a black and white version, but the European site is also showing cream white, crew navy, and acid yellow versions of the shoe. Find images of those below.
