Priority 600X Adventure Bike Preorders Open

Preorders are open for the new Priority 600x Adventure that we saw Ryan Van Duzer testing out on the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route earlier this summer. The new 600x Adventure bike is an aluminum hardtail 29er optimized for bikepacking and long-distance trekking. It features fast-rolling knobby tires, a Pinion gearbox with 600% gear range, a Gates Carbon Drive belt drive system, and extra braze-ons for hauling more gear. Production frames will have rear rack mounts, bolt-on frame bag mounts, plus additional cage mounts on the seatstays, under the downtube, and on the top tube as well. The complete bike comes finished with a 100mm RockShox Reba RL fork, WTB KOM Tough wheels, and WTB Ranger 29 x 2.25″ tires. Find the full build kit and purchasing options below.

Note: the photos shown are prototypes and do not reflect the final specs of the 600x.

Build Kit

  • Frame Ultralight 6061 T6 Aluminum
  • Fork RockShox Reba RL, 100m Travel
  • Rims WTB KOM Tough i25 29″ Tubeless Ready
  • Rear Hub Boost Cassette, Four Cartridge Bearing, High Pawl Engagement
  • Front Hub Boost Sealed Bearing, 110x15mm Thru Axle
  • Tires WTB Ranger 29 x 2.25″ TCS Tough
  • Shifter Pinion DS2
  • Brakes Tektro HD-M745 Four Piston, 160mm Rotor
  • Belt Gates Carbon Drive CDX
  • Front Cog 30t Gates CDX
  • Rear Cog 32t Gates CDX
  • Seatpost 30.9 Alloy
  • Saddle WTB Pure
  • Stem Alloy
  • Headset FSA Sealed Bearing,Tapered

Priority Cycles is offering the 600x Adventure bike for $3,500 USD as a complete, but by preordering with a $500 deposit you get an additional $500 off that total price. Deposits are refundable at any time before shipment. Ground shipping in the continental USA will be included in the price, but worldwide shipping is available as per their standard shipping rates. The Priority 600x Adventure bike comes in three sizes: small, medium, large, and will only be available as a complete build, offered for a limited time.

Head over to PriorityCycles.com to learn more.
