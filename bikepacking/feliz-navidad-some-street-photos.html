Feliz Navidad… and some street photos

I thought I’d post a collection of people photos from the stretch between Ixtapan and Oaxaca. Some of these were taken as hip shots and others were just point blank. It seems like Mexican people don’t mind having their photos taken, for the most part. I have gotten several ugly looks and a couple people who have turned away. It sucks when that happens because it throws off your nerve for a couple of days.
