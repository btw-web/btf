SOUTH: The Tour Te Waipounamu Story (Film Trailer)

The Tour Te Waipounamu is a bikepacking race that travels 1,300 kilometers from the northernmost point of New Zealand’s South Island to Slope Point, the most southerly tip. Race founder Brian Alder put together a unique route that travels through backcountry New Zealand, incorporating many private stations that are otherwise inaccessible to the public. It also navigates a mixture of walking trails and historic tracks and paths. South is a film that charts the concept of the race and follows a cross-section of athletes from a variety of disciplines as they take on the inaugural challenge.

The full 1.5-hour feature is available on Vimeo On Demand now, which allows you to rent the film for a 24-hour period or buy a digital copy of your own. Watch the trailer below and then head over to the landing page to learn more.

The 2022 Tour Te Waipounamu is coming up soon on January 23, 2022. Check out the event listing to learn more.
