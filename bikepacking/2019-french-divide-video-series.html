2019 French Divide Video Series

Due to the circumstances of COVID-19, the organizers of the French Divide have decided to release their annual event film in five separate episodes. The first being Episode ‘0’, followed by an episode for each checkpoint along the route. For those unfamiliar, the French Divide is a 2,200km race between the Franco-Belgian border to the Franco-Spanish border. There’s a time limit of 15 days, and it mostly follows the mythical route of Santiago de Compostela. The first two episodes are live and ready to watch below. We’ll be adding in the next three as they go live.

Watch the first two episodes and check out some photos below. We’ll continue to update this page as the videos become available. A reminder for non French speakers to switch on closed captions in the video player.
