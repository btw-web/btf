Cannondale Topstone: Born to Roll & Roam

Just announced, the Cannondale Topstone is a new gravel bike available in three different complete builds that range from $1,000 to $2,000. All three configurations feature an aluminum frame with carbon fork, front and rear thru-axles, triple bottle cage mounts, top tube bag mounts, clearance for 700x45mm tires, a threaded bottom bracket, and dropper post compatibility.
