Check out this Rail Bike with Folding Outriggers

We came across this interesting rail rig the other day and thought it was worth sharing. Built by Cam Engineering, the two custom railroad attachments fold up onto the bike so it can be ridden on the road and then unfolded on the tracks to ride the rails.
