2020 Salsa Timberjack Models Announced

Salsa’s aluminum Timberjack trail hardtail has returned for 2020 with fresh colors, graphics, and build specs. This year’s new models feature the same frame sizes (XS-XL) and specs as before, including a 67.4Â° headtube angle, adjustable 420-437mm chainstays, room for up to 29 x 2.6″ or 27.5 x 3.0″ tires, and alternator dropouts. It’s also available as a frame only, and if aluminum just isn’t enough, the Timberjack Ti frame is also back for another year, too (you might remember Logan’s dream build from 2018). Find a quick look at the 2020 Timberjack models below.

Timberjack XT 29

  • U.S. MSRP $1,999
  • RockShox Sektor RL Fork (130mm travel)
  • Shimano MT501 Brakes/Rotors
  • Shimano MT400/510 hubs with WTB ST i29 TCS 2.0 29″ Rims

Timberjack XT 27.5+

  • U.S. MSRP $1,999
  • RockShox Sektor RL Fork (130mm travel)
  • Shimano MT501 Brakes/Rotors
  • Shimano MT400/510 hubs with WTB ST i40 TCS 2.0 27.5″ Rims

Timberjack SLX 29

  • U.S. MSRP $1,699
  • RockShox Recon RL Fork (130mm travel)
  • Shimano MT401 Brakes/Rotors
  • Shimano MT400/510 hubs with WTB ST i29 TCS 2.0 29″ Rims

Timberjack SLX 27.5+

  • U.S. MSRP $1,699
  • RockShox Recon RL Fork (130mm travel)
  • Shimano MT401 Brakes/Rotors
  • Shimano MT400/510 hubs with WTB ST i40 TCS 2.0 27.5″ Rims

Timberjack Frame Only

  • Aluminum U.S. MSRP $449
