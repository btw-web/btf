Collective Reward #026: Framework Designs Sight Seeker Basket Bag

For Collective Reward #026, we’ve teamed up with Australian custom bag maker Tia Evans and her one-woman shop, Framework Designs, to give away a made-to-order Sight Seeker bag, specifically designed to fit neatly and snugly in the Wald 137 basket. One member of our Bikepacking Collective will receive a Sight Seeker in their choice of fabric and color, a $185 AUD ($130 USD) value.

Sight Seeker Features

  • Press studs + hook and loop straps that run over the rim and down the sides of the basket
  • A strap that tightens over the rim from the front to the back of the basket
  • A detachable padded insert made from recycled plastic bottles, that keeps your possessions protected from bumps and can be used as a pillow when camping!
  • Durable, waterproof, poly/cotton canvas exterior
  • Waterproof, lightweight, polyester lining
  • Two external side pockets
  • Two external back pockets with magnetic close flaps
  • Three internal pockets
  • A detachable, adjustable strap with two attachment places
  • Antique brass hardware
