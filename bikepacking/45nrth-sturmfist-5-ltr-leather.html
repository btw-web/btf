45NRTH Released a Leather Version of the Sturmfist 5

The 45NTH Sturmfist 5 is my go-to glove for the cold days of winter here in the southeast. Temps can hover around freezing in the depths of December, January, and February, and it’s proven to be the perfect glove for riding in such conditions. The new LTR version takes all the functionality of the softshell Sturmfist 5 that I’ve come to love and wraps it in supple Durable Water-Resistant (DWR) distressed goat leather.​ Find photos and specs below.

  • Wind- and water-resistant
  • Merino wool-blend liner for moisture control and comfort
  • Hook-and-loop closure around wrist
  • Finger length tailored for cycling fit to maximize insulation performance
  • Suede nose wipe on thumb
  • 100g Polartec® Alpha insulation
  • Durable water-resistant (DWR) distressed goat leather outer shell
  • Leather palm
  • Merino wool-blend shell lining

The 45NRTH Sturmfist 5 LTR will be available mid-November in X-Small, Small, Medium, Large, X-Large, and 2X-Large sizes and is rated for temps from 15°F (-9°C) to 35°F (2°C). Unfortunately, it has the fairly hefty price tag of $115 (the regular Sturmfist 5 is $85), so it definitely won’t be for everyone. With that, we hope it’s a glove that will last through a decade of use—for the record, I’ve been using my regular Sturmfist 5 for about four winter seasons and they’re holding up quite well. For more, head over to 45NRTH.com. Also, note that they simultaneously released the Sturmfist 4 LTR for extreme winter temps.
