Downtime Podcast on Racism in Cycling

The latest episode of Downtime, The Mountain Bike Podcast features an excellent conversation on the topic of racism in cycling and the world of outdoor sports between host Chris Hall, downhill mountain biker / commentator Eliot Jackson (@eliotjackson), and snowboarding pioneer / outdoor industry veteran Phil Young (@philskills). Their wide-ranging chat touches on a lot of important topics, presents insightful reflections from Eliot and Phil, and and offers resources for listeners to help make cycling a more welcoming and diverse sport.
