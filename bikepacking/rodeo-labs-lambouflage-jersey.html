Rodeo Labs’ Lambouflage Jersey is back!

The 2019 Rodeo Labs Lamb Transport Jersey (pictured below) earned a spot in Logan and Virginia’s 2019 Year End Picks article last year, appreciated not only for it’s incredibly cute graphics, but it’s wool-blend construction, three generously sized rear pockets, and substantial warmth to weight ratio. The 2020 Lambouflage Jersey retains the same construction as the Lamb Transport Jersey, but features new graphic from artist/illustrator Brian Biggs, and a more robust YKK zipper. Find a full description from Rodeo Labs below.

Rodeo Labs: We’re happy to present to you the 2020 Lambouflage jersey. Camouflage is dead, long live Lambouflage. Wearing this jersey on a fall day you may vanish into the orange tree canopy from a distance – like a ninja on a bike, but once the cold muted palette of winter sets in the jersey’s warm colors come to life, not to mention the dozens and dozens of lambs staring back at you from the void upon closer examination – each with its own personality.

Our jersey retains the same construction as our previous Woolie and Lamb Transport jerseys with their luxurious Italian Merino / synthetic fabric blend. The jerseys are warm, but not to warm that you can’t also wear them on a clear, slightly crisp sunny day throughout the year. This year we upgraded the YKK zippers to a more robust spec while retaining our reinforced rear pockets.

We have a certain number of jerseys in stock and ready to ship for the holidays for those of you who are quick to the trigger. We expect that this jersey will sell out similar to last year’s. Once sold out we will allow a backorder period until Wednesday December 11th at which point we will once again halt sales of the design forever. Backordered jerseys will take about a month to produce before shipping, so look for those to go out mid January.

Consult the sizing chart carefully. If you order the wrong size and need to exchange we will do our best to help but in the event that we don’t have your size to exchange we will offer to let you exchange from the second batch of jerseys or request a product refund. Only un-ridden jerseys in brand new condition can be exchanged or refunded. Our woolie jersey is unisex, but check back in 2021 for other jerseys as we expand our women’s specific sizing in collaboration with the Rodeo Adventure Posse women’s team.

The Rodeo Labs Lambouflage Jersey costs $135 USD, comes in seven sizes, and is available now on Rodeo-Labs.com. Currently they only offer unisex sizing, but Rodeo Labs promises that the line will be expanded with women’s specific options for 2021. Stay tuned.
