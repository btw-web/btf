Collective Reward #069: Maxxis Tires

We’ve teamed up with Maxxis for Collective Reward #069, and two randomly selected Bikepacking Collective members will receive a set of any Maxxis bicycle tires of their choosing. Winners can pick from Maxxis’ expansive range of tires for mountain, gravel, road, cyclocross, fat bikes, and more. Check out their full line of tires here.

Over the years, we’ve had great results riding a ton of Maxxis tires on all kinds of review bikes through varying conditions on a wide array of trips around the world. We’ve also noticed them in a ton of our weekly Reader’s Rig posts and on an increasing number of Bikepacking Race Rigs.

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, it isn’t too late to get signed up to be eligible for this giveaway. Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible, and membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world. Members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards (including a set of these tires), receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and more.
