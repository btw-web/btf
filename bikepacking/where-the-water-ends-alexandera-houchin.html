Where the Water Ends: Video Profile of Alexandera Houchin

We’ve been enjoying following Alexandera Houchin’s pedal-powered accomplishments for a while now, and most recently we had the pleasure of working together with her on Ditibise, a feature story that our Bikepacking Collective members will be able to read shortly in the third issue of The Bikepacking Journal.

This new video profile, produced by the University of Minnesota, Duluth, lets viewers in to learn more about her personal life, her cycling origin story, her motivations, and some of her accomplishments on the bike in the typically humble manner we’ve grown to admire her for. Give it a watch here:
