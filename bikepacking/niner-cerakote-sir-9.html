Niner Cerakote SIR 9 Dream Build

Niner’s Graphic Designer Nate Adams didn’t hold back with his recent SIR 9 build, building it up as a “Swiss army bike” that will handle anything he throws at it. He went with a Cerakote paint job, something we’ve seen Rodeo Labs using a lot lately, which is one of the highest performing finishes available right now. Cerakote is extremely durable, lightweight, hydrophobic, and can be applied to nearly any type of material. The build is kitted out with a top-end SRAM 12-speed drivetrain, ENVE wheels, and components from Deity. Find the full build detailed below.

Build Kit

  • Frame Niner SIR 9
  • Fork Rockshox Pike Ultimate 120mm
  • Wheelset Enve M630 29″
  • Handlebar Deity Black Label
  • Stem Deity Copperhead
  • Seatpost RockShox Reverb
  • Saddle SDG Radar
  • Derailleur SRAM XO1
  • Cassette SRAM XO1
  • Crank SRAM XO1
  • Brakes SRAM Guide G2 RSC
  • Pedals Deity Black Kat
  • Tires Maxxis Rekon (F) Rekon Race (R)

For the full story and more insight on the build, you can find the original blog post over on the Niner Bikes website.

Niner updated the SIR 9 paint job and build kits for 2021, and it’s available as a frame only for those looking to build up a dream bike of their own. Learn more at NinerBikes.com.
