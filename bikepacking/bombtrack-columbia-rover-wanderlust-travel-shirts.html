Bombtrack × Columbia Rover and Wanderlust Travel Shirts

In a somewhat unsuspected collaboration, Bombtrack Bicycle Co. and Columbia Sportswear just teamed up to launch two versions of what they’re calling the “ultimate shirt for off-road adventures.” The Rover and Wanderlust travel shirts both have short sleeves and are sewn from a lightweight, breathable technical fabric that wicks away sweat to help keep you fresh. Rear side pleats help provide an unhindered range of motion whether you’re down in the drops or bushwhacking through the undergrowth. The Rover and Wanderlust shirts feature a spread collar, a reinforced placket, and flat buttons for secure closure. Two button-down, pleated chest pockets and a smaller zippered pocket offer additional storage.

Talking about the new collaboration, Bombtrack founder Manuel Schürholz, who’s been wearing Columbia clothing since he first started touring in the 1990s, said, “I’m really excited about the collaboration. It’s come full circle for me. When I first started riding I didn’t feel comfortable wearing closefitting cycle jerseys, so I turned to Columbia as it was the best adventure apparel on the market. Now, this partnership almost 30 years later means I can ride with pride in a shirt with two logos that define two stages in my life spent on two wheels.”

The Rover and Wanderlust are available in two men’s colors (Shark and Stone Green) and one women’s color (Nocturnal). Both models come in men’s sizes S to XL and women’s sizes S to L. They’re available exclusively through Bombtrack distributors at a price of €69.99 (or local equivalent).
