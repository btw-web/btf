Collective Reward #040: Big Agnes Air AXL Pillows

Our fortieth Collective Reward comes from the good people at Big Agnes – makers of ultralight tents, sleep systems, apparel, and more – who are giving an Air AXL inflatable camp pillows to five members of our Bikepacking Collective.

After years of packing spare clothes into a stuff sack as a makeshift pillow, most of us on the BIKEPACKING.com team have arrived at the realization that having a proper camp pillow can make all the difference for a good night’s sleep, especially when it comes at almost no weight or space penalty in the case of the Big Agnes Air AXL. And it comes packed with a range of features, including built-in heat reflective technology, an internal antimicrobial treatment, a four-way stretch nylon construction, and more.

  • Packed weight 1.6oz / 45g
  • Packed size 4.5″ x 2.5″ x 2″ / 10 x 6 x 5cm
  • Inflated size 16″ x 10″ x 4″ / 41 x 25 x 10cm
  • Additional details BigAgnes.com

As always, Bikepacking Collective membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, for the same price. Members are automatically entered to win Collective Rewards. Not yet a member? Join the Bikepacking Collective before 11:59 pm EST on Thursday, October 17th, 2019, and you’ll be automatically entered to win a Big Agnes Air AXL Pillow (along with many other Collective Rewards) and your membership will begin with The Bikepacking Journal 03.
