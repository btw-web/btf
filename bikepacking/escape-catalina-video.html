Escape to Catalina (video)

Feature photo by Josh Weinberg

Escape to Catalina is the newest episode in Kitsbow’s “36 Hours,” a series that focuses on the fact that adventure is just outside our door. In this episode, three friends, separated by geography who don’t get to see one another very often, set out on the desolate dirt roads of Catalina Island to reflect and escape from city life…

Riding a bike is kind of like meditation. Long days in the saddle give you plenty of time to reflect…

Find the full story over at Kitsbow.com. Also watch the previous episode of 36 Hours here. And, be sure to check out Yuto and Shirley’s story and photo gallery from Catalina Island, Making Weekends Count.
