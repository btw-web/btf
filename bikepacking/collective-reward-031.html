Collective Reward #031: Bedrock Cairn 3D Pro Adventure Sandals

We’ve teamed up with Bedrock Sandals for Collective Reward #031, and two lucky members of our Bikepacking Collective will take home a pair of ultra-grippy Cairn 3D Pro Adventure Sandals in their choice of size and color, a $120 value. Cass took a pair of the original Cairn Adventure Sandals on a bikepacking trip through Baja California and had a lot of good things to say in his full review.

You’ve probably seen the California-based company’s sandals pop up somewhere on social media as they become increasingly popular among bikepackers for their all-day comfort, but they’ve actually be around since 2011, when founders Nick and Dan launched their first model on Kickstarter. Since then, they’ve continued to expand their line of offerings, all the while maintaining a commitment to building durable, repairable footwear. The new Cairn 3D Pro is the latest step in the evolution of their lightweight, highly adjustable sandals, built for long days of riding, hiking, or whatever else you get up to.
