New 8-Bit System from Wolf Tooth Components

We’re big fans of Wolf Tooth Component’s ever-evolving line of made-in-USA line of multi-tools. Their original Pack Pliers were released back in 2018, and in 2020 they added the 17-function 8-Bit Pack Pliers, which is based around the same folding design. Today, Wolf Tooth launched the entire 8-Bit System, which looks to complete the toolkit by including a more comprehensive assortment of functions that should help get you out of nearly any sticky mechanical situation out on the trail. The new system is made up of two entirely new tools that pair with the 8-Bit Pack Pliers. Learn about the new 8-Bit Chainbreaker + Utility Knife Multi-Tool and 8-Bit Tire Lever + Rim Dent Remover Multi-Tool below.

Wolf Tooth 8-Bit Chainbreaker + Utility Knife Multi-Tool

The 8-Bit Chainbreaker + Utility Knife Multi-Tool has three functions. It has a chain breaker on one end and a retractable utility blade on the other. The chain tool is operated by an included 3mm hex L key that doubles as a tire plug inserter. A machined slot fits the L key and converts the entire multi-tool into a tire plug inserter, with the forked end of the L key extending outward. The handles of this multi-tool slide open to provide storage for tire plug inserts, a valve core, and a spare mini utility blade.

  • Material: 7075-T6 aluminum
  • Weight: 71 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: USA
  • Price: $69.95 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com

Wolf Tooth 8-Bit Tire Lever + Rim Dent Remover Multi-Tool

The 8-Bit Tire Lever + Rim Dent Remover Multi-Tool has two functions. A slender, ergonomic plastic tire lever is on one end opposite of a machined aluminum rim dent remover. The tire lever will work with all tubed and tubeless tires, while the rim dent remover is compatible with all metal rims.

  • Material: 7075-T6 aluminum, high-strength nylon composite lever
  • Weight: 29.5 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: USA
  • Price: $19.95 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com

These tools were designed, engineered, and machined at Wolf Tooth in Minnesota with strong 7075-T6 aluminum. They are now available on WolfToothComponents.com and with all bikeshops that stock Wolf Tooth products. These new multi-tools can be purchased individually (priced above) or as a complete 8-Bit Kit One set for $139.95 USD that includes all three.
