Wizard Works x Get Wild Matty Limited Edition Collaboration

It’s always great to see talented makers teaming up to create one-of-a-kind products. There’s no question that most bag makers have some level of artist influence, but working with someone like Matty Waudby (@GetWildMatty) is bound to yield something special. In their second collaboration, Wizard Works and Matty Waudby are producing a super limited run of embroidered bags, known as the “Follow The Yellow Grav Road” collection. They’re creating just five Lil Presto! Barrel Bags and five Voila! Snack Bags—all hand embroidered in York and crafted in London from a modern dry waxed canvas, sustainably manufactured in Rochdale.

“The material chosen for this project is a dry wax canvas produced by British Milleraine. One of the original wax canvas mills, British Milleraine have been making wax canvas in the UK since 1880. The waxed canvas used is registered with the better cotton initiative and is dyed and finished at their UK facility, under stringent environmental and quality standards. There is zero waste and water use in the wax process and the fabric is free from fluorinated carbons often found in water repellent fabrics.” -Wizard Works

Both bags will be available at Wizard.Works on June 1st at 6:00pm London time. Remember, there are only five of each, so act fast!
