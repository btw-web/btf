SimWorks Super Yummy Now in 26″

Designed by SimWorks and produced by Panaracer, the Super Yummy is their versatile off-road tire for riders seeking some extra performance from their bike. And as of today, the SimWorks Super Yummy is available in 26″ in addition to the previously available 27.5″ and 29″ versions. All diameters are 2.22″ wide, feature SimWorks’ “Peanut Butter” sidewalls, and are tubeless compatible.

Here’s what SimWorks has to say about the new Super Yummy: “Built with the same tread pattern as our 27.5” and 29” varieties, 26” Super Yummy is tubeless compatible when mated to an appropriate rim with ample depth and a prominent hook. Our favorite “Peanut butter” sidewalls and moto-inspired neo-retro Super Yummy font add style points to a tire that will provide sure footing on a wide array of surfaces.”

Learn more at Sim.Works.
