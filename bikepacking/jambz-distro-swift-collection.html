Limited Edition Jambz Distro × Swift Collection

Inspired by Jambz Distro founder Jorja Creighton’s pig, Pamela, the new Jambz Distro × Swift Industries collection comes in a soft pink colorway and with a Jambz Distro patch so you can represent every day. The collection includes the Swift Zeitgeist, Bandito, and the Side Kick stem pouch, which are available to purchase separately or as a complete kit for 10% off.

Jambz Distro x Swift Bandito Handlebar and Saddle Bag

The Bandito Handlebar and Saddle Bag is the perfect size for your tools, a windbreaker, and your small stuff. It includes two Voile Nano straps, has D-rings for a shoulder strap, and a daisy chain for lashing on gear or lights. The Bandito costs $147 AUD and is available here.

Jambz Distro x Swift Side Kick Stem Pouch

The Swift Side Kick Stem Pouch is a multi-functional bag designed to fit water bottles and other essentials. It can secure directly to your bars, a basket, or a number of Swift’s other bags. It costs $80 AUD and is available here.

Jambz Distro x Swift Zeitgeist Bag

The Zeitgeist can be used as a handlebar or saddle bag. It has two sets of mounts: one that matches the standard spacing of integrated saddle loops and another that’s set further apart for use on the bars. It has an HDPE liner for added structure, compression straps to keep things tight, two side pockets, and a daisy chain for the dangles. It costs $307 AUD and is available here.

Find the entire collection and more at JambiJambi.com.
