Collective Reward #018: Two Pairs of Schwalbe Tires

All of us on the BIKEPACKING.com team have enjoyed riding various Schwalbe tires on different bikes over the years, from classic Marathon Mondials, to studded Ice Spiker Pros, to light and fast Thunder Burts. And for Collective Reward #018, we’re excited to be able to give two lucky members of our Bikepacking Collective a pair of Schwalbe tires of their choosing.

If you’ve already joined the Bikepacking Collective, thanks for your support! You’re entered to win. If you haven’t yet signed up, consider doing so for your chance to win a set of Schwalbe tires, plus tons of additional Collective Rewards and other great benefits.
