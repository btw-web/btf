Collective Reward #100: Bikepacking the World Map by Alex Hotchin

It’s hard to believe we’ve already hit the milestone of 100 Collective Reward giveaways, and we’ve lined up something special to celebrate the occasion. Even better, everybody wins! For Collective Reward #100, all of our Bikepacking Collective members will be receiving a copy of Australian artist Alex Hotchin’s new and revised “Bikepacking the World” map along with their copy of the upcoming seventh issue of The Bikepacking Journal.

Hand-illustrated by Alex, one of our favorite artists, “Bikepacking the World” is a beautifully detailed map that includes dozens of iconic bikepacking routes around the world, plus a vibrant array of rich and colorful imagery associated with the various corners of the world through which they travel. The maps we’ll be including with the new issue are printed in Germany using high-quality inks and paper and measure around 53cm (21″) wide by 40 cm (16″) tall. As long as you’re signed up for our Bikepacking Collective before the October 17th cutoff, you’re all set to receive one!

Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible. If you enjoy reading the site, please consider joining our Bikepacking Collective. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards, receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.
