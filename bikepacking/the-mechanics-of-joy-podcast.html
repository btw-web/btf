New Podcast: The Mechanics of Joy

Our friend Jon Woodroof (@jonwoodroof) and his co-host, Kike Molares, recently launched a freewheeling new monthly podcast that they describe as “a show about the nuts and bolts of finding your stoke.” Mixing conversations on an unexpected mix of two-wheeled topics, interviews, and an eclectic mix of musical selections, The Mechanics of Joy promises to be well worth an hour of your time on the third Thursday of each month. You can listen live on Amsterdam-based Echobox Radio or wait for episodes to be posted to Mixcloud 10 days after airing.

Listen to the first episode above, and mark your calendars for next month’s episode at 2 p.m. CET on Thursday, September 16th, which Jon says will have a focus on bikepacking, with a recap of the Bohemian Border Bash, their first in-studio guest, and more.
