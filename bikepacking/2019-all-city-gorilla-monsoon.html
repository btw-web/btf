All-City Announces 2019 Gorilla Monsoon

Other than the new dropper post on the complete and the frameset’s iridescent, lizard-like, blue-green fade paint scheme, not much has changed on the 2019 All-City Gorilla Monsoon. Either way, orange haters will rejoice, and we’re excited to see a quick turnaround on this award-winning machine. You can read our full review of the original Gorilla Monsoon here, see it and more 650B “Gravel Plus” rigs here, and find a few photos and specs on the 2019 frameset and complete below.

2019 All-City Gorilla Monsoon Frameset

The 2019 All-City Gorilla Monsoon frameset will be offered at $850 with the following features and specifications:

  • Frame Spec: 612 Select CroMoly steel; double-butted down, top, and seat tubes; externally tapered, ovalized and dimpled chainstays; tapered seatstays; replaceable derailleur hanger
  • Fork: 4130 CroMoly tapered fork blades, mountain lugged crown, 15mm thru-axle dropouts, straight steerer tube, IS disc brake tabs
  • Rear Spacing: 142 x 12mm
  • Brake Compatibility: Disc only, 160mm front and rear minimum rotor size
  • Seatpost Diameter: 30.9mm
  • Steerer Tube / Headset: 1-1/8″ threadless, EC34 upper and lower, crown race 30mm
  • Bottom Bracket: 73mm English
  • Tire Clearance: 27.5 x 2.4″
  • Extras: Rack and fender mounts, E.D. coated for durability and rust prevention

2019 All-City Gorilla Monsoon Complete

The 2019 All-City Gorilla Monsoon will be available for $2,099 with the following build kit:

  • Headset Cane Creek 40 Series
  • Stem Promax 3D forged stem
  • Handlebar Salsa Cowchipper
  • Bar Tape All-City silicone bar tape w/locking end plugs
  • Shifter/Brake Lever SRAM Apex 1 mechanical (1×11)
  • Rear Derailleur SRAM Apex 1, long cage
  • Brakeset Hayes CX Expert, 160mm front and rear
  • Crankset FSA Comet 1x, direct mount 36T ring
  • Bottom Bracket MegaExo
  • Seatpost TranzX dropper, 30.9, 80mm travel, 31.8mm trigger clamp
  • Saddle All-City Gonzo
  • Cassette SunRace 11-speed, 11-42t
  • Chain SRAM PC-1110
  • Hubs WTB 32H, Shimano driver, 6-bolt rotor mount
  • Rims WTB ST i25 TCS 2.0, 27.5″, 32H
  • Tires WTB Riddler TCS, 27.5 x 2.4″

The The 2019 All-City Gorilla Monsoon will be available through dealers immediately. For a detailed report, make sure to read our review and check out Joe Tonsager’s custom build. Plus, stay tuned for more photos of this new frame. In the meantime, head over to AllCityCycles.com for additional details…
