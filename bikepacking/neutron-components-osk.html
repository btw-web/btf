Pre-Orders Open for Neutron Components’ Oh Sh!t Kit

Neutron Components’ new Oh Sh!t Kit (O.S.K.) joins a growing number of multi-tools designed specially to tuck away in your mountain bike’s bar ends. Their O.S.K. requires no modifications or tools to setup, and fits all handlebars with an inside diameter of 19mm or larger. There are two parts of the kit, for each end of the bars—a multi-tool that includes a barebones selection of standard 1/4″ driver bits, and the chain breaker. Both tools can be purchased separately, or as a package for $89.00 NZD ($52.00 USD).

The body of the wrench is made from a composite to keep weight down, while the driver bits and chain breaker are both made from a cast steel. Two different sized o-rings are included to ensure a snug fit in the bar end. Scroll down for a breakdown of what the complete O.S.K provides.

  • Hex drivers: 2.5, 3, 4, 5, 6mm
  • Torx driver: T25
  • Phillips driver: #1
  • Spoke wrench: 3.2, 3.4, 3.6, 4.3mm
  • Total weight: 130g
  • Valve core tool
  • Tire Lever
  • Chain breaker
  • Bottle opener
  • Designed, built, and tested in New Zealand

Note that there will be some delays in shipping if you order an O.S.K. now. According to the company, “Neutron Components supports the level 4 lockdown in New Zealand to reduce the spread of COVID-19. Any items purchased on the online store will be fulfilled and sent as soon as possible after the lockdown has been lifted. There’s a money back guarantee on all orders placed during this period.”

To learn more, head over to NeutronComponents.com.
