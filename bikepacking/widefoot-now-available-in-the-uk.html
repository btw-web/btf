WideFoot Now Available in the UK!

WideFoot is behind some of the best US-made bottle and gear cages out there. We’ve personally tested their products over thousands of miles and can vouch for their quality. Designed and manufactured in the US, their products are utilitarian and high-quality, easily suited for the rigors of bikepacking and everyday life. Although they have loads dealers across the United States, there are only a small selection of overseas dealers to choose from for folks outside of the US. Fortunately, our friends at Wizard Works in London have just come on as the first UK retailer of WideFoot products, making it easier and more affordable for their UK customers to get their hands on some solid WideFoot gear.

They are stocking the LitreCage, CargoMount, and Widefoot’s co-branded Deuce Trowel (my personal favorite)—all of which are available now through Wizard.Works. Check out the price and specs on each below.
