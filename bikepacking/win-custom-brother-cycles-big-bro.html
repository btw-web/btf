Win a Custom Painted Brother Cycles Big Bro Frameset

UK-based Brother Cycles is giving away a one of a kind custom painted Big Bro frameset (size medium), and entering couldn’t be easier. The frame is custom painted by Cole Coatings Workshop, a multi-award-winning, London-based custom paint specialist. All you have to do is sign up for the Brother Cycles newsletter using the form on this page to put yourself in the running for a rad new bike.

As the team at Brother Cylcles describes the color, “This one of a kind ‘Big Bruv’ paint job features an orange rubble textured rear end which merges into a forest green splatter with a repeating leaf pattern across the front half of the frame. The leaves are a mix of dark and light green (some transluscent!), copper and orange which is actually the original frame colour showing through.”

Want to learn more about the Big Bro? Cass reviewed one last year, and was impressed with the bike’s handling, capability, and old-school vibe. Read the full review here. We also featured Tim Fairbrother’s Big Bro in a Rider & Rig article earlier this year.
