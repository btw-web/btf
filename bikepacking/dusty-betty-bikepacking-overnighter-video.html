Dusty Betty Ladies Night Out (videos)

Tess at the Dusty Betty YouTube channel has made a few bikepacking videos over the years, including this one from the Gila River Ramble route. This time, she made two videos around the Dusty Betty “ladies night out” bikepacking overnighter. In the first video, Tess talks through the bike and gear as it was prepped and packed for the trip, and the second follows their crew for a local overnighter in beautiful Sedona.
