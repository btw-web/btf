Twin Six Standard Rando 2.0 Available in Saffron and Black

Minnesota-baed Twin Six recently released their Standard Rando 2.0 in two new colors for presale through May 15th. The Standard Rando 2.0 improves upon the original with a new T6 Middle Finger dropout system, flat mount brakes, front and rear thru axles, 44mm headtube, optional color-matched carbon fork available soon, and a load of bottle mounts. Find details and pics below
