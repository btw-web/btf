The State 6061 Black Label All-Road is Fast, Light, and Affordable

Announced last week, the new 6061 Black Label All-Road is State Bicycle Co.’s latest addition to their Black Label series. All the bikes within the series are said to be light and responsive, thanks to their ultra lightweight aluminum frames. The 6061 Black Label All-Road is positioned as a fast, versatile gravel bike, with the option for 650b or 700c wheels, an 11-speed drivetrain, and mounts for fenders and multiple bottles. Although it lacks mounts for racks or additional cargo, it appears to be a well-priced option for those looking to give gravel riding a shot.

It comes with a mix of SRAM Apex and State-branded components to keep the cost down, a carbon fork, and front and rear thru-axles. The frame has clearance for 700c x 45mm or 650b x 50mm tires, and State gives you the option to buy two wheelsets for an additional $349.99, complete with rotors, cassettes, tires, and tubes to making switching back and forth a breeze. As an added bonus, State is also offering an option to upgrade to their new carbon Monster Fork for $179.99—which provides mounts for Anything-style cages, fenders, and a rack or dynamo light on the crown. Find the complete build kit below.

Complete builds start at $1,399.99 USD and come in either Copper Brown or Pigeon Gray. It’s also available as a frameset for $679.99 USD.
