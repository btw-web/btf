King Cage Releases Titanium Side Loader Cage

Colorado-based King Cage just launched their new Side Loader Cage, building on their impressive, high-quality line of US-made cages and accessories. We’ve always had great experiences using King Cage products, specifically their Universal Support Bolts for adding cages onto frames and forks, and the Manything Cage for hauling Nalgenes and Klean Kanteens.

The new Side Loader Cage is manufactured from 3-2.5 titanium tubing and uses a unique mounting design that can be positioned in the centre or offset to the left or right for easier bottle access. The Side Loader Cage has been in development for a while now, but they’ve finally gotten the tooling and production to a point where they’re ready to sell them. Check out two videos Ron posted about the cages below.

The new cages cost $65 USD per piece and are available now from KingCage.com. For a limited time, those who purchase a Side Loader Cage and provide feedback to King Cage will be put into a draw for a King Cage Hoodie.
