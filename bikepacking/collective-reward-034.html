Collective Reward #034: Knog PWR Trail Lights

For Collective Reward #034, three members of our Bikepacking Collective will receive a Knog PWR Trail headlight. But it’s not just another bike light, it’s also a 5,000 mAh power bank. And the clever offset mounting bracket allows you to run the PWR Trail above or below your handlebars, all the while keeping it out of the way of other bar-mounted devices. With a maximum output of 1,000 lumens and an elliptical beam, it’s more than bright enough to illuminate the trails and roads ahead, and its adjustable modes let you get up to 300 hours of runtime using Eco Flash mode.

The Knog PWR Trail light is part of a modular ecosystem of interchangeable light heads, batteries, and accessories. And there’s a compatible range of headlamps, speakers, lanterns, and more on the way soon. This video below walks through the PWR lineup:

  • Light Output: 1000 Lumens
  • Power Bank: 5000 mAh
  • Size: 128 x 35mm
  • Weight: 230g
  • Fits Handlebars: 22.2mm – 31.8mm
  • Includes: Light, Side Mount, Gimbal Shim, USB Cable

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, join today and you’ll be eligible for this Knog giveaway (and a growing list of other Collective Rewards), and you’ll also receive two issue of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, along with several other great member benefits. If you’ve already joined, you’re entered to win. Good luck!
