PAUL gets the blues…

PAUL Components, Chico, California fabricator of made in the USA, solid and reliable bike components, introduces a blue colorway for their anodized aluminum parts. These Blue parts — including a few of our favorites, the Boxcar Stem, Klamper mechanical brakes, and Love Levers — will be made in small batches over the next 6 months, then retired until blue is back in fashion again… every once in a blue moon. Get ’em while their cool, and available.
