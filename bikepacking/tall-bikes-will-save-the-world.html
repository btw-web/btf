Tall Bikes Will Save The World

“The tall bike remains on the fringes — only embraced by the daring, the creative, and the foolish.” As referenced in Cass’ On One Inbred retrospective/rebirth article earlier today, Tall Bikes Will Save The World retraces the visionary Zenga Brothers’ quest to change the world through the power of art bikes and the tall bike. This film was originally released in 2017. If you missed it, make sure to check it out. And if you’ve already seen it, watch it again…

“The ridiculous nature of it became ridiculously practical. And all the sudden, I’m like, it’s the only bike I want to take to the grocery store. It’s the only bike I want to tour the world on.”
