Lindarets Titanium Valve Stems Now in 60mm Version

It might not be the first upgrade we’d recommend for a new bike, but it’s hard to argue with Lindarets’ attention to detail when it comes to the design of their Terske Titanium Tubeless Valve Stems. With replaceable rubber gaskets, a larger internal thru-hole, and a design that’s made to last, there’s something to be said for investing in high-quality components where it counts.

If you missed it, we put together an in depth look at the Terske Titanium Valve Stems, including a Q&A with designer Marc Basiliere. Find that here. At the time, the valves were only available in a 44mm length, but they’ve now been expanded to include a 60mm length for folks running deep section rims. They share all the same features as the standard version, but are only available in a natural Ti or off-black finish.

I’ve been running my standard 44mm valve stems for several months now, and have had no issues at all. They’ve held tight on the rim, haven’t leaked, and after installing some new tires the other day I didn’t notice any sealant build up in the thru-hole, either. Overall, I’ve been impressed.

  • Place of Manufacture: Taiwan
  • Price: $49.95 USD / $9.95 for Refresh Kit
