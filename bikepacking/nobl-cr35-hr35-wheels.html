New CR35 and HR35 Gravel Wheels from NOBL

NOBL Wheels just added two new gravel wheels to their lineup, the carbon CR35 and HR35. Both are designed to be lightweight and versatile enough to handle both roads and trails, yet still durable enough for your bikepacking rig. The CR35 is clincher rim, while the HR35 is hookless. Both are 35mm deep, providing a nice mix of aerodynamics and comfort, without too much extra weight. They feature front and rear rim specific layups, with a 40g difference between the two. While both models are 29mm wide externally, the C35 clincher version has a 22mm internal width and the hookless H35 is 24mm inside. NOBL worked with a number of manufacturers to produce steel molds for the highest tolerances and a blemish free finish. The rims are ready to ride right from the mold, complete with a scratch resistant surface, raw finish, and subtle graphics.

The NOBL HR35 is most applicable as a bikepacking wheelset, with the the ability to effectively run tires up to 50mm wide, and with recommended pressures between 20 to 35 psi. Both are available for purchase as a rim only, or in several complete wheelset builds using hubs from DT Swiss, Industry Nine, Onyx, or Chris King. Find specs and NOBL’s product launch video below.

Nobl HR35

  • Hookless design is best suited to larger tires and lower pressures.
  • Internal Rim Width: 24mm
  • Front: 24 spoke, Rear: 28 spoke
  • Front: 400g, Rear: 440g
  • Price: $400 per rim
  • Wheelsets start at $1,200 USD

Nobl CR35

  • Bead hook is essential for higher pressure applications.
  • Internal Rim Width: 22mm
  • Front: 24 spoke, Rear: 28 spoke
  • Front: 410g, Rear: 450g
  • Price: $400 per rim
  • Wheelsets start at $1,200 USD

NOBL Wheels offers a lifetime warranty on all of their wheels. That means that they’ll send you a replacement rim at no charge if you break one while riding. For non riding accidents, NOBL offers a 50% discount on a replacement. This coverage lasts indefinitely for the original purchaser. Learn more at NOBLWheels.com.
