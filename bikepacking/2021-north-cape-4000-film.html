2021 North Cape 4000 (Video)

Back in July, over 190 riders lined up for the fourth edition of the annual North Cape 4000. The 4,450km route crossed through 10 different countries: Italy, Slovenia, Hungary, Slovakia, Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, Finland and Norway. The 2021 event saw athletes from 30 different nations, and nearly half of those at the grand depart didn’t finish the event.

With the 2022 NorthCape 4000 already scheduled for July 23rd in Rovereto, Italy (and registration opening on December 1st), the organizers have released a tribute to this year’s event.
