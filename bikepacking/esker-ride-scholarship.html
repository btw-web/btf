Applications open for Esker Ride Scholarship

The new Esker Ride Scholarship is designed to provide a rider with the opportunity to get out on a ride or trip on a new mountain bike. The winning applicant will receive an Esker Japhy or Hayduke in their color preference and size and is expected to share their experience with Esker through their medium of choice. Read on fir the full press release…

Esker Cycles

Esker Cycles wants to help get you out on the trail. This summer, we are announcing the Esker Ride Scholarship, intended to give a selected applicant the opportunity to get onto a new mountain bike and enable them to do anything from a multi-day adventure, to a favorite local ride, or to simply enjoy a first time experience on a mountain bike. To apply, fill out the application below by July 31, 2021, tell us why you think you would be a good fit for the Esker Ride Scholarship, and what it is you plan to do with your Esker mountain bike. We are especially encouraging newer riders with a vision for a particular ride or adventure and an interest in telling their story to share their ideas with us by applying for this scholarship.

It is expected that selected riders will carry out their ride or adventure within a year of receiving the bike, and will share their mountain biking experience with us through the medium of their choice. Selected riders will receive a Hayduke or Japhy complete in the size and color of their choice.

Esker started with a mission of getting more people outside on bikes. This ongoing scholarship is just one small way for us to continue with that mission and to enable more people to enjoy the sport of mountain biking like we do. For questions or additional information, please reach out to us at info@eskercycles.com

To apply, Esker has provided some simple questions to learn more about the applicant. You will be expected to share a bit about yourself, what you plan on using the bike for, and how you will share your experience. The Esker Ride Scholarship is open to anyone, and the application form can be found at EskerCycles.com.
