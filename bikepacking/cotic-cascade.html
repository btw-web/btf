Introducing the Cotic Cascade

Just launched, the Cotic Cascade is a new 29er drop bar mountain bike, gravel bike, bikepacking rig… and probably a bunch of other things as well. According to UK-based Cotic Cycles, it drops neatly into the space between their SolarisMAX hardtail mountain bike and the Escapade road/gravel bike. It’s meant to be a versatile character in their lineup, and can be run stripped down with a carbon fork, beefed up with Cotic’s rigid steel fork and custom bags, or dressed up with a 100mm travel suspension fork and dropper post.

The Cascade is built up around a Reynolds 853 Chromoly steel front triangle and seat tube, a 4130 Chromoly steel wishbone seat stays, and bridgeless S-bend chainstays. It’s based around boost hub spacing, with clearance for 29 x 2.4″ tires in the rear end and 2.6″ up front. It can also be set up with 27.5 x 2.8″ or 3.0″ tires as well. The frame has bosses for bolt-on frame bags, racks, fenders, top tube bags, and more. Cotic is offering the Cascade with your choice of three forks: their steel Alpaca fork, a carbon Salsa Firestarter, or a Rockshox SID SL Ultimate 100mm travel fork for getting rowdy.

Cotic Cascade Specs and Features

  • Flat Mount +20 brakes—use your favourite drop bar brakes with proper sized 180 front/160 rear rotors
  • New Sureshot geometry running short 50-70mm stems with drop bars
  • Cotic 46cm flared drop Valley bars as standard. PWN Coast 52cm optional.
  • Mountain bike Boost hub spacing and 1x drivetrains, 148x12mm rear, 110x15mm front
  • Big tire clearance: 29 x 2.6″ front and 2.4″ rear
  • Optional, frame specific, UK-made frame bag

There are a number of ways to purchase a Cascade, from bare frames to complete bikes and everything in between. The frame/steel fork starts at £849 (~1,150 USD). A rolling chassis (frame, fork, wheels) start at £1,265 (~$1,710 USD) and complete builds start at £2,099 (~$2,840 USD). The build kits are completely customizable, so if you want a specific set of tires or a different saddle, Cotic can work with you on the perfect build.

The Cotic Cascade is offered in three colours (Lichen, Nimbus, and Smoke) and four sizes. Head over to Cotic.co.uk for all the details.
