Collective Reward #009: Komoot Route Planning / Navigation App

Komoot is an intuitive, powerful route planning and navigation app, and one that’s well suited to bikepacking. It can be synched with a wide range of bike computers, and it has advanced integrations for the Wahoo ELEMNT, ELEMNT Bolt, and several Garmin devices. Even better, there’s a completely free version available, but we’ve teamed up with the folks from Komoot to offer something special to Bikepacking Collective members.

Komoot’s suite of tools includes a browser-based route planning tool, a mobile navigation app, and an adventure sharing platform. We’ve used it extensively for routing and navigation on bikepacking trips in the Balkans and Middle East, and we think there’s a lot to like. With features such as surface type breakdown, the ability to design a route based on bike type or around waypoint highlights, and detailed elevation analysis, it certainly offers a few things that other tools lack. We plan to write up a deeper look at it in the future, but for now you can find plenty of details at Komoot.com.

For our ninth Collective Reward, a dozen Bikepacking Collective members will receive a code to unlock the Komoot Complete Package, allowing for unrestricted access to base maps for the entire world. If you’re already a member, you’re signed up. If not, Join the Bikepacking Collective to be entered!
