Brooks Adds Three New Bags to Their Scape Range

Brooks England just added three new options to their Scape range of bags, which was originally announced in late 2020 and designed with a focus on waterproof materials, modular functionality, and hardwearing fabrics. Including the trio of new bags, the Scape collection now boasts 13 bags in total, all of which are designed to work together to accommodate different styles of packing, from full-on bikepacking setups to a more traditional rack and pannier approach. Find details on the new Scape Full Frame Bag, Handlebar Case, and Feed Pouch below.

Scape Full Frame Bag

With maximum capacity made to fit most standard bike frames, the Scape Full Frame Bag is at home when rides take you far from yours. Protecting essentials from the elements, the Scape Frame Bag is secure, waterproof, and organized. It’s built with a 100% waterproof welded construction and features two side pockets that provide access to the bag’s inner compartments, easy-pull waterproof zippers, a front-facing TPU cable hole, and a reflective logo.

  • Capacity 5L
  • Dimensions W 480 x H 290 x D 60 mm
  • Weight 390g
  • Max Load 3kg
  • Price $140
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com

Scape Handlebar Case

Practical for getting at what you need without having to stop and unpack, the waterproof Scape Handlebar Case connects quickly and is the perfect place to store your map, phone, wallet, and more. A KLICKfix attachment system is included, and the bag features a 100% waterproof welded construction, full internal lining with two zippered pockets and two lateral mesh sleeves, and a Hypalon patch and daisy chain loops to attach a shoulder strap. A reflective logo on the front pocket provides extra visibility.

  • Capacity 8L
  • Dimensions W 260 x H 225 x D 170 mm
  • Weight 650g
  • Max Load 5kg
  • Price $160
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com
