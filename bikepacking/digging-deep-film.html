Digging Deep (Film)

Originally published April 2020; updated January 2021 (full film now available for free)

Digging Deep is a self-shot film from Jakob Carlsen (@jakobcarlsenphotography) documenting his experience training for and completing two rides of the Arizona Trail Race—1,250 kilometres from the Mexican border to Utah. The film is a collection of footage Jakob gathered during training rides and from his GoPro during both events. Watch the full film below, followed by a detailed gearlist from Jakob, which includes some helpful information for those considering an attempt at the Arizona Trail.

Jakob rode an Ibis Ripley V1/V2 with a mix of bags from Revelate Designs, Topeak, and Apidura—as well as a homemade frame bag and handlebar harness. Find Jakob’s complete gear list below:
