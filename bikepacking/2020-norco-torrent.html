2020 Norco Torrent Looks Shred-packing Worthy

With an undeniably slack 64° head angle and a steep 76° seat tube, the chromoly 2020 Torrent is definitely an aggressive trail/all-mountain hardtail. But for those looking for one bike that can double as a big trail slayer and a singletrack weekend bikepacking rig, it could certainly be interesting. At any rate, it looks cool, and I’d like to ride one. Here’s the launch video and a few photos…

The 2020 Norco Torrent comes in two builds: the Torrent HT S1 (metallic black) 1 with a 150mm RockShox Lyric Ultimate/Eagle NX/GX drivetrain for $2999, and the Torrent HT S2 (purple) with a Rockshox 35G fork/Eagle SX drivetrain for $2,199. You can also get a frame (in black) for $699. Learn more over at norco.com/torrent-steel
