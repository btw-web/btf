Watch & Win YouTube Giveaway

In case you missed it, we started a YouTube channel earlier this year and have since published nearly 50 videos, ranging from short gear reviews, to interviews, to longer how-to guides. We want to keep this effort going, but we need your help to do so. Gaining more subscribers is crucial, but we also want to gain an understanding of what viewers would like to watch. As such, we’ve partnered with Revelate Designs, Wolf Tooth Components, and Kona to offer up three excellent prize packages including some of our favorite new gear from 2020. Three winners will be drawn at random to win the prizes listed below.

(3) Prize packs include the following: (1) Revelate Spinelock 10L seat bag, which won Best Bikepacking Gear in our 2020 Gear of the Year Awards; (1) Wolf Tooth 8-Bit Pack Pliers multi-tool, which won Best in Tools and Gadgets; and (1) Kona Hoody, featuring a cool new bikepacking illustration on the back.

To sign up, just complete these three simple steps:

1. Subscribe to our YouTube channel

Simple, just head to our YouTube channel and hit the rectangular Subscribe Button to the right of our logo. If you’re already subscribed, there’s NO NEED TO UNSUBSCRIBE AND RESUBSCRIBE, just skip to step two…

2. Click the Bell Icon

Once you’ve subscribed, click the bell icon just to the right of the Subscribe button and select “all.” This ensures that our newest videos are visible in your feed when you log in to YouTube.

3. Submit the Form Below

Enter your email address below and fill out this quick three-question survey. You can find the complete rules at the bottom of the page, as well as a video from Neil introducing the contest.
