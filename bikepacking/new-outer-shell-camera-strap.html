New Outer Shell Camera Strap Announced

We’ve heard good things about Outer Shell’s Camera Strap. This unique made-in-San Francisco design features quick-cinch adjustment and a sternum stabilizer, which according to Outer Shell “strikes a perfect balance between quick camera access and stability while riding.”

The redesigned version includes a tripod-mount anchor for a third anchor point, further enhancing its stability and adding compatibility with more camera strap mounts (such as “triangle” split rings, webbing slots on DSLRs/mirrorless cameras, and cord-based anchor points on compact cameras).

The Outer Shell Camera Strap is available in 11 color options and sells through their website for $58. Learn more over at OuterShell.com.
