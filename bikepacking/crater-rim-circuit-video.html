Crater Rim Circuit – A Montage (Video)

Deane Parker’s latest video captures a unique two-day bikepacking trip around New Zealand’s South Island with his riding partner, Rose, and is guaranteed to inspire some wanderlust. Beginning with a leisurely chairlift ride up to the top of the Christchurch Adventure Park, they take in some of the area’s singletrack trails and back roads through the stunning volcanic landscape around Lyttelton Harbour on the Banks Peninsula.

Following a ferry ride, some big climbs, and a night in a mountain home, they make their way past several historical landmarks and eventually ride back down through the Adventure Park in Christchurch, celebrating the end of their trip with pizza and beer. It looks like a perfect overnighter with a little bit of everything mixed in, set in what’s surely one of the most beautiful bikepacking destinations in the world.

To see more from Deane, check out Forgotten Highways, Fluid Trails, and Looking Down is Looking Up. And don’t miss the Top of the South (TOTS) Route from Eszster Horanyi, a stunning 20-day bikepacking route on New Zealand’s South Island.
