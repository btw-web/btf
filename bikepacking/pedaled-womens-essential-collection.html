New PEdALED Women’s Essential Collection

Following the men’s PEdALED Essential Collection we shared last week, the new women’s collection consists of a merino short sleeve jersey and bib shorts, both of which are designed to be comfortable and versatile pieces for spring/summer riding. Find photos and details on both below.

Women’s Essential Merino Jersey

The women’s Essential Merino Jersey is naturally breathable, odor-resistant, and temperature regulating, making it a great option for changing weather conditions and long days on the bike. The jersey features a slim cut and lightweight design, three cargo pockets, and a subtle overall style. It comes in three different colours and several sizes. The fabric of the jersey is certified STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX, one of the world’s best-known voluntary “ecological-label” for textile products and accessories tested for harmful substances.

  • Material: 67% Merino Wool, 22% Polyamide, 11% Elastane
  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $168 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com

Women’s Essential Training Bib Shorts

Sharing a similar overall look to the jersey, the Essential Training Bib Shorts are a summer-weight bib designed to function well in hot conditions. They have mesh straps, a slim fit, and are finished with hand-stitched details and reflective elements for increased visibility. The bib shorts are only offered in black, but also come in the same size range as the jersey. They also meet the same OEKO-TEX standard and are also certified Global Recycled Standard (GRS), which promotes responsible and sustainable development in the textile sector.

  • Material: 78% Recycled Polyamide, 22% Elastane
  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $174 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com
