Wizard Works Samples and Seconds Sale in London

If you’re in the London area this weekend, we recommend swinging by the first-ever samples and seconds sale from our friends at Wizard Works, who’ll be opening up the loading dock space behind their workshop tomorrow, July 31st, for customers to browse their selection of “heavily discounted perfectly imperfect bags ready for their forever homes.”

Among the available bags will be custom bags with mixed-up colors, samples that never made it into the webshop, and other bags with very minor imperfections that didn’t quite make the cut. They’ve been saving these up for some time in anticipation of Saturday’s in-person sale. If you’re interested in stopping by, the sale will start at noon in the loading bay at 3-5 Latona Road, London, SE15 6RX, and the lovely folks at Wizard Works just ask that you wear a mask and adhere to social distancing guidelines.
