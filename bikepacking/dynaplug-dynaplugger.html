The New DynaPlugger from Dynaplug

I’m a huge fan of Dynaplug’s line of tubeless repair kits and rarely leave the house without my Dynaplug Pill packed in my repair kit. Their brass-tipped tubeless plugs are simple and work well, which is the most I can ask out of a tubeless plug tool. The new DynaPlugger is a bombproof and wallet-friendly option that packs the same technology into a lightweight glass-filled nylon tool that can hold multiple insertion tubes and plugs. Dynaplug says the tool is extremely strong, despite its minimal 35-gram weight, and would make a great first tubeless plugging kit for newer riders. What’s more, Dynaplug collaborated with artist and cyclist Chas Christiansen (@notchas) on some great looking packaging.

  • Body Material: Glass Filled Nylon Body and Cap / 6061 Aluminum Insert / Silicone End Cap
  • Size: 4.875” x .875”
  • Weight: 35g
  • Place of Manufacture: California, USA
  • Price: $29.99 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Dynaplug.com

If you haven’t seen it yet, I reviewed the new Dynaplug Covert bar end tubeless repair kit this winter, which is a really slick option for those who love ODI lock-on grips. Check that out here.
