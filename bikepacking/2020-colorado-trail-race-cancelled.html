2020 Colorado Trail Race Cancelled (Video)

A few days ago, Jefe Branham made an announcement that the 2020 Colorado Trail Race grand depart is officially cancelled this year. This will come as little surprise to most in light of COVID-19, but since the Colorado Trail has been on many folks’ radars, we wanted to chat with Jefe to find out what influenced his decision. In our latest YouTube video, host Neil Beltchenko and Jefe talk about the status of the Colorado Trail Race and more. Watch it below.
