Collective Reward Roundup: 43 New Giveaways

With the deadline to join our Bikepacking Collective and have your membership start with the seventh issue of The Bikepacking Journal just around the corner on Sunday, October 17th, we’ve rounded up our latest batch of Collective Reward gear giveaways and will be drawing a fresh batch of winners this weekend! Prizes in this round include a Fairlight Faran 2.0 frameset, several handmade bikepacking bags, as well as racks, handlebars, saddles, and more.

Join our Bikepacking Collective before 11:59 pm EDT this Sunday and you’ll be eligible to win one of the prizes you see below. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, for the same price. By joining, you’ll also receive the upcoming issue of The Bikepacking Journal in your mailbox, will have access to exclusive discounts from an ever-growing list of bike and outdoor companies, and other great benefits. Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible—help us grow!
