Brother Cycles’ 10th Anniversary Special Edition Paint Job

Words by Will Meyer (@thisiswilma), photos by Ollie Harrop (@ollieharrop)

Ten years ago, we came up with a plan to produce a single product: a simple steel track frame which at the time had no name or branding. We never planned to build an actual bicycle company. We just wanted to create a product that was not readily available at the time, something we wanted to own and ride ourselves.

Ten years later (almost to the day—our first product launched in May 2010!) and our mission has not really changed. We still only make products that we’re personally interested in. However, this now includes a whole range of bikes that span multiple cycling disciplines. And this all comes under the brand name of Brother Cycles. This is much more than a hobby now; it’s our livelihood and something we are incredibly proud of.

To celebrate our 10th anniversary we’ve created this special edition paint job—a black to gold fade and splatter. We have produced a very limited number of these on the Kepler, Big Bro, and Allday framesets which are now available online and from your local Brother dealer. All orders will receive a free 10th Anniversary cycle cap and pin badge. Frameset prices are as follows; Kepler: £575, Big Bro: £699, and Allday: £549. Head over to BrotherCycles.com to learn more.

Happy anniversary Brother Cycles! We can’t wait to see what you come up with next.
