The Helmut Equipement Story (Video)

We’ve been following Helmut Equipements’s range of handcrafted bike bags for some time, and were pleased to find this new video introducing the small brand from Cognin, France. Shot by Antonin Pergod and narrated by Helmut founder Rodolphe Pascuito, the two-minute video offers a behind-the-scenes look inside the Helmut workshop and shares the story of how the brand got started and where its name comes from. Watch it below.

If you missed it, check out our recent Reader’s Rig featuring Antonin Michaud-Soret’s Edelbikes randonneur, fully kitted out in Helmut Equipement bags. To learn more about Helmut’s range of bike bags and accessories, head to HelmutEquipement.com.
