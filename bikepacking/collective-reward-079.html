Collective Reward #079: Brooks Scape Bags

Announced back in November, the Scape range from Brooks is the brand’s first foray into the world of bikepacking bags and consists of 10 different products that combine somewhat traditional styling with modern features. The 100% waterproof bags in the Scape series are made from 600D polyester and PVC-free nylon and use a high-frequency welding construction technique to ensure they’re completely sealed from the elements. For our latest Collective Reward, one lucky Bikepacking Collective member will be receiving a set of six bags from the Brooks Scape collection, worth a combined total of $585. Find details on all the bags in this giveaway below.

Brooks Scape Handlebar Roll

Built specifically for off-road bikepacking, the Scape Handlebar Roll is a two-piece harness system made to securely hold its own adjustable, waterproof dry bag. The bag has two internal compartments. It has a capacity of 10-12L and weighs 670g. Find more details here.

Brooks Scape Handlebar Pouch

The Scape Handlebar Pouch is a practical bag that’s useful for any ride and is fully waterproof thanks to its welded materials and all-round weatherproof details. It can be attached to the Handlebar Roll to expand your front-end carrying capacity on longer trips. It has a capacity of 3L and weighs just 140g. See additional details here.

Brooks Scape Seat Bag

Similar to bags like the Revelate Terapin and Porcelain Rocket’s Mr. Fusion, the Scape Seatpost Bag is a saddle pack designed around a holster attachment and its own waterproof dry sack. It has a capacity of 8-10L and weighs 500g. Find more details here.

Brooks Scape Saddle Roll Bag

The Scape Saddle Roll Bag is a svelte bag that’s designed to accommodate smaller loads that don’t require the full-sized Seat Pack. It features a roll-top closure and has a minimal footprint under the saddle. It has a 1L capacity and weights 75g. Find out more here.

Brooks Scape Frame Bag

The Brooks Scape Frame Bag is designed to fit most standard-sized frames. Like other bags in the lineup, it’s made with welded PU-coated nylon and is 100% waterproof. The bag features two full-length zips and attaches to the bike via five Hypalon velcro straps. It has a 3L capacity and weighs 200g. See additional details here.

Brooks Scape Top Tube Bag

The Brooks Scape Top Tube Bag is welded from the same waterproof material as the other bags in the Scape line, making it ideal for electronics and other personal items you want readily available. According to Brooks, it’s equipped with a 100% waterproof KCC zipper to keep contents dry. It has a capacity of .9L and weighs 100g. Learn more here.
