A Dog’s Tale (Video)

Narrated by 13-year-old Raven, an older dog who has slowed down but has a lifetime of runs down loamy trails in British Columbia behind her, A Dog’s Tale is a lighthearted video from Shimano that celebrates the many joys of mountain biking from a dog’s perspective. Following an assortment of lovable trail dogs and their humans, the spectacularly shot video features incredible displays of canine athleticism and highlights a handful of world-class trails and the people who ride and maintain them. Enjoy the nine-minute short below, along with a small gallery of still images of some of the furry friends featured in the video.

“Not every dog is satisfied with the fenced-in parks. Some just need to be free…”

See more from A Dog’s Tale over at Shimano.com, including an introduction to the cast of characters, lots more photos, and a veterinarian’s take on how to keep your pup safe on the trails.
