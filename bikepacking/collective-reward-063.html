Collective Reward #063: Light & Motion Vis Pro 1000 Trail Lights

We’ve teamed up with Light & Motion for our 63rd Collective Reward gear giveaway, and three randomly selected Bikepacking Collective members will be receiving one of their impressive Vis Pro 1000 Trail headlights, a $134.99 value. Building on Light & Motion’s legacy of quality lights, the Vis Pro 1000 Trail packs a ton of features into a stylish and small package. I’ve been running Light & Motion bike lights for more than a decade, and short of having to replace a couple of rubber attachment straps, they’ve been nearly indestructibleâ€”surviving crashes, extreme weather, and lots of runtime.

As the name implies, the Vis Pro 1000 Trail headlight has a maximum output of 1,000 lumens on high. Its minimum output is 250 lumens on low, and it’ll run for up to 12 hours on 500-lumen “SafePulse” mode on a 2.5-hour charge. Among the features that make it an appealing option for bikepackers are its minimal 121-gram weight, waterproof construction (up to one meter), quick high/medium toggle, and Infinite Power Assistance technology, which lets you run the light off portable power source. It’s also available with a variety of mounts, whether you like to run your light on your bars, helmet, or GoPro mount. And as with all of Light & Motion’s bike lights, it comes with a two-year warranty, backed by their great customer service.
