Custom Wizard Works Alakazam and Shazam Available Now

We just got word from UK-based Wizard Works that they’ve opened up a handful of orders for some of their most popular bags. As a small manufacturer, their bags are all made to order and often sell out quickly, so you’ll want to jump on this if you’re hoping to have a bag for the spring. They have five of each of the following bags available to order: Alakazam 137, Alakazam 139, Shazam, and Mini Shazam. Each bag is custom-made for you, with several different custom options to choose from. This batch is planned to ship out by the end of April.

If you haven’t seen it yet, make sure to check out my review of the Shazam saddle bag / handlebar bag and the Alakazam Basket Bag. In short, both are extremely well-built, aesthetically pleasing, and having the option to choose your own fabrics and colours is a fun experience. Order your custom bag at Wizard.Works.
