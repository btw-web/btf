REEB Cycles Updates the Sam’s Pants Gravel Bike for 2020

Made in Colorado by the Oskar Blues bike spin-off, REEB, The Sam’s Pants is a straight-up drop-bar 29er. The Sam’s Pants features clearance fro 29 x 2.1″ tires, a 73mm MTB bottom bracket shell, Paragon Machine Works sliding dropouts, and a 44mm head tube (compatible with straight or tapered steerers). The frame is available in steel or titanium and designed around a suspension corrected rigid fork. The 2020 REEB Sam’s Pants was just announced and now includes a third set of bottle bosses under the down tube, plus an updated 31.6mm seat tube to fit modern dropper seatposts. Sam’s Pants is available with several customizable options in a few build kits or as a frame only for $1600 (steel). Find more info over at REEBcycles.com (note the current page doesn’t reflect the 2020 updates).
