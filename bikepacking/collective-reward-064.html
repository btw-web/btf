Collective Reward #064: Handmade Bag Bundle 02

For Collective Reward #064, we’re continuing our five-part handmade bagstravaganza featuring dozens of bags in all shapes and sizes from a collection of 25 cottage industry brands. Each maker contributed a couple of items, including seat packs, frame bags, tool rolls, accessory pouches, handlebar bags, and lots more. One randomly selected member of our Bikepacking Collective will take home all 14 items in this second giveaway, valued at around $600.

Handmade Bag Bundle 02

Behind each one of these tiny brands is an individual or small group of people who created something for this Collective Reward giveaway. Some items are one of a kind, and nearly all of them were made to order. Find the complete list of makers and their contributions below, along with pricing, their location, and links where you can learn more. Only one winner will take home this set of bags, but we encourage everyone to help the bikepacking community thrive by supporting small makers, even if they aren’t on this list.

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, it isn’t too late to get signed up to be eligible for this giveaway.
