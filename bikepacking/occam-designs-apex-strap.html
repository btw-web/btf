Occam Designs Apex Strap

Denver, CO-based Occam Designs’ flagship product has a few distinctive features that set it apart from the typical velcro frame strap. The Apex Strap features an X-Pac construction with a sticky Rhinotek interior to keep your spare tube or other gear in place on the frame. And the whole thing wraps up and cinches down with a BOA FIT SYSTEM. According to Occam, “adjusts to fit nearly any bike in existence.” Other features include two elastic retention cords and BOA’s latest TX4 lace, made of Dyneema instead of metal cable.

The Occam Designs Apex Strap weighs 28 grams and sells for $34.99. Learn more over at OccamDesigns.com.
