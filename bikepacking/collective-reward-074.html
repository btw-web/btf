Collective Reward #074: Tumbleweed Prints by Sean Hipkin

We love the pair of prints that was recently released as a collaboration between Tumbleweed Bikes and artist Sean Hipkin (you might remember his Reader’s Rig). They speak perfectly to Tumbleweed founder Daniel Molloy’s vision for his bikes and the places they can take you, brought to life in Sean’s unique and beautiful illustration style.

Today, we’re excited to be able to give a handful of them away to our supporters. For Collective Reward #074, four members of the Bikepacking Collective will receive one to adorn their wall, and winners can choose between the “Starry Night” or “Craggy Peaks” print. They measure 18 x 24″ and were printed in the USA on a heavy archival-grade paper stock. Find some additional details over in the shop at Tumbleweed.cc.

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, it isn’t too late to get signed up to be eligible for this giveaway. Just join before 11:59 PM EDT on Saturday, October 17th, and you’ll automatically be entered to win. Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible, and membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world. Members are eligible for bi-monthly Collective Rewards, receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and more.
