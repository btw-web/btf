Kitsbow Announces the Haskell Pant

I can’t say enough good things about Kitsbow’s Haskell shorts. They’re comfortable, fit like a glove, and are pretty much invincible. With that, I was pretty stoked to see Kitsbow release the Haskell Pant. Based on photos and details, they appear to have all the bells, whistles, and trimmings of the shorts, including the same stretch woven fabric and burly snap. And they come in a lot of sizes, too.

The Kitsbow Haskell Pant is made in Old Fort, North Carolina, as a “Make-Mine” made-to-order product. It comes in a whopping 59 sizes with three color options. The Haskell Pant is an investment at $219, but as mentioned, the durability level of the Haskell short is impressive. The new pants will begin shipping on December 22, 2021; ordering now secures your place in the queue. Details at Kitsbow.com.
