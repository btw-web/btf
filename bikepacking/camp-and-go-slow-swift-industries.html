Camp and Go Slow × Swift Industries Collab

We’re suckers for a good collaboration, and it’s exciting to see Camp and Go Slow working with Swift Industries on a small line of custom bags, which will available tomorrow at noon PST. Each bag is made from burly 1000d Cordura with custom trim that matches their popular rattlesnake bar tape. Bags will be available in two colours: Western/Rust and Eastern/Moss.

The Motherloaf is a brand new basket bag that’s based on the same design as the popular Sugarloaf Basket Bag. It has a front buttoned pocket, zippered main opening, and large webbing straps for carrying off the bike. It can carry a whopping 25L and is designed to fit in the Wald 139 basket. Swift will also be selling the Motherloaf basket bag, but not until April 2nd, so keep an eye out for that. The collab also includes a Zeitgeist saddle / handlebar bag and a Sidekick stem pouch.

Head over to CampAndGoSlow.com tomorrow at 12 p.m. PST to learn more.
