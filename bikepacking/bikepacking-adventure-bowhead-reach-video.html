Bikepacking Adventure on a Bowhead Reach (video)

On December 3rd, 2020, Christian Bagg and Chris Brown headed out on two Bowhead REACH Adaptive Mountain Bikes for an unsupported overnight bikepacking trip from Lesueur Creek to Devils Gap in Alberta, Canada. Their route had never been attempted before on a Bowhead Reach and many people were skeptical about the feasibility of their trip. Watch the video below.

We’re in the middle of some of the most beautiful country on the planet… a bucket list place… and I couldn’t do it without this equipment.
