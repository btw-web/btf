Two Years on a Bike 2/4 (Video)

In the second of his four-part video series offering a behind-the-scenes look at his book, Two Years on a Bike, our friend and contributor to The Bikepacking Journal Martijn Doolaard (@_espiritu.libre_) shares another handful of stories and video vignettes from his bikepacking trip between Vancouver and Patagonia. This time around, he walks through his time in Utah, Baja California, Mexico, and Central America. Watch the video below, and be sure to check out part one in the related posts grid at the bottom of this post.

You can find more details about Martijn’s 416-page “Two Years on a Bike” book over at gestalten.com.
