American Classic Launches Eight New Tire Models

Three years after closing its doors, American Classic is back and has re-entered the cycling market with a diverse line of eight fresh tire models. The range includes five gravel, one city/touring, and two road tires, nearly all of which are available in two colors and a variety of sizes. Nearly all tires are tubless-ready, and all are manufactured in-house and priced in the $30 to $35 range. For now, they’re only available in the United States via a direct-to-consumer model through Amazon and the American Classic website. Everything in the new line-up includes a two-year warranty against manufacturing defects, and is also covered under their Road Hazard Replacement Policy, which provides customers with a one-time discount of 50% on a replacement tire, should theirs puncture or tear while riding.

Of particular interest is the new American Classic gravel tire collection, which features designs that span from center-slick for hardpacked gravel and mixed terrain to full-on knobby for riding gnarlier tracks and trails. The entire range of models includes American Classic’s Rubberforce G tire compound and Stage 5S Flat Protection, and the brand says they’re built to “surpass the demands of today’s gravel riders.” Find overviews of all five new gravel models below (provided by American Classic), as well as the new city/touring tire, which may be interesting for some readers riding more pavement-heavy routes.

American Classic Kimberlite

Thanks to the perfect balance of volume and a fast-rolling tread, the Kimberlite is the go-anywhere tire that rides like a road tire on the pavement and hooks up on the dirt roads and hardpack singletrack you’re relentlessly seeking. A continuous center section rolls smoothly with minimal resistance, while strong side knobs let you lean into dirt corners. A tubeless-ready 120 TPI casing and three-layer Stage 5S flat protection provide a responsive handling tire that staves off most punctures to keep you rolling.

Kimberlite FEATURES

  • Fast and efficient tire from pavement to mixed surface
  • 700Cx40mm, 700Cx50mm, 650Bx47mm
  • Tubeless Ready
  • Stage 5S Flat Protection
  • Rubberforce G gravel tire compound
  • Continuous center tread is road-bike fast
  • Black or tan sidewall

The Kimberlite is available direct-to-consumer only at Amazon Marketplace for $35.

American Classic Aggregate

The Aggregate is our adventure road and gravel tire, created for minimal road vibration and consistent traction. Precisely spaced tread knobs provide predictable handling across pavement, compact dirt, and variable mixed terrain. Our signature Rubberforce G compound is designed to hook up in dirt from east to west, with Stage 5S bead-to-bead flat protection and Tubeless-Ready casing for maximum puncture protection to keep rolling across almost anything.

Aggregate FEATURES

  • Fast and efficient tire from mixed surface to hardpacked dirt
  • 700Cx40mm, 700Cx50mm, 650Bx47mm
  • Tubeless Ready
  • Stage 5S Flat Protection
  • Rubberforce G gravel tire compound
  • Regularly spaced profile knobs
  • Black or tan sidewall

The Aggregate is available direct-to-consumer only at Amazon Marketplace for $35.

American Classic Udden

The Udden features an innovative, fast-rolling, mud-clearing tread design for riding through the worst conditions without sacrificing pedaling efficiency on hardpack. Central chevrons maintain straight-line speed while widely spaced side knobs allow you to carve through ruts and muddy corners and power over the top of slippery transitions. The Udden is a versatile tire — air them up for the pavement ride out of town, let it down to a lower PSI for improved traction when the county service ends. Our Stage 5S flat protection and a tubeless-ready 120 TPI casing will keep you rolling through the harshest conditions.

Udden FEATURES

  • Fast and efficient tire from hardpacked dirt to loose gravel
  • 700Cx40mm, 700Cx50mm, 650Bx47mm
  • Tubeless Ready
  • Stage 5S Flat Protection
  • Rubberforce G gravel tire compound
  • Fast-rolling, mud-clearing tread design
  • Black or tan sidewall

The Udden is available direct-to-consumer only at Amazon Marketplace for $35.

American Classic Wentworth

The Wentworth features tightly-spaced, low-profile center knobs with more aggressive cornering tread for an efficient tire that works well across a variety of hardpack and gravel conditions without sacrificing traction through muddy and loamy sections. The Wentworth is suited for long days of aimless exploration and lining up on rainy race days where anything could be around the next corner. Bead-to-bead Stage 5S flat protection and a tubeless-ready 120 TPI casing help to keep the miles rolling by through harsh gravel conditions.

Wentworth FEATURES

  • 700Cx40mm, 700Cx50mm, 650Bx47
  • Tubeless Ready
  • Stage 5S Flat Protection

  • Tightly spaced, low-profile center tread
  • Black or tan sidewall

The Wentworth is available direct-to-consumer only at Amazon Marketplace for $35.

American Classic Krumbein

The American Classic Krumbein is designed for gravel roads and any singletrack trail that may lie around the corner. Aggressive side lugs provide cornering grip across loose and muddy transitions, while chamfered center tread lugs help to minimize rolling resistance without sacrificing off-road traction. A Tubeless-Ready 120 TPI casing with our bead-to-bead Stage 5S flat protection provides a responsive riding tire that can fend off most punctures and tears.

Krumbein FEATURES

  • Fast and efficient tire from loose gravel to singletrack
  • 700Cx40mm, 700Cx50mm, 650Bx47mm
  • Tubeless Ready
  • Stage 5S Flat Protection
  • Rubberforce G gravel tire compound
  • Chamfered center tread minimizes rolling resistance

The Krumbein is available direct-to-consumer only at Amazon Marketplace for $35.

American Classic Lamplighter

The Lamplighter is our modern E-bike and city tire designed to get you where you need to go with ease thanks to its fast-rolling tread, perfect for riding in all conditions. Stage 7 flat protection makes it nearly impervious to flats, and our urban tread pattern provides traction across varied pavement and light trail surfaces. The continuous center section is road-bike-fast, with side siping to clear water and improve traction when you venture off the pavement.

Lamplighter FEATURES

  • E-bike and commuter approved
  • 700Cx40mm, 700Cx50mm, 650Bx47mm
  • Tube Type
  • Stage 7 Flat Protection is nearly impervious to flats
  • Rubberforce R transit compound
  • Fast-rolling tread
  • Shoulder siping improves traction in the wet
  • Black sidewall

The Lamplighter is available direct-to-consumer only at Amazon Marketplace for $30.

You can learn more about the new line of American Classic (including the road tires, which we didn’t highlight here) by visiting AmClassic.com. And stay tuned for more, as it looks like they’re also planning to relaunch their range of wheels as well.
