WOHO × OPEN “CYBER-CAMO” WI.DE.

We just got word about the new WOHO × OPEN special edition “CYBER-CAMO” WI.DE. The bike has a custom paint job designed around WOHO’s new Xtouring bags. The frame bag and handlebar bag feature a dark camo pattern and are welded from a PU-coated weatherproof fabric—which, I might add, are almost identical in design and construction to a couple of Porcelain Rocket’s originals: the 52hz frame bag and Nigel handlebar bag.
