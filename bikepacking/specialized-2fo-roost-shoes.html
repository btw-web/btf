Specialized Releases 2FO Roost Clipless and Flat Pedal Shoes

It’s hard to argue whether or not Specialized makes great cycling shoes. The proof is in the pudding. We awarded the 2FO Cliplite (this shoe’s predecessor) with a Gear of the Year Award a few years ago after they lasted longer than almost any other shoes we’ve tried. More recently, we gave both the Rime 2.0 and Recon 2.0 very high marks for their performance and durability. For their latest release, the big S ushered in the new 2FO Roost lineup with what they claim was a “nerd on the inside, party on the outside” design approach. The Specialized 2FO Roost range includes both a Clip and Flat version, each in two color schemes with a new 3rd generation SlipNot rubber sole, a lighter Xpel Airmesh padding, and a relaxed aesthetic featuring a synthetic leather/suede upper. We’re excited to test both of them out. In the meantime, here are a few details.

Specialized 2FO Roost Flat

The most visible differentiator on the new 2FO Roost Flat is a redesigned sole. Specialized claims that the 3rd generation SlipNot rubber is “designed to stick to and hug pedal pins, giving riders a sticky, bounceless feel.” It features a hexagonal lug pattern made to direct pedal pins for a secure “locked-in” feeling and help reduce overall wear off the bike. The Specialized 2FO Roost Flat are available in two colors (Black/Slate or Oak Green/Black) in sizes ranging from 36-49 for $120. Find the specs from Specialized below and learn more over at Specialized.com.

  • Body Geometry sole construction and footbeds are ergonomically designed and scientifically tested to boost power, increase efficiency, and reduce chance of injury by optimizing hip, knee, and foot alignment.
  • SlipNot ST rubber sole for unsurpased flat-pedal grip and connection
  • Leather and textile upper with added protection keeps you comfortable and is built to last.
  • Cushioned EVA foam midsole for comfort and impact absorption
  • Relaxed Fit last for a balance of pedal feel and off-the-bike comfort.
  • Approximate weight: 360g (1/2 pair, Size 42)

Specialized 2FO Roost Clip

The clipless version of the 2FO Roost also has the 3rd gen SlipNot outsole and adds a cleat pocket which Specialized states is roomier than previous models, making it easier to engage and disengage pedals in the technical terrain. Both the Clip and Flat models nixed the traditional foam padding in the upper and replaced it with Xpel Airmesh, a lighter weight, water-repellant material. The Specialized 2FO Roost Clip comes in two colors (Black/Gum and Taupe) for $130 and is available in sizes ranging from 36 to 49. Find the specs from Specialized below and learn more over at Specialized.com.

  • Body Geometry sole construction and footbeds are ergonomically designed and scientifically tested to boost power, increase efficiency, and reduce chance of injury by optimizing hip, knee, and foot alignment.
  • Stiff Lollipop nylon composite plate for high performance pedaling and off-the-bike comfort.
  • SlipNot FG rubber sole for confident traction.
  • Extended length cleat slot (4mm) for rearward cleat set up option.
  • Synthetic leather upper conforms to the foot and is built to last.
  • Cushioned EVA foam midsole for comfort
  • Relaxed Fit last for a balance of pedal feel and off-the-bike comfort.
  • Two-bolt cleat pattern fits all major mountain pedals.
  • Approximate weight: 367g (1/2 pair, Size 42)
