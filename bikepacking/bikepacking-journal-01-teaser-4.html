Bikepacking Journal 01 Sneak Peek: Lael Wilcox on the French Divide

In The French Divide, ultra-endurance athlete Lael Wilcox recaps the highs and lows of her experience in the event of the same name, a 1,415-mile self-supported mountain bike race from Flanders to the southern French Pyrenees. It’s a wonderful story written in her own dynamic and sincere style, full of camaraderie and speed, with a great selection of photos by Rue Kaladyte.
