2020 Surly ECR Now in Tank Green

The 2020 Surly ECR is looking truly apocalypse-ready with a fresh ‘Tank Green’ paint job. Sitting right between shades of ‘Pinetop’ and ‘Hemlock Green’, the latest ECR colourway looks great and fits in nicely beside the 2019 colours: ‘Norwegian Cheese Brown’ and ‘Blacktacular’.

The component list hasn’t seen any updates, and the Tank Green ECR remains available in both 27.5+ (sizes XS-M) and 29+ (M-XL) builds. The complete build retails for $1,900 USD and the frameset is available for $750 USD. Head over to your local Surly dealer to order one for yourself.
