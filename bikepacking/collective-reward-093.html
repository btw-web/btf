Collective Reward #093: Custom Straight Cut Design Frame Bag

We’ve been seeing more and more beautiful bags from Straight Cut Design (@straightcutdesign) popping up in the time since Ross O’Reilly’s small Edinburgh-based brand hit the scene in 2018, and we’re excited to be able to give one of his fully custom frame bags to a lucky member of our Bikepacking Collective for our 93rd Collective Reward. Valued between £120 and £195+ ($165 and $270+) for a partial or full frame bag, respectively, our winner will have their choice of colors, fabrics, and other options, all with a made-to-measure fit for their frame.

Using his digital fit technique, Ross will personally work with our winner to ensure proper fit and walk through his full suite of options to personalize to tailor the bag for their unique use case. These include deciding on the shape and what other objects (such as bottles) to work around, whether or not a water-resistant liner is necessary, picking a Velcro, lace-up, or bolt-on attachment, and other personalizations, such as pockets and internal dividers.

Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible. If you enjoy reading the site, please consider joining our Bikepacking Collective. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards (including this custom bag from Straight Cut Design), receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.

Learn more about Straight Cut Design’s range of readymade and custom bikepacking bags at StraightCutDesign.com.
