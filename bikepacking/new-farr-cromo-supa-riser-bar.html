New Farr Cromo Supa-Riser Bar

Our friends at Farr are at it again with their new Cromo Supa-Riser Bar, bringing all the joy and style of an old-school BMX riser bar to your favourite mountain bike. It’s constructed from 4130 Chromoly Steel and features the crossbar styled after classic V-Cross BMX bikes from the 1980s. They are generously wide at 820mm, but can be cut down to 720mm if needed, and have a comfortable 12° sweep and 70mm rise.

Keeping with true BMX style, the Cromo Supa-Riser Bar has a 22.2mm clamp diameter (but is compatible with any 31.8mm shim set as well) and has a black ED finish. The bars are available for pre-order now, with an expected delivery date of March 2022.

  • Material: 4130 Chromoly Steel
  • Width: 820mm
  • Clamp: 22.2mm
  • Sweep: 12°
  • Weight: 846g (820mm width)
  • Place of Manufacture: Taiwan
  • Price: $99 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: RideFarr.com
