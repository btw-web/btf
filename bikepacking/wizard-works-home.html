Wizard Works From Home: Weekly Newsletter

Bag-maker Wizard Works is launching a weekly newsletter entitled Wizard Works From Home, with a primary goal of providing things for us to do while in various states of lockdown. Plus, each week someone will win a bag from Wizard Works! The first email goes out on Monday April 20th. You have until Thursday April 16th to sign up to the mailing list and one person, chosen at random, will win a free custom Lil Presto barrel bag. The prizes and mailing list are open to to anyone in the world. Judging by their message yesterday, I’d expect some cool make-your-own-gear tutorials as well!

All Wizard Works needs is your first name and email address. Here is the sign up link.
