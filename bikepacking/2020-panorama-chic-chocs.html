Panorama Cycles Updates Chic-Chocs Fatbike for 2020

New for 2020, Quebec-based Panorama Cycles has updated their trusty Chic-Chocs fatbike to include more tire clearance and fresh graphics. This time, Panorama Cycles partnered with Vancouver based artists (and cyclists) Pellvetica (@pellvetica) to create a downtube graphic that’s a tribute to the Atlantic caribou. Panorama goes on to explain that “In 2016, the total population of Caribou living in eastern Canada, mainly in the Chic-Chocs Mountains in Gaspésie, was 140. Today the count is less than 90. This is one of the eight mammals threatened with extinction in Canada.”

The new Chic-Chocs is now designed around 27.5 x 4.5″ tires, and the updated frame and fork are compatible with both 26 x 5″ and 29 x 3″ tires. Three bottle mounts, cargo cage mounts on the fork, and rear rack mounts have you covered for hauling gear, and the internally routed carbon frame is available in a single complete build. Check out the complete component spec list below.
