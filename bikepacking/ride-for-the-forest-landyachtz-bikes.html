Ride for the Forest with Landyachtz Bikes

Vancouver, British Columbia-based Landyachtz Bikes and Sierra Club of BC have teamed up to announce the inaugural Ride for the Forest, a self-supported bicycle photo scavenger hunt. The ride is open to anyone and any form of cycling whether it be road, gravel, MTB, BMX, or whatever you chose to ride. All you need to do is take a few simple photos along the way and you’ll have the chance to win a new Landyachtz AB1 frameset and a limited edition laser engraved Landyachtz skateboard. The best part is, all of the registration proceeds will go directly to Sierra Club of BC, a key player in the conservation of old-growth forests in British Columbia.

To participate, register online ($30 entry or $50 for entry and a t-shirt), collect specific photos between now and October 31st, and then post them on Instagram as a story or in your feed, tagging @landyachtzbikes and using the hashtag #LYridefortheforest2020. Examples of photos include: best view from your ride, biggest tree, and best pumpkin.

Head over to LandyachtzBikes.com to learn more!
