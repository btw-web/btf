Search and State’s “Tropic Thunder Skull” Jersey

Each season, NYC-based Search and State likes to step out of the box and make a piece that’s ultra-memorable. This year, SAS commissioned a local New York artist to create the new Tropic Thunder Skull graphic for their latest jersey.

The hand-drawn skull is printed on the back of their made-in-NYC S2-R jersey. The S2-R features a technical mid-weight synthetic base fabric with an athletic-cut five pocket design. The jersey has a Riri zipper, inside neck tape, rear locker loop, silicone waist gripper, and a sharp trim package.

As always with SAS’ limited-edition prints, quantities are finite, and once they’re gone, they’re gone. The Search and State Tropic Thunder Skull Jersey will be available Thursday 5/20 at 11:00am EST at SearchAndState.com.
