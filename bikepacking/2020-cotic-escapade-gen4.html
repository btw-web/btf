2020 Cotic Escapade Gen4 Announced

UK-based Cotic Cycles just announced the latest version of their ultra-versatile gravel / all-road bike, the Escapade. Although the frame’s geometry and overall specs remain the same, Cotic has introduced two new colour options, front and rear thru-axles, and a completely redesigned cable management system they call Inside Out, allowing for a mix of internal and external cable routing, depending on rider’s needs and preferences. Their frame is made from custom butted Fe heat treated Chromoly steel, with their signature Ovalform top tube and wishbone stays. The Escapade Gen4 still comes with their RB5 full carbon fork, complete with flat mount brakes, a tapered steerer tube, and clearance for up to 650b x 50mm tires (47mm on the frame).

Although features like mounts for racks, fenders, and multiple bottles all remain the same, the Escapade now comes specced with Cotic’s new Valley bars, providing a 46cm width and 15° flare for better off-road control and comfort.

Cotic offers nine different build options, ranging from £1,299 to £2,574, but if you’re looking for a completely custom build they can offer that as well. The two new paint jobs, as pictured, are called Supernova Orange and Dark Grey. Find a breakdown of the different standards builds below.

  • 700c Bike – Bronze – Shimano Sora 2×9: £1,299
  • 700c Bike – Silver – Shimano Tiagra 2×10: £1,569
  • 700c Bike – Gold 1x – SRAM Apex 1×11: £1,749
  • 700c Bike – Gold – Shimano 105 2×11: £1,899
  • 700c Bike – Platinum – Shimano GRX800 1×11: £2,224
  • RoadPlus Bike – Silver + Hope wheels – Shimano Tiagra 2×10: £1,899
  • RoadPlus Bike – Gold 1x + Hope wheels – SRAM Apex 1×11: £2,099
  • RoadPlus Bike – Gold + Hope wheels – Shimano 105 2×11: £2,249
  • RoadPlus Bike – Platinum + Hope Wheels – Shimano GRX800 1×11: £2,574

Head over to Cotic.co.uk to learn more.
