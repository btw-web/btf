Lezyne Rolls Out Their Own Line of Bikepacking Bags

Lezyne, a California-based company known for their pumps, tools, GPS devices, and other various accessories, has just expanded their product line even further. Their new collection of Lezyne bikepacking bags, or ‘Adventure Bags’ as they’ve named them, includes the Bar Caddy handlebar roll, XL Caddy seat pack, Stuff Caddy stem bag, and the Energy Caddy top tube bag. All are made from a woven nylon materiel, and feature water-resistant zippers, labeled organizational pockets, large pull loops, and reflective logos for increased night visibility. Here are the details on each:

Bar Caddy

The Bar Caddy is a medium capacity handlebar bag with about 7 liters of space. It has double sided roll-type enclosures for a compressible design and is made from a highly water-resistant nylon. Additional features include adjustable straps and a simple velcro mounting system. External draw cords hold extra gear, and reflective features and light mounts add additional visibility and safety.

  • Size ?
  • Capacity 7 Liters
  • Price $49.99
  • Place of Manufacture Taiwan
  • Contact Link

XL Caddy

The XL Caddy is a seat bag with a roll-style closure and external drawcords to hold extra gear. The Caddy is made from water-resistant, durable materials with reflective features and light mounts for increased visibility and safety.

  • Size 420 x 170 x 140 mm
  • Capacity 7.5 Liters
  • Price $69.99
  • Place of Manufacture Taiwan
  • Contact Link

Stuff Caddy

This simple stem bag looks to be a similar design to most standard ‘feed bags’. It is designed to mount to the handlebar, frame, fork or anything else you can strap it to. It features adjustable straps to increase versatility and an integrated drawcord to tightly hold its contents. As with the other bags in the line, it has a water-resistant construction with reflective features for added safety.

  • Size 80 x 60 x 115 mm
  • Capacity 1.3 Liters
  • Price $26.99
  • Place of Manufacture Taiwan
  • Contact Link

Energy Caddy XL

The Energy Caddy XL is a simple top tube organizer for quick access to snacks and tools. The water-resistant, zippered main compartment has side sleeves that are large enough to hold a smartphone. The Energy Caddy is made from durable material with reflective features for additional safety.

  • Size 215 x 100 x 55 mm
  • Capacity 0.8 Liters
  • Price $29.99
  • Place of Manufacture Taiwan
  • Contact Link

For more information, visit Lezyne.com.
