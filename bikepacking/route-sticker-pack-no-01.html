Route Sticker Pack No.01

We’re excited to announce our new Route Sticker Pack series, something that’s been in the back of our minds for a while now. Each sticker pack feature a collection of three vinyl stickers sold through our online store. The first pack consists of two of our favorites from the southwest and one from the northeastern United States. Expect more packs of three with routes from all over the world. Route Sticker Pack No.01 will be available soon alongside some new hats, shirts, and more patches and stickers. Stay tuned for the announcement. Bikepacking Collective members will be notified first via email.
