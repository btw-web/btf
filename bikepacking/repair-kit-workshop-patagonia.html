Build a First Aid Gear Repair Kit Workshop

We’re advocates for repairing gear before replacing it, but we also realize that it’s far easier said than done. We often talk about what bike tools and spares are crucial for bikepacking, but don’t often talk about the other gear we’re using and how clothing, bags, and other softgoods also fail while out on the trail.

In addressing this subject, our friend and regular contributor Neža Peterca (@t.w.w.c) has teamed up with Patagonia Innsbruck to answer all of your questions. Beyond being a badass bikepacker and gear maker extraordinaire, Neža is also the repair tech at the shop and has published several articles in our make your own bikepacking gear (MYOBG) series.

What: Think of a question you’d like Neža to answer and submit it before the event, which will be hosted in Microsoft Teams. Joining info will be emailed to you.
When: Wednesday, February 17th at 7:30 – 8:30pm CET
How: RSVP using this link.
