Cedaero × Esker Hayduke Custom Frame Bags

The Hayduke is Esker Cycles’ versatile and longstanding Chromoly steel mountain bike, with clearance for both 27.5 x 3.0″ and 29 x 2.3″ tires, swing-arm swappable dropouts that allow for multiple drivetrain setups. In short, it’s Esker’s vision of the perfect hardtail. The Hayduke has all the fixings for a great bikepacking bike, and its generously sized main triangle and wealth of mounting points are begging for a frame bag. To make that a little easier, Esker has just teamed up with Minnesota-based bag maker Cedaero on some Hayduke-specific frame bags that can now be purchased directly through Esker while you’re picking up a complete bike or frame.

The custom frame bags are 100% bolt-on and designed specifically for the Esker Hayduke. The bags are offered in either a gray Cordura or brown waxed canvas, and they fit frame sizes XS to XL. The bags have a single drive-side zippered opening, Cedaero and Esker patches, and utilize every last little bit of space inside the main triangle to maximize carrying capacity. The Cordura bags cost $200 USD and the waxed canvas versions are $230 USD.

Head over to EskerCycles.com to learn more.
