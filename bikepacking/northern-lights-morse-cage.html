New Northern Lights Cerakote Ti Morse Cage from Wolf Tooth

Back with another limited edition version of their popular Ti Morse Cage, Wolf Tooth Components stepped things up with a Northern Lights Cerakote finish. The finish is dominantly blue but can also appear green or gold depending on the light. Cerakote is a durable industrial-strength coating that can be found on tools, engines, and has been making more appearances in the cycling industry as a high-end finish option on bikes and other bits. The titanium Morse Cages are built from hand-bent hollow titanium tubing, weigh just 33 grams, and offer a versatile array of finely tunable mounting positions thanks to their dot-dash-dot-dash mounting pattern.

The Northern Lights Morse Cage is priced at $84.95 USD, compared to the standard Ti Morse cage at $69.95. Buy it now at WolfToothComponents.com.
