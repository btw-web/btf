Vic Divide 550 2021: Hamish and Adam’s Big Adventure (Video)

Early on the morning of February 27th, nearly 100 riders departed from Melbourne for the third edition of the Victoria Divide 550 bikepacking race, which snakes its way through the Victorian section of Australia’s Great Dividing Range.

Among them were six riders representing Curve Cycling, including Adam Lana and Hamish McGowan, both of whom were doing some final testing on prototype Curve GMX+ Steel bikes (a reminder that we’re giving away a yet-to-be-released GMX+ Steel next week, see details and get signed up here!). They put together a short video to document their experience of riding 550 kilometers along the stunning off-road route. Watch it below, followed by a small gallery of photos from Joe Grace (@averagemrjoe).
