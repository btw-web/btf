Dakine Hot Laps 5L Review (video)

The Dakine Hot Laps 5L is a large hip pack made from a 100% 450D ripstop recycled polyester (rPET) exterior with a wealth of features, including an optional 2L Hydrapak lumbar water reservoir. With storage capacity comparable to small a backpack, the Hot Laps 5L is a versatile hip pack that offers a lot of options and has become Neil’s favorite over the last year.
