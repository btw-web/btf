Discovery and Despair on the Colorado Trail (Video)

With his regular race schedule on hold due to the pandemic, Payson McElveen shifted his focus to longer and longer rides. In May, he climbed the height of Mount Everest as part of the Everesting Challenge… entirely on singletrack. For part of an upcoming Matchstick Productions Film, he linked together 100 miles of trails in Crested Butte in a day. In late September, he had an opportunity to head out on his first bikepacking trip on the Colorado Trailâ€”a rugged 530-mile route from Durango to Denver. After a two-week crash course in everything bikepacking, Payson set out to tackle the route as fast as possible, which ultimately led to a rollercoaster of emotions, and left Payson with a newfound passion.
