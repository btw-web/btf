Bikepacking Vancouver Island, by Jenny Tough (Video)

Over the past decade, Jenny Tough has lived a life of adventure while traveling and exploring different cultures and faraway places. However, as we all know, things are a little different. This year, Jenny’s finding adventure from the front door of her home in British Columbia. In her latest video, Jenny cycles the length of Vancouver Island avoiding bears and soaking in the tremendous scenery that the island has to offer.

This year is the year to adventure from your front door. – Jenny Tough

“It’s amazing to get moving again. To get mud on my skin and sleep under trees every night. I didn’t have to get far to start feeling like myself again…”

Find more from Jenny Tough on Instagram @jennytough. Also, be sure to check out her other videos we’ve featured: Go Solo: You’ve Got This, Bikepacking the Balkans, and Trans Continental Race No.5… Scratched.
