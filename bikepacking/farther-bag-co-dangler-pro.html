Farther Bag Co Dangler Pro

Non-danglers might be wondering, “Isn’t the back of a saddlebag a questionable place to hang a camp mug that you intend to drink from later?” It can indeed be a real issue, and I’m sure it’s one that keeps seasoned danglers up at night. Thankfully, Farther Bag Co from Memphis, Tennessee, has the answer to your dangling woes.

The new Dangler Pro from Farther Bag Co is like a little jacket for your favourite camp mug, allowing you to dangle worry-free in the most trying conditions. Or as Farther Bag Co puts it, it’s just one of their “unnecessary products to fix all the non problems in your life.” The sleeve is designed to fit a standard 16oz camp mug and has a slick drawstring closure to keep the dirt out. It is currently offered in multi-camo X50, brown X10, or olive X10, making it highly water-resistant and durable.

  • Capacity: 16oz mug
  • Material: X50 / X10 X-Pac
  • Height: 5.75″ expanded / 3.5″ cinched
  • Place of Manufacture: Tennessee, USA
  • Price: $35 USD

The Dangler Pro is open for preorder now, with a ship time of late August/early September. It costs $35 USD and promises to change your life forever. Learn more at FartherBag.com.
