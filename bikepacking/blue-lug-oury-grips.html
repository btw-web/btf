New Oury x Blue Lug Grip Colours

Oury has created quite the reputation for themselves over the years, and it’s not uncommon to find their colourful US-made rubber grips specced on custom bike builds that are in need of a little extra flare. And who doesn’t like more options? Tokyo-based Blue Lug recently teamed up with Oury to create four new colours of the V2 grips, made exclusively for Blue Lug.

Joining the gum-coloured grips that were released last year are the new tan, burgundy, and olive Oury V2 grips, all pictured below. They’re 135mm wide, 33mm in diameter, and weigh 133 grams. They are made of a single compound of soft rubber that’s both grippy and comfortable. Blug Lug has an international online store for those shopping from North America and the grips retail for $16.45 USD.

Head over to BlueLug.com to check them out.
