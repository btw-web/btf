Unforgettable (Film Trailer)

Last spring, a group of bikepackers set out to ride around the perimeter of Michigan’s Upper Peninsula, an approximately 1,600-mile journey that traced the outline of one of the most unique geographic and cultural regions in America. Ironically, the Upper Peninsula, or U.P., is often left off of maps due in part to data glitches and an inconvenient silhouette. The group documented the entire trip and will be releasing a full-length film soon. Until then, you can find the trailer below.
