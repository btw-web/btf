Lynskey Announces 2020 Adventure Edition GR300

Lynskey’s new titanium GR300 ‘Adventure Edition’ gravel bike looks to be an interesting option for those on the hunt for a comfortable, versatile, titanium gravel bike. As Lynskey puts it, the new GR300 Adventure Edition is for “gravel and adventure-focused riders who demand a bike that ticks all the boxes: road, gravel, rugged commuter, and bike packing. Those who long to ride on road one day and gravel the next, but crave that titanium comfort ride with a smooth finish.” We definitely can’t argue with the benefits of riding a titanium frame, and with generous tire clearance, nice details, and a good selection of bottle and accessory mounts, the new GR300 looks like a pretty fun bike. Find specs below, and more details on build options further down.

  • Titanium 3AL-2.5V Tubeset
  • Internally Routed Cables
  • Top Tube Mount and Three Bottle Mounts
  • Rack and Fender Mounts
  • Tapered Headtube
  • 68mm English Threaded BSA Bottom Bracket Shell
  • Wright style dropouts with replaceable aluminum hanger
  • 27.2mm Seatpost Diameter
  • Flat Mount Disc Brakes

In true Lynskey fashion, the GR300 is not available in one clear-cut build, but has over 15 different builds to choose from, including various frame finishes, graphic colours, and wheelsets. To get an idea, the base SRAM Rival 2x build, including a full set of Nittany Mountain Works bags, is priced at $4,063. This price can easily be increased with upgraded wheels, finishes, and small components. Depending on desired components, the complete build could be closer to $10,000 if you want a Shimano Dura Ace Di2 drivetrain, Stan’s Avion Team Disc wheelset, and other high-end components.

Head over to LynskeyPerformance.com to check out all of the options available, or to build one for yourself.
