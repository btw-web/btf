Preorders Open For All New Jetboil Stash Cooking System

First teased on social media a few days ago, we’ve been eager to see what Jetboil has been cooking upâ€”pun intended. The new Jetboil Stash is a complete cooking system (stove, pot, lid) designed to be lightweight and compact for when space and weight are paramount. The Stash is Jetboil’s lightest system to date, weighing just 200g (fuel stabilizer not included), while still offering a generously sized 0.8L pot. The Stash stove burner head is made of titanium, the hard-anodized aluminum pot has an integrated head exchanger or Flux Ring for better efficiency, and the entire kit can pack inside the pot alongside a 100g fuel canister.

Designed for folks who prioritize a fast and light kit, the Stash doesn’t have a regulator and is best suited to boil-only meals and hot drinks. It also lacks an integrated Piezo ignitor, strainer, or cozy, but it is compatible with their Silicone Grande Coffee Press and Hanging Kit.

  • Pot Weight: 120g
  • Stove Weight: 80g
  • Fuel Stabilizer Weight: 27g
  • Output: 4,500 BTU
  • Volume: 0.8L
  • Boil Time: 2min 30sec per 0.5 liter
  • Packed Size: 4.4″ x 5.1″
  • Place of Manufacture: China
  • Price: $129.95 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Jetboil.JohnsonOutdoors.com

The Jetboil Stash is available for preorder now, with shipping expected to start on March 1st. We’re hoping to get our hands on the Stash soon to test out. Until then, find lots of other options on our complete list and guide of stoves for bikepacking.
