The Search Brigade is Back!

In 2017, NYC’s Search And State set out on a long-distance bike trip across America and for their inaugural Search Brigade. After a pandemic-induced hiatus, SAS just announced that the Brigade is back for an August trip in Colorado. Read the press release below, then scroll down to watch their video from 2017.

It is with great excitement we can finally announce the Search Brigade is back! After a pandemic-induced hiatus we have given the Brigade the green light and this August we will be wheels-up and back on the trail. This email will serve as a save the date and an invitation to email us for more information, or to outright say yes and put your name in the hat to give the Brigade a go.

For our grand return to adventure, we are taking “Brigade Thin Air” way up high and we are heading to Colorado. On August 22nd we will depart Denver and make our way up to Rocky Mountain National Park for a “warm up” to about 12,000ft. From there we move south to tackle the incredible Mount Evans and climb the highest paved road in North America. The Brigade will summit on Evans at a massive altitude of over 14,000ft. But, we’re not done yet. After Evans, we will cross the Continental Divide by way of Loveland Pass, and then finally grind our way south to climb Independence Pass into Aspen a week later. High Altitude. Mountain Peaks. Canyons. Dirt. Thin Air. This one has it all. The Brigade rolls on!

Please email us at info@searchandstate.com for more information. A few bullet points are below if you wish to start planning. And for some insight into Brigade logistics, requirements, and previous rides we have done please click the link below.

Thanks for reading.
-Search and State

  • What: The Brigade is a self-supported, group bike-packing expedition into some of the most interesting parts of the world.
  • Where: Denver to Aspen
  • Departure Date: August 22nd
  • Arrival Date: Tentatively August 29th
  • By way of: Estes Park, Mt. Evans, Loveland Pass, Independence Pass, and more
  • Info: Please email us at info@searchandstate.com
