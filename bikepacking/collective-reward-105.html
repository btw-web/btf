Collective Reward #105: Wizard Works Frame Bags

In case you missed it last week, the team at Wizard Works in London just announced an all-new partial frame bag, the Framebagracadabra, and we’re excited to be giving away a couple of them for Collective Reward #105. We’ll be randomly selecting two members of our Bikepacking Collective to receive a Framebagracadabra in their choice of size and color, a roughly $130 value for each lucky winner.

Available in three sizes (1.9L, 2.5L, and 3.5L) and five colors (Black Camo, Splatter, Olive, Rust, and Hot Pink), the Framebagracadabra features several thoughtful design touches that help it look at home on nearly any frame. It also sports an extra-burly zipper, lace-up top attachment, an inside mesh pocket, and a recycled HDPE bottom that helps it maintain its shape, even when empty. You can find additional details at Wizard.Works or in our Dispatch announcing the new bag, which is linked below.

Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible. If you’re a regular reader of the site, please consider joining our Bikepacking Collective. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards (including one of these frame bags), receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to a bunch of exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.
