Outdoor Provisions Adds Two New Nut Butter Flavours

UK-based Outdoor Provisions launched their Nut Butter lineup back in January, and apparently they’ve been a hit. Their compostable packaging and natural ingredients caught our attention but with just two flavours to choose from, there was clearly room to grow. They just doubled their lineup with the addition of two new flavours: Hazelnut & Cacao and Peanut, Almond, and Raspberry—and we’re eager to try them out for ourselves.

Ingredients

  • Hazelnut & Cacao: Hazelnut (77%), Coconut Sugar, Cacao Powder (5%), Coconut Oil, Vanilla Powder, Sea Salt
  • Peanut, Almond, and Raspberry: Peanuts (70%), Almonds (18%), Dried Raspberry (7%), Coconut Oil, Sea Salt

With the addition of the new flavours comes a new Starter Pack, which includes four energy bars and four nut butters for just £12 (with the code TRYOP). Outdoor Provisions also offers free shipping within the UK. Head over to OutdoorProvisions.co.uk to check them out.

Luke Douglas, the co-founder of Outdoor Provisions, has also contributed several articles to the site, including the Straight Outta Manchester route in England and the story of his trip through Kyrgyzstan and Tajikstan on the Pamir Highway.
