The Arbuckle Pass (film trailer)

Photos and video by PeteroftheSpoon

In the summer of 2020, John Finlay (Marc Grau) and Young Nils (Nils Kamminga) set off on the trip of their lifetime in the post-Covid-19 era. One of the destinations on this unforgettable journey is the Arbuckle Pass, where the two young riders would take on some of the best bikepacking terrain in the region. What they didn’t account were the dangers afoot. Along the way they encounter the mysterious lumberjack, Richard Speck aka Dirty Richard (Dustin Firestone), who is no friend to outsiders. Watch the trailer for this faux-film, which might just be the first bikepacking action-thriller to ever make it to the not-so-big screen.
