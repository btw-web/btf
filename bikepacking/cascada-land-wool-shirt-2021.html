Four New Cascada Land Wool Shirt Colors

Cascada just added four new colorways to the Land Wool Shirt lineup, a piece we reviewed last year and also included in our roundup of Cycling-Friendly Flannels and Button-Downs roundup this past winter. Made from a super soft and stretchy polyester/wool blend that’s comfortable next to the skin and warm enough to stay cozy on crisp fall mornings, it has a casual style with a slim fit and a slightly dropped hem that helps provide additional coverage without making it look too cycling-specific. Cascada made some bold choices with their new colors—Westbound, Woodland, Charcoal, and Earth Shadow—all of which you can check out below.

The Cascada Land Wool shirts are made in Italy and come in men’s sizes XS to XXL. The women’s Land Wool Shirt comes in a single colorway in sizes XS to Large. They retail for €120 (~$140 USD).

Head over to Cascada.cc to see more.
