Limited Edition 2021 Panaracer GravelKings

Panaracer just announced the addition of two limited edition colours to their GravelKing tire lineupâ€”Pansy Blue and Flamingo Pink. The fresh new colours are paired with classy brown sidewalls and are limited to the 700 x 32mm and 700 x 38mm sizes. The limited run will include three different tread patterns: the SS, SK, and Slicks. These special GravelKing tires are made with the same ZSG tyre compound technology, anti-flat casing, and 120tpi construction as the rest of the lineup. All Gravelking tires 32mm and up are tubeless compatible as well.

Look for the 2021 Limited Edition Gravelking colors to be shipping early to mid-June directly from PanaracerUSA.com and via their distributors in North America and Europe. If ordering from the Panaracer USA site, customers can sign up to be notified via email once the tires are in stock and ready to be shipped. This will be a single-run production, so once they’re gone, they’re gone!

All models cost $49.99 USD per tire. Sign up to be notified when they’re available here.
