Check out the Cercle Concept Bike

Drawing its name from the French word for (you guessed it) circle, the Cercle is a concept touring bike that features a massive circular frame at the center of what’s designed to be an all-in-one touring rig. The circular cavity houses a folding bed that transitions to a desk and cooking station to provide a place to sleep and work while out rambling.
