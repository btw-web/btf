PEdALED’s new Jary All-Road Merino Hooded Jersey

PEdALED just sent us some info and photos of their new Jary All-Road Merino Hoody. PEdALED’s Jary Collection is their first line of cycling clothing designed for riding off-road. The collection currently includes a merino jersey, boxers, riding shorts (which we love), gloves, a jacket, and as of recently, the new Jary All-Road Merino Hooded Jersey. The new hoody is designed for early mornings at camp or late season rides, combining cold-weather protection and a casual fit that is perfect for long, shoulder season rides and bikepacking trips.

The Jary All-Road Merino Hooded Jersey features ripstop chest panels for added durability and to block the wind. It has a balaclava-style hood, windproof cuffs, and large pockets on the back. PEdALED describes the fit to be slightly looser for more freedom of movement, and is currently only offered in men’s sizing.

  • Sizes: XS – XXL
  • Material: 49% Merino Wool, 49% Polyester, 2% Elastan
  • Colours: forest green, black, and mustard
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $216 USD
