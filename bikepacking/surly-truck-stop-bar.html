Surly Introduces the Truck Stop Bar

Designed to combine “all that’s good about drop bars with the benefits of a riser,” the Surly Truck Stop Bar offers a more relaxed, upright riding position with 30mm of vertical rise. This aluminum bar is available in 42, 45, or 48cm widths and has a 12Â° flare. Watch the video (be sure to turn the sound on) and find full specs below…

Surly claims the Truck Stop Bar has a lightweight aluminum construction, although the weight is yet to be published. It has an ED-coated black finish with a 31.8 clamp diameter. Widths at the drops are 475, 505, and 535mm for the 42, 45, and 48cm versions. For details, check SurlyBikes.com. The Surly Truck Stop Bar is available now from your local dealer, although we couldn’t find a published price. UPDATE: a reader reported a retail price of $60 USD from Surly.
