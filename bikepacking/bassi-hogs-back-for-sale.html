The Bassi Hog’s Back we reviewed is for sale!

Our British Columbia-based readers might be interested in the Bassi Hog’s Back that we reviewed a few months back. We dropped it off at Kissing Crows Cyclery in Vancouver recently and they’ve just listed in on their online shop, so it could be all yours!

For those unfamiliar, the Hog’s Back is a beautiful steel all-road touring bike from Quebec-based Bassi Bikes. This particular build is specced with some pretty flashy parts, including a dynamo front hub, mechanical BB7 brakes, Brooks Cambium saddle, and more. The cherry on top is the massive custom frame bag we had made for the bike from Field and Forest, who is also based out of Vancouver. It’s a size 58, perfect for dirt road touring and commuting, and is priced at $2,750 CAD. Check out our full review here.
