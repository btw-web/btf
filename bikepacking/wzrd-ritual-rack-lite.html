WZRD Ritual Rack Lite in Support of BC Flood Relief

The new Ritual Rack Lite from British Columbia-based WZRD Bikes is an all-in-one bar-cargo system that allows you to use whatever stem you want, unlike their original version, which had an integrated stem. The bar has an built-in cargo cage positioned just in front of the stem clamping area, which WZRD describes as “the perfect bar for hauling shit into the woods.”

The bar and lower supports are made from 4130 Chromoly steel, the rack and upper supports are made from 304 stainless steel. You have your choice of finish, either painted black or a clear coat over a raw finish, and custom options are available upon request. Shims are available for both 31.8 and 35mm stems.

Preorders are open now, and $50 from every sale will be donated to BC flood relief mutual aids and community funds. For those not aware, parts of British Columbia are currently in a state of emergency after a record-setting rainfall on Sunday evening, which resulted in major landslides, flooding, and evacuations across the province. The damage is still being accessed and rescue efforts are underway, but thousands of people have been separated from their families, people have lost their homes, and there are others are unaccounted for after mudslides wreaked havoc on some of BC’s major highways.

Right now, the preorder is only open to customers in Canada, but you can email WZRD to get on a waitlist if you live in the US.

  • Width: 800mm
  • Back Sweep: 10°
  • Up Sweep: 3°
  • Rise: 42mm
  • Weight: 950 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: British Columbia, Canada
  • Price: $500 CAD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WZRD.Bike
