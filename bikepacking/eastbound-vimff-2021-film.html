Eastbound + VIMFF 2021 (Film)

As part of the Vancouver International Mountain Film Festival’s (VIMFF) Adventure Show, the North American premiere of Eastbound will be going live at 12 p.m. PST today. The film tells the story of Jenny Graham’s record-breaking ride around the planet to become not only the first woman to complete the circumnavigation unsupported, but also the fastest—shaving 20 days off the previous record. There are three other films being showcased as well, including Fly the Roof, Lost at Sea, and Venture Out.

This is a paid online screening and is part of VIMFF 2021. You have the option to pay for this show only or you can buy festival passes to have access to all the films released in this year’s event. Watch the trailer for the entire festival below, then head over to Vimff.org to purchase your ticket.

VIMFF is going virtual for 2021 from February 19 – 28th. This year’s program and includes 10 shows, 3 feature films, 3 interactive virtual workshops, and 3 virtual panel discussions. Learn more here.
