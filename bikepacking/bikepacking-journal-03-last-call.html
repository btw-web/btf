Last Call for The Bikepacking Journal 03!

Heads up! The deadline to join our Bikepacking Collective and have your membership start with Issue 03 of The Bikepacking Journal is today, October 17th, by 11:59 PM EST! New members joining after that will still receive all the same member benefits, but will have to wait until spring 2020 to receive the next issue in the mail.

Scenes from Issue 03—left to right: Ben Page (Romania), Donalrey Nieva (Peru), Dave Taylor (Timor-Leste)

Simply put, if you enjoy reading BIKEPACKING.com and think it’s a valuable resource, the single best thing you can to do support us is join our Bikepacking Collective. As a largely member supported site, our backers enable us to create and publish daily original bikepacking routes, authentic stories, and quality guides and reportage.

Extras Included with Issue 03:

Along with your copy of The Bikepacking Journal 03, you’ll also receive two member-only bonuses that we’re quite excited to be giving away. Every one of our backers will receive a hand-illustrated map of the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route (Tour Divide Course) created by one of our favorite artists, Melbourne-based Alex Hotchin. As an added thank you, we’re also including a Bikepacking Collective Member Patch, exclusively offered to those of you who support the site. View Map | View Patch

Still need some convincing? Read our Bikepacking Journal Manifesto or peek at Issue 02 and Issue 01. And remember, the deadline to get signed up for Issue 03 is today, October 17th, by 11:59 PM EST!
