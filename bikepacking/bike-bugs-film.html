Bike to Bugs (film)

Kona Ambassador Gretchen Leggitt and friend Robin Kodner recently took on a massive bikepacking journey on Canada’s Great Trail from Bellingham, Washington, to climb in the Bugaboo Mountains. Over the course of a couple weeks, the duo pedaled over 900 kilometers through crazy weather, lots of bushwacking, and more adventure than could possibly be explained in photos. With that in mind, Gretchen created this incredible animation to showcase the emotions of the journey.
