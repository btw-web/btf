Hyperlite Mountain Gear’s “Get a Long Time Gone” Giveaway

Hyperlite Mountain Gear’s “Get a Long Time Gone” giveaway is your one-way ticket to all types of adventures. They’ve teamed up with Salsa Cycles and Alpacka Raft to put together an excellent prize package, worth over $5,000, and all it takes is an email address to get signed up. The giveaway is open to all residents of the US and runs from October 7th to October 28th, 2020. The winner will be announced on October 29th.

One lucky winner will receive:

Hyperlite Mountain Gear

  • 4400 Porter Backpack
  • UltaMid 2 Pyramid Tent
  • UltaMid2 Insert w/DCF11 Floor
  • Porter Stuff Pocket
  • UltaMid Pole Straps
  • Ultralight Stake Kit

Salsa Cycles

  • Blackborow GX Eagle Fat Bike

Alpacka Raft

  • Caribou – The Bikeraft with Cargo Fly
  • Aqua-Bound Manta Ray Hybrid 4-Piece Paddle

To enter, head over to HyperliteMountainGear.com.
