Gravel & Gratitude: Leonardo Brasil and his Salsa Stormchaser

Photos by Adam Andres Pawlikiewicz Mesa

In the second installment of their Gravel & Gratitude storysite, Salsa Cycles introduces us to Colorado-based photographer Leonardo Brasil (@leonardobrasil_photography), who offers some thoughts on how freedom, adventure, and self-sufficiency are key components of what fuels his passion for pedaling, as well as the journey he’s been on from his early days of riding to today.

Visit SalsaCycles.com to learn more about Leonardo and his Stormchaser, once again with still images by our friend Adam Andres Pawlikiewicz Mesa another excellent short video from Evan Green. And if you missed it, be sure to catch up on part one, which featured Krystal Salvent and her Warbird. Check that out here.
