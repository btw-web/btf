Swift Fuses Old and New with Horizon Line X10 Cotton Duck

Swift just announced the new Horizon Line, a fresh take on their Zeitgeist Saddle Bag, Sugarloaf #basketpacking bag, and Paloma Handlebar rando bag. The Horizon Line is made from a new material called X10 Cotton Duck. It’s the very first traditional canvas fabric to incorporate the modern magic of XPac. X10 Cotton Duck is a lightweight, waterproof cotton canvas, made here in the United States. According to Swift, no other fabric summarizes their brand better. Our two favorites are the:

Swift horizon Line Zeitgeist Saddle Bag

While many technical bikepackers appreciate a streamlined seat pack, the traditional saddlebag is still very popular for its classic stylings and easy access. The Zeitgeist Saddle bag attaches to the strap loops built-in on some saddles, or can be cradled by a support rack designed exactly for this bag. Now the with the new X10 Cotton Duck, old certainly meets new. Learn more at builtbyswift.com

Swift horizon Line Sugarloaf Basket Bag

Yes, basketpacking is a thing. Just ask Cass. Now Swift has updated the Sugarloaf with the X10 making it more waterproof and lighter than before. Learn more at builtbyswift.com.
