New Smokestone Bikes Mr Harry: 29+ Gravel

Best known for their lineup of fat bikes, UK-based Smokestone Bikes has released their first gravel bike, and it appears to have a lot going for it. Mr Harry is Smokestone Bikes’ take on the adventure bike, and their first attempt at designing a bike that is more ride specific (outside of fat bikes, that is). Mr Harry is a drop bar gravel bike based around 29″ wheels, with clearance for up to 29 x 3.0″ tires. The frame features three bottle cage mounts, rear rack mounts, a large main triangle for running a frame bag, internal dropper post routing, boost rear axle spacing, and is suspension corrected for running 100mm front suspension like the Fox AX Fork or MRP Baxter. Scroll down for photos, frame specs, and geometry charts.

Smokestone Bikes Mr Harry Specs

  • Material Titanium grade 9 (3Al/2.5V)
  • Headset Size Top cup = IS 42, Lower cup = IS 52
  • Rear Axle12 x 148mm
  • Seat Post Size31.6mm
  • Seat Post Clamp Size34.9mm
  • Dropper PostInternal Stealth cable routing
  • Frame Sizes52cm, 54cm, 56cm, 58cm, 60cm
  • Bottom Bracket72mm threaded

As with all of Smokestone Bikes’ offerings, Mr Harry is available as a frame or a complete custom build. The frame only will set you back £1,600 (~$2,080 USD), while the build photographed would retail for £3,800 ($5,000 USD).

For those living near Gloucester, Smokestone Bikes strongly recommends visiting their shop at Slam69 to get perfectly sized or to take a demo out for the day. Otherwise, you can find more details at SmokestoneBikes.co.uk.
