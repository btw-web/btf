Collective Reward #053: Revelate Designs Spinelock Seat Bags

In case you missed it, Logan recently published his comprehensive review Revelate Designs’ innovative new Spinelock Seat Bag system, and he had lots of good things to say about it. In short, the longtime creators of quality bikepacking gear have pushed the envelope once again, designing a clever new system for attaching your seat bag to your saddle. It’s one of the most stable bikepacking seat packs on the market, is completely waterproof, has an exceptionally lightweight and minimal mounting system.

For our latest Collective Reward, two Bikepacking Collective members will win their choice of a 10L or 16L Spinelock (the latter being the largest seatpack Revelate has ever made). If you haven’t yet become a supporter of the site, consider signing up and you’ll be eligible for this giveaway.

In Logan’s words, “From the outside, the Revelate Spinelock System seat bag looks a lot like any other bikepacking-style saddle pack, but its patented mounting system is different from anything else on the market.” So, how’s it work? The system consists of three parts: the saddle clamp, seat bag mounted “Spine” baseplate, and pin. The ultra-low-profile clamp stays mounted to your saddle rails, and mounting the bag is as simple as lining it up with your desired mounting position on the “Spine” baseplate and sliding the pin through to connect the two.
