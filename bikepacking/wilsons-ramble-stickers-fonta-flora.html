Wilson’s Ramble Stickers at Fonta Flora

Following our first Route Sticker Pack (which is still available in our online store, by the way) we decided to make a holographic badge for one of our favorite new routes and give them away to participating riders via a local business located on the trail.

If you’re riding The Wilson’s Ramble, just stop by the Fonta Flora Whippoorwill Farm for a refreshment and let the barkeep know you’re bikepacking the route and you’d like a free sticker. Note that we sent these stickers to Fonta Flora early this week, so expect them to be there and ready to pick up sometime late next week!


The Wilson’s Ramble is a quintessential 3-4 day bikepacking route that loops through several spectacular geographic zones around the Wilson Creek watershed and the Linville Gorge Wilderness. The route uses an orchestrated mix of flowing singletrack, remote and rugged forest roads, and graded gravel to provide a great long weekend riding experience that takes in all the highlights of this magical region, including one of the best breweries on the East Coast.
