Hawgin’ The Chilcotins (video)

Just about a 4-hour drive from Vancouver lies this pristine wilderness where you can ride your bike into the alpine in under half a day, and continue in it for days on end. But, the Chilcotin Mountains are no joke. It’s a rugged area full of bears, rocks, blow down, and all forms of technical singletrack, or lack thereof. In Fox’s latest latest episode of “Send the Search,” Margus Riga, Kenny Smith, Chester Bush, and Fraser Newton set out on an 8-day Hawgin’ mission into the depths of this zone.
