Rapha Women’s 100 Launch Video

The Rapha Women’s 100 launched today. This annual event will be held on September 6th and is designed to inspire women around the world to set and reach a common goal of riding 100 kilometers. While this may have been a group ride event under normal circumstances, this year they’re celebrating the “Invisible Peloton” by encouraging riders to complete the 100 kilometer distance on their own terms, or in small groups as permitted by regional COVID-19 restrictions. Watch the video below, learn more about the Rapha Women’s 100 collection here, and be sure to mark your calendars.
