Cedaero Bags in Emerald City and Sticky Purple Waxed Canvas

Who doesn’t love a classic waxed canvas bag! Karl, the Production Manager/Designer over at Cedaero let us know that through a stroke of good fortune they were able to get their hands on some spicy green (Emerald City) and purple (Sticky Purple) waxed canvas fabric. These new colors will be available in both their standard bag offerings and for custom bags. Cedaero’s standard bag line includes a variety of top tube and stem bags, as well as a few smaller saddle bags. They also make custom frame bags and panniers, and have produced some impressive bags for the Salsa Blackborrow and Trek 1120.

“Our waxed canvas collection is for those who prefer the classic look and feel of natural fibers. These packs combine the hard-wearing performance of waxed canvas with our bombproof nylon liner and #9 water resistant zippers. They patina with age and use and eventually tell the story of the roads (or lack thereof) theyâ€™ve travelled. Usually, waxed canvas comes in earth tones (which are great!), but we’re especially stoked to add some groovy colors to the lineup, Sticky Purple and Emerald City!” – Karl Mesedahl, Cedaero Production Manager/Designer

Waxed canvas adds $10 to the price of standard packs and $30 to custom frame packs. Learn more at Cedaero.com.
