2022 Kona Unit and Unit X Announced

Previously listed under Kona’s hardtail mountain bike lineup, the new 2022 Kona Unit and Unit X now fall into a category of their own: bikepacking. The update is quite fitting for the Unit as it has consistently offered some of the best value and versatility we’ve seen in a steel hardtail mountain bike, making it a great option for anyone hoping to ride lots of different terrain on one bike.

The Unit models are based around Reynolds 520 Chromoly steel frames, 29 x 2.6″ tires, plenty of mounts, and a versatile geometry that’s practical for nearly any situation. Although not much (other than a $200 price increase) has changed for 2022, the Unit X received some additional top tube mounts, and both the Unit and Unit X get new paint jobs and build kits. Find details on both below.

2022 Kona Unit X

The geared Kona Unit X offers a lot of bike for the price. Sliding dropouts, thru-axles, and big 29 x 2.6″ WTB Ranger tires are all stock for $1,599 USD. Straying from both the 2020 and 2021 models, the 2022 Kona Unit X is based around a Shimano Deore 1×12 drivetrain but gets a new “Gloss Metallic Dragonfly with Charcoal & Nimbus” colorway.

2022 Kona Unit

It shares all the same specs as the Unit X, minus the gears, and retails for $1,399 USD for the complete build. For 2022, the Kona Unit comes with a “Gloss Metallic Dusty Rose w/ Chocolate & Bronze” paint job, and we’re digging it.
