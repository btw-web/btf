Home Brewed Bike Bags by Bedrock

Durango, Colorado-based Bedrock Bags has been making original, high-quality bikepacking bags since 2012. They just released this cool little home-brewed, promotional video. If you aren’t familiar with Bedrock, make sure to check out our offsite visit here. Plus, find reviews of their Entrada handlebar bag, Dakota top tube bag, the relatively new Sinbad roll-top accessory, and Black Dragon, a dropper-post specific seat pack.

We’re also currently testing Bedrock’s new Greysill hip pack, which is quite nice. It features a burly zipper closure and double-buckle flap to keep things tight and secure. Greysill comfortably fits a large, full-frame mirrorless camera, too. Here are a couple teaser shots. Stay tuned for our big Low Down on hip packs in the not too distant future. In the meantime, find out more over at BedrockBags.com.
