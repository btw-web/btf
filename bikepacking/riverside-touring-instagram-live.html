Instagram Live Q&A with Riverside Touring

Riverside Touring by Decathlon, the brand behind the Touring 920 that we’re currently reviewing, has worked closely with the bikepacking and touring community when designing new products. To continue the conversation, they’ve planned a live Q&A on Instagram tonight to answer your burning questions. There’s no specific topic; they’re simply providing a place to answer questions about their bikes, future bags, design process, and the current state of the cycling industry. We’ll be tuning in and hope to see you there too.

  • Date: March 22nd, 2021
  • Time: 6:30pm GMT / 2:30pm EST
  • Where: Riverside Touring’s Instagram (@riverside_decathlon)
  • Cost: Free!

Not familiar with Riverside Touring by Decathlon? Check out Neil’s first look of the Riverside 920 on YouTube.
