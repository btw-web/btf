State Bicycle Co.’s National Parks Collaboration

The new State Bicycle Co. x National Park Foundation collection features an assortment of limited-edition bikes, apparel, and accessories that all feature imagery from some of America’s most scenic National Parks. A portion of the proceeds from each item will go directly toward NPFâ€™s mission to protect national parks and connect people to them.

State x NPF Bikes

The collection features five frames, each painted with special imagery and motifs to pay homage to a park. The limited-edition: 4130 All-Road (Joshua Tree), 4130 Road Bike (Glacier), Core-Line (Redwood), and two Klunkers (Grand Canyon & Yellowstone).

State x NPF Clothing

Each clothing item in the collection is made from recycled 100% post-consumer waste, using garments from State Bicycle Co.’s Sustainable Clothing Collection. Each tech-T, cycling jersey, and bib set in the SBC Sustainable Clothing Collection is constructed with Repreve, a polyester primarily recycled from water bottles.

State x NPF Bar Bags

Five matching handlebar bags inspired by the parks have been released as part of the collection. Designed for storing snacks and supplies for long rides, these universal bags fit on almost all bikes.

  • Capacity: 1.7L
  • Material: 600D polyester, Waterproof Zipper
  • Dimensions: 21 x 9cm
  • Price: $39.99 USD

The entire collection is available now at StateBicycle.com.
