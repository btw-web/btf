New SimWorks Cowcow Stealth Bar and Wider Fun 3 Stealth Bar

Despite huge delays elsewhere around the cycling industry, SimWorks keeps pushing forward with cool new products. The latest release includes the new Cowcow Stealth Bar and a wider version of their Fun 3 Stealth Bar. Find photos, specs, and details on each below.

SimWorks CowCow Stealth Bar

The new SimWorks CowCow Stealth Bar is a high-riser bar that’s designed to be comfortable, lightweight, and attractive—perfect for every day riding and the odd off-road meander. The CowCow Stealth Bar is manufactured by Nitto in Japan, made from heat-treated aluminum, and has a standard 31.8mm clamp size.

  • Material: Heat Treated Aluminium
  • Width: 750mm
  • Clamp Size: 31.8mm
  • Bar Diameter: 22.2mm
  • Rise: 80mm
  • Sweep: 12°(Back) / 5°(Up)
  • Weight: 380g
  • Place of Manufacture: Japan
  • Price: $120 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Sim.Works

SimWorks Fun 3 Stealth Bar

SimWorks’ Fun 3 Stealth Bar is now offered in a wider 750mm version for those who need it. SimWorks says it’s one of their most popular bars because it works well on so many different styles of bikes. Plus, it’s bent three times, which makes it three times the fun. Can’t argue with that. The Fun 3 is half riser bar and half promenade bar, with a 20mm rise and 35° sweep. See the full specs below.

  • Material: Heat Treated Aluminium
  • Width: 750mm / 680mm
  • Clamp Size: 31.8mm
  • Bar Diameter: 22.2mm
  • Rise: 20mm
  • Sweep: 35°
  • Weight: 395g
  • Place of Manufacture: Japan
  • Price: $120 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Sim.Works
