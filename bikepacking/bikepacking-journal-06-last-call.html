Last Call for The Bikepacking Journal 06!

Heads up! The deadline to join our Bikepacking Collective and have your membership kick off with Issue 06 of The Bikepacking Journal is tomorrow, April 17th, at 11:59 PM EST. If you join after the cutoff, you’ll have to wait until the fall to receive your first issue. We can’t wait to share our latest collection of stories in print and we don’t want you to miss out!

Images from the upcoming issue by Simón Mendizabal (@simonsaysadventure), Tom Powell (@tom_powell_photo), and Andrew Bydlon (@andrewbydlon)

Inside this issue, you’ll find another 160 pages of the most inspiring writing and extraordinary photography from bikepacking trips all around the world. Our contributors bring us to New Zealand, Mexico, Colorado, Peru, Canada, and many other locations. Plus, we’re shipping the new issue with a limited-edition Bikepacking Collective member bandana that features our revamped skull design, the entire ecosystem of drivetrain critters, and each of our three taglines, as well as an updated Bikepacking Collective badge.

Join the Bikepacking Collective

Becoming a Bikepacking Collective member is the best way to support our work at BIKEPACKING.com, where we’re committed to sharing in-depth articles, inspiring stories, original routes, and news on a daily basis. In addition to receiving The Bikepacking Journal, joining also means you’ll be entered to win one of 47 prizes in our upcoming giveaway drawing, gain access to exclusive discounts from an ever-growing list of great companies, and much more.
