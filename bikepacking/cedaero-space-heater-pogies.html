New Space Heater Pogies from Cedaero

Pogies are the best defense against frozen fingers, and they’re almost essential for winter riding in cold temperatures, especially for those of us living in places that see extreme temperatures. Minnesota-based Cedaero has been quietly working on a new pogie design for some time, and by the looks of it, they’re pretty dialled.

The new Space Heater Pogies are constructed from your choice of Cordura or Waxed Canvas with a Polartec fleece interior. They have a zippered vent along the front to help regulate the temperature, an adjustable fleece cuff, and a lightweight internal structure to keep the pogies’ shape and provide lots of room for your hands. They are designed to work with any flat bars and attach using a standard bar end plug (not included). Cedaero recommends using an expanding plug for quick removal.

The Cedaero Space Heater Pogies are made to order, and you can choose up to five coloured exterior fabrics plus your choice of three liner colours, for a totally custom look. The pogies start at $199.95 USD and go up depending on fabric choice and the number of colours used.

  • Exterior Material: 1000D Cordura or 15oz Waxed Canvas
  • Interior Material: Recycled 300-weight Polartec fleece
  • Place of Manufacture: Minnesota, USA
  • Price: $199.95 USD

Head over to Cedaero.com to check them out.
