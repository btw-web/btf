V2 All-City Cycles Electric Queen Rereleased with New Paint and more…

The All-City Cycles Electric Queen doesn’t feature any extra mounts or bikepacking bells and whistles, but as Cass saw it, this bike was expressly made for fun. “The ultimate trail hardtail” as All-City dubbed it, has a short wheelbase, somewhat aggressive geometry, a 120mm suspension fork, and fits 27.5 x 3.0” or 29 x 2.25” tires.

It also features the modern suite of mountain standards: stealth dropper routing, BOOST spacing, thru-axles, a 44mm head tube, and two bottle mounts.

New for this second edition of the Electric Queen, All-City offers a segmented rigid fork to compliment the steel frame (included with the frameset purchase for $999). If you get a hardtail complete ($2399) and also want a rigid fork, no worries, painted to match forks will be available in both the cyan splatter and the original yellow/pink fade splatter. The new All-City Electric Queen is available now from retailers.
