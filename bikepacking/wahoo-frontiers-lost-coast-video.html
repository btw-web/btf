Wahoo Frontiers: Lost Coast (Video)

In the first episode of season two of the Wahoo Frontiers series, Ian Boswell, Pete Stetina, and Colin Strickland head out along California’s Lost Coast. The trio pedal out for an adventure turned training camp, leaving with fresh perspective and motivation for challenges ahead.

From Wahoo: “Last year we documented the journey of five athletes as they explore what the bike means to them and pushed on to explore what endurance sport can mean to us all. These athletes stand out because of their mindset, cycling can be more than just simply pushing out the watts and can also be as simple as throwing your leg over the top tube and riding off for the pure pleasure of it. Setting big goals is something that elite athletes do but is not unique to just them. This is Frontiers, the challenge we all set for ourselves is our own journey it is our own personal Frontier.”
