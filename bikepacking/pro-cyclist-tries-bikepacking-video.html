When A Pro Cyclist Tries Bikepacking (video)

As a pro cyclist, James Lowsley-Williams has always been coddled and catered to during races. But with a couple of big multi-day bikepacking events slated on his 2020 schedule he needed to learn the essentials of self-supported bike travel. In GCN’s latest video, around the world record holder Jenny Graham took James out on an overnight trip to show him the ropes.
