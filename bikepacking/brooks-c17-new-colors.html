The Brooks C17 Saddle is Available in Three New Colors

With out-of-the-box comfort and a naturally flexible rubber construction, the Brooks Cambium C17 is a popular option among bikepackers all over, including many of us on the team here. Unlike their popular leather saddles, the Cambium saddles require no maintenance or tightening and aren’t affected by rain, making them a great option for those who ride no matter the weather. The original C17 features a reinforced nylon construction, steel rails, and anodized aluminum rivets. It’s 164mm wide, sitting right between the narrower C15 and wider C19, making it a good all-round saddle that fits lots of different body shapes.

Brooks just announced three new colors for those on the hunt for a new saddle to match their build. The C17 is now offered in eight colors: octane, mud, orange, black, brown, and the latest additions, navy blue, silver, and yellow.

The Brooks C17 saddle retails for $120 USD and can be purchased online through BrooksEngland.com or your local dealer.
