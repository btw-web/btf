Swift Industries x Kitsbow Mini Collaboration

Seattle-based Swift Industries and North Carolina-based Kitsbow have teamed up on a limited edition collaboration, consisting of an Anchor Hip Pack, the Icon wool flannel, and a Sidekick Stem Pouch. Each is 100% designed and built in the USA, blending Kitsbow’s “tech-forward design” with Swift’s more traditional adventure aesthetic! Find a quick breakdown on each item in the collection below.

The Swift x Kitsbow Anchor Hip Pack

The 2.5L capacity Anchor Hip Pack features an all-new design that comes in two colors and features an expanding external sleeve for some added carrying capacity. The Cargo Flap is accented with Kitsbow’s special Umatilla wool lining by Pendleton. There are extra attachment points for adding on a Sidekick Stem Pouch, which looks like an ideal spot for stashing an extra water bottle or some small snacks. The Anchor Hip Pack looks like a pretty clever design and is different from anything we’ve seen or used before.

  • Capacity: 2.5L
  • Material: X-Pac VX21
  • Place of Manufacture: Washington, USA
  • Price: $120 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: BuiltBySwift.com

The Swift x Kitsbow Icon Shirt

At the heart of the collection is Kitsbow’s beautiful Umatilla Wool shirt by Pendleton. Swift wove in some of their own DNA into their iconic design, focusing on details like the Umatilla colorway featuring sage green shoulder and elbow reinforcements, a custom cobranded embossed leather logo, and a subtle label proclaiming Northwest By Nature—a gesture to Swift’s home in Seattle, Washington.

The Swift x Kitsbow Icon Shirt will be the first women’s-fit Icon Shirt offered in classic Pendleton Umatilla wool! Swift sees this as an important nod to their female-identifying customers, an ode to their Woman-Owned Business, and a joyful celebration of the progress America just chose in the White House. Make sure to check out Cass’ review of the original Kitsbow Icon Shirt here.

  • Size Range: Women’s Small-XXL / Men’s Small-XL
  • Material: Pendleton Wool
  • Place of Manufacture: North Carolina, USA
  • Price: $220 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: BuiltBySwift.com

The Swift x Kitsbow Sidekick Stem Pouch

The Swift x Kitsbow Sidekick Stem Pouch is a cute little feed bag-style bag that is designed to used as an add-on to the Anchor Hip Pack or a standalone bag on your handlebar or front basket. It features a single-hand closure, drain hole, several different MOLLE attachment points, and is available in navy or dark green X-Pac VX21.

  • Capacity: 1L
  • Material: X-Pac VX21
  • Place of Manufacture: Washington, USA
  • Price: $49.99 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: BuiltBySwift.com

Find the entire collection at BuiltBySwift.com.
