Last Call for The Bikepacking Journal 05!

Just a quick reminder that the deadline to join our Bikepacking Collective and have your membership kick off with Issue 05 of The Bikepacking Journal is today, October 17th, at 11:59 PM EDT. If you join after the cutoff, you’ll have to wait until spring 2021 to receive your first issue. We’re incredibly excited about the collection of stories in The Bikepacking Journal 05 and don’t want you to miss out!

Inside this issue you’ll find another 160 pages of the most engaging writing and inspiring photography from bikepacking trips all around the world. Our contributors bring us to Tanzania, Colombia, Sweden, Colorado, Mexico, and many other locations. Plus, we’re shipping the new issue with a sticker sheet featuring seven different stickers, including our new coyote and hare drivetrain animals.

Becoming a Bikepacking Collective member is the best way to support our work at BIKEPACKING.com, where we’re committed to sharing in-depth articles, inspiring stories, original routes, and news on a daily basis. In addition to receiving The Bikepacking Journal, joining also means you’ll be entered to win one of 40 prizes in our upcoming giveaway drawing, gain access to exclusive discounts from some of our favorite companies, and much more.
