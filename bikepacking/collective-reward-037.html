Collective Reward #037: Patches for all Members!

Our Bikepacking Collective membership program is now about a year old. While growth has been slow and steady, we can assure you that it’s been successful in helping support our efforts on the website, route creation, and The Bikepacking Journal. As a little thank you to all of our members, we’re including a new, limited edition patch to be packaged with issue 03 of the Bikepacking Journal, which is set to be printed in mid-October and shipped out at the beginning of November.

To all of our current and future members, thanks again for your support! We have other exciting members-only benefits, website functionality, and interesting news in the works; so stay tuned for those. And don’t forget to log into the Bikepacking Collective member portal to find current discounts and benefits; we’ll be adding more soon, too.

If you’re not yet a member of the Bikepacking Collective, sign up and you’ll get one of these limited edition patches, two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, and ongoing member-exclusive discounts from major brands. You’ll also be eligible for future Collective Rewards and merchandise.
