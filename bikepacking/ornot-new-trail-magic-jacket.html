Ornot Launches New Trail Magic Jacket

Based on their popular Magic Shell Jacket, the new Trail Magic Jacket from California’s Ornot (@ornotbike) features the first combination of Polartec NeoShell and removable Polartec Alpha Direct insulation that we’ve seen. The jacket’s outer shell is constructed from highly breathable, waterproof Polartec NeoShell fabric for a stretchy softshell that offers the water-shedding performance of a hardshell. Inside, there’s a removable Polartec Alpha Direct vest, which can be removed and worn as a layer of its own, used in tandem with the jacket, or removed entirely, making the Trail Magic Jacket quite a versatile three-in-one system that can be fine-tuned depending on the conditions.

Ornot Trail Magic Jacket Features

  • 99% windproof and waterproof
  • PFAS Free DWR coating + bluesign®-approved fabric
  • Extremely breathable microporous hydrophobic (waterproof) membrane
  • Taped seams + YKK 2-way double zipper
  • Durable and stretchy Polartec® NeoShell® fabric
  • Polartec® Alpha® Direct vest with integrated stuff sack
  • Active air exchange (.5cfm) significantly enhances breathability
  • 2 front pockets + 1 full width rear pocket
  • Adjustable hood fits over a helmet

The Ornot Trail Magic Jacket features a fit that’s optimized for riding, but they say it’s also loose enough for casual everyday use. It’s available in two colors (gray and black) and six sizes (XS to XXL) to fit a variety of riders. The Trail Magic is priced at $384, which includes a one-year warranty against all defects.

Find additional details about the new Trail Magic over at OrnotBike.com.
