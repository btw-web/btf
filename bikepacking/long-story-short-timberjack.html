Long Story Short on the New Salsa Timberjack

A lot of people talk about how new mountain bikes are becoming more “slack,” “steep,” and “progressive,” but not everyone describes those geometry buzzwords and how they affect the ride. Salsa design engineer Pete Hall sits down to talk about the new Timberjack’s geometry in terms that are easy to digest in their latest episode of Long Story Short.

There’s no rule that says bikepacking has to be bland, where you just have to grind out miles to get to the campground.
