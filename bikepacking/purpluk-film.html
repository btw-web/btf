Purpluk (film)

Artist and Salsa-sponsored rider Kim McNett is known for bringing a sketch pad and drawing tools on rides to document what she sees. “Visual journaling has become a critical component of my experiences in nature,” says Kim. In Salsa’s latest video short, our friend Bjorn Olson created this little film following along as Kim sets out on a fall ride to capture the MukLuk in the landscape…

Find more from Kim and the Seabird Memorial art installation here, and find an incredible collection of Bjorn’s films and writing here.
