Updated SimWorks Chromoly Bars

SimWorks’ updated chromoly bar rollout has been in the works for some time, and they’re finally up on their web store. The latest iteration of their chromoly steel handlebars showcases what we can expect from SimWorks for the foreseeable future, with a new look that’s right on par with the timeless aesthetic they’ve developed over the years.

Here’s what’s changing:

  • All chromoly bars are now available in a chrome-plated finish.
  • Black handlebars are only available in the Gataround and Little Nick bar.
  • After the first batch of black bars, they will no longer be producing black bars for the foreseeable future.
  • After the first batch, they will no longer be offering the Fun 3 Bar.
  • SimWorks chromoly bars are no longer clear-coated. They now come with a full set of decals so that you can pick and apply your own.

Head over to Sim.Works.com to learn more.
