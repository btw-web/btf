Velo Orange Launches Polyvalent Low Kicker

The Velo Orange Polyvalent is their most versatile frameset—perfect for touring, commuting, big day rides, and rough gravel roads. The Polyvalent Low Kicker builds off of the same platform, but features a ultra-low top tube to make mounting and dismounting a breeze and is useful for riders with limited flexibility. Finding a modern, yet beautiful, bike with a dropped top tube isn’t an easy task, so the Polyvalent Low Kicker is actually quite unique.

In true Polyvalent fashion, the frame is kitted out with rack and fender mounts, several bottle mounts, and clearance for 650b x 2.1” or 26 x 2.3″ tires. It comes in four sizes (Small – XL), a Sage Metallic paint job, and retails for $795 USD. Find additional specs and the geometry chart below.

  • Frame 4130 double butted chromoly steel
  • Fork 1 1/8″ threadless, 4130 chromoly steel with elegant bend
  • Wheel Size 650B or 26″
  • Tire Clearance 650B x 48mm, 26 x 2.3″ (either with fenders) / 650b x 2.1 or 26 x 2.3 knobby
  • Rear Spacing 12 x 142mm, thru-axle with replaceable hanger
  • Front Spacing 12 x 100mm, thru-axle
  • Bottom Bracket English threaded 68mm
  • Brakes IS mount disc
  • Seatpost 27.2mm
  • Bottle Bosses Triple on top of downtube, one set on underside of downtube

Learn more at Velo-Orange.com.
