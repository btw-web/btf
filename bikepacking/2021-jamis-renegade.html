2021 Jamis Renegade Range Updated

Jamis just rolled out their entire 2021 Renegade gravel bike lineup, which includes two carbon, three steel, and an aluminum model that come in at several different price points. Although not much has changed with the higher-end carbon Renegade C1 and C2, the steel models now all sport top tube mounts and triple pack mounts on the fork legs, and they all receive new paint and build kits. Find highlights and photos of each below.

2021 Jamis Renegade C1

The top end 2021 Jamis Renegade C1 is still built around Shimano’s GRX RX810 drivetrain and Ritchey components, but now comes with Stans Crest MK3 PRO wheels set up with WTB Riddler 700 x 37mm tires. It appears the frame itself remains unchanged, still sporting 12mm thru-axles, a nice selection of mounts, dropper post compatibility, an Enduro EVO PressFit 30 bottom bracket.

  • Shimano GRX 8100 2×11 Drivetrain
  • Shimano GRX BR-RX810 hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • Stans Crest MK3 PRO Wheels
  • WTB Riddler Tubeless 700 x 37mm Tires
  • 12mm Thru-Axles
  • Carbon Frame and Fork

The Renegade C1 comes in six sizes (48, 51, 54, 56, 58, 61cm), the single Navy Pearl colour, and weighs 19 pounds. It has a MSRP of $3,699.95 USD. More at JamisBikes.com

2021 Jamis Renegade C2

Like the C1, the 2021 Renegade C2 gets a new build kit and paint job, priced slightly lower due to more affordable drivetrain components, brakes, and wheels. See the full build kit below.

  • Shimano GRX 8100 /600 2×11 Drivetrain
  • Shimano GRX BR-RX400 hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • STANS CREST S1 Wheels
  • WTB Riddler Tubeless 700 x 37mm Tires
  • 12mm Thru-Axles
  • Carbon Frame and Fork

The Renegade C2 comes in six sizes (48, 51, 54, 56, 58, 61cm), the single Sandstone colour, and weighs 21 pounds. It has a MSRP of $2,599.95 USD. More at JamisBikes.com

2021 Jamis Renegade S2

The 2021 Jamis Renegade S2 is the highest-end steel model, made from Reynolds 631 Chromoly Steel, complete with rack and fender mounts, multiple bottle mounts, top tube bosses and triple pack mounts on the fork legs. It’s built up with a Shimano GRX 1×11 drivetrain, Stans Crest wheels, and WTB Raddler 700 x 40mm tires. The smallest two frame sizes come with 650b wheels, while the larger five sizes comes with 700c.

  • Shimano GRX 8100 /600 1×11 Drivetrain
  • Shimano GRX BR-RX400 hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • STANS CREST S1 Wheels
  • WTB Raddler Tubeless 700 x 40mm Tires
  • 12mm Thru-Axles
  • Chromoly Steel Frame and Carbon Adventure Fork

The Renegade S2 comes in seven sizes (44, 48, 51, 54, 56, 58, 61cm), the single Storm Grey colour, and weighs 23.5 pounds. It has a MSRP of $2,099.95 USD. More at JamisBikes.com

2021 Jamis Renegade S3

The 2021 Jamis Renegade S3 receives all the same updates as the S2, but is now based around a GRX 400 2×10 drivetrain, WTB ST i23 TCS 2.0 wheels, and wire bead WTB Riddler Comp 700 x 37mm tires or 650b x 40mm WTB Byways for the smaller sizes. The S3 is made from a more affordable Reynolds 520 Chromoly Steel tubing as well.

  • Shimano GRX 400 2×10 Drivetrain
  • Shimano GRX BR-RX400 hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • WTB ST i23 TCS 2.0 Wheels
  • WTB Riddler Comp 700 x 37mm Tires / WTB Byway 650b x40
  • 12mm Thru-Axles
  • Chromoly Steel Frame and Carbon Adventure Fork

The Renegade S3 comes in seven sizes (44, 48, 51, 54, 56, 58, 61cm), the single Mash colour, and weighs 24.25 pounds. It has a MSRP of $1,649.95 USD. More at JamisBikes.com

2021 Jamis Renegade S4

The 2021 Jamis Renegade S4 is the most affordable steel Renegade available, and due to its SST Reynolds 520 Chromoly Steel frame, is priced just above $1,000 USD. Even at this price point, it still receives the same frame updates as the others, but is now based around a more entry level Shimano Sora drivetrain, Tektro mechanical brakes, and an overall more affordable component selection.

  • Shimano Sora 2×9 Drivetrain
  • Tektro Lyra mechanical Brakes
  • WTB STX i23 TCS 2.0 Wheels
  • WTB Riddler Comp 700 x 37mm Tires / WTB Byway 650b x40mm
  • 12mm Thru-Axles
  • Chromoly Steel Frame and Carbon Adventure Fork

The Renegade S4 comes in seven sizes (44, 48, 51, 54, 56, 58, 61cm), the single Riptide colour, and weighs 23 pounds. It has a MSRP of $1,299.95 USD. More at JamisBikes.com.

2021 Jamis Renegade A1

Last but not least, the 2021 Jamis Renegade A1 is the most affordable option in the Renegade gravel series. It’s built around a 6061 aluminum frame, aluminum Jamis Adventure Fork, and a Shimano Claris drivetrain. Although it’s specced with more affordable parts, it still has bottle mounts on the fork legs, top tube mounts, plus fender and rack compatibility.

  • Shimano Claris 2×8 Drivetrain
  • Tektro C310 mechanical Brakes
  • WTB STX i23 TCS Wheels
  • WTB Riddler Comp, 700 x 37mm Tires / WTB Byway 650b x40mm
  • Quick Release Axles
  • 6061 aluminum frame and fork

The Renegade A1 comes in seven sizes (44, 48, 51, 54, 56, 58, 61cm), the single Gloss Black colour, and weighs 24 pounds. It has a MSRP of $899 USD. More at JamisBikes.com
