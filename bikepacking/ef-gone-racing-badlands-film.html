EF Gone Racing: Badlands (Film)

Curious about what it takes to finish in first place at a the inaugural edition of a grueling 700km gravel event? EF Gone Racing: Badlands provides a personal look at pro cyclist Lachlan Morton’s experience during the event, which follows some of the most remote and challenging terrain Europe has to offer—complete with massive amounts of climbing, riding at night, and only 19 minutes of stopping during an incredible 42 hour and 30 minute effort. Watch the full film below.
