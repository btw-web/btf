It’s International Singlespeed Day… Go Vote!

Last year, Paul Price—the man, myth, and legend behind PAUL Components, and maker of the very first singlespeed mountain bike hub—and his friend Amanda declared November 2nd to be International Singlespeed Day (it also happens to both of their birthdays). Their charge? Go out and ride your singlespeed! Or if you don’t have one, ride your geared bike in just one gear. As it happens, it’s also Election Day here in the US. So, go ride that one gear to the polls and cast your vote for local government representatives. It’s never been more important.
