Collective Reward #022: Ruby Coffee Roasters Care Package

Coffee lovers, rejoice! The fine folks at Ruby Coffee Roasters are hooking one lucky Bikepacking Collective member up with a care package that includes two 12oz bags of their delicious colorful coffee, a camp mug, and two water bottles, all packed inside a limited edition tote bag.

Ruby was launched in 2013, when the first bags of coffee were roasted in founder Jared Linzmeier’s family garage in rural Wisconsin. They’ve since moved the operation to the bigger – but still very small – town of Nelsonville, with a population of just under 200 and tons of great outdoor access.

A lot has changed since the days of roasting in Jared’s garage, and Ruby has received plenty of accolades and awards in the past several years. As a former monthly coffee subscriber myself, I think the team at Ruby sources and roasts some of the best coffee beans out there, and I’m excited to be able to share this gem with someone in our community.

This Ruby Coffee Care Package Includes:

  • 2x 12oz bags of coffee, freshly roasted in Wisconsin.
  • 2x 26oz water bottles, made in the US by Specialized.
  • 1x 12oz camp mug, made by Miir.
  • 1x Colorful Coffees Tote made of 100% cotton and measuring 15″ x 16″.
