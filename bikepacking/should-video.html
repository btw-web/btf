Should (video)

For those unfamiliar, the Adventure Syndicate is a UK-based non-profit organization that aims to use the power of bikes and adventure to engage teenage girls in outdoor activity. In the Syndicate’s latest video, they get a perspective of what it means to be a female in the otherwise male-dominated world of sports and outdoor adventure. Watch it below.
