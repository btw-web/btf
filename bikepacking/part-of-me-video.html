Part of Me (Video)

Photos by Ashley Voykin and Graeme Meiklejohn

The latest video from Norco, Part of Me, shares the story of how Canadian mountain biker Stephanie Dupont battled her way back from a sudden Crohn’s Disease diagnosis and an ensuing initial bout with the chronic disease that saw her hospitalized and uncertain about the future. She shares how she first fell in love with mountain biking and how the supportive community of other women riders in the Vancouver area motivated and encouraged her to get back on the bike and doing what she enjoys most. Watch it below, then scroll on for a handful of images from beautiful British Columbia.

“What kept me going was the desire to get back out on my bike. I really wanted to keep going with the girls every week…” —Stephanie Dupont
