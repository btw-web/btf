Riding Fixed Up Mountains with Leo Rodgers

The 14th installment of “Riding Fixed, Up Mountains, With Pros” from State Bicycle Co. features cyclist Leo Rodgers—who lost his left leg in a 2007 motorcycle accident—sharing part of his story and his thoughts on a range of topics, from how he first got started cycling, to his new signature Crust Bikes frame, to the work he’s doing through his foundation, and a bit of everything in between. You can see the full 17-minute video below, along with a couple of behind-the-scenes images from this episode’s corresponding post over on the State Bicycle Co. blog.
