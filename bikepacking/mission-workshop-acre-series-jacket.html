Mission Workshop Releases Acre Series Jacket

Designed to perform well during fast and light adventures, the Acre Series Jacket from Mission Workshop is the latest addition to the brand’s collection of cycling-focused apparel and accessories. It’s constructed from a DWR-coated Pertex Quantum BLUESIGN certified fabric outer shell with 40 grams of PrimaLoft Gold insulation inside. The Acre Series Jacket features a dropped rear, two handwarmer pockets, two open inside pockets, a two-way YKK zipper, reflective details for added visibility, a large rear pocket, and a soft microfiber collar. It packs into its own front pocket for storage.

The Acre Series Jacket is available in four sizes (small, medium, large, extra large) and two colors (olive and charcoal). It’s made in Vietnam and retails for $235. Note that it has what Mission Workshop calls “performance fit,” and they recommend sizing up if you’re between sizes. Unfortunately, they didn’t specify weights or pack size, but we have a request out for more details.

Learn more about the new Acre Series Jacket over at MissionWorkshop.com. Through December 19th, you can get a 20% launch discount by using the code ASJ21 at checkout.
