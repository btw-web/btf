10 Bikepacking Tips for Short Winter Days (video)

As the winter Solstice draws near, days are getting shorter and colder here in the Northern Hemisphere. When it comes to bikepacking (unless you’re a night owl, or a Minnesotan) that usually means fewer hours to ride and more time at camp. It’s not all bleak and dark though. There are a few ways to keep things festive and make the most of your time at camp. In our latest YouTube video, Neil walks through 10 tips for bikepacking on short days.
