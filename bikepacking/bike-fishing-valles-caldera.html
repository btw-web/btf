Bike-Fishing Valles Caldera

Colt and Matt recently went bikepacking with fly rods in the Valles Caldera National Preserve outside of Sante Fe, New Mexico. In search of Rio Grande Cutthroat trout, the two camped just outside of the National Preserve and visited some hot springs along the way. If you’re interested in the route they were following be sure to check out our Valles Caldera Super Volcano Explorer weekend bikepacking ramble (a few shots from that route below).
