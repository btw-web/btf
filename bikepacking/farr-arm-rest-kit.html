New Farr Arm Rest Kit and Aero Storage Bag

After receiving requests for additional forearm support from users of their Carbon Aero Bolt-On, Farr listened, and they’ve just released the new Farr Arm Rest Kit. It replaces the lower mounts of the Aero Bolt-On to accept an adjustable L-bracket, which then mounts to two nylon injected arm rests. The clamps and brackets of the Arm Rest Kit are CNC machined from Alloy 6061, the pads (made by Profile Designs) use 10mm EVA foam, and the entire system adds just 265g to your cockpit. The kit is designed to fit 31.8mm bars and should work with most bar / stem configurations.

  • Weight: 265 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Taiwan
  • Price: $$79 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: RideFarr.com

Farr Aero Storage Bag

The Farr Aero Storage Bag is designed specifically for Farr’s Aero Gravel and Aero MTB Handlebars, filling the unused real estate at the front of the bar with a nifty little bag. The Aero Storage Bag is constructed from 500D Cordura, attaches to the bar via velcro straps, and has an interior mesh pocket to keep heavier items in place. The top cover is held in place by a bungee over the stem, and has an additional zippered pocket for smaller items.
