PEdALED Launches 2020 Men’s Mirai Collection

PEdALED’s Mirai Collection is designed specifically for warm weather and long days on the bike. As they describe it, “Whether heading off the grid for a multi-day bikepacking trip or pushing personal limits, the men’s Mirai cycling jersey, bib shorts, jacket, vest (or gilet) and cycling socks are the perfect all-around kit for a non-stop season of riding.” The entire kit uses a lightweight synthetic blend to help wick moisture and regulate body temperature, features a slim performance fit, and is available in five bold colours. Scroll down for some photos and a price breakdown of the entire collection.

PEdALED Mirai Collection Pricing

  • Mirai Jersey: 135€ (~$162 USD)
  • Mirai Bibshorts: 155€ (~$186 USD)
  • Mirai Jacket: 180€ (~$216 USD)
  • Mirai Vest: 130€ (~$156 USD)
  • Mirai Socks: 15€ (~$18 USD)
  • Mirai Bottle: 10€ (~$12 USD)
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Manufacturer’s Details: PEdALED.com

PEdALED will be releasing their women’s Mirai Collection in the next few weeks, so head over to PEdALED.com for more details and pricing.
