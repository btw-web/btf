Search and State Wool Overshirt

Search and State—makers of the Field Shirt and Field Shorts—just added a winter-ready Wool Overshirt to their collection. We’re big fans of SAS’s sustainably made in New York City apparel and this one looks quite nice, too. According to SAS, the Wool Overshirt is made from sustainably sourced, non-mulesed, and Responsible Certified Wool, and fits so you can layer underneath, wear it open, or button it up to the top and use it on the bike or off. The Overshirt also features locally-sourced corduroy facing in the collar, cuffs, and chest pocket, and sustainable coconut buttons…

The SAS Wool Overshirt comes in a Stone Grey plaid pattern in sizes small through 2XL for $185. Learn more over at SearchAndState.com.
