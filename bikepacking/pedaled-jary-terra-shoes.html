New PEdALED Jary Terra X2 Ergolace Shoes

PEdALED is no stranger to fun collaborations, and their latest is a new shoe made in tandem with Fizik, which looks to be well suited to rides that include hike-a-bikes and time spent riding off-road. The new PEdALED Jary Terra X2 Ergolace shoes use an ergonomic lace-up closure and minimal padding for a slender but tough shoe that’s built for long days on the bike in a variety of terrain.

The shoe features a PU-laminated toe cap, tear-resistant ripstop uppers, compressed EVA midsole, and Vibram Megagrip rubber tread. The Fizik X2 outsole is stiff enough to offer better pedalling efficiency while offering enough flex for off-the-bike comfort.

The PEdALED Jary Terra X2 Ergolace Shoes come in one exclusive burgundy colorway and in sizes 38-47 (including half sizes). They retail for $160 USD and are available now from Pedaled.com.
