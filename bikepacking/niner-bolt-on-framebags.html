Niner Bolt-On Framebags for RLT and MCR Gravel Bikes

Niner just released a line of bolt-on bags for their 2020 Niner RLT 9 RDO, RLT 9 Steel, RLT 9, and MCR 9 RDO gravel bikes. The line includes a form-fitting half-frame bag, top tube bag, and matching ride wallet. The frame bag is available in five sizes to fit all RLT frame sizes. It features a pliable internal spine to help maintain the bag’s shape. All of the bags are constructed from a water-resistant canvas with a sealed zipper and padded interior. Niner has put together a helpful sizing guide to ensure you get the right fit, check that out here.

The frame bag retails for $65, the top tube bag for $25, and the ride wallet is $35. All of the bags should be available any day now through NinerBikes.com or your local Niner dealer.
