Win Lachlan Morton’s Alt-Tour Bike

In case you missed it, Lachlan Morton tackled his own version of the Tour de France this summer, which gained a lot of attention from the media. Inspired by the inaugural 1903 edition, he rode every stage and every transfer unsupported, covering a distance of 5,510 kilometres (3,424 miles) with 65,500 meters (214,895 feet) of climbing in a remarkable 18 days. He also raised more than 521,000 Euros ($709,000) for World Bicycle Relief in the process.

In an effort to raise even more for World Bicycle Relief, Cannondale, EF Education-NIPPO, and Rapha are putting on a draw where one lucky winner will walk away with Lachlan’s personal team-issue SuperSix EVO bike. All money raised will go toward mobilizing children and communities in need across the world via World Bicycle Relief. The winner, and four others, will also receive an EF Education-NIPPO Pro Cycling 2021 jersey signed by Lachlan.
