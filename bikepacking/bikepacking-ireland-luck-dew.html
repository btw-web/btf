Bikepacking Ireland: The Luck of The Dew

For their latest installment of ‘The Dew Files’, Kona sent their intrepid wanderer to Ireland for a St. Patrick’s day exploit. Rather than letting Dr. Dew loose to partake in the revelry and libations in Dublin, Kona put him on a fully loaded Sutra LTD to explore the quiet and picturesque backdrop of the Emerald Isle far from the Blarney Stone kissing tourists. From the video footage and photos below, you’ll discover that Ireland truly delivers the right amount of ‘magic’ for a great bikepacking adventure.
