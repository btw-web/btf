Restrap × CHPT3 Announce New Devesa Bags

UK-based Restrap and CHPT3 just released a limited series of bags that are screen printed by hand and laser cut in-house with the unique CHPT3 Devesa pattern, a design based on the bark from the trees of the same name in Girona. The lineup includes the Canister Bag, Tool Roll, and classic Musette Bag. The Devesa pattern is screen printed in reflective ink on the Canister and the tool roll is etched with the ‘3’ pattern by precision laser. Watch the video of the bags being made below, then scroll down for details on each one…

Restrap × CHPT3 CANISTER BAG

Made from textured nylon and with a top YKK zip, the Canister Bag features elasticated side pockets and a barrel shape. The Canister Bag also has a Hypalon loop to attach a front light. Handmade in Restrap’s workshop in Yorkshire.

The Restrap × CHPT3 Canister Bag sells for £54.99 / €65.99 / $82.99. Details at Restrap.com.

Restrap × CHPT3 MUSETTE BAG

Made from waterproof PU coated Nylon and lined with 6oz waterproof coated nylon, the musette is designed with durability in mind. It features two snap buttons to keep contents inside and an adjustable shoulder strap. Handmade in Restrap’s workshop in Yorkshire.

The Restrap × CHPT3 Musette Bag sells for £29.99 / €35,99 / $44.99. Details at Restrap.com.

Restrap × CHPT3 TOOL ROLL

The tool roll contains four tool slots and an elastic strap for any size tube. The shape is laser cut with an engraved Devesa pattern on tough Hypalon material. It accommodates a multitool, tire levers, CO2, as well as a spare pouch for chain links and other small parts. It can also attach to your saddle rails with a magnetic strap.

The Restrap × CHPT3 Tool Roll sells for £39.99 / €47.99 / $59.99. Details at Restrap.com.
