Wild Aerial Shop Tour of Wolf Tooth and Otso Cycles (Video)

We’re big fans of Wolf Tooth’s line of made-in-USA accessories and components, and their sister company Otso Cycles’ interesting bikes. While we’ve toured their shop before, they just sent us this wild new aerial video that guides us through the life cycle of a Wolf Tooth ReMote Dropper Lever making its way from engineering to machining, lasering, assembly, shipping, and finally to its recipient. Along the way, you get to see the facility and their staff, and finish by checking out the Otso Cycles space.
