Collective Reward #104: Rockgeist BarJam Harness

Following Logan’s recent review of Rockgeist’s super stable and cleverly designed BarJam handlebar harness system (find it linked below), we’re giving one away to a randomly selected member of our Bikepacking Collective for Collective Reward #104, a roughly $200 value. Our lucky winner will be able to choose their harness color, strap length, and clamp size to suit their style and bike’s configuration.

Designed to withstand riding over even the roughest terrain, the complete Rockgeist BarJam Harness system combines two aluminum brackets, a carbon fiber bar, a Voile strap, an X-Pac harness, and a Dyneema drybag to create one of the most stable front roll setups on the market. It’s also fully waterproof and has the unique benefit of allowing cables to run unencumbered behind the harness—a common drawback of more conventional front rolls.

Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible. If you’re a regular reader of the site, please consider joining our Bikepacking Collective. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards (including a BarJam), receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to a bunch of exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.
