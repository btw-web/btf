Redshift Kitchen Sink Handlebar Now Available

Redshift’s Kitchen Sink Handlebar is what the Philadelphia-based company describes as their “take on the ultimate gravel handlebar setup.” The bars are available in 440, 470, and 500mm widths (measured at the hoods) and with or without an integrated aero loop. The Kitchen Sink bars have a 25° flare with 7° of backsweep, a 65-70mm reach, and a 20mm rise. Along with Redshift’s new Endurance Dropbar Grip System—one pair of wing-style grips that fit under the tape on top of the bars and another pair that fit at the ends of the drops—these bars offer a boatload of comfortable hand positions that might be of interest to Tour Dividers and other long-distance endurance cyclists.

  • Width At Hoods: 440mm, 470mm, 500mm
  • Total Width: 550mm, 580mm, 610mm
  • Rise: 20mm
  • Flare: 25°
  • Sweep: 7°
  • Drop: 110mm
  • Reach: 70mm (44), 67mm (47), 65mm (50)
  • Weight (base version): 362g (44), 373g (47), 383g (50)
  • Weight (loop version): 472g (44), 486g (47), 497g (50)
  • Material: 6061 T6 Aluminum
  • Price: $99 – $119 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: RedshiftSports.com

Right now, it looks like their optional grips are out of stock, but the bars and their extra long bar tape should be available to order immediately. Learn more at RedshiftSports.com or give your local dealer a call to check on availability.
