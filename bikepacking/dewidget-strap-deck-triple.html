DeWidget Strap Deck now available in three sizes

We already covered the diJon Bagworks / DeWidget Strap Deck in our Cargo Cage Gear Index, and I’ve since put it to use on a couple occasions. The size medium Strap Deck mounts to a standard pair of bottle cage bosses with two M5 bolts, or anywhere you can think of by using extra straps. With the right straps, it can carry a bottle, bag, or whatever cargo you might need to fit. Now it’s available in three sizes: medium, large, and triple, which mounts to a three-pack bottle boss configuration. Pick one up over at Backcountry.scot. They ship anywhere.
