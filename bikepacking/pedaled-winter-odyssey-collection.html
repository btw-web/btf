The New PEdALED Winter Odyssey Collection

With warmth, breathability, and waterproofness on the forefront, the PEdALED Winter Odyssey Collection is designed for riders participating in the toughest events or riding particularly challenging routes. The latest additions to the collection look pretty promising. The Winter Odyssey Collection includes a new jersey, bib tights, a waterproof jacket and pants, and an insulated jacket and pants. Check them all out below.

ODYSSEY LONG DISTANCE JERSEY

The Long Distance Jersey is a winter weight long sleeve jersey made from 80% polyamide and 20% elastan. It’s designed to carry loads of snacks with large rear pockets, a water-resistant side pocket, and a chest pocket for quick access items. The polyamide fibers are said to be highly breathable and moisture-wicking to better regulate body temperature. The Long Distance Jersey is currently only available in XS – XL in men’s sizing.

  • Sizing: XS-XL
  • Material: 80% polyamide, 20% elastan
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $216 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com

ODYSSEY LONG DISTANCE BIB TIGHTS

Offering many of the same features as the jersey, the Long Distance Bib Tights are made with a long-distance-specific chamois, multiple pockets, and reflective elements for riding at night. They are made from Power Wool by Polartec, which uses a next-to-skin merino wool layer and an outer synthetic layer for better durability.

  • Sizing: XS-XL
  • Material: 53% Nylon, 26% Elastan, 21% Wool
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: TBD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com

ODYSSEY WATERPROOF HOODED JACKET

The Odyssey Waterproof Hooded Jacket is 100% waterproof with taped seams, waterproof zippers, a helmet-friendly hood, and thumbholes at the cuffs. It can pack into its own pocket when not in use, and also has reflective details for added visibility. It is offered in two colours: dark grey and lime.

  • Sizing: XS-XXL
  • Material: 100% Nylon
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $288 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com

ODYSSEY WATERPROOF PANTS

Matching the jacket, the Odyssey Waterproof Pants have a slim fit, zippered ankles, and a versatile enough for on and off the bike. Like the jacket, they are 100% waterproof with taped seams, reflective details, and the ability to pack down into their own pocket. They are also offered in dark grey and lime.

  • Sizing: XS-XXL
  • Material: 100% Nylon
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $192 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com

ODYSSEY INSULATED JACKET

The Odyssey Insulated Jacket is a packable synthetic-filled jacket for when the temperature drops. The DWR-treated ripstop nylon shell can shed light rain, the two-way zipper allows for easy temperature control, and it can also be packed down into its own pocket. There are dual side pockets, reflective details, and a dropped tail design for better overall coverage. It is offered in dark grey and lime.

  • Sizing: XS-XXL
  • Material: 100% Nylon + 100 Polyester
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $264 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com

ODYSSEY INSULATED PANTS

There might not be anything cozier than some insulated pants, and although they might not be in everyone’s gear list, they actually make a lot of sense. The Odyssey Insulated Pants feature the same synthetic insulated and DWR-treated ripstop nylon construction as the jacket, while zippered ankle cuffs make them easy to put on and off without removing your shoes. As expected, they also pack down into their own pocket and have reflective details for better visibility at night.

  • Sizing: S-XXL
  • Material: 100% Nylon + 100 Polyester
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $168 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Pedaled.com
