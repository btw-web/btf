Updated Farr Arm Rest Kit v2

Last year, the team at Farr released a minimal forearm support kit for their popular Aero Bolt-On Extension, which they could hardly keep on the shelves despite its relatively expensive price tag (owing to its CNC construction and the need for an Aero Bolt-On in the first place). Farr’s been working on an updated version, and the Arm Rest Kit v2 is the result.

Instead of relying on their Aero Bolt-On Kit, the new Farr Arm Rest Kit simply clamps directly to any 31.8mm bar. It’s manufactured from a mix of forged and CNC machined alloy, and includes two custom 7mm EVA foam pads. While the kit can now be used on its own, it still has the ability to be paired with their Aero Bolt-On extension for a clean and minimal alternative riding position. They are currently open for pre-order and expect to start shipping in October.

  • Material: Forged and CNC Machined Alloy
  • Weight: 222 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Taiwan
  • Price: $115 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: RideFarr.com
