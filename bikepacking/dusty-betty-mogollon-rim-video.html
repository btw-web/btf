Dusty Betty on the Mogollon Rim (Video)

Tess Hume, host of the Dusty Betty YouTube channel, recently got out on a bikepacking trip with a group of friends to the Mogollon Rim in Arizona. She put together a great video of the trip, which is equally entertaining as it is informative. Check it out below.

This route takes you through the Coconino National Forest, into barbershop canyon, to the Mogollon Rim, and to Knoll Lake. It follows forest roads and is gravel bike and mountain bike friendly. We did the 56-mile route over a weekend at a very leisurely pace, which left us with plenty of downtime to enjoy the spectacular views and take relaxing breaks.
