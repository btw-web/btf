The Dyed In The Wool Bar Roll Got a Makeover

The Dyed In The Wool Bar Roll just got a little makeover. Similar to many other burrito-style bags, the made-in-Poland DITW Bar Roll is a great little bag for urban exploring or day rides to carry essentials on your handlebars. The folks at DITW thought it would work perfectly as a small shoulder bag while off the bike, too, so they added two D-rings and straps to accomplish this. The bag also features two cam-locks for quick on-off access, and an elastic bungee to stash layers or other items.

Features include:

  • HDPE reinforcement
  • HD foam
  • Waterproof lining in hot pink
  • Key holder inside
  • Two side pockets
  • Adjustable shoulder strap
  • Front elastic bungee
  • Two cam locks
  • Water resistant Alpha zip
  • Color choice without any extra charges

The DITW Bar Roll is available in Cordura for $95, X-pac for $115, and Dyneema for $190. Lean more over at DyedInTheWool.eu.
