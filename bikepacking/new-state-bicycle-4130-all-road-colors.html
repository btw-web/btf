State Bicycle Company Introduces Two New 4130 All-Road Colors

State Bicycle Company just announced two fresh color options for their affordable and highly sought-after 4130 All-Road bike, which is now available in a total of four colors. Black Canyon and Copper Brown are the latest additions, joining the existing Sonoran Tan and Pigeon Grey that we introduced last year. Check those out here.

As always, buyers can choose between 700C or 650B wheels (or both for an extra $389.99), and the 4130 All-Road comes specced with a State-branded 11-speed drivetrain, Promax flat mount mechanical disc brakes, thru-axles, and a frame and fork with five sets of mounts. It’s available in four sizes (XS to L) to fit riders from 5’1″ to 6’5″ tall. The retail price for a complete bike is between $849.99 and $899.99, depending on color. Framesets are also available in both new colorways for $429.99.

Bikes are currently available in all four colors (though not in all sizes) and are expected to ship in early August. These sold out quickly last time, so we’d recommend moving swiftly if you’re in the market for an all-road rig and this one ticks all your boxes.

Learn more or order a complete bike or frameset over at StateBicycle.com.
