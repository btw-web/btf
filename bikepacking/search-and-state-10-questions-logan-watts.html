Search and State’s 10 Questions with Logan Watts

For their latest installment of “10 Questions With,” New York City-based apparel manufacturers Search and State sat down with BIKEPACKING.com founder Logan Watts. The wide-ranging interview hits on a number of topics, from how he first got started bikepacking, to how we develop new routes, to his thoughts on why bikepacking has exploded in popularity in recent years, and more.

You can find the full interview at SearchandState.com. If you want to see more from a few of the trips and locations Logan mentions in the interview, check out our route guides to the Trans-Cuba, Caucasus Crossing in Armenia, the Trans-Uganda, and the Prairie Breaks route in Montana. We’re also always seeking new submissions from your backyard to help grow the Local Overnighter Project, our catalog of quick one-night and weekend trips that leave right from your front door.
