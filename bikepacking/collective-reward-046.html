Collective Reward #046: AeroPress Go Coffee Brewers

Whether in the kitchen or at camp, the new AeroPress Go makes an incredible cup of coffee with a short brew time and super easy cleanup. After I grew to love this little brewer while using it for my full review, I’m excited that we’re able to give away three of them to our Bikepacking Collective members.

Recently launched as an even more travel friendly version of the original AeroPress—which was already a favorite among bikepackers—the AeroPress Go is a slightly smaller version that packs conveniently into its own mug and fits into a frame bag or handlebar bag with ease.

Out of the box, the AeroPress Go has everything you need to get brewing:

  • Brewer
  • 15oz mug with lid
  • Stirrer
  • Scoop
  • 350 paper filters
  • Filter case
  • Filter cap
