Wolf Tooth Components ReMote BarCentric Dropper Lever

The new ReMote BarCentric dropper post lever from Wolf Tooth Components is another clever component designed to make the most of valuable bar space when things get crowded. Those running a front derailleur or suspension lockout lever will appreciate the slender design of the ReMote BarCentric, as it will fit neatly next to your grips to avoid any interference with existing levers. The lever has four positions, uses a large sealed ball bearing, and is compatible with all cable actuated dropper posts. The ReMote BarCentric weighs 41g is machined from 6061-T6 aluminum, and is made in the USA.

The Wolf Tooth Components’ ReMote BarCentric is available now from WolfToothComponents.com for $74.95 USD, or head into your local dealer to order one!
