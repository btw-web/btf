Ergon’s Limited Edition Oil Slick Clamps

After seeing how popular their newly released GE1 EVO Factory grips with oil slick clamps were earlier this year, Ergon is back with a limited edition run of oil slick grip clamps for a number of their grips. The oil slick clamps come in two different clamp options, designed to fit the following grips: GE1 and GE1 Evo models, as well as GA2, GA2 Fat, GA3, GD1, GD1 Evo and GFR1 grips. By this point, you can probably cover your entire bike in oil slick components, if you wanted to get really flashy.

Ergon oil slick clamps retail for $19.95 USD and are available now in limited supply exclusively though the Canadian and US Ergon web stores. See more at ErgonBike.shop.
