Wolf Tooth Releases B-RAD Strap Base

The all-new Wolf Tooth B-RAD Strap Base includes two silicone-backed hook and loop straps and an aircraft-grade aluminum base with ED coated hardware. Like other boltless bottle cage mounts, the Strap Base system allows riders to add a bottle cage to forks or frames that are lacking bosses. The silicone-backed hook and loop straps are designed to offer a grippy, non-slip connection and can be installed or re-positioned as needed. In addition, the Strap Base alone can function as a mini B-RAD base, offsetting existing bottle cage bosses by up to 22mm (.85 in) to make room for half frame bags, shocks, or other accessories.

The entire B-RAD Strap Base system weighs 33 grams and is available now for $24.95. Find more over at WolfToothComponents.com.
