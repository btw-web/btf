Cane Creek Releases Parts in “Small Batch IPA” Color

As you might have gathered from our Appalachian Gravel Growler and Appalachian Beer Trail bikepacking routes, Western North Carolina is known for its rugged trails, seemingly endless gravel roads, and some of the best beer makers in the country. Based in Asheville NC, Cane Creek is in the thick of this regional beer culture, and to pay homage, they’re offering their own small batch series of products. Starting today, the HELM MKII fork, Hellbender 70 headset, Hellbender 70 bottom bracket, and crank preloader are all available in a deep amber color they’re calling Small Batch IPA. These products are available in extremely limited quantities and are only sold factory-direct.

Every order of a Cane Creek Small Batch IPA product will include a commemorative poster and orders over $200 will also include a pint glass featuring the same design. Like the beers they are named after, these small batch products are expected to go quick so visit canecreek.com and get your order in fast.
