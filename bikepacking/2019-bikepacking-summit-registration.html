Registration Open for 2019 Bikepacking Summit

Last year’s Bikepacking Summit, held in Gunnison, Colorado, was easily one of our favourite events of the year. Both Logan and Miles attended, and participated in several group rides and countless clinics. Everyone left feeling inspired and eager to get on their bikes. We put together a recap, highlighting the main takeaways, which you can read here.

For 2019, the Bikepacking Summit has moved eastward! This year’s summit will be hosted at the cycling oasis of Mulberry Gap Mountain Bike Getaway in Ellijay, Georgia, and will take place from October 4th-6th. The retreat is located in the foothills of the Southern Appalachian Mountains of northwest Georgia, home to IMBA Epic mountain bike trails, rustic cabins, and camping. Group rides will take place in the Pinhoti Trails, providing a great mix of singletrack and gravel to suit all kinds of riders.

Check out the sample itinerary below, and a few photos from this year’s venue. Keep in mind that this will likely see some changes leading up to the event…
