New Fizik Terra Atlas Shoes Announced

Fizik’s latest shoe, the Terra Atlas, was designed to balance comfort and performance wherever the path leads, from rolling gravel roads to techy singletrack. Based on their incredibly popular Terra X5, Fizik calls the Terra Atlas their “versatile and reliable all-rounder, for all-terrain gravel, mountain biking, and beyond.” It features a redesigned nylon outsole with a roomier overall fit, a simplified single L6 BOA closure, a hidden nylon footplate, chunky lugs, removable toe studs, and a mid-range stiffness index of 5. Fizik says it might be the most well-rounded shoe in their entire range, and it’s designed to spin efficiently and provide all-day comfort whether you’re on or off the bike.

The Terra Atlas has a claimed weight of 355 grams (per shoe) without the 20-gram footbed and is available in EU sizes 36 to 48 (with half sizes in 37 to 47). They’re offered in the four colors pictured here: Grey/Black, Pink/Grape, Black/Black, and Army. As for pricing, they come in at $159/£159/€159, which, if their performance matches their good looks, will make them a strong competitor for an all-road shoe at their price point.

Find complete details about the new Fizik Terra Atlas over at Fizik.com, and stay tuned for our upcoming first look.
