Spurcycle Launches Compact Bell for Flat Bars and Singletrack

The Spurcycle Compact Bell builds on the success of their original “precision bike bell” that was Kickstarted in 2013. The original bell remains in the line up, but the new Compact Bell has been tweaked with a 50% slimmer mount and a tidy and trim lever that Spurcycle claims to be more interface-friendly with various brake, shifter, and dropper lever combos.

The new Compact Bell features a brass alloy dome (with black DLC coating), a composite nylon lever, and mounts via a fixed 22.2mm diameter clamp. According to Spurcycle, fewer parts and less complexity allows the Spurcycle Compact Bell to ring in $20 cheaper than the original black bell.

  • Clamp Diameter 22.2mm
  • Price $39
  • Place of Manufacture Parts from Taiwan; assembled in Oregon
  • Manufacturer’s Details spurcycle.com

Spurcycle’s Compact Bell is available now at spurcycle.com. It will also be stocked and available through Spurcycle’s dealer network.
