Ride Buddies Episode 3: Rebecca Rusch and Meg Fisher

Rebecca Rusch and decorated paracyclist Megan Fisher are two incredible women who have spent their careers redefining what people are capable of. Together they share a collection of Paralympic medals, Guinness world book records, and even an Emmy Award. In the latest episode of Ride Buddies, Rebecca and Meg talk about overcoming obstacles, finding strength in the struggle, Betsy the wonder dog, winning races in order to eat, and crushing a male ego or two.
