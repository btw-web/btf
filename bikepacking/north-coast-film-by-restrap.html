NORTH COAST – A Cycling Film by Restrap

Five friends from Restrap, a Leeds-based bike bag company, had planned their annual cycling trip around the sunny coast of Croatia. As was the case for many of us, the pandemic derailed their itinerary. The team quickly pivoted and made last minute plans to go to Scotland to cycle the famous North Coast 500, a legendary road route along the rugged Scottish coastline and into the Highlands. They captured a lot of footage along the way, resulting a film that chronicles the reality of touring in a country fresh out of a pandemic lockdown and the importance of camaraderie in times of relentless weather and harsh conditions. Watch it below, then scroll down for a few photos from the trip and a map of their route…

“There’s something unique about exploring a place that feels like it doesn’t want you to be there.”

The Restrap crew followed a route based on the North Coast 500, a 516-mile scenic route around the north coast of Scotland, starting and ending at Inverness Castle.
