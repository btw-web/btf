The Nomad (Film)

In Blackburn Design’s latest film, filmmaker Brian Vernor (@vernor) sets out with Erick Cedeño, aka Bicycle Nomad (@bicycle_nomad), to tell his story of how traveling by bicycle has been so important to his mental and physical health and the wellbeing of those around him. The Nomad film has one goal: to help illustrate that everyone belongs out there.

The film is just one of many collaborations between Blackburn and Erick Cedeño since Erick came on as an ambassador back in October. They’ve partnered with a commitment to encourage inclusivity and better representation within the cycling industry, and we’re excited to see what they come up with next. Watch the film below, and then scroll down for more…

“I’m not from here. I’m not from there. I’m from everywhere. And I belong there.” —Erick Cedeño

You can also find a couple photos of the Bicycle Nomad Café in our Fool’s Loop bikepacking route. The café was the original start/end point of the route. Also, be sure to check out this short interview with Erick to learn more about his plans and role as a Blackburn Design Ambassador.
