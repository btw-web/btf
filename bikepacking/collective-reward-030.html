Collective Reward #030: Hand-Stitched “Pedal Further” Hats

We’ve loved the quirky hand-stitched hats made by artist Matty Waudby since we first saw them years ago, so we were thrilled when he brought up the idea of doing a few custom pieces for us. And for Collective Reward #030, three members of our Bikepacking Collective will receive a one of a kind hat featuring “Pedal Further,” the motto of The Bikepacking Journal, alongside original artwork by Matty.

Some words about the design from Matty: “I wanted to come up with something fun that was an interpretation of my idea of bikepacking. I figured wheelies are pretty great, and we all love mountains (especially the downhills!), and the rest fell into place. They still look a bit shiny and new for me and could do with some sun-bleached patina, but I’m sure the lucky winners can add that!”

Pedal Further Hat Details:

  • Sewn on an AScolour Finn 5-panel hat
  • One size fits most
  • 100% cotton
  • Low profile design
  • Gets better with age!

Not yet a supporter of the site? If you join our Bikepacking Collective by 11:59 pm EST on Wednesday, April 17th, you’ll be eligible to win one of these hand-stitched hats (along with several other Collective Rewards) AND you’ll receive the upcoming second issue of The Bikepacking Journal, our biannually printed publication. If you join after that deadline, your first issue will be Issue 03 in October. Already a member? You’re entered to win!

You can find more from our friend Matty on his photography portfolio, his Etsy shop, or on Instagram.
