Brooks Introduces a Trio of New Cambium Colors

Inspired by last year’s “Whatever the Road” project, Brooks England just announced three new C17 saddle colors informed by the hues of the landscapes along the Yorkshire coast, the American Southwest, and Dartmoor National Park. The three special-edition C17 Whatever the Road saddles released today are the C17 Devon (Sand top with Octane rivets), C17 Arizona (Mud Green top with Bronze-Orange rivets), and C17 Yorkshire (Navy top with Electric Blue rivets).

All the new models feature the same weatherproof, UV/abrasion-resistant vulcanized natural rubber top, tubular steel rails, and out-of-the-box comfort as previous Cambium saddles and are priced at $140/€130/£115, a small upcharge over the standard C17 models.

Find additional details or purchase one of the limited-edition C17s over at BrooksEngland.com.
