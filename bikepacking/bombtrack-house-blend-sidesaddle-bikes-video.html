Bombtrack House Blend: Sidesaddle Bikes (Video)

The year’s first installment of Bombtrack’s House Blend videos provides a mini-tour of Sidesaddle Bikes (@sidesaddlebikes) in Vancouver, British Columbia. It introduces some of the team, their thoughts on what makes the shop special, and what attracted them to Bombtrack’s bikes. The video also walks through the build of a custom Hook EXT designed specifically for Vancouver, featuring parts from local companies and a set and handmade bags from local maker EsotericFabworks (@esotericfabworks). Watch the video below, then scroll down to find a handful of images of the complete bike.
