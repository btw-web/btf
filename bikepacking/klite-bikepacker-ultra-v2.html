New Dynamo-Powered kLite Bikepacker Ultra V2 Announced

Although not everyone rides deep into the night, there are many who do, and they often require different gear to do so comfortably and safely. kLite’s latest dynamo-powered light, the Bikepacker Ultra V2, caters directly to serious ultra-endurance athletes who want the best and brightest light available. The new light builds on the kLite Bikepacker Ultra that was first announced in 2018, and still offers different beam patterns for mountain biking or road riding, a powerful stand light, and integrated wire looms for a truly plug-and-play setup. The new Ultra V2 also receives new dual-port USB chargers, is 25% smaller, and the entire device encased in rubber.

There’s a lot going on in kLite’s products, so here’s a quick breakdown on the new Ultra V2. Kerry also put together a product launch video that explains all of the updates in more detail. Check that out below.

  • Cree LEDs
  • 1300lm output
  • SquareTECH optics (made for trail riding not flashlights)
  • 3D printed case
  • 180deg WIDE FLOOD beam
  • Waterproof / Vibration proof (internally potted)
  • 135g / 4.7oz
  • Light can be run facing up or down, it does not make a difference to light pattern
  • Now with a smaller XT30 plug to fit the new Bar switch V2 w smaller XT30 plug

The new kLite Bikepacker Ultra V2 is slated to start shipping out later this month, alongside a variety of new mounting solutions. The Ultra V2 retails for $349 AUD and is available online from kLite.com.au or from your local dealer.
