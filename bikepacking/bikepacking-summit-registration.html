Registration Opens for 2nd Annual Bikepacking Summit

From today until September 7th, registration is open for the 2018 Bikepacking Summit to be held from September 14th – 16th in Gunnison, Colorado. It’s gearing up to be a pretty awesome event—don’t miss out! Check out the itinerary below and make your way over to the bikepackingsummit.com to get signed up.

We aim to create a passion-fueled weekend full of discussion, learning, and general bikepacking camaraderie in the cycling-friendly town of Gunnison, Colorado.

PRE-EVENT | THURSDAY SEPT. 13 – FRIDAY SEPT. 14

Overnighter at Hartman Rocks – (open to the public) 2:00pm

  • 2:00pm start
  • Gunnison > Hartman Rocks > Gunnison
  • 16 – 20 mile round trip
  • Gravel and mountain bike route options
  • Bring camping gear, cook gear, food and beverages
  • We will provide firewood
  • We are simply out to ride, laugh, sleep under the stars and enjoy each others company

  • Meet + Greet– 5:00pm

  • After Party in Downtown Gunnison – 8:30pm
