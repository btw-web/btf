Collective Reward #005: Lezyne Mega XL GPS, Pump, Lights, and More

To celebrate the launch of The Bikepacking Journal, our new printed publication, we’re giving away a prize package to one lucky Bikepacking Collective member for every week since it was announced. We’ll be drawing the five winners on July 26th and announcing them shortly thereafter. Not a member of the Bikepacking Collective yet? Get signed up before July 26th and you’ll automatically be entered to win – in addition to helping us grow BIKEPACKING.com and a whole host of other great benefits. Already a member? You’re all set. Plus, stay tuned for more rewards, as we’ll be announcing several more between now and the launch of the first issue of The Bikepacking Journal in October.

For our fifth Collective Reward, we’re offering up an assortment of gear from Lezyne. One lucky Bikepacking Collective member will get a brand new Mega XL GPS unit (not shown in the group shot), one Micro Floor Drive XL pump, a pair of rechargeable lights (the Lite Drive 700XL and rear KTV Drive Pro), a Power Cage (one of our favorite water bottle cages), a Lever Patch kit, and a Purist Baja Divide x BIKEPACKING.com water bottle.

Be sure to join the Bikepacking Collective by July 26th for your chance to win this prize package from Lezyne, along with the four other prize packages we’ve already announced from Porcelain Rocket, Search and State, Revelate Designs, and artist Alex Hotchin.
