Pass the Mic: Intersectional Advocacy in Cycling

Pass the Mic is an in-person panel discussion elevating marginalized voices in cycling at the Northwest Arkansas regional and national levels. The third instalment is presented by Experience Fayetteville and coincides with the 2022 Walmart UCI Cyclo-cross World Championships, and it’s being live streamed from The Garden Room in Fayetteville this Friday. We’re happy to see our friend Devin Cowens among the panelists, as well as All Bodies on Bikes co-founder Kailey Kornhauser. Learn more here.
