Sign up for the 2021 Restrap Solstice Century

Restrap’s Solstice Century challenge is back for a third edition! This year’s winter challenge will take place over the solstice weekend of December 18th and 19th, during which participants are challenged to make the most of the limited daylight by riding 100 miles in one shot or broken up into multiple rides. Your Solstice Century challenge might take the shape of a long day ride, a few sessions on the trainer, or a loaded bikepacking trip—whatever it takes to get in the miles.

All riders who complete the challenge will be able to redeem an exclusive Solstice Century patch for their efforts, and alongside the opportunity to earn a patch, all entrants will be submitted into Restrap’s prize draw. One lucky rider will receive a huge bundle worth over £500 and consisting of a gilet, long sleeve jersey, and bib tights from Albion, as well as a Restrap Bar Pack. The competition is open worldwide.

To Enter, sign up via the form at Restrap.com. You’ll receive an email on December 20th asking you to share a link to your completed Solstice Century ride via Strava, Komoot, RideWithGPS, or any other ride tracking platform.
