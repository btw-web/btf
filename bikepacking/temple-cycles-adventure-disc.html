Temple Cycles Adventure Disc 1 and 3 Announced

Temple Cycles first released their Adventure Disc platform back in 2018, designed as a reliable and versatile touring bike for exploring Great Britain’s pathways and beyond. The Bristol, UK-based bike brand just announced two brand new models, the Adventure Disc 1 and Adventure Disc 3, complete with updated specs and build kits, and offerring multiple bottle mounts, rack and fender mounts, and externally routed cables.

Watch Temples Cycles’ Adventure Disc overview video below, followed by photos and complete build kits for both of the new models.

Temple Cycles Adventure Disc 1

“A Swiss army knife. Timeless yet progressive. Years of testing and refinement. This bike is the only one you’ll need.” ​Matt – Temple Cycles Founder

The Adventure Disc 1 is Temple Cycles’ newest flagship adventure bike. Its Reynolds 725 steel frame comes equipped with Shimano’s top tier GRX 800 gravel groupset, complete with thru-axles and some nice parts from Hunt, Brooks, and Panaracer. The Adventure Disc 1 comes in three different colours: Slate Blue, Lichen Green, and Peat Grey. It is available in four sizes and starts at £2,595 (~$3,282 USD).

Build Kit

  • Frame TIG Welded Reynolds 725 Steel
  • Fork Lugged, Straight Leg with Flat Mount Brakes
  • Bottom Bracket Shimano Ultegra BSA Threaded 68mm
  • Crankset Shimano GRX RX-810 42T
  • Rear Derailleur Shimano GRX RX-812 11-Speed
  • Shifter Shimano GRX RX-810 STI
  • Cassette Shimano GRX M8000 11-42T
  • Chain Shimano GRX 11-Speed
  • Brakes Shimano GRX RX-810 Hydraulic
  • Rotors Tektro 160mm
  • Wheelset HUNT 4-Season
  • Tires Panaracer GravelKing SK 38mm, Tubeless Ready
  • Stem Temple Polished, 90mm
  • Seatpost Temple Alloy, 27.2mm
  • Saddle Brooks Cambium C17 All-Weather
  • Handlebars Temple AL-6061 Flared, 42cm
  • Grips Brooks Cambium Rubber Bar Wrap
  • Headset Temple Sealed 1 1/8th

To learn more about the Adventure Disc 1, head over to TempleCycles.co.uk.

Temple Cycles Adventure Disc 3

The Temple Cycles Adventure Disc 3 is designed to be a touch more versatile than the Adventure Disc 1, built up with a 3×9 drivetrain, TRP Spyre mechanical brakes, and more traditional gravel-touring style. It’s offered in all the same colours and sizes as the higher-end Adventure Disc 1, but is the more budget option at £1,495 (~$1,890 USD).

Build Kit

  • Frame TIG Welded Reynolds 725 Steel
  • Fork Lugged, Straight Leg with Flat Mount Brakes
  • Bottom Bracket Shimano UN25 Sealed
  • Crankset Shimano 105 R7000 50-34T
  • Rear Derailleur Shimano Deore GS 9-Speed
  • Front Derailleur Shimano Sora R3030
  • Shifter Shimano Sora R3030 STI
  • Cassette Shimano HG400 12-36T
  • Chain Shimano GHG53
  • Brakes TRP Spyre Mechanical
  • Rotors Tektro 160mm
  • Wheelset Shimano RX
  • Tires Schwalbe Road Cruiser 32mm
  • Stem Temple Polished, 90mm
  • Seatpost Temple Alloy, 27.2mm
  • Saddle Temple Bristol Saddle
  • Handlebars Temple AL-6061 Flared, 42cm
  • Grips Temple Leather Bar Wrap
  • Headset Temple Sealed 1 1/8th

Learn more at TempleCycles.co.uk.
