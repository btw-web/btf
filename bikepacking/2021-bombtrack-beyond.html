Announcing the 2021 Bombtrack Beyond Lineup

Photos by Jason Sellers

As part of Bombtrack’s 2021 bike rollout, the Beyond lineup has been simplified to include just three different models: the Beyond 1, Beyond 2, and Beyond Junior. Find a complete breakdown on new specs and build kits below, accompanied by product launch videos and photos.

2021 Bombtrack Beyond 1

The Beyond 1 is still based around a double butted Chromoly Steel frame, but has a completely revised design, including a new tapered head tube, rack and fender mounts, and a rear light mount. It comes complete with a 2×10 drivetrain, 27.5 x 2.0″ tires on x-small and small sizes and 29 x 2.0″ on medium and large. The Beyond 1 now comes with Bombtrack’s GRAD and ARC disc brake hubs with 15 and 12mm through axles, wider 23mm internal with rims, and WTB Ranger Light / Fast Rolling tires on all sizes.

  • BB Standard: BSA – 73mm shell width
  • Headset Standard: EC34 / 28.6 | EC44 / 40
  • Fork Max Tire Clearance: XS – S 27.5″ x 2.1″ / M – XL 29″ x 2.1″
  • Frame Max Tire Clearance: XS – S 27.5″ x 2.1″ / M – XL 29″ x 2.1″
  • Maximum chainring clearance (1x): 36T with 48mm chainline
  • Maximum chainring clearance (2x): 42 / 28T with 49.5mm chainline
  • Seat Post Diameter: 27.2mm
  • Brake mount front/rear: Post Mount / Flat Mount
  • Maximum rotor clearance front/rear: 203/180mm
  • Front through axle: 15mm x 100mm
  • Rear through axle: 12mm x 142mm
  • Crankarm Length: XS 165mm / S-M 170mm / L-XL 175mm
  • Spoke count front/rear: 32 / 32
  • Handlebar Width: XS-S 420mm / M-L 440mm / XL 460mm
  • Stem Length: XS-S 60mm / M-L 80mm / XL 90mm
  • Stem angle: 17°

It is still based around fast-rolling 29″ wheels, has loads of mounts, and wide handlebars. It now comes with a complete Supernova dynamo lighting system, including front and rear lights, plus a front rack.

2021 Bombtrack Beyond Junior

The 2021 Bombtrack Beyond Junior remains mostly unchanged, besides being specced with their new Bombtrack ARC disc brake hubs and a new paint job. The Beyond Junior is their child-specific Beyond model, based heavily off of the larger Beyond 1 sized bikes. It comes complete with 24-inch wheels, a light aluminium frame, and flared bars keep things manageable for smaller riders. A 9-speed MicroShift Advent drivetrain, with short-reach lever designed specifically for smaller hands, and clearance for 2.1″ tires, has the Beyond Junior looking pretty awesome for those 1.3 to 1.6 meters tall (4.25-5.35 feet), which might include some adults as well.

  • BB Standard: BSA – 73mm shell width
  • Headset Standard: EC34 / 28.6 | EC34 / 30
  • Fork Max Tire Clearance: 24 x 2.1″
  • Frame Max Tire Clearance: 24 x 2.1″
  • Maximum 1x chainring clearance: 36 T with 48mm chainline
  • Maximum 2x chainring clearance: 42 / 28 T with 49.5mm chainline
  • Seat Post Diameter: 27.2mm
  • Brake mount front/rear: Post Mount / Flat Mount
  • Maximum rotor clearance front/rear: 180/160mm
  • Crankarm Length: 152mm
  • Spoke count front/rear: 32 / 32
  • Handlebar Width: 380mm
