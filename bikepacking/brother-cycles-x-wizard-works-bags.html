Brother Cycles × Wizard Works Bags

Brother Cycles just announced a final round of products in their collaboration with London-based bag maker, Wizard Works. The joint effort is based around Brother’s custom black on tan splatter pattern printed on a tough waterproof canvas. The fabric pattern was developed with Wizard Works and is exclusive to Brother Cycles. Find details on each bag below.

Brother Cycles × Wizard Works Go-Go Top Tube Bag

“Slim, sturdy and oh-so easy to access, Go-Go sits neatly atop your top tube like a little cyclist’s fuel tank; fit a perfect wedge of cheese or an expertly tailored sandwich, and with a port for cables, Go-Go’s great for gadgets too! While a staple of the bikepacking diet, Go-Go is an excellent bag for any ride no matter the distance.”

  • Constructed out of a tough water resistant canvas
  • Featuring the ‘Brother Cycles x Wizard Works’ collab label
  • YKK Aquaguard water-resistant zipper
  • Includes 3 x velcro securing straps and 1 x dob-bone spacer
  • Size: 21w x 7d x 11h cm

The Brother Cycles × Wizard Works Teeny Houdini is made in London and sells for £60. Learn more at BrotherCycles.com.

Brother Cycles × Wizard Works Teeny Houdini

“Teeny Houdini is an easy to install, minimal saddlebag designed to hold a gravel/mtb tube, tyre lever, multi tool, CO2 and even a couple of nice rocks you might pick up along the way. Why not free up space in your bar bag (for more snacks) by keeping your boring things in the rear, or set and forget so you’re never caught unprepared for a puncture (or snack) emergency again!”

  • Constructed out of a tough water resistant canvas
  • Large flap opening allows access whilst still attached to your bike
  • Fidlock magnetic buckle for easy opening and closing on the move

The Brother Cycles × Wizard Works Teeny Houdini is made in London and sells for £42. Learn more at BrotherCycles.com.
