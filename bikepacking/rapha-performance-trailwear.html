Rapha Launches Performance Trailwear MTB Collection

Rapha entered the world of mountain biking yesterday with their announcement of an entirely new range of men’s and women’s trailwear that features almost everything you need to ride your local trails, including jackets, technical t-shirts, pants, shorts, and accessories. They also shared a fresh roster of athletes they’ll be supporting and new commitments to advocacy initiatives. Find a quick look at everything below.

Rapha Performance Trailwear

Rapha describes the new line as “durable, repairable mountain biking apparel made with the most technical fabrics, designed to perform in all conditions.” The MTB collection includes five men’s pieces (a 3/4 sleeve jersey, shorts, bibs, a lightweight jacket, and a technical t-shirt), six women’s pieces (a 3/4 sleeve jersey, shorts, a liner short, a lightweight jacket, a technical t-shirt, and a trail tank), as well as a number of accessories (a collaborative Smith helmet, socks, glasses, and a hip pack). Nearly every piece in the new collection is available in a range of colors. A repair kit is included with most items, and the pieces in the new collection are eligible for free repairs for anything you can’t fix yourself.

TRAIL ADVOCACY AND STEWARDSHIP

Starting this year, Rapha will be launching a series of initiatives to support organizations around the United States and United Kingdom that work to increase access to mountain biking. In partnering with groups such as the Oregon Timber Trail (who work on the ground to build and maintain trails) and NICA (who works to educate the upcoming generation of mountain bikers), and by using their resources available through the Rapha Foundation to improve access for underrepresented communities, Rapha says it’s committed to giving back to the sport.

Head over to Rapha.cc to find complete details on the new Performance Trailwear line.
