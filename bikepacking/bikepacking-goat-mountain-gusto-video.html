Bikepacking Goat Mountain Gusto (Video)

On July 3rd of this year, Chris Wilson went on a ride based on the Goat Mountain Gusto overnighter route in Washington state. Equipped with a new Tenkara rod to try his luck at Deadman Lake, he set out for a solo overnighter. With the extreme heat in the Pacific Northwest, road closures forced Chris to reroute to the Green Creek Trailhead instead of the Goat Mountain Trailhead, but he was still able to ride most of the route.
