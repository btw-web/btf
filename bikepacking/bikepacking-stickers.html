New BIKEPACKING.com Skull Stickers!

If you’re a member of the Bikepacking Collective, you’ll be receiving the first issue of our bi-annually printed publication, The Bikepacking Journal, in your mailbox in October. Be sure to look inside for one of these new stickers, included as a little extra thank you for helping us grow the site and produce the journal.

And if you’re still on the fence and haven’t yet joined the Bikepacking Collective, you have just a couple weeks before the deadline to receive the inaugural issue (along with a bunch of other great benefits). When they’re gone, they’re gone!
