For Sale: Tour Divide Maps by Alex Hotchin

We commissioned Australian illustrator Alex Hotchin (@alex_hotchin) to create this beautiful hand drawn map of the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route to accompany Eddie Clark’s piece “Decade on the Divide” in the third issue of The Bikepacking Journal last fall. It measures an impressive 25cm x 80cm (9.8″ x 31.5″) and depicts several of the most memorable features along the route.

We included a free folded map with every copy of The Bikepacking Journal 03 we shipped to our Bikepacking Collective members, but we also asked the printer to keep 200 maps unfolded so they’d be more suitable for framing. Today, we’re selling off those remaining copies for $25 + $5 shipping, anywhere in the world. Note that we’re waiving the shipping fee for the Bikepacking Collective members (members, check your email for the discount code).
