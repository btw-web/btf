Check out the new Whisky No.9 CXLR Fork

Revealed this morning, the Whisky No.9 CXLR Fork is a sleek new thru-axle carbon fork designed with light-duty loaded bikepacking in mind. It features three-pack and mid-blade low-rider rack mounts on each side, fender compatibility, and internal routing for brake lines and dynamo wiring. It’ll clear 700C x 42mm or 650B x 50mm tires, has a 45mm rake, accepts a max rotor of 160mm, and is made for flat-mount disc brakes. Despite being a cargo-focused fork, the crown isn’t drilled for mounting a light or small rack.

The new No.9 CXLR Fork weighs in at 540 grams and is priced at $550. You can find more details and images over at WhiskyParts.com.
