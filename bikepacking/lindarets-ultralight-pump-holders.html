New Lindarets Ultralight Pump Holders

We’re seeing more and more stock bikes being plastered with extra bosses, which can be really handy for hauling extra gear, but we’d be lying if they sometime go unused. Marc Basiliere over at Lindarets (the one behind my beloved Titanium Valve Stems and many popular Wolftooth Components designs) just announced a slick little 3D printed mount that can be used to hold a pump or similarly shaped object without adding much weight or bulk to your frame. The Ultralight Pump Holders consist of two laser-sintered nylon bases with 75mm silicone-backed cinch straps that bolt on directly to your frame.

They are designed to hold pump-shaped objects (think frame pumps, EDC pumps, tent pole bundles and some Tenkara rods) between 25 and 35mm in diameter. The holders are rated for up to 400g per pair, which should be more than enough for most hand pumps, and weigh just 12g per pair. They come with M5 button-head stainless steel screws and are specifically designed to be used in pairs, so don’t think about running one solo.

  • Material: laser-sintered nylon / velcro straps
  • Weight: 12 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Bases are made in USA
  • Price: $14.95 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Lindarets.com
