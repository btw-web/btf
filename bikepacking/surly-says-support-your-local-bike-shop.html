Surly Says Support Your Local Bike Shop, if You Can

There’s no doubt that all of us are feeling the effects of COVID-19 at this point. Some of the harshest repercussions are impacting small businesses, many of which have already been devastated by the aftermath of forced closures and restrictions. While not yet as widely affected as the restaurant industry, many local bike shops are being closed, limiting services, or simply feeling the pinch from a lack of customers. In this message sent out by Surly Bikes, they remind us to not forget our local bike shop…
