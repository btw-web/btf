Rapha Announces 2021 Women’s 100

Mark your calendars for Sunday, September 12th, when the ninth annual Rapha Women’s 100 will take place. Whether you’re a seasoned rider or just getting started, Rapha is challenging cyclists worldwide to ride 100 kilometers in whatever way suits them, and to “celebrate what you and the women around you can achieve.” Road ride? Gravel grind? Bikepacking overighter? There’s no right or wrong way to ride your 100.

This year’s theme is “Everyday Trailblazers.” As Rapha puts it, “Every rider who sets themselves a new goal or pushes themselves to try something different is blazing a trail. Whether it’s cycling to the office or across a continent, joining a group ride or leading one, every act of bravery on the bike, big or small, is worthy of celebrating.”

Sign up on Strava

You can join the thousands of riders around the world who are taking part in this challenge by getting signed up for the Women’s 100 on Strava. Every rider who completes a 100-kilometer ride on the 12th and shares it on Strava will be eligible to claim a special reward. A sign-up will be released on Rapha’s site soon.

Join a Rapha Group Ride

Setting out from Rapha clubhouses and partner cafes, there will be a calendar of training rides ahead of the challenge, as well as several local group rides rolling out on the 12th of September. As with the Strava challenge, more details on participating shops and a sign-up will be available via Rapha shortly.

Find additional details and check out the new Women’s 100 Collection at Rapha.cc.
