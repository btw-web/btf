The PEdALED Fall Jary Collection Gets a Long-sleeve Merino Jersey

With cooler weather on the way (or already here for some of us), PEdALED has expanded their Jary All-Road Collection with some fresh options for fall, including a new Merino Long Sleeve Jersey and Padded Tights, along with a number of returning pieces, including their Merino Hooded Jersey, All-Road Cycling Shorts, and Jary Terra X2 Ergolace Gravel Shoes. Watch their launch video and find some details on what’s new below.

Jary Merino Long Sleeve Jersey

The Merino Long Sleeve Jersey is made from a merino-blend fabric called Dryarn (40% Wool, 35% Polyamide, 25% Polypropylene), making it fast drying, odour resistant, and temperature regulating. It features a casual fit that’s looser than traditional cycling jerseys and looks like it would make a good merino layer for bikepacking. It’s offered in three different colors: Raven (grey), Burgundy, and Forest Green.

  • Material: 40% Wool, 35% Polyamide, 25% Polypropylene
  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €130 ($160 USD)

Jary All-Road Padded Tights

The All-Road Padded Tights are designed to be layered underneath your favourite pair of riding shorts. They are insulating and windproof, and if you can believe it, feature the first gravel-specific chamois from Elastic Interface. They have reflective detailing on the lower legs, use a four-way stretch fabric, and are only offered in black.

  • Material: 85% Polyamide, 15% Elastane
  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €140 ($180 USD)
