Search and State S1-J Jacket now in Utility Green

The made-in-NYC S1-J riding jacket is one of Search and State’s best-selling pieces, and for good reason. This lightweight jacket is minimal and packs small, and it’s also highly wind and weather proof. Features include Schoeller C-Change Fabric, touted for its breathable, high-end Riri zippers, and a military-grade injection-molded aquazip at the pocket.

As of today at 11AM EST, the S1-J will be available in Utility Green, which looks really sharp. At $275, the S1-J isn’t cheap, but like other SAS products, they’re hand-stitched in New York from high quality and thoughtfully sourced materials. And, they are built to last. If you’re on SAS’ mailing list, you also get a 20% discount code. Learn more over at SearchAndState.com.
