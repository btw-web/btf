Collective Reward #017: Porcelain Rocket Dumpling

Hip packs make a great option for carrying a camera, phone, or other essentials while trail riding and bikepacking. We’re reviewing several as we speak, actually, and we love this one. Alberta-based bikepacking bag maker Porcelain Rocket recently released one of the only waterproof hip packs out on the market, the Dumpling. One lucky Bikepacking Collective member will win one of their own. We’ll draw the winner on December 31st, so if you’re not already a member of our Bikepacking Collective, get signed up for your chance to win a Dumpling and many other great prizes. In the meantime, here are a few pics and specs.

  • Roll-top closure
  • Seam-welded, weatherproof construction
  • Bungee closure doubles as stash point for layers
  • Internal phone/wallet sleeve
  • 3L max capacity
  • MOLLE webbing on waist wings
  • Only available in LightBlack™️ (for now)
  • Foam-padded in back/hip panels
  • 220g complete
  • $140CAD (~$107USD)
