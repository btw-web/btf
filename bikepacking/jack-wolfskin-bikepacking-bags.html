New Jack Wolfskin Bikepacking Bag Collection

Germany’s Jack Wolfskin just entered the bikepacking market with the introduction of a full suite of five new water-resistant bags designed with multi-day touring in mind. The whole collection offers a combined total of 40 liters of storage, and all of the bags are made from durable 100% recycled polyester with reflective details built in.

Included in Jack Wolfskin’s debut bikepacking bag collection are the 22-liter Rack Bag pannier with roll-top close; 4-liter Handlebar Bag 2 in 1, which doubles as a hip pack or handlebar bag; 10-liter Seat Bag, which features an outer tent pole holder and an elastic bungee for lashing on quick-access items; 0.8-liter Top Tube Bag with a transparent top window; and and 3.5-liter Takeoff Bag, a dual-access partial frame bag.

The new bags range in price from €39.95 up to €99.95 and are available now. You can find details on all five bags, as well as the new bikewear collection that was also launched today, by visiting Jack-Wolfskin.com.
