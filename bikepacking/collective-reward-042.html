Collective Reward #042: Wolf Tooth Morse Cage and B-RAD Roll-Top Bag

Our latest Collective Reward comes courtesy of Minneapolis-based Wolf Tooth Components, makers of a wide array of practical and ingenious bike bits. For Collective Reward #042, three lucky members of our Bikepacking Collective will win a stainless steel Wolf Tooth Morse Cage and a B-RAD Roll-Top Bag.

Wolf Tooth Morse Cage (Stainless Steel)

Designed collaboratively by King Cage and Wolf Tooth Components, the Morse Cage is a lightweight, low profile bottle cage with a clever design that boasts loads of mounting options. Earning its name from its dot-dash-dot-dash mounting pattern, the Morse Cage offers four positions across 32mm of vertical adjustment, fully capturing one mounting bolt in every position to prevent movement. Built by hand by King Cage in Durango, Colorado, the Morse Cage is a seriously high quality product. We love ’em and even gave the Morse Cage an award last year. Best of all, they’ll never mar your bottles and can be bent back into shape if damaged.

Wolf Tooth B-RAD Roll-Top Bag

The B-RAD Roll-Top bag is Wolf Tooth’s do-it-all storage option. It comes in a versatile 1.1L size (as well as a 0.6L Mini version) that’s large enough to store a jacket or complete repair kit, yet small enough to easily tuck out of the way in a variety of locations on the bike. Pair it with your existing B-RAD mounting system using the optional base plate or just strap it anywhere on your rig with the silicone-backed strap. The B-RAD Roll-Top bag is built from a durable abrasion-resistant PVC backed 420D ripstop nylon with a beefy and secure buckle closure.
