The New Sonder Signal ST is Steel and Rowdy

Previously only available in titanium, the new Sonder Signal ST sees a fresh new 4130 Chromoly Steel frame as well as three new build kits ranging from $1,605 to $2,810 USD. Although the Signal ST isn’t outfitted with additional bosses or rack mounts, it looks like a fun bike for those on the hunt for a nearly indestructible hardtail for exploring mountain passes and singletrack exploration. The Signal ST is designed for 29″ wheels, with clearance for up to 29 x 2.6″ tires, uses BOOST hub spacing, a 73mm threaded bottom bracket, external cable routing, and internal dropping post routing. The frame is optimized for 130mm travel forks and Sonder claims the 66° head tube and 74° seat tube angle “give you the confidence to bomb the descents and while still being able to climb like a mountain goat.”

The Sonder Signal ST is available in three different complete builds, based around SX, NX, and GX Eagle drivetrains with varying levels of components to offer three different price points. See the complete build kit for the mid-level Sonder Signal ST NX Eagle build below.
