Firefly Bikepacking Hardtail at Enve’s Builder Round-Up

Enve is hosting its annual Builder Round-Up, featuring several frame builders and bikes in a virtual format. Boston, MA-based Firefly Bicycles built this particular bikepacking-specific hardtail frame for one of their customers prior to the event. The frame offers plenty of versatility with threaded bosses, long-travel internal dropper compatibility, proprietary dropouts, an Enve BOOST MTN fork, and clearance for up to 29 x 2.6″ tires. In fact, it just finished a three-day bikepacking trip, right before getting cleaned up for the show.
