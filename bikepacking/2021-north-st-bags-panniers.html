Three New Panniers from North St. Bags

North St. Bags specializes in bike bags and accessories that are geared toward the everyday cyclist, commuter, and bikepacker. Over the last few years, they’ve expanded their bag lineup to include lots of great panniers, backpacks, and accessory bags, many of which are being made using 100% recycled polyester sailcloth and manufactured in Portland, Oregon. North St. Bags just added three new pannier options to their inventory, which you can check out below.

North St. Bags Venture Pannier

The Venture Pannier is a large-volume (30L) pannier that features a waterproof recycled EcoPak sailcloth construction, a roll-top/flap main top closure, an easy-access zippered top pocket, and two deep pocket on either side that can fit 3.25″ diameter water bottles. It uses a simple, serviceable hook and bungee attachment that is compatible with most racks out there, but North St. offers modifications if it won’t work with whatever rack you’re using. Currently, the Venture is offered in six different colours and costs $180 USD.

  • Capacity: 30L
  • Material: EcoPak
  • Weight: 997 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Oregon, USA
  • Price: $180 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: NorthStBags.com

North St. Bags Route Pannier

Although the Route Pannier isn’t new for North St. Bags, it did receive a redesign that includes an EcoPak sailcloth liner and 1000D Cordura exterior fabric, a roll-top closure, two deep side pockets, and reflective detailing. The Route Pannier holds 24L, is offered in six colours, and costs $150 USD.

  • Capacity: 24L
  • Material: Cordura / EcoPak
  • Weight: 725 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Oregon, USA
  • Price: $150 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: NorthStBags.com

North St. Bags Macro Pannier

Designed as a larger alternative to our personal favourite, the Micro Pannier, the Macro Pannier is a lightweight, simple option that’s the perfect addition to your bikepacking setup when a little more storage is required. The Macro Panniers hold 21L (compared to the 14L Micro) and they’re now made with 100% recycled EcoPak fabric. They feature a roll-top closure, a single zippered exterior pocket, a hook and bungee attachment system, and are offered in eight different colours.

  • Capacity: 21L
  • Material: EcoPak
  • Weight: 408 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Oregon, USA
  • Price: $130 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: NorthStBags.com
