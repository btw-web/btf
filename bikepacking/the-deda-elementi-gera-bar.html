The Deda Elementi Gera Bar Looks Funky

Italian brand Deda claims the Gera is all about comfort for long gravel rides and ultra-endurance bikepacking. And believe it or not, the Deda Elementi Gera Bar is made from 6061 aluminium. Its complex shape is made possible via a mechanical forming process. The handlebar features a 10° backsweep angle and 5° downsweep all in the name of comfort for long distance bikepacking. It also has a 10mm rise, 24° of flare, and 3° outsweep at the drops for added stability on challenging terrain. Additional features include a 55mm reach, 100mm compact drop, and 90mm wide center area to install bags and accessories. Deda Elementi Gera Bar is available in 42, 44, 46 and 48mm widths. Find specs and pics below.

  • Shape: EOS (Endurance Optimized Shape)
  • Material: 6061 aluminium, double butted
  • Diameter handlebar: 31.7 mm
  • Reach: 55 mm
  • Drop: 100 mm
  • Flare: 24 degrees
  • Outsweep: 3 degrees
  • Rise: 10° / 10 mm
  • Backsweep: 12° / 12mm
  • Downsweep: 5°
  • Width: 42 – 44 – 46 – 48 cm (outside-to-outside at the lever position)
  • Full width: 52 – 54 – 56 – 58 cm
  • Weight: 310 g (42cm)
  • Finish: POB (Polish on black)

Although it’s currently unavailable, the Deda Elementi Gera Bar will retail for £123.99. Find details at dedaelementi.com.
