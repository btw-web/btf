Titanium mini-pump, anyone?

Anyone looking for an ultra-lightweight, ultra-high-end, made-in-the-USA, titanium pump? Boston’s Firefly Bicycles just released the new Firefly Titanium Pocket Pump featuring a fully butted titanium construction, premium leather piston gasket, brass check-valve, and pump internals sourced directly from the Silca Pocket Imperio Pump. The pump works with presta valves only and comes in a brushed or blasted finish, or an “Ultra-high Voltage” anodized fade and tips the scales at 135 grams… “because why not?”

  • Length: 8″ (20.3cm)
  • Weight: 135 grams
  • Max Pressure: 120psi maximum pressure
  • Price (Blasted or Brushed finish): $220
  • Price (Ultra-high voltage anodized fade): $300
  • Place of Manufacture: Massachusetts, USA
  • Availability: Made-to-order; lead times are 2-3 weeks
  • Manufacturer’s Details: FireflyBicycles.com
