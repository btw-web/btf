Bombtrack House Blend: Citybiker

Bombtrack Bicycle Co. heads to Vienna, Austria, for the latest video in their ongoing House Blend series, where they meet the crew at Citybiker (@citybiker.at), a small shop in the heart of the capital that specializes in touring, gravel, and commuter bikes. The eight-person team at Citybiker shares a little of their philosophy and some thoughts on why they’re drawn to Bombtrack’s bikes, and offers a glimpse of a special Bombtrack Hook EXT Ti build they have in the stand, which you can find some photos of all built up below.
