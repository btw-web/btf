ENVE Introduces New Full-Carbon Adventure Fork

Announced this morning, ENVE’s latest offering is an all-carbon fork that’s designed with features and geometry to suit adventure-oriented frames. They’re calling it the Adventure Fork, and it looks like it ticks a lot of boxes for bikepackers looking for a high-end carbon fork for their next build. It features an adjustable rake, huge clearance, internal dynamo routing, three-pack mounts on both blades, and more.

ENVE Adventure Fork Full Features:

  • Flip-Chip for ride tuning rake at either 49mm or 55.5mm
  • Clearance for up to 29 x 2.3″ or 650B x 2.6″ tires (depending on rake)
  • Internal dynamo routing
  • Drilled crown for rack/light mounting
  • Three-pack mounts on both legs
  • Molded one-piece design
  • 575 gram weight

As Jake Pantone, ENVE’s VP of Product describes the new fork, “Like all ENVE products, the Adventure Fork was inspired from time in the saddle and in this case, our custom frame builder partners. Our frame building partners needed an ENVE solution that was both beautiful and functional. Our endurance, adventure, and bike packing athletes and customers needed a fork with more cargo hauling options, greater tire clearance, and dynamo compatibility for when riding in the dark is not optional.”

The new ENVE Adventure Fork retails for $625 USD and includes lifetime incident protection and a five-year factory warranty. Forks will be available from ENVE or your local dealer in mid-October. Find additional specs or pre-order an Adventure Fork of your own at ENVE.com.
