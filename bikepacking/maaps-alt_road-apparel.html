MAAP’s Alt_Road Apparel Lineup

Following months of testing, MAAP revealed their new Alt_Road collection, a lineup of apparel made for exploits far off the beaten path. The Melbourne-based brand’s new collection features jerseys made with “NATURALMATCH” wool, an exclusive fabric made in partnership with Miti Spa in Bergamo, Italy. Utilizing a blend of extra-fine merino wool and high-performance, 50% Recycled PET synthetic fibers, this material is made for temperature regulation, anti-microbial and anti-odor properties, and moisture management.

Other products in the lineup include an Apidura bag collaboration, gloves, merino socks, thermoregulating vests, and windproof thermal jackets, all designed with a color palette inspired by earth tones.

“Alt_Road is a natural evolution for us as a brand. The days are gone where riders are loyal to one discipline. The product reflects the needs of our athletes and riders around the world, engineered to perform at an elite level, whatever the road surface,” said Ollie Cousins, MAAP co-founder.

The Alt_Road collection is available now via maap.cc or your local MAAP dealer.
