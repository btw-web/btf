North Face Summer Base Camp

Join The North Face for a free online camp experience created for outdoors-curious kids. The Summer Base Camp teaches campers of all ages how to find adventure at home. For two weeks, The North Face will host seven new educational, hands-on activities for campers and parents to complete at their own pace, at home. Activities will be guided by North Face’s sponsored team athletes. Some of more interesting highlights include Jimmy Chin’s Adventure Photography class, Map-Making with ultra-runner Coree Woltering, and Pack Snacks with climber Ashima Shiraishi. Find the full schedule over at TheNorthFace.com.
