New Wolf Tooth B-Rad Everywhere Base

Although frames plastered with mounting points are becoming more and more common, we sometimes need a little extra help to mount an accessory cage or bottle. Thankfully, companies like Wolf Tooth Components are creating clean solutions to mount extra gear to your bike when mounting points are limited. So, put your roll of electrical tape down and check out the new B-Rad Everywhere Base.

The redesigned “Strap Base,” which is now called the Wolf Tooth B-Rad Everywhere Base, is a simple velcro-mounted aluminum base that can be strapped onto your frame to provide an additional mounting point. The velcro straps are silicone-backed to hold the base securely in place, foam pads with adhesive backings are included to protect your frame, and the Everywhere Base comes in both a two-bolt and three-bolt version, the latter being more appropriate for cargo cages and heavier loads.

While the B-Rad Everywhere Base might look like a great option for mounting a cage to your suspension fork, Wolf Tooth makes it clear that the Everywhere Base is not designed or intended to be used on forks.

  • Material: 6061-T6 aluminum
  • Weight: Everywhere Base 2 (38g), Everywhere Base 3 (50g)
  • Place of Manufacture: USA
  • Price: $24.95 USD / $29.95
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com
