Bikepacking Journal Issue 03

We’re happy to announce Issue 03 of The Bikepacking Journal, set to print and start shipping to our Bikepacking Collective members all over the world in early November. The latest issue is our biggest one yet—we’ve increased the number of pages and rounded up a fresh batch of stories and essays from bikepacking trips in nearly a dozen picturesque new locations. And as an added bonus, each issue will ship with a hand-illustrated, fold-out map of the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route and a Bikepacking Collective member patch.

If you’re already a member, be sure to log in to the member area to update your shipping address and verify that your payment details are correct for auto-renewal. If you haven’t joined our Bikepacking Collective yet, sign up to receive the new issue, support the site, and receive lots of great benefits. The cutoff to join and have your membership begin with The Bikepacking Journal 03 is just around the corner at 11:59 PM EST on Thursday, October 17th!

Still need some convincing? Read our Bikepacking Journal Manifesto or peek at Issue 02 and Issue 01.
