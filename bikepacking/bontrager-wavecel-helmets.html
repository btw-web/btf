Bontrager WaveCel Helmet Line Gets More Sizes, Lower Prices

Launched in 2019, Bontrager’s WaveCel helmet technology uses a collapsible cellular structure that lines the inside of specific helmets. The structure works like a crumple zone to absorb the force of an impact before it reaches your head. Although we’re all about safer helmets, the retail price of the initial line of WaveCel helmets was difficult for everyone to get behind. Announced today, Bontrager has expanded the line to offer new lower price points, and more sizes, from XL all the way down to kid’s sizes. Find details and pricing on the new Rally, Starvos, and Jet WaveCel helmets below.

Bontrager Rally WaveCel Helmet

Taking design cues from the Rally MIPS, the Rally WaveCel is a high-value off-road helmet with trail specific features like BOA Fit System and an adjustable visor. It’s available in four colors; black, marigold, olive grey / orange, and azure / nautical navy, and retails for $149.99 USD.

  • Virginia Tech 5 STAR safety rating
  • Adjustable visor offers multiple coverage options
  • Extended back-of-helmet shell offers additional rear protection
  • LockDown dividers make helmet strap management clean and easy to adjust
  • Soft, comfortable, and moisture-wicking washable helmet pads

Bontrager Starvos WaveCel Helmet

The Starvos WaveCel is a value-first road helmet for riders who want the protection of Bontrager’s WaveCel technology. Those with large noggins will appreciate that the Starvos goes all the way up to a size XL (60-66cm). It’s available in three colors; black, white, radioactive yellow, and retails for $99.99 USD.

  • Intuitive, one-handed Headmaster fit system makes height and circumference adjustments easy
  • LockDown dividers make helmet strap management clean and easy to adjust
  • Soft, comfortable, and moisture-wicking washable helmet pads
  • Available in XL sizing

Bontrager Jet WaveCel Kids Helmet

New for the WaveCel lineup is the addition of the Jet Youth and Children’s sized helmets. Both use a magnetic Fidlock buckle, which is easy for kids to handle themselves, and fit dial for a secure fit. Both models comes in four colors; black/volt, white/azure, vis Green/royal, magenta/ultraviolet, and retail for $99.99 USD.

  • Comes in Youth (50-55cm) and Children’s (48-52cm) sizing
  • Meets ASTM standards, whose tests use a smaller mass headform more realistic to younger riders
  • Fidlock magnetic buckle fastens easily for a quick, secure, kid-friendly fit
  • Dialed fit system provides a secure and comfortable fit

Like all Bontrager products, the new WaveCel helmets come with a 30-day guarantee—if you don’t love it, you can bring it back. Plus, Bontrager offers a crash replacement program within the first year on all of their helmets—if you happen to take a spill, they will replace it for free.
