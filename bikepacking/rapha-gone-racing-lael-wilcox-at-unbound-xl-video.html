Rapha Gone Racing: Lael Wilcox at Unbound XL (Video)

Photos by Rugile Kaladyte (@rugilekaladyte)

This year’s Unbound XL event in Emporia, Kansas surprised riders with an extremely challenging 350-mile route along rough double track, gravel roads, and mountainous terrain. Ultra endurance athlete Lael Wilcox pushed through, earning herself a first place finish in the women’s race, completing the route in just over 26 hours. The latest episode of Rapha Gone Racing documents Lael’s race. Watch it in full below, then scroll down for a gallery of images and a map of the route…
