Surly Adds Bruised Ego Purple and Blue Porta Potty

Surly has a knack for making folks want a new frame. Is it the catchy new colors, or the Surly thinktank’s proficiency at creating good color names? Either way, the 2019 Bruised Ego Purple Krampus is looking quite nice. Pick up a complete for $1500 or a frameset for $675. Both are available now from your local QBP dealer. I’ll say it again, it’s hard to beat the Krampus if you’re in the market for a do-it-all, no-nonsense bikepacking trail rig.

Also, the 2019 Karate Monkey gets a new Blue Porta Potty paint scheme. It will available from dealers this fall for $1500 for the complete or $675 for the frameset.

And, the Karate Monkey is still available in Lemon Lime Soda, as shown above right. Check SurlyBikes.com for details. Thanks to Andy from New River Bikes for the last minute intel on availability and pricing!
