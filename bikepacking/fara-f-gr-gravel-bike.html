Fara Releases New F/GR Gravel Bike

Norway’s Fara Cycling just launched the newest iteration of its gravel platform, the F/GR. This latest model features a refreshed design, new geometry (with a longer wheelbase and slacker angles), and increased clearance for 700 x 50mm tires. They say it’s focused on fun and is “perfect for those who want to push the limits of what drop-bar bikes are capable of.” The new F/GR rounds out the brand’s trio of models that they believe spans the gamut of drop-bar riding, from smooth asphalt to chunky singletrack.

Using Fara’s online configurator, buyers can tailor their F/GR to their needs, and bikes are assembled to order and shipped directly to consumers from the brand’s headquarters in Oslo. The Fara F/GR available in four sizes (Small/49cm, Medium/53cm, Large/56cm, and Large/59cm) and four colors (Fjord Green, Winter Sky Blue, Wet Asphalt, and Morell Red).
