John and Mira Dogpacking World Tour (EP.1 Montana)

Last summer, our friends John and Mira rode a boatload of bikepacking routes, including the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route, The Oregon Timber Trail, the Baja Divide, and the Trans Mexico Norte. This year, as the borders open, they’re setting off on their big Dogpacking World Tour, riding south toward the tip of South America. In the first video following their trip, the two begin just north of Helena at the world-famous Llama Ranch. Using a combination of the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route, the CDT, and the Western Wildlands Route, they enjoy some beautiful fall riding in northern Montana. Watch it below and find details about their dogpacking setup in the related content grid at the bottom of the post.
