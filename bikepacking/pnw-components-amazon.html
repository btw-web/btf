PNW Components Pauses Business on Amazon

Now more than ever, supporting our local bike shops is crucial for their success. The pandemic has affected these businesses in a number of ways, with the ongoing bike and component shortage taking a significant toll. To address this, PNW Components just announced that they will be pausing all future business with Amazon until further notice in order to provide more components for retailers. Read PNW’s explanation on the change below.

PNW Components Pauses Amazon

After a year of incredible demand and an overloaded supply chain, we recognize that we will continue to have limited supply and need to prioritize the sales channels that best benefit our customers. The expertise, experience, and personality of industry experts at bike shops simply can’t be replicated on a platform like Amazon so we have made the decision to shift this inventory into our wholesale channels.

“We’ve learned the best way to support our customers is to ensure they have the information and resources needed when making a purchase. Not only is this best for our customers, but it is good for business, too, as it minimizes returns that result from confusion on which spec to choose at the point of purchase,” said Aaron Kerson, Co-Founder and CEO of PNW. “Moreover, in a time when inventory is tight, the best way we can support our business partners is to empower them with product and make decisions that keep dollars inside the industry, especially important for small, independently owned businesses.”

To ensure the entire PNW Components supply chain is aligned, we have worked with our network of retailers to pull all products from Amazon across the site. Previous inventory dedicated to Amazon will be re-allocated directly to bike shops and retailers.

At a time when independent bicycle retailers are struggling to get enough inventory to support their businesses, channeling the revenue previously generated through Amazon into those retailers will help ensure the long-term health of these partners. To account for Amazon Prime customers who buy from Amazon for free shipping, we have lowered our threshold for free Expedited Shipping and now offer free 2-Day shipping within the contiguous USA for orders $40 or more.
