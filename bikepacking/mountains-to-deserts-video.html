Mountains To Deserts (Video)

Ryan Van Duzer is an outdoor adventure TV host, content creator, and motivational speaker from Boulder, Colorado. His work has appeared on the Travel Channel, Discovery Channel, Men’s Journal, National Geographic, and more. Ryan maintains an active YouTube channel, where we found this most recent video.

Mountains to Deserts documents his seven-day hut-to-hut bikepacking trip from Durango to Moab, taking advantage of the San Juan Hut system to ride lightly loaded mountain bike over some fantastic terrain. Ryan and his riding partner tackled about 30 miles per day, for a total of about 220 miles. Check out the video below, followed by a few photos they took along the way.

To see more from Ryan, check out his YouTube channel or visit his website, DuzerTV.com. Also, for those interested in a hut-supported bike trip from Durango to Moab, head over to SanJuanHuts.com to learn more.
