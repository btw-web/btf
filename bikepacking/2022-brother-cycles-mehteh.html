Pre-orders for 2022 Brother Cycles Mehteh Open Soon

Photos by Ollie Harrop

Since its release in 2019, the Brother Cycles Mehteh has been a compelling choice for those searching for “fast and agile” steel gravel bike designed for tough terrain. Like last year, Brother Cycles are making the ‘22 Mehteh available for pre-order, so if you’re looking to pick one up, make sure to get on their list when it opens tomorrow Tuesday, February 15th, at 10 a.m. GMT.

New for 2022, the Mehteh’s Carbon Gravel Fork is now color-matched to the frame’s lovely “Ochre” paint. More than just a visual upgrade, the Carbon Gravel Fork now comes with improved triple cage mounts, clearance for 650b x 2.2″ tires, dynamo cable routing, and flip-chip dropouts for adjusting the rake to dial in the handling. The Carbon Gravel Fork weighs 550g and is also available to buy separately if you’re looking to lighten up your metal gravel bike.

Proving the success of the refined Reynolds 725 steel design, the frame remains unchanged for this year. It features 12x142mm / 12x100mm thru-axles, compatibility with 1x/2x/3x drivetrains, stealth dropper post routing, and a 68mm threaded bottom bracket shell. The frame and fork have clearance for up to 27.5 x 2.1″ or 700c x 45mm tires.

The 2022 Mehteh costs £949 ($1,285) including UK VAT and framesets are expected to ship to customers in April 2022. The improved Carbon Gravel Fork (in black only) will also be available for £325 ($440) including UK VAT starting tomorrow. You’ll find all the specs and geometry charts at BrotherCycles.com.
