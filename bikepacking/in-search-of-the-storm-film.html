In Search of The Storm: Himalayan Winter.

“A few months ago, as we sat across the table sipping one cup of tea after another, we saw this tiny speck on the map and it had a name too, Chitkul. It was the last Indian village on the map and right ahead lay Tibet.”

This was the founding moment that led these gentlemen on a winter, fat bike journey to a small village at the Indo-Tibetan border. The route they followed was one that typically sees a steady influx of summer travelers, seeking breathtaking views and Himalayan scenery. But come the onset of winter, these same towns and villages are all but abandoned; even the heartiest of locals are driven to lower ground.

Watch this refreshing, personal account of a challenging and snowy traverse through one of the most beautiful mountain ranges in the world.
