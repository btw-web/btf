Custom Ghost Grapplers from the UK

Following our review of the new Surly Ghost Grappler earlier today, here’s something a little different. Surly’s UK distributor sent out Ghost Grappler framesets to several Surly dealers who specialize in custom builds. After they all had a chance to build them up and get a few rides in, they all sent over a few photos of their custom-specced Grapplers, each made their own for today’s launch.
