Wyatt Spalding’s Drawing of Cjell Mone’s La Roca… and more

In case you missed Monday’s feature, Cass Gilbert published a beautiful photoset and a thoughtful interview/reflection on frame builder and all around interesting human Cjell Moné. Today, artist and desert dweller Wyatt Spalding posted this drawing that he whipped up from one of Cass’ images in that article, so we thought we’d share it. In doing so, we also found a couple more of Wyatt’s drawings that he did from photos in the fifth issue of The Bikepacking Journal. Find those below, and scroll down to learn more about Wyatt’s work.

Drawn from Kyle Hughes’ photograph on an epic journey through the Andes.

All of Wyatt’s sketches are created digitally on the computer with a Wacom tablet.

Drawn from Conan Thai’s photo that was featured in Issue 05, as well as the digital feature Baldy Bruised.

If you missed it, make sure to read the feature “Cjell Moné and his La Roca” here, and find more of Wyatt’s work on Instagram @wyattspalding.
