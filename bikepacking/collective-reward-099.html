Collective Reward #099: Copies of The Bikepacking Journal 06

We’re offering up a bunch of copies of the sixth issue of The Bikepacking Journal for our latest Collective Reward giveaway, and 20 randomly selected new and existing Bikepacking Collective members who joined after the April 17th deadline to receive the previous issue will receive one to help complete their collection.

In the sixth issue of The Bikepacking Journal, our contributors used bikepacking as a lens through which to explore topics that included embracing aloneness, finding a sense of adventure, grappling with failure, nourishing relationships with friends and family, and forging new connections—themes many of us have had on our minds throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, whether on or off the bike.

Curious to know more about what’s inside? Take a look at our sixth issue here. And if you want to get a look at the kind of stories we publish in The Bikepacking Journal, check out the web versions of Slower, Baldy Bruised, and Ditibised, feature stories from previous issues.

Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible. If you enjoy reading the site, please consider joining our Bikepacking Collective. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards, receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.
