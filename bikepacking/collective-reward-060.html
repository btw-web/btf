Collective Reward #060: Crankbrothers Stamp 7 Pedals

For our latest Collective Reward gear giveaway, two randomly selected members of our Bikepacking Collective will receive a set of Crankbrothers Stamp 7 pedals in their choice of small or large and black or red. That’s a $159 value for each winner. These pro-level platform pedals feature a spacious platform with 10 adjustable pins per side that offer an impressive amount of grip. They come in two different sizes to accommodate riders with different shoe sizes (small is recommended for sizes 5-10, large for sizes 10-15), both of which feature a thin platform that minimizes weight and helps avoid pedal strikes. And they’re built to be incredibly durable, easy to service, and are backed by a five-year warranty from Crankbrothers.

While I mostly prefer to ride with clipless pedals, I’ve been running a pair of Stamp 7s on one of my bikes for the past few weeks and I’m blown away by just how grippy and comfortable they are. But don’t take my word for it: Logan and Gin took their older Stamp pedals to Cuba back in 2017, and their findings after 900 miles of bashing around on rough terrain were overwhelmingly positive (read the full review here).

Crankbrothers Stamp 7 Pedal Specs:

  • 10 adjustable pins per side
  • 100mm x 100mm (small) / 114mm x 111mm (large) sizes
  • Forged 6061-T6 aluminum body
  • Igus LL-glide bearings
  • Forged scm 435 chromoly steel spindle
  • Five-year warranty
  • 345g (small) / 375g (large) weight

See more details about the full line of Stamp pedals at CrankBrothers.com.
