New BIKEPACKING.com Merch is Coming!

Ah yes, it’s been a while since I’ve uttered those words. And in truth, we’ve had some stuff ready for well over a year now, but have been hesitant to get the ball rolling. The trepidation was based mostly on the Covid-related shipping delays we’ve seen with the Journal (for any members who haven’t gotten Issue 05, please be patient; some are still hitting mailboxes, over three months after shipping, believe it or not). At any rate, we’re almost ready to start releasing several batches of new BIKEPACKING.com goods. We have lots of stuff coming up, including multiple shirts, hats, socks, stickers, patches, and more. Here are some teaser images of a few of the items. Read on to find out more…

When and How

The first collection will go on sale within the next couple of weeks. One or two more collections will be released closer to spring/summer. We’ll be announcing these collections to our members exclusively at first as stock is limited. We expect them to sell out before reaching the public (but you never know). Bikepacking Collective members, look for an email announcement soon. If you’re interested in joining the Bikepacking Collective to help support this website and get perks such as The Bikepacking Journal, industry discounts, eligibility for regular Collective Reward giveaways, and first priority on merchandise, click here.
