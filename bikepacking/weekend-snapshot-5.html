Weekend Snapshot

Our new Weekend Snapshot series continues with another batch of photos from backyard rides, campouts, and more from our global community of bikepackers. In this installment, we check out three takes on riding (and pushing) in the mountains. Find snapshots from our readers’ recent weekend adventures below, then scroll down to the bottom of this post to submit your own!

Arizona

By Jay Scully

This shot was taken on the climb out of Green Valley near the Adobe ruins on the way toward Patagonia to finish the Sky Islands Odyssey loop, and the rider is Blake Bockius from Truckee, California.

Los Nevados, Colombia

By Hans de Neve (@deneve.hans)

We knew riding through the Colombian PÃ¡ramo would be one of the highlights of our stay in Colombia, but it exceeded our expectations. Climbing up to about 4,150 meters above sea level was tough, but cruising through this magnificent landscape made it well worth the effort! Our initial plan was to camp right in the middle of this remarkable ecosystem, but we felt that our bodies weren’t yet acclimatized enough, so we descended back into the jungle on the other side of the mountain. After a nasty puncture, we ended up relaxing in some hot springs. Can’t think of a better way to end a hard day of riding!

Santa Fe, New Mexico

By Daniel Pastrana

I took this photo after a light snowfall this past Sunday afternoon on the outskirts of beautiful Santa Fe, New Mexico. I was out doing a scouting research ride for a my next winter overnighter and found this stunning overlook. Can’t wait to come back and spend the night here!
