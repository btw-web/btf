PTAP Designs Chonker V3.0 Pre-Orders Now Open

After nearly a year of testing and development, version 3.0 the PTAP Designs Chonker is here open for pre-ordering through November 15th. Taking cues from classic English long-flap bags—but with bold color options and some modern touches—the Chonker is designed to work as a handlebar bag or saddlebag. Maker Dan Doggett says it’s the “perfect accessory for both your daily commute and your next bikepacking adventure.”

PTAP Chonker Features:

  • Tapered side pockets that fold down flat when not in use
  • Internal fiberglass batten for rigid mounting
  • Semi-rigid HDPE liner to help keep shape whether empty or full
  • 2 Voile Nano straps for handlebar/saddle mounting
  • Interior Dimensions 12”x8”x7”
  • 12L capacity

The Chonker 3.0 is priced at $190 in your choice of five colors. Bags will be made in batches of 10, and they’re only available during this pre-order window, which runs from today through November 15th. Note that the ordering window will close as soon as the 10 bag limit has been reached. Chonkers will ship toward the middle of December.

Find additional details or pre-order a Chonker at PTAPDesigns.com.
