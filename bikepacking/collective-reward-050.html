Collective Reward #050: Teravail Tires

It’s hard to believe we’re already on our 50th Collective Reward gear giveaway. We’ve teamed up with Teravail this time around, and two lucky Bikepacking Collective members will receive a set of tires of their choosing. Our lucky winners can pick from Teravail’s 35 tires in the gravel, all-road, or off-road category. If you’re not yet a supporter of the site, get signed up and you’ll be eligible to win.

You’ve probably seen Teravail tires pop up on bikes in various corners of the site, from our popular “Rigs of” posts, to review bikes, to our Editor’s Dozens. And for good reason: they’re making some of our favorite tires on the market right now, and it seems we’re not the only ones to have caught on. Logan ran a set of 29 x 2.3″ Ehlines on the gorgeous Kona Sutra LTD he built for his recent trip to Colombia, and I’ve got a set of Rampart 650Bs on my Curve Kevin. They’ve come up with an impressive range of tires to tune your ride for specific terrain, as well as several excellent all-rounder options. You can check out the full selection over at Teravail.com.

Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible. If you enjoy reading the site, please consider joining our Bikepacking Collective. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards (including a set of these Teravail tires), receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.
