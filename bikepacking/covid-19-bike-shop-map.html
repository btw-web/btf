Bike Shop Operations During the COVID-19 Pandemic – A Nationwide Map

The COVID-19 pandemic has had a massive and wide-reaching effect on small businesses everywhere, and no one is quite sure what the new normal will look like. In some places bike shops have been deemed an essential service, as cyclists rely more than ever on the bike as a form of transportation. Shop owners have been quick to react, offering one-on-one appointments, curbside pick-up, and new sanitation regimes to keep everyone safe. In hopes of helping more people find their local bike shop, and continue to support them, Portland Design Works and Chris King Precision Components have partnered to create an interactive map of the current operating procedures of bicycle retails in the United States. Are you a shop owner that wants to be included? Follow this link to add your shop.
