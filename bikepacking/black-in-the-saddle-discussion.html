Black in the Saddle Roundtable Discussion

This Thursday, September 3rd, Swift Industries will host an online roundtable discussion in which a diverse group of Black cyclists will share their cycling origin stories, most memorable cycling adventures, and how they’ve been handling this tumultuous year. Black in the Saddle will be moderated by Devin Cowens (who you may remember as a guest on our “What’s in your Bag?” YouTube series) and the panel will include Lydia Moore​, ​Kaiden Nia-Ali​, ​William Loyd​, ​Duncan Benning​, and​ Erick Cedeño​. The conversation kicks off at 5:30 PM PST / 8:30 PM EST on Thursday over on YouTube.

As Swift puts it, “The culture-shaping events of 2020 have awakened the Cycling and Outdoor industries to the existence of the many BIPOC communities whose incredible stories and experiences for too long have been ignored. ​Black In The Saddle​ is our effort in helping to amplify these important perspectives in advance of year’s Campout. We hope you’ll join us in celebrating these stories!”
