Introducing the New Easton EC90 AX Handlebar

The newest addition to Easton’s AX series of gravel-focused components is the EC90 AX Gravel Adventure Bar, a lightweight, flared, high-performance carbon version of their popular EC90 SLX and Aero bars. The new EC 90 AX bars feature a size-specific flattened ergonomic top section (with a graduated diameter and length) for more comfort and optimized positioning while on the bike. They weigh just 203 to 218 grams depending on size, feature a 16-degree flare, 80mm reach, 120mm drop, have Di2 bar-end routing, and are available in 40, 42, 44, and 46cm widths (measured at the hoods).

You can watch Eason’s nicely shot one-minute launch video below, which features a handful of their ambassadors, including Lael Wilcox (riding the Redington-Lemmon Loop in Tucson, which we recently shared on the site) and the CNCPT team, showing off the new EC90 AX bar in a wide variety of settings.

The new Easton EC90 AX gravel bars are priced at $299.99 USD and are available now at EastonCycling.com.
