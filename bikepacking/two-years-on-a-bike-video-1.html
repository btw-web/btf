Two Years on a Bike 1/4 (Video)

Photos by Martijn Doolaard, Two Years on a Bike, gestalten 2021

Our friend and contributor to The Bikepacking Journal Martijn Doolaard (@_espiritu.libre_) is currently sharing a four-part series of videos in support of his recently released book, Two Years on a Bike, which was published in hardcover by Berlin-based gestalten late last year.

In this first video, Martijn walks through the book and shares a series of stories, photos, and video vignettes from his two-year bikepacking trip from Vancouver to Patagonia. Watch it below, then scroll down for a small gallery of images (including one our Bikepacking Collective members might recognize as the cover shot from The Bikepacking Journal 03 and a link to learn more about the book.

You can find more details about Martijn’s 416-page “Two Years on a Bike” over at gestalten.com.
