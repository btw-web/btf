Fyxation Releases Mesa MP Subzero Flat Pedals

Wisconsin-based Fyxation winterized its popular Mesa MP pedal for the year round cyclist. Adding stainless steel replaceable pins and an electro-coated cromoly spindle, the Fyxation Mesa MP Subzero is designed for the winter commuter or fat biker looking for pedals that can withstand the elements. Like the original Mesa MP, the Subzero also features sealed bearing construction and a tough impact grade nylon body. We’ve been fiddling with a few nylon pedals over the last year and have been impressed, so it’s good to see another affordable model released with special attention to winter-specific needs.

Ben Ginster, Fyxation’s co-founder adds, “Here in the Midwest and in most parts of the country, snow, ice, salt and the elements can wreak havoc on your bike and gear. The Mesa MP Subzero was designed to prevent rust and keep you pedaling year round.”

Fyxation Mesa MP Subzero pedals are priced at $69.95 and available in four color combinations with several anodized spindle and pin options. Pedals are in stock now and will be at Fyxation dealers/distributors early next week. Learn more at Fyxation and check out their specs below:

Fyxation Mesa MP Subzero Specs (from Fyxation)

  • High impact nylon body built to take abuse
  • Smooth running sealed bearing and DU bushing
  • Electroplated cro-moly spindle
  • Electroplated stainless steel replaceable pins for added grip with shoes or boots
  • Nylon body doesn’t suck heat out of your feet like alloy pedals do
  • Installs with 15mm open end wrench or 8mm hex wrench
  • Pin Spec – M3x0.5 x 7.5mm
  • Spindle Thread: 9/16
  • Height: 14mm
  • Width: 105mm
  • Weight: 351g/pair
  • Color: Black/Black, Gray/Black, Black/Blue, Black/Orange
