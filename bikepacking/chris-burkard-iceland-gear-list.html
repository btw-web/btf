Chris Burkard’s Iceland Crossing Gear List (video)

Over the summer, photographer and filmmaker Chris Burkard set out on a bike expedition to cross Iceland from east to west along with fellow riders Emily Batty, Eric Batty, and Adam Morka. We’ll be publishing an exclusive piece about their trip in the forthcoming fifth issue of The Bikepacking Journal, so stay tuned. In the meantime, Chris put together this video gear list going over his bike and gear that he brought along for the trip. Watch it below and scroll down for a printed list. And keep an eye out for more details on what’s to come in issue 05.
