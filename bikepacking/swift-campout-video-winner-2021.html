Here’s the 2021 Swift Campout Video Winner

We’re always eagerly awaiting the entries to the Swift Campout Video Challenge when the summer solstice rolls around each year, a handful of which have taken home prizes in our annual Bikepacking Awards. The Seattle-based bag manufacturer recently announced this year’s winning video, which was created by Wira Samudra of Psychloop in Jakarta, Indonesia. Good vibes abound in this three-minute video, which features a group of friends on a short trip full of riding, swimming, cooking, and enjoying a night together beyond the city lights, all set to the perfect feel-good soundtrack.
