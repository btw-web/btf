Collective Reward #082: Goodyear Connector Tires

Goodyear announced a significant expansion of their all-road tire range last month, which included two new versions of their popular Connector, an all-rounder featuring a versatile tread pattern that rolls fast and offers plenty of grip—perfect for mixed-terrain bikepacking. And for Collective Reward #082, four randomly selected members of our Bikepacking Collective will win a pair of Goodyear Connectors in their choice of size and color.

Winners can pick from a set of 700C x 35mm, 700C x 40mm, 700C x 50mm, or 650B x 50mm tires with classic black sidewalls or the updated tan walls. That’s a $128 value for each winner, and this giveaway is open to our new and existing members anywhere in the world.

Goodyear Connector Specs

  • Size(s): 700C x 35mm / 700C x 40mm / 700C x 50mm / 650B x 50
  • Weight(s): 460g / 515g / 650g / 620g
  • Casing: 1-Ply 120tpi w/TC Liner
  • Color(s): Black or Tan
  • Pricing: $64 (€50/£43)
