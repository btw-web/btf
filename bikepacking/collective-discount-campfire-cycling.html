Collective Discount: Campfire Cycling!

Founded on April 29th, 2019 in Tucson, Arizona, Campfire Cycling evolved from our friend Josh Lipton’s previous company, BikeShopHub.com. If you’ve been touring or bikepacking for a while, you’ve likely heard of BikeShopHub or even ordered racks or bags from them at some point. Otherwise, know that Josh’s latest endeavor is the culmination of a 16-year journey running an ecommerce shop focused on bicycle touring accessories. 

In choosing to rebrand to Campfire Cycling, Josh brought the best of what he learned over the years and turned it something even better. Campfire Cycling offers a wealth of bikepacking and touring gear, including racks from Tubus, Salsa, and Surly; camping gear from Big Agnes, Hyperlite, and MSR; and bikepacking bags from Revelate, Oveja Negra, and Ortlieb. There are also plenty of other brands, such as Carradice, Brooks, Vargo, and more…

Now through December 31st, 2019, Bikepacking Collective members can take 15% all non-sale items in the web shop at CampfireCycling.com. Log in to the member area of the site to find it. If you’re not yet a member, sign up to take advantage of this discount as well as five others currently available.
