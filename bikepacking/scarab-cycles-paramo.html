Scarab Cycles Paramo at the Enve Builder Round-Up

Based in Medellin, Colombia, Scarab Cycles designs, builds, and custom paints all of their bikes in-house. The Páramo, named after Colombia’s unique high-altitude ecosystem, gets Scarab’s Chiva paint scheme, inspired by the highly customized off-road transport busses of rural Colombia. The Paramo features a T47 bottom bracket, a long and low geometry, an AXS Mullet drivetrain, and Enve Gravel Bars. Learn more about the build in this video.

To learn more about Scarab Cycles, head over to ScarabCycles.com. For more from day two of Enve’s Virtual Builder Round-Up, click here.
