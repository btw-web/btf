Wizard Works Launches New Framebagracadabra

Announced this morning, the new Framebagracadabra from Wizard Works (@wizard.works) is a partial frame bag that’s available in three standard sizes. Long in the works, the Framebagracadabra features a recycled HPDE plastic bottom to help maintain its shape, elastic upper webbing for securing it to your bike’s top tube, an inner mesh pocket to help with organization, and a chunky zipper that they say has a satisfying zrrrommmp.

In a market full of (often ill-fitting) partial frame bags, Harry and Ve at Wizard Works wanted to offer something that would look good across the full range of bike shapes and sizes. According to Harry, “We knew that with 3 sizes we weren’t going to be able to achieve a flawless fit for every bike – we spent so much time and made so many samples to optimise the shape of this bag so that it looks handsome even on a bike it doesn’t fit perfectly. The curved bum along with its hard-shell construction goes a long way to making the bag look deliberately short thanks to the lack of mandatory seat tube strap.”

The Wizard Works Framebagracadabra is offered in 1.9L, 2.5L, and 3.5L sizes in five stock colors (Black Camo, Splatter, Olive, Rust, Hot Pink). You can also pick and choose your colors via their custom program for a small upcharge. Prices for the new bags range from £92 to £99 ($125 to $135) including UK VAT (customers outside of the UK save 20%). As always, the Framebagracadabra is made by hand in Wizard Works’ London workshop, which we recently visited. Find that post linked below.

The Framebagracadabra is available now. Head over to Wizard.Works to learn more or order one own your own.
