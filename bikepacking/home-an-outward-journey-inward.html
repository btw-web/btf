Home (an Outward Journey Inward) now available on Vimeo On Demand

Between 2011 and 2015 UK adventurer Sarah Outen traversed the globe by bike, kayak, and rowing boat, travelling over 20,000 miles by nothing but human power. But travelling solo, the trip took its toll and a violent storm on the Pacific Ocean took Sarah to the physical and mental brink. Home, the full-length film documenting her journey, was stitched together from hundreds of hours of footage and won several awards at various film festivals.
