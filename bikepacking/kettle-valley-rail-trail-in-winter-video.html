Bikepacking The Kettle Valley Rail Trail in Winter (video)

If you’re not already familiar with solo adventurer Iohan Gueorguiev, you may have seen his photo in our recent ’20 in 20 photo annual. Iohan recently crossed the finish line of his southbound trip from Alaska to Argentina and returned home to British Columbia. But that didn’t stop him from pedaling on.
