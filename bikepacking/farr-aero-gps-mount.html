Farr Releases Carbon Aero Bolt-On GPS Mount

Farr’s Carbon Aero Bolt-On has proven to be quite successful, but a common request from its users has been for a compact and clean way to mount a GPS device on the front of the extension. Although there are ways to DIY a GPS mounting solution, Farr wanted to create a sleeker option with more adjustment that was designed specifically for the wishbone-shaped centre piece on the Aero Bolt-On.

The GPS Mount is made from CNC Machined Alloy, has multiple adjustment points for the perfect position, and ships with both Garmin and WAHOO mounts. They are available for preorder now, priced at $85 USD, and are expected to ship next month. This is the first of several other GPS solutions that Farr is working on, so stay tuned for more.

  • Material: CNC Machined Alloy
  • Weight: 40 grams
  • Price: $85 USD (preorder price)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: RideFarr.com
