The New Nordest Sardinha II

The Sardinha is Nordest’s bikepacking-specific frame with a trail-oriented geometry. The latest version, the Nordest Sardinha II got a few tweaks, including a steeper seat tube angle, larger tire clearance for 29er tires, internal dropper seat post routing, a shorter headtube, and most exciting, 3x threaded eyelets on each seat stay. Here are the details.

Nordest Sardinha II Frameset Specs

  • Frame CRMO 4130 double butted tubeset
  • Wheels 27.5″+ (up to 3″), 29″ (2.5″) or 29″ (2.6″)
  • BOOST rear axle with interchangable dropouts
  • TR44 head tube
  • Reach designed for 70mm stems
  • 31.6mm seatpost diameter
  • Internal dropper routing
  • Seatpost clamp included
  • Dropouts and rear thru axle included
  • Weight for M/L size: 2,750 grams
  • Fork travel from 100mm to 120mm
  • Cane Creek 40 1″1/8 headset
  • SARDINHA 110 fork

The Nordest Sardinha II will ship in August and is available for preorder for 412.40€ (about $475 USD) at NordestCycles.com.
