8bar Bikes Launches New Bikepacking Bag Range

Best known for their inexpensive, no-nonsense framesets, Berlin’s 8bar bikes just launched a completely redesigned range of bikepacking bags for the upcominng year. Their new release sees updated versions of their existing bags as well as some fresh products. The full range includes a seat pack, frame bag, top tube bag, handlebar roll, and a standalone dry bag. Most are made of waterproof nylon with welded seams, velcro and snap fasteners, and waterproof zippers.

Although the bags don’t look to offer anything particularly innovative or unique, and we haven’t had an opportunity to test their claimed waterproofness, they’re all reasonably priced and could be a worthwhile option for riders looking to pick up entry-level bikepacking bags. Find quick look at the entire collection below:

8bar Saddle Bag

The 8bar Saddle Bag features a roll-closure design and attaches directly to the saddle and seatpost via velcro and two buckles. It has a 6-13L volume and a fully waterproof, welded construction with an air purge valve on the underside. Elastic webbing up top allows you to store quick-access items outside of the bag.

  • Capacity: 13L
  • Dimensions: 72 cm x 35 cm x 16 cm
  • Weight: 700g
  • Price: 79.90€
  • Details: 8bar-bikes.com

8bar Handlebar Roll

More of an accessory bag, the pint-sized 8bar Handlebar Roll features two waterproof zippered compartments, an optional shoulder strap for use off the bike, and a velcro handlebar attachment with an elastic nylon cord for the headtube.

  • Dimensions: 26 cm x 13 cm x 13cm
  • Weight: 200g
  • Price: 29.90€
  • Details: 8bar-bikes.com

8bar Top Tube Bag

Made from hardwearing nylon with welded panels for protection from the elements, the 8bar Top Tube Bag features a single waterproof compartment for storing small accessories. It has three velcro attachment loops: two for around the top tube and a stabilizing strap for around the steerer tube.

  • Dimensions: 20 cm x 6 cm x 10 cm
  • Weight: 118g
  • Price: 29.90€
  • Details: 8bar-bikes.com

8bar Frame Bag

Offered in just one size, the new, smaller 8bar Frame Bag features a waterproof, welded construction, five velcro attachment points, and a single storage compartment with side-zip access.

  • Dimensions: 37 cm x 6 cm x 12 cm
  • Weight: 210 g
  • Price: 39.90€
  • Details: 8bar-bikes.com

8bar Dry Bag

Made of abrasion-resistant nylon, the 8bar Dry Bag is a roll-top dry bag with a snap buckle closure. Its 5L size is designed to pair with Anything-style cages. Two D-rings allow it to double as a shoulder bag.

  • Capacity: 5L
  • Dimensions: 42.5 cm x 13 cm x 13 cm
  • Weight: 42 g
  • Price: 19.90€
  • Details: 8bar-bikes.com

The collection is available online at 8bar-bikes.com and in the 8bar Showroom in Berlin Mitte.
