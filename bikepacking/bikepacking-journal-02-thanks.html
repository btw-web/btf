The Bikepacking Journal 02: Thank You

We’ve been excitedly watching the second issue of The Bikepacking Journal hit mailboxes around the globe over the past week. Thanks again to each and every one of our Bikepacking Collective members for enabling us to share our favorite bikepacking stories in the full glory of print! For us, it’s a labor of love, and we hope you take away some inspiration and enjoy reading the new issue as much as we enjoyed putting it together with our contributors.

Curious to see more? Find additional previews and a table of contents from the new 148-page issue here. If you missed out on this one, join the Bikepacking Collective and you’ll receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, starting with Issue 03 in the fall. A membership costs the same regardless of where you live, and also includes access to industry discounts (we’re currently offering member discounts on Revelate Designs, Voile Straps, Sea to Summit, Pelago racks, and Gaia GPS), Collective Reward gear giveaways, a voice in the future of the site, and more.
