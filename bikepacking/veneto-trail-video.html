Veneto Trail (video)

Italy’s jagged limestone Dolomites are undoubtedly one of the most spectacular formations in the Alps. The Veneto Trail is a 450km unsupported group ride that follows a loop from the Po Valley into these breathtaking mountains. The 2020 route and event is already in the works and Bombtrack made this short video to provide a glimpse of what this ride has to offer. Watch the video, find a selection of photos, and learn more about the event below…

According to past participants, the main reasons to take part in the Veneto Trail is the well crafted route put together each year by race organizer Andrea Securo. It’s a mountain bike-focused loop that’s different each year, but usually involves a mix of doubletrack gravel roads, rocky traverses, and rooty singletrack with very little tarmac. And, it’s guaranteed to have a lot of climbing as it takes in the some of the best scenery around the Dolomites.
