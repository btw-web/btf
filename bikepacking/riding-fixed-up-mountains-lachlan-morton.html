Riding Fixed Up Mountains with Lachlan Morton

For the latest video in their Riding Fixed Up Mountains with Pros series, the crew from State Bicycle Co. interviews pro cyclist turned bikepacker Lachlan Morton as they take on a 20-mile climb with over 3,000 feet of climbing in Colorado. Charismatic as ever, Lachlan and host Scott Piercefield dive into a range of impactful and nonsensical subjects in their occasionally bizarre and often funny conversation, even while flying back down the mountain on fixed gear bikes.
