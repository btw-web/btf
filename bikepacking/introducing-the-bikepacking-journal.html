Introducing The Bikepacking Journal

In partnership with Bunyan Velo, we’re proud to announce our latest project, The Bikepacking Journal, a beautiful new bi-annually printed publication, jam-packed with 130+ pages of the very best in pedal-powered storytelling and photography from around the globe. We’ve carefully considered every detail in the journal’s production, from paper stocks, to inks, to finishing techniques. To this end, it’s our hope that each issue will be a keepsake worth savoring and proudly displaying on your coffee table.

Join the Bikepacking Collective and your contributions will help build a better, more sustainable BIKEPACKING.com, and create original bikepacking routes, authentic stories, and quality guides and reportage. A Bikepacking Collective membership also includes a subscription to The Bikepacking Journal, as well as access to exclusive merchandise, industry discounts, prizes, and a voice in the future of this website.

We’d like to thank you for your support up to this point, and we hope you’re as excited as we are about this new chapter for BIKEPACKING.com and Bunyan Velo!
