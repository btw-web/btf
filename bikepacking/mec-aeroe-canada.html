Aeroe Now Available at MEC in Canada

New Zealand-based Aeroe hit the ground running with their clever seatstay-mounted Spider Rack and modular Cradles for hauling gear on your bars, fork, or via the rack. We had a chance to try out their Spider Rear Rack earlier this year and were impressed by its stability and modular design, making it a great option for bikes without rack mounts or those riding small frames with limited saddle bag clearance. MEC (Mountain Equipment Company) just announced they’ll be stocking Aeroe’s products at all 21 MEC stores across Canada.

“We are super excited to have our full range of products available in all 21 MEC stores across Canada,” said Aeroe Co-Founder Patrick Maguire. “To have a quality store like MEC bring Aeroe to the Canadian market is huge for us. The partnership between Aeroe and MEC is a great fit. The ability for MEC customers to easily carry their tents, sleeping bags, and drybags directly on the handlebars or the top or sides of our spider rear rack makes it easy for them to get out and explore their beautiful country on any type of bike, which is a great thing!”

Aeroe Spider Rear Rack

  • Weight: Rack alone: 641g (1.4 lbs), Rack with cradle & in-built straps: 979g (2.1 lbs)
  • Material: High grade stainless steel, anodized aluminium, glass reinforced nylon and silicone coated straps
  • Load Capacity: 16kg (35 lbs)
  • Price: $164.95 CAD

Aeroe Handlebar Cradle / Fork Cradle

  • Weight: 439g (1lb), includes in built straps
  • Material: High grade stainless steel, anodized aluminium, glass reinforced nylon and silicone coated straps
  • Load Capacity: 5kg (11 lbs)
  • Price: $99.95 CAD

Aeroe Spider Rear Rack Cradle

  • Weight: 329g (0.7lb), includes straps
  • Material: High grade stainless steel, anodized aluminium, glass reinforced nylon and silicone coated straps
  • Load Capacity: 4kg (9 lbs)
  • Price: $99.95 CAD

Aeroe Dry Bags

  • Weight: 233g (8L) / 308g (12L)
  • Material: Heavy duty TPU fully welded
  • Price: $74.95 (8L) / $89.95 (12L)

Keep an eye on MEC.ca to see more, or head into your local MEC store to check them out in person.
