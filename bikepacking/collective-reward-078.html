Collective Reward #078: Tailfin Cargo Cages

We’re back with a fresh batch of Collective Reward gear giveaways to kick off the new year! First up, we’re giving away a set of Tailfin’s versatile and practical new Cargo Cages to five randomly selected members of our Bikepacking Collective. Winners can pick between large or small cages to suit their hauling needs. That’s up to a $120 value for each lucky member. In our opinion, they make a great addition to any bikepacking setup, and we think our winners will agree.

Machined from 7075-T6 aluminum, the Tailfin Cargo Cages don’t have any welds or plastic bits that will fail over time, resulting in a super strong and simple construction, despite their minimal weight. The Cargo Cages can be mounted to a standard bottle mount or three-pack mounts, and their modular design with removable lower platform can easily adapt to whatever kind of load you need to haul. Writing about the Tailfin Cargo Cages in his recent review, Cass concluded, “These cages show a remarkable level of thought and design – and I love the resulting balance that they strike between stability, low weight, modularity, strap retention, and a svelte profile.”

Tailfin Cargo Cargo Specs

  • Construction7075-T6 Aluminium
  • Dimensions 157mm x 72mm (S), 221mm x 72mm (L)
  • Depth16mm
  • Weight (Cradle only/with Load Chip)56.6g/67.8g (S), 78.9g/90.2g (L)
