Collective Reward #076: Wolf Tooth Components Premium Headsets

The team at Wolf Tooth Components offered to donate some products during our recent visit to Wolf Tooth HQ in Minneapolis, and today we’re giving our Bikepacking Collective members a chance to win some Wolf Tooth bits of their own. For Collective Reward #076, three lucky winners will be receiving a Wolf Tooth Components Premium Headset in their choice of size and color.

Winners can choose from nine different colors and a full range of sizes to fit all modern headtubes in external cup, zero stack, and integrated standard. Wolf Tooth’s Premium Headsets are machined in Minnesota from aircraft-grade 6061 aluminum, have a triple-layer seal to protect internals from the elements, feature Enduro stainless bearings, and come with a lifetime warranty. Learn more about their headsets (and extensive range of other products) at WolfToothComponents.com.

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, it isn’t too late to get signed up to be eligible for this giveaway. Just join before 11:59 PM EDT on Saturday, October 17th, and you’ll automatically be entered to win. Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible, and membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world. Members are eligible for bi-monthly Collective Rewards, receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and more.
