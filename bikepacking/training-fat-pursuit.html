The Pursuit of Fattiness: Training for Winter Ultras

While some folks might assume that fat biking is a dying niche within the greater world of cycling, we can assure you that it’s alive and well. Devotees gather in cold and snowy places all winter long to ride fat bikes in the conditions for which they were designed. Jim Vreeland is one of those riders. Jim competes in winter ultra-endurance races such as the Fat Pursuit in Idaho, and this year in the Iditarod Invitational.
