New SR Suntour GVX Gravel Suspension Fork

Drop bar enthusiasts looking to stretch their bike’s capabilities further will be glad to learn of another short-travel suspension fork designed specifically for gravel bikes. Providing a more affordable alternative to short-travel forks from Fox, Lauf, and MRP, the new SR Suntour GVX gravel suspension fork is based around an adjustable air-sprung cartridge with rebound control and a lockout switch. It will be available in three different travel lengths; 40, 50, and 60mm, features a removable fender, and has full fender mounts. The Suntour GVX fork has tire clearance for up to 700c x 45mm or 650b x 50mm tires, and uses 32mm black-anodized 7000 series alloy stanchions. Could the lower price tag mean we start seeing more bike manufacturers offering a suspension gravel build alongside their fully rigid options?

Suntour GVX Specs:

  • 40mm travel has 436mm axle-to-crown with 45mm offset
  • 50mm travel has 446mm axle-to-crown with 46mm offset
  • 60mm travel has 456mm axle-to-crown with 47mm offset
  • Compatible with both 12 and 15x100mm thru-axles.
  • Flat mount disc brakes.
  • Weight: 1,725g
  • Price: $500 USD

That’s all we have for now. The Suntour GVX Gravel Suspension Fork should be available August 2020, and we’ll be sure to update this post as more details become available.
