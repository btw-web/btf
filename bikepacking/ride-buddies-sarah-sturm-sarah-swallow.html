Ride Buddies Episode 2: Sarah Sturm and Sarah Swallow

For their second episode of Ride Buddies, Ride with GPS turned over the mics to Sarah Sturm and Sarah Swallow, who will keep you entertained during a 40-minute, laughter-filled chat about pantless hike-a-bikes, epic journeys, and pushing the cycling industry in a new direction. These two incredibly talented off-road cyclists not only share the same initials, but they both also live in Durango, Colorado, are sponsored by Rapha and Specialized, and are youth cycling coaches.
