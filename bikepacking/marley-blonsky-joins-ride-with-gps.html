Marley Blonsky Joins Ride with GPS as Global Ambassador for 2022

Our friends at Ride With GPS just announced their exciting new partnership with athlete and advocate Marley Blonsky (@marleyblonsky), who will join the team as a Global Ambassador for 2022. Since getting into cycling and bikepacking, Marley co-founded All Bodies on Bikes, a movement to create and foster a size-inclusive bike community, and today works tirelessly to change the cycling world to be more inclusive of riders of all sizes. A self-identified “fat cyclist,” Blonsky frequently speaks on panels and at conferences, consults brands, hosts community bike rides, teaches classes for other larger folks wanting to ride bikes, and also dabbles in racing.

About the new partnership with Ride with GPS, she said: “I’m so excited to join Ride with GPS as an ambassador to share with others the ease and helpfulness of the program. And I am stoked to have a bigger platform to help other people too!” The Global Ambassadors are a diverse group of riders unified by their “passion for cycling and a commitment to inspiring others to get out there and ride.” We’re excited to see what you’ll accomplish with this new platform, Marley!

You can follow Marley’s adventures and see her routes on her Ride with GPS profile. She appeared in the highly popular film All Bodies on Bikes last year, which rose to the top in our 2021 Bikepacking Awards. You’ll also catch her in part four of our Rad Women of Bikepacking Series.
