New Wolf Tooth 8-Bit Tire Lever + Disc Brake Tool

The new 8-Bit Tire Lever + Disc Brake Multi-Tool is the latest addition to Wolf Tooth’s 8-Bit System of tools. It’s a double-sided tool with a tire lever on one end and an 8mm flare wrench and rotor truing tool on the other. The wrench is designed to open and close compression nuts on hydraulic disc brakes, and the rotor tool slots onto rotors up to 2.3mm thick to easily straighten them when they get wonky.

The tool can be purchased on its own for $19.95 or as a Lever Kit One alongside the other lever/rim dent tool for $34.95. You can also purchase the complete 8-Bit Kit Two, which includes the Pack Pliers, Chainbreaker/Utility Knife, and the new Tire Lever Multi-Tool, for $139.95.

  • Material: 7075-T6 aluminum, high-strength nylon composite lever
  • Weight: 29.5 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: USA
  • Price: $19.95 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com
