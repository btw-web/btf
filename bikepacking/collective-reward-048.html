Collective Reward #048: Outer Shell Camera Straps

Outer Shell offers the only camera strap on the market to feature quick-cinch adjustment and a sternum stabilizer, making it ideal for shooting while riding. It’s a clever way to have easy access to your camera while keeping it stable enough to prevent it from swinging around and getting in the way when you’re bouncing along dirt roads. You can quickly and securely cinch it up, then release some slack when you’re ready to take a shot, all with one hand.

For Collective Reward #048, two of our Bikepacking Collective members will receive an Outer Shell Camera Strap of their own. Miles has been using one for some time for his recent review, and based on his positive feedback, we’re happy to be able to get these into the hands of two of our supporters.

Outer Shell Camera Strap Features

  • Removable sternum stabilizer strap
  • Fidlock magnetic ‘slide release’ buckle
  • Adjustable length (22-40)”
  • Ultra-tough molded nylon cord connection points
  • Handmade in California
