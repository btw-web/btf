8bar Bikes Announces the TFLSBERG Steel Bikepacking Rig

Named after Devil’s Mountain, a man-made hill outside of Berlin that was home to a U.S. listening station during the Cold War, the TFLSBERG is the 8bar’s all-new “bikepacking 2.0” rig. The 8bar TFLSBERG is designed to carry a lot of stuff and perform well on long days in the saddle. It features a reinforced steel frame and aluminum fork with a geometry that’s more relaxed in contrast to 8bar’s MITTE gravel bike. Find a list of specs and details from 8bar and photos by our friend Stefan Haehnel below…

MOUNT ANYTHING

The TFLSBERG has a lot of mounting options, including five bottle cages on the frame (two in the triangle and three under the downtube) and four three-pack Anything Cage mounts on the fork and seat stays.

CURVED SEATSTAYS

The TFLSBERG features curved seat stays that are designed to offer increased flex for a comfortable ride. In addition, this helps avoid heel interference with luggage mounted to cages on the stays.

REINFORCED FRAME CONSTRUCTION

The TFLSBERG is designed for long off-road bikepacking tours and has butted tubing and frame reinforcements at the head tube to beef it up for added carrying capacity.

DROPPER SEATPOST OPTION

The frame has internal routing for a dropper seat post.

SUSPENSION FORK (OPTIONAL)

A suspension fork is possible on the TFLSBERG with an installation length of about 500mm (480mm fork length).

8bar TFLSBERG Specs

  • Frame: heat-treated double butted 8bar CrMo steel
  • Thru axle: 100x12mm / 142x12mm
  • Brake standard: Flat Mount
  • Tire clearance: 29×2.25 / 27.5x 2.8
  • Head Tube: 44mm / 1 1/8″ – 1 1/2″ headset
  • Seat post: 27.2mm
  • Bottom bracket: BSA / 68mm (English)
  • Brake discs: 140 / 160mm
  • Weight frame: ~2,200g
  • weight fork: ~1,000g
  • Carrying capacity (bike, rider and luggage): 140kg

The 8bar TFLSBERG is available as a black or silver frame for 599.90€, or with the aluminum fork for 799.80€. You can also get a custom color for an upcharge. And with their online configurator, you can choose the fork, tires, wheels, and more to create your own 8bar TFLSBERG. Find more details at 8bar-bikes.com.
