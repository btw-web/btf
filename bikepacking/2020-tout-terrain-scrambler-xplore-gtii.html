The New Tout Terrain Scrambler Xplore GTII

German bike manufacturer Tout Terrain has introduced two new off-road bikes designed specifically for epic two-wheeled gravel-heavy adventures. The Scrambler Xplore GT II 275 and the Scrambler Xplore GT II 28 combine the classic look and feel of a steel frame with a future-proof Pinion gearbox to create a rig that’s designed with reliability, utility, and performance in mind. Watch the launch video below, then scroll down for details on the bikes…

Both new Scrambler models feature the same geometry with clearance for 650B x 57mm and 700C x 50mm tires, and come with either 650B x 47mm or 700C x 40mm rubber. Both have mounts for fenders, bags, racks, cages, and lights. Also, the frame’s main triangle has been enlarged to maximize space for a frame bag and the chainstays were kept long to provide stability and traction while carrying a rear load.

Tout Terrain also shifted their focus on a reliable drivetrain for this iteration of the Scrambler. The Pinion gearbox is known to provide dependable shifting in all conditions, without as much of a risk of mechanical issues as a traditional derailleur-based drivetrain. For shifting with drop bars, Tout Terrain employed their own Cinq Shift:R Road brake levers to work with the Pinion. Here are the basic specs from Tout Terrain:

  • Cinq Shift:R Road option for improved shifting ergonomics
  • Ultra-reliable Pinion gearbox
  • Drop-bar options provide aerodynamics and multiple hand positions
  • Stratocrom Tubeset balances strength, weight and ride characteristics
  • Gates Carbon Drive belt-compatible frame for a strong, clean and reliable drivetrain
  • Classic diamond style frame
  • Frame braze-ons for optional Tubus rear rack, or go with bikepacking bags

The new Scrambler comes in several color options and configurations. All bikes are painted and assembled by hand Gundelfingen, Germany. Complete models start at €3,890.00, and The Scrambler Xplore GT II 275 and the Scrambler Xplore GT II 28 can be ordered now directly or through your local Tout Terrain dealer with deliveries starting in early 2021.
