The All New 2021 Giant Fathom Series

We’re always happy to see another affordable alloy hardtail that still offer a progressive trail-ready geometry, good components, and bikepacking-friendly features. A hardtail mountain bike with slightly larger than average tires is a great option for bikepacking, particularly for newer riders who are just getting started. The new 2021 Giant Fathom lineup has received some major updates, including a redesigned aluminum frame, a 12-speed drivetrain, and an additional bottle mount. With prices ranging from $1,250 to $1,600 USD, the new Fathom looks like a nice value-focused option for a versatile hardtail. Find highlights, photos, and build options below.

2021 Giant Fathom Overview

  • New ALUXX SL aluminum frame with predominately 6011 alloy, offers great strength-to-weight ratios.
  • New forged chainstay for larger tire clearance (up to 27.5 x 2.6″) and shorter rear centre.
  • 27.5 x 2.6″ and 29 x 2.5″ builds available.
  • All models come with Giant Crest 34 130mm fork with adjustable volume spacers and damper.
  • All models come with Giant Contact Switch dropper post and lever.
  • Medium, large, and X-large frames can now carry up to three bottles.
  • All models are now based around 1x drivetrains, allowing larger tire clearances.
  • Boost hub spacing across the board.
  • Internally routed cables.
  • More balanced geometry, featuring 66° headtube and 75° seattube angles.

There are a total of four different Fathom build options for 2021, offering two different sub-$2,000 price points in both 27.5″ and 29″ wheel options. The base Fathom 1 27.5″ is available in one colour, while the other builds are all offered in two. Here’s a quick breakdown on each of the different builds.

2021 Giant Fathom 1

  • SRAM SX Eagle 1×12 Drivetrain
  • Shimano MT401 Hydraulic Brakes
  • Giant AM 27.5, tubeless ready rims
  • Maxxis Ardent Race 27.5 x 2.6″ tires
  • Giant Crest 34 RCL, 130mm fork
  • $1,600 USD

2021 Giant Fathom 2

  • Shimano Deore / SX Eagle 1×12 Drivetrain
  • Tektro TKD143 Brakes
  • Giant AM 27.5, tubeless ready rims
  • Maxxis Ardent Race 27.5 x 2.6″ tires
  • Giant Crest 34 RCL, 130mm fork
  • $1,250 USD

2021 Giant Fathom 29 1

  • SRAM SX Eagle 1×12 Drivetrain
  • Shimano MT401 Hydraulic Brakes
  • Giant AM 29, tubeless ready rims
  • Maxxis DHF, 29×2.5″ (front), Maxxis Aggressor, 29×2.5″ (rear)
  • Giant Crest 34 RCL, 130mm fork
  • $1,600 USD

2021 Giant Fathom 29 2

  • Shimano Deore 1×12 Drivetrain
  • Tektro TKD143 Brakes
  • Giant AM 29, tubeless ready rims
  • Maxxis Minion DHF, 29×2.5″ (front), Maxxis Aggressor, 29×2.5″ (rear)
  • Giant Crest 34 RCL, 130mm fork
  • $1,250 USD
