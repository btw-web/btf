Wolf Tooth’s New Quick Release Seatpost Clamp

Wolf Tooth’s new machined-in-Minnesota Quick Release Seatpost Clamp is made of 6061 aluminum and is compatible with carbon, steel, alloy, and titanium frames that have a circular seat tube in one of six different diameters: 28.6, 29.8, 31.8, 34.9, 36.4, and 38.6mm. The clamp is designed with a brass bushing and a cylindrical steel bolt to open and close the clamp, which Wolf Tooth claims operates smoothly and ensures a very precise level of force when closed.

The Wolf Tooth Quick Release Seatpost Clamp comes in eight color options—red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, black, and silver—that match other anodized Wolf Tooth products, such as their headsets, dropper levers, and bottle cage bolts. As mentioned, the QR clamp is also available in six sizes to fit about any bike. Find more over at WolfToothComponents.com.
