Collective Reward #084: Copies of The Bikepacking Journal 05

We’re offering up a stack of copies of the fifth issue of The Bikepacking Journal for our latest Collective Reward giveaway, and 20 randomly selected new and existing Bikepacking Collective members who joined after the October 17th deadline to receive the previous issue will receive one along with their copy of next month’s upcoming sixth issue.

In the fifth issue of The Bikepacking Journal, we were thrilled to present new stories and photos from a handful of people we’ve long admired, as well as a few fresh voices and first-time writers of whom we’re incredibly proud and excited about highlighting. Some shared about the joy and grief they were processing during the most tumultuous year many of us can remember. Others looked back through their archives to relive and reexamine trips that won’t be possible again for the foreseeable future.

Want to know more about what’s inside? Take a look here. And if you want to get a look at the kind of stories we publish in The Bikepacking Journal, check out the web versions of Slower, Ditibised, and Plains Perspective, feature stories from previous issues.
