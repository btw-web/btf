Collective Reward #092: Ortlieb Handlebar-Pack QR Bags

Continuing our latest batch of Collective Reward gear giveaways, we’ve teamed up with Ortlieb and three lucky members of our Bikepacking Collective will be receiving one of their interesting new Handlebar-Pack QR bags, which you can read all about in Logan’s recent review here. These innovative front bags are fully waterproof, require relatively little vertical clearance, and feature Ortlieb’s unique and super stable Bar-Lock attachment system, which also helps eliminate cable rub and interference.

Ortlieb Handlebar-Pack QR Specs

  • 11-liter capacity
  • 530g weight
  • Roll-top closure
  • Fully waterproof construction
  • External side pockets
  • Internal compression straps
  • Reflective accents
  • Outer straps with CamLock fasteners

Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, and members are automatically entered to win bi-monthly Collective Rewards (including one of these Ortlieb bags), receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, have access to exclusive industry discounts, and lots more.

Find complete details on the Handlebar-Pack QR over at Ortlieb.com.
