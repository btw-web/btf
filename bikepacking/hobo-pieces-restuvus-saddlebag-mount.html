Introducing Hobo Pieces ‘Restuvus’ Saddlebag Mount

Interested in bikepacking with a traditional saddlebag, but your saddle doesn’t have haul loops? Entrepreneur Chad Roddy has a solution. The Restuvus is a CNC aluminum clamp mount designed allow larger saddlebags to install on non-traditional saddles. The Restuvus weighs 118g and will come in six anodized colors. According to Chad, the weight capacity is about 20 pounds (9 kg).

In addition, Hobo Pieces will be launching co-branded bag collaborations including the Ellum Bag Works Ursa Major saddlebag and a leopard print Outer Shell Stem Caddy. There is also a slew of goodies including hats, stickers, and a scarf to coincide with the brand launch.

The Restuvus saddlebag mount and corresponding gear is available through Hobo Pieces online store. Also, follow @HoboPieces on Instagram to stay up to date. Photos by Aimee Nutt.
