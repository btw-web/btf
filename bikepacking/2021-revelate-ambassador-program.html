2021 Revelate Designs Ambassador Program Announced

Revelate Designs just opened up applications for their 2021 Ambassador Program—a great opportunity for bikepackers involved with their cycling community and who advocate for change within our industry. Successful applicants will have access to gear, community connections, and support from Revelate Designs.

Here are additional details from Revelate Designs:

Things we love: Thoughtfully taken photos of your adventures, learning about ways you use our products, involvement in growing the bikepacking community, creatively told stories and photos of your adventures, new route development, cyclists pushing the envelope and raising the bar in adventure biking, advocates for public land use, diversity, equity and inclusivity in cycling, creative minds using their love of human powered adventures to inspire their art. If this sounds like you then click on, if not, keep reading as there might be a better way for us to connect.

What we’re not looking for: Sponsoring single trips or races, donation requests, requests for event or team sponsorships.

Find where you fit: Did you just go bikepacking or hear about our product, have you taken some photos on a trip or have a sweet one planned? That’s awesome and we’re honored to be a part of your journey! If you have social media please follow @revelatedesigns and tag us in your photos (big and small), follow us on Instagram or Facebook to be notified of exciting contests and more ways to connect with the community!

How to apply: The application can be found here.

We get a lot of requests for sponsorship and as much as we would love to fulfill them all, we simply are unable to. Please don’t feel discouraged if you are not selected. You should still celebrate the freedom and joy that adventuring by bike brings! Your presence in the bikepacking community is needed and your contributions are appreciated. We keep applications on file and if a future opportunity arises that we feel you would excel at, we will reach out to you!

The deadline to apply is November 15th, 2020. Find all the details and the application form at RevelateDesigns.com.
