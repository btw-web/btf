Ayesha McGowan on Representation in Cycling (Video)

Speaking on behalf of QBP at Frosbike 2019, Ayesha McGowan (@ayesuppose) gave a presentation on diversity and inclusivity, and why representation matters within the cycling industry. Ayesha is a professional cyclist with Liv Racing, a creator of music, video, and photography, as well as a granddaughter, daughter, and wife. Take the time to watch her entire presentation below to learn more about what you can do to encourage representation in cycling.

Find more of Ayesha’s work, including some helpful workshops, on her website AQuickBrownFox.com. And be sure to check out her podcast on SoundCloud.
