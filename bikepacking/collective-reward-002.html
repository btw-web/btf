Collective Reward #002: Porcelain Rocket Bags

For this month’s second Collective Reward, Canadian bikepacking bag maker Porcelain Rocket has offered up a brand new Mr. Fusion seat pack (complete with mini rack, harness, seat post collar, and dry bag) as well as a waterproof 52Hz Frame Pack. One lucky Bikepacking Collective member will take them both home. If you’re not already a member of The Bikepacking Collective, get signed up for your chance to own these bags!

52Hz Frame Pack

The 52Hz Frame Pack is Porcelain Rocket’s expedition-ready frame bag. Its welded-seam construction and roll-top closure make for a fully waterproof way to keep your gear dry. We’ve been using these packs on dusty tracks around the globe since they were first announced and can wholeheartedly recommend them for their durability and ingenuity. One winner will receive a brand new 52Hz Frame Pack in the size of their choice.

Mr. Fusion Seat Pack

Logan’s green Mr. Fusion seat pack has seen a lot. Even with well over 5,000 miles, it still works perfectly. Its stable design and removable waterproof bag make the Mr. Fusion a top notch seat pack. Like the 52Hz, Mr. Fusion was listed in our Bikepacking Gear That Lasts list. One winner will receive a brand new Mr. Fusion in the size of their choice.
