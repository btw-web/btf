Bikepacking with Dzil Ta’ah Adventures on the Surly Blog

Jon Yazzie of Dzil Ta’ah Adventures just published a good read over on the Surly Bikes blog, diving deeper into the sub-24-hour bikepacking trip that earned a top pick in our 2021 Film & Photo Bikepacking Awards. The film, It Has Become Beauty Again, documents Jon’s journey to connect with his Native land through bikepacking and find his place in the world. For a written reflection on the same theme, head over to the Surly blog.
