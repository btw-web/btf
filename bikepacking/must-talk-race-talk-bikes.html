Why We Must Talk about Race When We Talk About Bikes

Bicycling Magazine just launched a series of 14 stories titled, Why We Must Talk about Race When We Talk About Bikes. The series aims to tackle systemic racism within cycling and includes stories from Black athletes, advocates, and activists. Read each of the individual stories over at Bicycling.com.
