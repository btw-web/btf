To all a good night…

This year held challenges for all of us on many different levels. But despite the unprecedented isolation and tragedy that 2020 brought with it, we’re grateful that it’s also been a year of positive change and production. We sincerely thank all of you for coming along for the ride. With that, we’re signing off for the holidays and preparing for our respective Good Night 2020 campouts, to reflect on 2020, bid it farewell, and ponder what’s to come. We look forward to “seeing” you all out there between December 26th and the 31st to reenergize and dream about what’s around the corner. If you haven’t already, make sure to sign up and learn about the overnighter challenge below. Enjoy the holidays and have a good night…

About Good Night 2020

Good Night 2020 is a local overnighter challenge between December 26th and 31st, inviting everyone to sleep under the stars, have a good night outdoors, and send off an otherwise crappy year. Learn more about the event and how to win one of five new bikepacking bags from Rockgeist while participating.

We’ll be back on Monday with a partial schedule and more fresh content.
