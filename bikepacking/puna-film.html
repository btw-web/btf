Puna (film)

Filmmaker Fernando Biagioni (@biciclico) and his riding partner spent two weeks bikepacking into the far corners of the high-altitude Argentinian altiplano. Along the way he made this film that beautifully captures this magical place. Like many bike trips, the film is about asking questions and trying to understand why we choose to travel through remote landscapes. Every trip is a chance to reconnect with ourselves, and in the current world scenario, a moment to reinvent this collective loneliness which we are facing so vividly. Watch the film below and scroll down find a gallery of photos and the route map from the trip…

What is the impulse that makes us leave home towards the unknown? Perhaps this is the moment to reinvent this collective loneliness in which we live in. Reinvent life. Reinvent reinvention.

We rode 600km in two weeks through the Argentinian altiplano. We came back without many of the answers we were looking for. But we came back with the certainty that we were looking in the right places.
