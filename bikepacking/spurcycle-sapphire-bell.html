Spurcycle’s Sapphire Bell Supports Bicycle Non-Profits

With an unmistakable chime that’s both pleasant and loud, the Spurcycle Bell is one of the nicest bicycle bells out there. All of Spurcycle’s original bells are made in the USA, and with such obvious attention to detail, they’re more like a small piece of art than your standard bike bell. Spurcycle’s latest limited edition run, the Sapphire Bell, features a beautiful sapphire brass striker and Spurcycle is donating 25$ from every bell sold to two community non-profits: San Francisco Bicycle Coalition and Bend Bikes.

The Spurcycle Sapphire Bell retails for $69 USD and is available exclusively through Spurcycle.com.

Logan was very impressed with the Spurcycle Bell in our Bike Bell Shootout, calling it the most “attractive of the bunch” with the longest and highest pitch.
