Collective Reward #027: Thomson Components

For Collective Reward #027, we’re giving away a complete cockpit from Thomson Bike Products. One member of our Bikepacking Collective will receive their choice of Thomson’s Elite X4 Stem, Elite Seatpost, Seatpost Collar, and Alloy Drop Bars or MTB Aluminum Bars. All total, that’s around $400 worth of beautifully machined parts.

Thomson’s precision components hardly need an introduction. They’ve earned their reputation as manufacturers of incredibly high quality bike bits, and there’s a reason you’ve likely noticed them on most of the “dream builds” featured here on the site. They’re super strong, lightweight, and really good looking (at least in our opinion). And if you’re a supporter of the site, this suite of parts could soon be yours.

If you join our Bikepacking Collective by 11:59 pm EST on Wednesday, April 17th, you’ll be eligible to win this Thomson cockpit AND you’ll receive the upcoming second issue of The Bikepacking Journal, our biannually printed publication. If you join after that cutoff date, you’ll still be eligible for the Thomson giveaway (and all the current Collective Reward drawings), but your first issue of The Bikepacking Journal will be Issue 03 in October. Already a member? You’re entered to win. Thanks for your support!
