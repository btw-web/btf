New Lezyne CNC Tubeless Drive and CNC TLR Valves

Just announced, Lezyne’s new multi-function hand pump and tubeless valves both show some merit from a bikepacking perspective. The CNC Tubeless Drive is a high-volume hand pump with a tire plugging kit and C02 inflator integrated into the body of the pump, while the CNC TLR Valves add a bit of flare to your bike and have two different tools built right into their valve caps. Find specs, photos, and videos on both below.

Lezyne CNC Tubeless Drive

Combining bike tools into handy all-in-one designs is nothing new, but is certainly less common when it comes to hand pumps. We’ve been big fans of a few of Lezyne’s products for some time, and the new CNC Tubeless Drive looks like a great option to check a few of those mandatory bikepacking tools off your list. It’s a 3-in-1 mountain bike hand pump designed specifically with tubeless repair and inflation in mind—combining a tubeless tire repair kit, CO2 inflator and a high-volume hand pump within a machined aluminum body.

The head of the pump is compatible with standard presta valves, with or without the valve core installed, and the C02 inflator is there to help reseat stubborn tires. Under the threaded top cap, there is room for the included tubeless repair kit, which includes five plugs and a fork-style insertion tool, as well as one 20g C02 cartridge and inflator adapter. The pump has a max pressure of 30 PSI (2 BAR), weighs 276 grams, and is 173mm (6.8″) long. Watch Lezyne’s product launch video below for a closer look.

  • Material: Machined Aluminum
  • Weight: 276 grams
  • Price: $79.99 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Ride.Lezyne.com

Lezyne CNC TLR Valves

The new Lezyne CNC TLR Valves are constructed from CNC machined aluminum, and come equipped with an O-ring and oversized aluminum locknut for a better seal. As a bonus, the valve cap is grooved for better grip and has a valve core tool and a T25 tip for tightening the valve body on the rim. The CNC TLR Valves are avaiable in four colors; blue, red, yellow, and black, and 44mm, 60mm, and 88mm lengths.

  • Material: Aluminum
  • Weight: 6.4 grams (44mm), 14g (60mm), 16g (80mm)
  • Price: $19.99 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Ride.Lezyne.com

The CNC Tubeless Drive and CNC TLR Valves are available through your local Lezyne dealer. Find yours here.
