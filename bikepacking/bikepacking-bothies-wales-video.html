Bikepacking & Bothies in the Elan Valley, Wales (Video)

Juliet Elliot is a multi-discipline cyclist, a journalist, freelance creative, British Cycling coach, and editor of Bikes n’ Stuff. Her most recent video follows three riders on a bikepacking trip through the Elan Valley in Wales, linking together backcountry bothies, and what appear to be more than a few river crossings. Juliet’s personality is contagious and she has a great way of connecting with her audience. Check out the video, plus a great set of photos provided by Tom Farrell (@fomtaz) of UK-based The Woods Cyclery, below.

The group would like to thank Brother Cycles and The Woods Cyclery for the support. And we’d like to thank the group for having such a good attitude.
