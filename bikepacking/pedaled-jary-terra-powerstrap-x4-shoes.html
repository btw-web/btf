PEdALED x Fizik Jary Terra Powerstrap X4 Shoes

Collaborating with the team at Fizik, PEdALED has released a new shoe as part of their off-road-specific Jary Collection, the Jary Terra Powerstrap X4. Here’s what PEdALED has to say about the new shoe: “Designed for the dirt, the Jary Terra Powerstrap combines comfort and performance to help you power up steep climbs, stay locked in on loose descents-even hike your bike when needed. From quick escapes down quiet roads to longer trips further ahead, the Jary Terra Powerstrap, just like the rest of our Jary Collection, is built for the kind of rides that start where the pavement ends.”

The PEdALED Jary Terra Powerstrap X4 comes in a fresh colourway to match the rest of the Jary Collection, including some subtle gold Japanese lettering. Otherwise, the shoe features all the same specs as Fizik’s regular Terra Powerstrap X4, including their powerstrap closure, a reliable rubber sole, and a stiffness rating of 6/10 for all-day comfort.
