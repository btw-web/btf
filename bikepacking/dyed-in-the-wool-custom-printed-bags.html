Check Out These Custom Printed Bags From Dyed In The Wool

Looking for a special bag with an interesting pattern or illustration? Poland-based Dyed in the Wool now offers custom printed Cordura and X-PAC VX21 with any color, pattern, or picture that you can dream up. The process is imprinted on the fabric using high resolution digital print, so DITW insists that no detail will get lost.

These particular bags are printed on Dyneema with graphics from Hashimoto Gahō. The bags are fully tape sealed with waterproof alpha zippers, Fidlock clasps, and the signature DITW branded tape. The set consists of mini seat pack, top tube bag and The Everywhere bag.

Dyed In The Wool custom printed bags cost is an extra €60 and require up to four weeks for production. Learn more over at DyedInTheWool.eu.
