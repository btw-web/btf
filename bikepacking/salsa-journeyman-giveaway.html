Salsa Journeyman Sora 650B Highlights

Journeyman Specific Aluminum Tubing

Journeyman utilizes the same custom toptube and downtube we developed for the Warbird Aluminum, with new seatstays, chainstays, and dropouts in 6061-T6 aluminum to match. The entire frame is then heat treated to T6 alloy standards for extra strength and durability

650b x 2.1” Wheel/Tire Spec

650b wheels with 2.1” tires provide a high-volume, plush ride and increased traction for increased stability on rougher surfaces

Salsa Cowbell Drop Bar Handlebar

Drop bars provide multiple hand positions, increasing comfort and efficiency on long rides. They also provide the rider a more aerodynamic position when stiff headwinds arise

Fantail Deluxe Fork

Feature-packed carbon fork with a tapered steerer and flat-mount brake. A crown light mount, fender eyelets, mid-blade mounts for low-rider racks, and Three-Pack mounts for our Anything Cages or water bottles help add to the potential length of your rides

Journeyman Geometry

Journeyman geometry was developed from years of riding our Vaya and Warbird. Taking cues from both, Journeyman is comfortable stable and confidence inspiring whether it’s your daily driver, you’re ripping up dirt paths into and through town, exploring unfamiliar terrain, or entering your first gravel race. Spirited when you want it to be, but relaxed enough to leave you with the energy for long rides

Full-Length Internal Cable Routing

Full-length housing improves shifting performance in poor conditions and increases use time between maintenance intervals. Internal routing for a clean, uncluttered look.

Front and Rear Fender Mount

Integrated front and rear fender mounts for drier riding in inclement weather.

Front and Rear Rack Compatible

Low-rider rack capability on the fork with mid-blade mounts with integrated mounts for a rear rack. For optimal rear rack fit, we recommend our Wanderlust Rack

Toptube Bag Mounts

Install bolt-on top tube bags using Journeyman’s integrated toptube mounts
