Trade Garbage for Bikepacking Bags with Dyed in the Wool

Dyed in the Wool’s (DITW) latest project is an awesome one that we hope everyone can get involved in. The concept is simple. In an effort to keep our trails clean, DITW is trading photographic proof that you collected garbage out on the trail for discounts on their bikepacking bags.

They’re offering 5% off for every two bags of rubbish collected from your local area, with a maximum discount of 15% on orders over 100€. The more you collect, the more you can save. To claim your discount, take a photo before and after, plus a picture of all the collected bags with rubbish, then email it to them at info@dyedinthewool.eu and tag #ditwtrailcleanup on your Instagram or Facebook. There is no limit on entries, so let’s get cleaning!

Additionally, they’ll draw a winner from all the entries every six months. The randomly selected person will receive a surprise gift from their collection.

Learn more at DyedInTheWool.eu.
