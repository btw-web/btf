Hi Hey Hello Magazine Women’s History Month Giveaway

Hi Hey Hello is a print magazine celebrating women’s outdoor culture, showcasing a new wave of women’s style, fresh attitudes, and the need for increased inclusion. The magazine is owned by Sierra Domaille & Juhee Kim, who’ve teamed up with seven other women-founded businesses to bring an awesome giveaway in celebration of Women’s History Month. Prizes include gift cards from Swift Industries, Wild Rye clothing, a coffee subscription from Equator Coffee, Fernweh dehydrated meals, subscriptions to the magazine, and much more.

All you have to do is add your email on the giveaway page to enter; two winners will be chosen at random. The giveaway is only open to US residents only and you must be over 18 to enter. The contest ends on March 30th at 11:59 p.m. PST. Head to HeyHiHelloMagazine.com to get signed up for the drawing.
