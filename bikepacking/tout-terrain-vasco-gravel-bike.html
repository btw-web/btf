Introducing the Tout Terrain Vasco Gravel Bike

The new Tout Terrain Vasco is a performance-minded drop-bar bike that looks to push the capabilities of the gravel/all-road category. Although the Vasco may not have the massive tire clearance we’ve come to expect from many gravel bikes, it looks like a versatile option for those riding a mix of gravel and paved roads. It’s built up around a chromoly steel frame, either a carbon or steel fork, and has clearance for 29 x 1.75″ or 27.5 x 2.1″ tires.

The Vasco is offered in seven complete builds—three with 27.5″ wheels and four with 29″. Tout Terrain describes the Vasco to have a “sporty, yet comfortable geometry” that can handle challenging trails. Find highlights below, followed by details on Tout Terrain’s three different gravel forks.

Tout Terrain Vasco Highlights

  • Available in two distinct models: Vasco GT 275 and Vasco GT 28
  • Seven builds to choose from
  • 4130 Chromoly Steel frame and either carbon or steel fork
  • 1x and 2x drivetrains options
  • Rear rack mounts, two bottle mounts, and bolt-on frame bag compatibility

Tout Terrain Allroad Fork

  • Material: Chromoly Steel
  • Fork Length: 395mm
  • Fork Offset: 45mm
  • Axle: 12x100mm thru-axle
  • Tire Clearance: 29 x 1.5″ / 27.5 x 1.75″
  • Weight: 1,275g

Tout Terrain Expedition Fork

  • Material: Chromoly Steel
  • Fork Length: 405mm
  • Fork Offset: 45mm
  • Axle: 12x100mm thru-axle
  • Tire Clearance: 29 x 1.75″ / 27.5 x 2.25″
  • Weight: 1,255g

Cinq Touring Fork II

  • Material: Carbon
  • Fork Length: 405mm
  • Fork Offset: 47mm
  • Axle: 12x100mm thru-axle or 15x100mm
  • Tire Clearance: 29 x 2.0″ / 27.5 x 2.10″
  • Weight: 540g

Complete build prices range from €1,890 to €2,590 (~$2,217 to $3,038 USD) and are available now on Tout-Terrain.de.
