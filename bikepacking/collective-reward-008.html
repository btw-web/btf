Collective Reward #008: Rockgeist Custom Framebag and Saddlebag

Designed and sewn in the Appalachian Mountains, Rockgeist’s thoughtfully crafted custom and universal fit gear is some of the best out there. For our eighth Collective Reward, Rockgeist is giving away a custom Fiber Flight Framebag and a Foxglove Saddlebag to one member of the Bikepacking Collective. If you’re already a member, you’re entered to win. If not, click here to join the Bikepacking Collective.

Custom Fiber Flight Framebag

On offer is one custom Fiber Flight Framebag in the winner’s choice of Dyneema 40, Cuben Fiber Hybrid, or X-Pac LiteSkin fabric. Rockgeist makes all of their custom frame packs specific to the bike using their PhotoFit process, so all the winner will need to do is take a photo with a measuring tape and email it in. The winner of this Collective Reward will have their choice of a Mudlust Triangular, Mudlust Fully (for full-suspension bikes), Mudlust Half Frame Bag, Mudlust Rolltop, or a Custom Wedge Frame Bag like the one we used on the All-City Gorilla Monsoon.

Foxglove Saddlebag

The Foxglove is Rockgeist’s minimalist saddlebag designed for maximum volume to weight ratio. Topping out at 7.5 liters, the Foxglove is a mid-sized saddlebag weighing only 7.9 oz (223 g). This design is intended for the minimalist bikepacker, endurance racer, or anyone looking to travel efficiently. The stable shape profile, conservative volume, and cam-action webbing ensures a solid, sway-free anchor to your seat.

The Foxglove is also an excellent choice for riders with minimal space between the rear tire and saddle (like size small frames, bikes with rear racks, 29ers, or 29+ tires). Only 6″ between the tire and the rails of the seat is required, and as little as 2″ of exposed seatpost. The Foxglove is built with Hypalon® rubber reinforcement, a VX42 bottom panel, HDPE plastic support on sides and bottom, closed cell foam panels at base, and an optional reflective strip. As with other Rockgeist bags, the winner can select from an array of color choices. See more details about the Foxglove here.
