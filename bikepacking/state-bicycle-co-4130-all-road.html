The New State Bicycle Co. 4130 All-Road

State Bicycle Co.’s 4130 All-Road is the first offering in the brand’s newly announced Offroad Division. Marking a departure from their well-known line of single speed city bikes, the new 4130 All-Road is packed with features that State Bicycle Co. believes makes it “one of the most capable and affordable bikes on the market for gravel, commuting, touring, and beyond.” At first glance, it looks like they may be right. The 4130 All-Road is based around a 4130 Chromoly Steel frame, has options for both 700c and 650b wheels, a 1×11 drivetrain, and geometry centred around all-day riding. The frame is kitted out with two standard bottle mounts, four-pack mounts on the fork, front and rear rack mounts, as well as full fender compatibility.

State Bicycle Co. decided to offer the choice between 700c wheels with 38mm tires or 650b with 1.9” tires for the same price, or you can buy two complete wheelsets for an extra $349.99 to easily swap between the two. This price includes complete wheels, rotors, cassettes, tires, and tubes, which is great to see. The complete builds are specced with State’s flared All-Road bars, crankset, cassette, derailleur, and shifter, plus a standard BSA 68mm threaded bottom bracket and 27.2mm seatpost. The frame also uses 12 x 100/142mm thru-axles, which is a pleasant surprise considering it’s sub-$1,000 price tag.

  • Frame 4130 Chromoly Steel

  • Headset FSA ZS-4D
  • Handlebars SBC All-Road Bars, 18° flare / 500mm wide
  • Stem Alloy 3D Forged (90,100,110mm)
  • Seatpost Alloy 27.2mm, 350mm
  • Saddle State All-Road Saddle, Steel Rails
  • Crankset SBC All-Road 1 Forged 7075 AL, 42t Ring
  • Bottom Bracket Hollow Axle BSA 68mm
  • Chain YBN 11-Speed
  • Hubs Sealed-Bearing Thru-Axle, 12×100 / 12x142mm
  • Wheels 700c or 650b, 19mm internal width
  • Tires Panaracer Gravel King, Tubeless, 700 x 38 or 650b x 47mm
  • Brakes Promax DSK-300R Flat Mount, 160mm rotors
  • Cassette SBC 11-42T 11-Speed
  • Shifters SBC All-Road 1, 11-Speed

The New State Bicycle Co. 4130 All-Road comes in two colours: Pigeon Gray or Desert Tan, and four difference sizes to fit riders from 5’1” to 6’5” tall. Although they offer some add-ons such as locks, lights, and pedals, the stock complete build retails for just $799.99 (with either 700c or 650b wheels) or $1,149.98 for both wheel sizes. The 4130 All-Road is also available to purchase a frameset for $369.99, and all of State Bicycle Co.’s new All-Road components are also available to purchase separately—including their All-Road 1×11 groupset. Learn more at StateBicycle.com.
