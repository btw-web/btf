The Magic Hour Solstice Cycling Challenge

Presented by The Service Course (@theservicecourse), the Magic Hour is an invitation to all cyclists around the world to celebrate the summer solstice by making the most of the longest days of the year.⁣ Every hour you spend riding between today and June 21st earns you one entry into the prize drawing.

Prizes include a full Service Course kit (cap, jersey, gilet, bib shorts, bottles, and socks), a year’s supply of Pelotan sun protection, a Wahoo ELEMNT BOLT GPS bike computer, and a pair of Alba Optics sunglasses of your choice. There are also bonus prizes for the most amazing rides and best photos posted to Instagram and tagged with @pelotan.cc.

How to Enter

There’s no right or wrong way to ride your Magic Hours and you can take on any terrain using any bike. To enter to win, submit your total number of hours ridden and links to your rides between the 18th and 21st using the short form on the Service Course’s Magic Hour landing page, linked below. Winners will be notified via email on Friday, June 25th.

Find complete details on the Magic Hour and register your ride over at TheServiceCourse.cc.
