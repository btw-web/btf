Check out the New Hunt Land Cruiser

Debuted at this year’s Hunt 1000 event, the Land Cruiser is the latest frameset from Australia-based Hunt Bikes. It’s a titanium drop-bar gravel bike designed for fast, off-road riding that’s made to “take you almost anywhere.” The frame has externally routed cables, a standard and triple pack mount inside the main triangle, additional mounts under the downtube and on the top tube, plus rack and fender mounts. The frameset is paired with Hunt’s adventure carbon fork, which has triple pack mounts, internal brake routing, and dynamo routing. The fork also has a flip-chip style dropout for either a 47mm or 52mm offset.

The Hunt Land Cruiser features 12mm thru-axles, clearance for 29 x 2.0″ or 27.5 Ã— 2.35″ tires, and has internal dropper post routing for those looking to get rad. It’s offered in four sizes (S-XL) and is available now via preorder. Preordered frames have a lead time of around 12 weeks.

  • Bottom Bracket: 68mm T47
  • Seatpost: 31.6mm
  • Rear Axle Spacing: 142x12mm
  • Front Axle Spacing: 100x12mm
  • Brake Mount: Flat mount 160mm rotor
  • Chainring Clearance: SRAM Single 42T round or 50/34T Double

The Hunt Land Cruiser costs $3,521 AUD (~$2,560 USD) and can be found at HuntBikes.com.
