Bikepacking Kangaroo Island (video)

Kangaroo Island is southwest of Adelaide off the mainland of South Australia. Over one third of the island is protected as a nature reserve, home to native wildlife such as sea lions, koalas, and an array of bird species. Mick Turnbull and Danielle O’Hara loaded their rigs onto the ferry and set out to explore the island by bike. Along the way, Mick captured footage for this video using a Panasonic GH5 with 10-25mm f/1.7 and 35-100mm f/2.8 lenses. Watch it below and be sure to check out Mick’s previous film “The Old Ghan” linked in the related content grid at the bottom of the post…

“If gravel roads, beautiful beaches, bird-watching, and seal-spotting are your thing, get the ferry, get on your bike, and get exploring.”
