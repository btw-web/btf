Wizard Works Go-Go Top Tube Bag Now in 3 Sizes

Wizard Works just added a new size to their range of Go-Go Top Tube Bags. Based on feedback, some of their customers thought the original Go-Go Top Tube Bag was a little too tall for some bikes with shorter steerer tube stack or a racier profile. To answer those requests and round out the range, the third size has the same footprint as the regular Go-Go with a little less height.

The new 110 gram Small Go-Go has a volume of 0.8L (0.2L less than the Regular) and measures 21cm wide, 7cm deep, and 8cm tall. As with the other two sizes, the Small Go-Go features recycled HDPE inserts to help it keep its shape and make is easy to open and close with one hand. The Go-Go also comes with a “Dog-Bone” foam spacer for stability where it meets the stem bolts, and has a grippy Hypalon daisy chain and comes with three Velcro One-Wrap straps to give it the ability to work with about any bike and a variety of other bags.
