Brixton Builds: Surly Ghost Grappler (Video)

In case you’re not yet tired of Surly Ghost Grappler coverage, here’s one more for ya. This new video from Brixton Cycles in London documents their custom Ghost Grappler, which was built for launch day last week. Watch it below, followed by a basic build list. From Brixton: “The only way to celebrate the release of this long-awaited Surly Ghost Grappler was, of course, a full Brixton Build. We put our collective heads together to spec a robust, trail-ready bike that can be treated accordingly – a carefree riding experience, without fear of needing a loan to cover the cost of that smashed derailleur.”

  • Frame – Surly Ghost Grappler
  • Wheels – Halo Ridge Line
  • Tyres – Teravail Honcho 27.5” x 2.6” (Durable)
  • Bars – Salsa Woodchipper
  • Seatpost – Thomson Masterpiece
  • Chainset – Surly OD 1x
  • Shifters – MicroSHIFT Bar-End
  • Rear Derailleur – Shimano Deore 10-speed
  • Brakes – Avid BB7-S

To learn more about this build, head over to Brixton’s blog.
