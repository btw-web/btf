Collective Reward #019: Copies of The Bikepacking Journal 01

Firstly, we’d like to thank everyone who supported and trusted our vision for launching a print magazine by joining the Bikepacking Collective even before the first issue was out in the world. However, we know many of you joined after our inaugural issue had sold out (and it happened very quickly!) for one reason or another. Lucky for you, after fulfilling mail returns and damaged freight, we have a handful of extra copies left over. And for Collective Reward #019, we’re giving away our last 20 copies of the sold out first issue of The Bikepacking Journal to members who joined between October 17th, 2018, and next Friday, February 1st, 2019. Here’s your chance to help support the site and to have a complete set of Bikepacking Journals on your bookshelf.

Inside The Bikepacking Journal 01:

The inaugural issue of The Bikepacking Journal is bursting with over a dozen pedal-powered stories and essays that span 148 colorful pages. From remote fjords in Greenland, to muddy passes in the Andes Mountains, to dusty roads in South Africa, we’ve searched the globe for the best writing and most beautiful photographs captured from the saddle of a bicycle. We invite you to join our contributors as they share the stories of the many interesting people they’ve encountered while riding, reflect on what they’ve learned along the way, and offer intimate glimpses of some of our planet’s most beautiful places.

As always, Bikepacking Collective membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, for the same price (we print The Bikepacking Journal in beautiful Berlin, after all). Members are automatically entered to win Collective Rewards. In this special instance, only members who missed the October 17th cutoff for Issue 01 are eligible. Not yet a member? Join before 11:59 pm EST on Friday, February 1st, 2019, and you’ll be automatically entered to win a free copy of The Bikepacking Journal 01 (plus all the other great Collective Rewards and additional benefits).
