WTB Venture Tires Available in 700 x 40 and 50mm Sizes

It’s clear WTB is taking the unmistakable surge in gravel bike popularity seriously, offering up a great selection of tires that are best suited for going fast on a gravel, hard pack, and occasional pavement. The Venture, released earlier this year, delivers optimal performance across the widest range of conditions. Originally only offered in a 47mm option, the updated version is available in 700c by 40mm and 50mm widths. A hard centre tread provides speed on hard pack surfaces, while the raised outer knobs offer some traction while cornering in the loose stuff. We don’t know about you, but that new 50mm option definitely caught our eye and we hope to get our hands on a set soon…
