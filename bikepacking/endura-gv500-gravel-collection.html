Endura’s New GV500 Gravel Collection

Endura is expanding its fall/winter collection to include a gravel-specific lineup for those riding deeper into the wet and cold of winter. The new GV500 collection includes a waterproof jacket, convertible pants/shorts, an insulated jacket, a short-sleeve jersey, a merino blend T-shirt, bib shorts, and riding shorts. Find details on the more interesting waterproof pieces below.

Endura GV500 Waterproof Jacket

The GV500 Waterproof Jacket shares the DNA of Endura’s MTR Shell. The jacket is pared down on features to keep bulk down, and as a result, the pack size is tiny and it’s lightweight. While you won’t find zippered vents on the jacket, it does have a chest pocket, which it can pack into, replacing the need for a separate stuff sack. It also features a hood and a long drop tail for rain and spray protection.

  • Fabric: 3-layer Exoshell40 fabric, PFC-free DWR finish
  • Sizes: XS-XXXL
  • Colors: Paprika, Olive, Black
  • Price: $199.99 USD

Endura GV500 Waterproof Trousers / Shorts

Also made from highly breathable, lightweight three-layer waterproof fabric in a fully seam-sealed construction and treated with a PFC-free, non-toxic durable water repellent finish, the GV500 Waterproof trousers and shorts provide lightweight packable protection for the legs. They use an elastic waistband, have reflective detailing, and zippered calves for easier removal.

  • Fabric: Three-layer Exoshell40 fabric, PFC-free DWR finish
  • Sizes: XS-XXXL
  • Colors: Black
  • Price: $159.99 USD (trouser), $99.99 (short)

Find details on the entire GV500 collection over at EnduraSport.com.
