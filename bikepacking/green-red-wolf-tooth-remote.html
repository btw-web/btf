New Green and Red Wolf Tooth Components ReMotes

Just in time for the holidays, Wolf Tooth Components just announced two new limited edition colors to their ReMote dropper post lever lineup: you guessed it, green and red. The ReMote is a high-quality mechanical dropper post lever with an oversized ball bearing pivot, low-profile lever, integrated cable adjustment, and a unique break-away axle pivot with inexpensive axles readily available. It’s an incredibly smooth and solid lever, making an easy upgrade to any mechanically actuated dropper post.

For a limited time, Wolf Tooth is offering the ReMote these colors that match existing Wolf Tooth red and green headsets, seatpost clamps, and anodized color accents. So for those who want to get matchy-matchy, and it’s the first time they’ve offered the ReMote in green. They are available only as a 22.2mm clamp option, unlike the black ReMote, which has a host of mounting options in both standard and light action. Once they sell out, they likely won’t be back for a while.

  • Material: 6061 T6 aluminum with Delrin axle
  • Weight: 38 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: USA
  • Price: $69.95 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com
