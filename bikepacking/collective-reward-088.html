Collective Reward #088: Wizard Works Lil Presto + Teeny Houdini Sets

It’s no secret that we’re admirers of London-based Wizard Works (@wizard.works), both for the bags the small British manufacturer is producing and the leadership role owners Veronica Lowe and Harry Major have taken on in the bikepacking community. Throughout the challenging economic times of the COVID-19 pandemic, they’ve been sharing an occasional Wizard Works From Home newsletter with DIY resources to make and modify your own gear. Last month, they donated more than $4,000 to benefit Refuge, a charity organization working to end domestic violence and support its victims. And, of course, they make an array of outstanding bags, many of which we’ve reviewed on the site.

Today, we’re glad to announce that we’re giving away three sets of Wizard Works bags to randomly selected members of our Bikepacking Collective. Each winner will receive Lil Presto barrel bag and a medium Teeny Houdini saddle bag in Wizard Works’ signature splatter colorway, as pictured above. That’s around a $150 value for each lucky member. Find details on each below.

Lil Presto in Black Camo

Wizard Works Lil Presto Specs

The Lil Presto is a burrito-style handlebar roll that’s designed to fit day-ride essentials, such as an extra layer, phone, snacks, etc. It can be mounted on the handlebars or on a saddle with saddle loops. There are a number of similar bags on the market, but this one packs in an impressive number of small features that set it apart. Its highly water-resistant construction has two side stash pockets, a daisy chain on the front for clipping on lights, and top loops for attaching a shoulder strap. It uses a Voile Nano strap handlebar attachment plus a headtube strap for extra stability, and an HDPE liner helps it keep its shape.

  • Volume: 2.15L
  • Size: 22cm wide x 14cm diameter
  • Weight: 250g
  • Price: £65 ($90)
  • More Details: Wizard.Works
Teeny Houdini in “Wiz Viz” Fluro Orange and Reflective

Wizard Works Teeny Houdini Specs

The Teeny Houdini is a minimal saddlebag that’s easy to install and is designed to hold the essentials, including a tube, tire lever, multi-tool, CO2, and other small bits. It fastens with a Fidlock magnetic buckle and can be opened without removing it from the bike. Inside, a bright yellow X-Pac liner adds extra visibility for finding small objects.

  • Volume: 0.4-0.5L
  • Size: 14 x 9 x 7 cm
  • Weight: 75g
  • Price: £42 ($58)
  • More Details: Wizard.Works
