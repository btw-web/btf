Collective Reward #025: Brooks Cambium Organic Light Saddles

Officially announced today, the new Cambium Organic Light saddles from Brooks England mark the next step in the evolution of the innovative Cambium line. Using the same vulcanized natural rubber and organic cotton top, the new Cambium Organic Light reduces the weight and replaces parts of the metal with liquid wood, a biopolymer made of 100% biodegradable surplus material.

Four of our Bikepacking Collective members will take home a new Brooks Cambium Organic Light saddle of their own, valued at $150. Thanks to the folks at Brooks England, we’re giving away a C15 Organic Light in natural, a C17 Organic Light in natural, a C17 Organic Light in brick, and a C19 Organic Light in natural.

I’ve been running a Cambium on both my commuter and my bikepacking rig for the past year and I’ve really enjoyed them. Personally, I find the Cambium immediately comfortable, hardwearing, and stylish. As such, I’m looking forward to sending these off to four of our supporters to try for themselves soon.
