FFF: 4 bikepacking videos that might make you head for the woods

The Escape

I love this video… I have watched it several times and it’s actually led me to look into getting a trailer. What a great way to do a trip! It’s worth watching on a bigger screen if you have the technology to do so.

the escape from Union Production Co. on Vimeo.

Bikerafting Alaska’s Lost Coast: Yakutat to Glacier Bay

I think I heard about this trip and video before I even knew that bikepacking was a thing. One day, I’d like to pull off such a trip…

Bikerafting Alaska’s Lost Coast: Yakutat to Glacier Bay. from LaceMine29.com on Vimeo.

The Hickory Stick

An awesome little bit of entertainment from the guys at Republic of Doom.

the hickory stick from Steve fassbinder on Vimeo.

Stargo Bohemia

Not the greatest quality for the actual video footage, but the content is nice, and in my opinion worth a watch… if only for the music track—a little Nebula, the former Fu Manchu guys.

stargo bohemia from Martin Dinse on Vimeo.
