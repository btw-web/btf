Preorders Open for New Alpkit Soloist XL

Alpkit describes their Soloist tent as a “lightweight, compact and stable backpacking tent with plenty of head room for multi-day wilderness adventures or extended bike tours.” It’s designed to be tall enough to sit up in, stable, and free-standing, and uses a single-pole structure that is quick to pitch. The tent uses compact pole segments and lightweight fabrics for a total weight of 2.6 pounds (regular) or 2.8 pounds (XL), and packs down to about the size of a wine bottle. The new Soloist XL offers an additional 20cm of interior length for a total of 220cm (7.2 feet), which is perfect for taller folks or for anyone who just wants some extra room at night.

Both sizes of the Soloist have a small integrated front vestibule, internal mesh pockets, and a hanging loop. The tent has multiple pitching options, including just the mesh inner or the fly + footprint for an ultralight setup. Like all Alpkit products, the Soloist comes with their 3 Year Alpine Bond, which is a warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Find additional specs below.

  • Total Weight: 1200g (regular) / 1318g (XL)
  • Packed Dimensions: 42 x 12cm (regular) / 44 x 13cm (XL)
  • Floor Dimensions: 200 x 95/77cm (regular) / 220 x 95/77cm (XL)
  • Outer Material: 20d Silicone coated Ripstop Polyester with PU backer
  • Inner Material: 20d Nylon Breathable Ripstop
  • Floor Material: 20d Ripstop Polyester PU
