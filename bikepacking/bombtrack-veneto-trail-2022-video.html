Bombtrack Presents: The Veneto Trail 2022 (Video)

Photos by Andrea Securo (@andreasecuro)

To announce the 2022 edition of the Veneto Trail unsupported bicycle adventure in Italy, Bombtrack and the organizers put together a lively video recap of last year’s event, which paints a vivid picture of the route as it winds its way through the Dolomites. It features a soundtrack by our friend Tommy (don’t miss his Reader’s Rig) and shows off the variety of tracks, terrain, and riders that make the event special.

We were there for 2021 to review a pair of Bombtrack Cale hardtails (find that linked below), and we can wholeheartedly recommend the Veneto Trail as a laid-back ride packed with impossibly scenic vistas and dotted with charming Italian towns along the way. Watch the video below, then scroll down for a small gallery of still photos from organizer Andrea Securo.

The 2022 Veneto Trail event will kick off on June 25th. Learn more and get registered over at VenetoTrail.eu.
