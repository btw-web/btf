Behind the Scenes with Lachlan at GBDuro (Video)

GBDURO is a self-supported bikepacking race following the 2,000 kilometer GB Divide from Land’s End to John O’Groats on gravel, singletrack, tarmac, and everything in between. As the organizers put it, GBDURO is best described as “a scrappy rolling picnic through Britain’s ever changing landscapes.” Last year, Rapha and EF Pro Cycling put together a long-form video documenting the 2019 GBDURO—which we awarded with our Best Feature Film of 2019—and they just released this behind the scenes video highlighting Lachlan Morton’s experience racing and winning the event.
