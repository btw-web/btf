Wolf Tooth Teklite Roll-Top Bags: First Look

Adding to their B-RAD System lineup, Wolf Tooth just announced two new ultralight, roll-top accessory bags. The new Wolf Tooth Teklite Roll-Top Bags follow the same profile as the original B-RAD Roll-Top Bag, but are constructed from a new super light TekLite material made from Ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene (UHMWPE), which looks very much like Dyneema Composite Fabric. The Teklite Roll-Top Bag comes in two sizes and each has a printed Wolf Tooth logo toward the bottom of the bag, a roll-top closure with a plastic stiffener, and a three-position daisy chain on the back to secure the strap. The Teklite Roll-Top Bags can be used with a B-RAD base or without and come with a base, mounting hardware, and a velcro strap. Find more details below.

Wolf Tooth Teklite Roll-Top Bags come in two sizes, a small 0.6L option (shown on Virginia’s new Ripley AF below), which is the perfect size for a spare tube, lever, a multitool, and tubeless plugs, and the larger 1L option. We haven’t tried the 1L Teklite bag yet, but it looks like it would be good for the items that fit into the 0.6L option plus a tool-roll or a spare layer like a small rain jacket or windbreaker.

As mentioned, the Wolf Tooth Teklite Roll-Top Bags can be used with or without the B-RAD base. If used with the included base, it bolts onto a standard pair of bottle bosses. The included strap can then thread through the slots on the base to secure the Teklite bag. We’ve used the B-RAD bases for this purpose before and it works well. Similar to the Bedrock Sinbad, with the tacky silicone stripes on the strap, you can simply lash the Teklite bag directly to your frame as well.

The Teklite bags are available now and come in just one color. You can purchase either bag with the aforementioned parts and accessories or just as a bag/strap combo. Prices and specs on each are as follows…

Wolf Tooth Teklite Roll-top Bag (0.6L)

  • Actual Weight: 51 grams (including strap and bag)
  • Bag Dimensions: 3″ wide x 2.5″ tall x 5″ to 7″ long
  • Strap Dimensions: 2″ wide x 13″ long
  • Place of Manufacture: Vietnam
  • Price (with base): $44.95
  • Price (bag + strap only): $39.95
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com

Wolf Tooth Teklite Roll-top Bag (1L)

  • Actual Weight: 60 grams (including strap and bag)
  • Bag Dimensions: 4″ wide x 3″ tall x 5-8″ long
  • Strap Dimensions: 2″ wide x 16″ long
  • Place of Manufacture: Vietnam
  • Price (with base): $49.95
  • Price (bag + strap only): $44.95
  • Manufacturer’s Details: WolfToothComponents.com
