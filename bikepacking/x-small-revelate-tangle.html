The Revelate Tangle Frame Bag is Now Available in X-Small

Revelate Designs’ made-in-Alaska Tangle is the original half-frame bag. It’s been a go-to frame bag for me over the years, especially on gravel and drop-bar bikes. It’s inexpensive, durable, and works on just about any bike. Revelate Designs recently expanded that last statement with an X-Small (extra small) version of the Tangle, fit for smaller riders and bikes.

The X-Small Tangle has a packing volume of about 3-liters and weighs 207 grams (7.3oz). Here are dimensions for the full Tangle line followed by a sizing chart, for reference…

Revelate Tangle Sizing and Weights

X-Small: Volume: 3L; top length 15.25″; height 4.5″; Weight: 207g (7.3oz)
Small: Volume: 3.5L; top length 17.5″; height 4.5″; Weight: 224g (7.9oz)
Medium: Volume: 4L; top length 19.75″; height 4.5″; Weight: 249g (8.8oz)
Large: Volume: 6L; top length 21″; height 6.5″; Weight: 337g (11.9oz)

All Tangles feature:

  • Revelate exclusive RevX-PAC panels that are 2 times as abrasion resistant as standard VX material
  • Zipstretch™ construction – Pairs a bomber, wetsuit style molded-tooth zipper with our exclusive stretch Cordura panels that reduce zipper stress when closing bag
  • Exit port in main compartment for hydration tube or wires
  • Zippered left side pocket with dividers organizes small items
  • Urethane coated webbing attachments don’t absorb water or grit
  • Daisy chain front top tube attachment accommodates any top tube bag configuration
  • Padded with closed cell foam along all tube sections
  • Internal pump straps along top tube
  • Low-profile cam-lock buckles

The Revelate Tangle is made in the USA with domestic and imported materials and retails for $90. Learn more over at RevelateDesigns.com.
