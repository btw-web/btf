Collective Reward #023: Alpkit Frame Bag Bundle

For Collective Reward #023, one lucky member of our Bikepacking Collective (if you’re not a member yet, you can join here) will receive a Frame Bag Bundle courtesy of the folks at Alpkit. Designed and sewn right at their headquarters in the UK, Alpkit’s bikepacking bags are built to be lightweight, waterproof, and super durable. The bundle we’re giving away provides all the bags you need to outfit your rig for a bikepacking trip. What’s more, the winner can mix and match whatever sizes and colors they’d like to make their kit unique. The total value of this giveaway is over $260, and we’re excited to get it into the hands of one of our readers.

This Alpkit Frame Bag Bundle Includes:

  • 1x Koala seat bag in 7 or 13-liter size
  • 1x Possum frame bag with twin compartments
  • 1x Kanga lightweight handlebar harness
  • 1x Fuel Pod top tube bag in 1 or 1.5-liter size
  • 1x Stem Cell stem-mounted feed bag

Be sure to join our Bikepacking Collective before Sunday, March 3rd, if you’d like to be eligible for this giveaway, along with many other Collective Rewards (and a whole bunch of other great benefits, including two copies of The Bikepacking Journal mailed to you each year, anywhere in the world).
