Live Tonight! What’s In Your Accessory Bags?

At 7:00 p.m. EDT tonight, for our fourth Thursday in a row, Jess Daddio, Joe Cruz, Miles Arbor, and Neil Beltchenko will be unpacking their bikepacking bags live on YouTube. Also, last week’s special guest will be returning as well. Devin Cowens is an Atlanta resident who works for East Coast Greenway Alliance managing WTF Bikexplores ATL. In addition, we’ll be revealing the results of our “What accessory bags do you use?” survey that’s already received over 700 individual responses.

This informal chat series will showcase the bags everyone is using, what they carry in each, and why they carry those specific items in that location. Tonight, we talk top tube bags, feedbags, hip packs, and more. Tune in below, and be sure to subscribe to our YouTube channel to get notifications for upcoming live events and videos.
