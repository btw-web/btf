Surly Gets a New Website (and a Cargo E-Bike)

This past Friday, some folks were a little shocked (no pun intended) at Surly’s move to electric on the Big Easy cargo bike. But really, it’s a compelling and clever solution that may just replace a few commuter cars, family vans, and even work trucks. Alongside that launch, Surly rolled out a new website design with bigger photos, and more of them. They also fine tuned the navigation and wrapped it all in a new look. Check it out here.
