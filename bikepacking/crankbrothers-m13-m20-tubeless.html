New Crankbrothers M13 and M20 Have Integrated Tubeless Repair Tools

Crankbrothers M multi-tools are longstanding favorites among mountain bikers and bikepackers. The company just updated the M13 and M20 with a tubeless repair tool and a case that holds tire plugs and other small items. Watch the launch video below and scroll down for details…

Crankbrothers M13

The new M13 features 13 high tensile steel multi-tools, including an integrated tire plug tool. Bacon-strip style tire plugs come included with a plastic piggyback storage case that snaps directly onto the tool. The case can be used to store other bits and bobs, too, such as patches or chain links. Find the complete list of functions below.

  • Hex Wrenches: #2, 2.5, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8
  • Length: 89mm
  • Screw Drivers: Phillips #1 & #2, flat #1
  • Specialty Tools: Tire plug tool, tire plugs
  • Torx: T-10, T-25
  • Warranty: Lifetime
  • Weight: 164g

The Crankbrothers M13 comes in Gold, Matte Black, Black/Red, and Nickel and retails for $25.99. Find more over at Crankbrothers.com.

Crankbrothers M20

The Crankbrothers M includes, you guessed it, 20 high steel tools, including the same integrated tire plug tool that’s on the M13. The M20 gets the same case too, including tire plugs. Find the full list of functions below.

  • Chain Tool: 8/9/10/11/12 speed compatible
  • Hex Wrenches: #2, 2.5, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8
  • Length: 89mm
  • Open Wrench: 8mm
  • Screw Drivers: Phillips #1 & #2, flat #1
  • Specialty Tools: Tire plug tool, tire plugs, valve core removal tool, rotor straightener
  • Spoke Wrench: #0, 1, 2
  • Torx: T-10, T-25
  • Warranty: Lifetime
  • Weight: 203g

The Crankbrothers M20 comes in Gold, Matte Black, Black/Red, and Nickel and retails for $39.99. Find more over at Crankbrothers.com.
