New Agave Finishworks Why Cycles Ti Bar

I first met Peter of Agave Finishworks during my visit to Bedrock Bags HQ in Durango, Colorado, about two years ago. At the time, Peter was just getting things rolling with custom-finished titanium products, including Vargo pots and mugs, King Cage bottle cages, and bicycle frames. He’s been busy dialling in his offerings over the last few years and has added custom Cane Creek eeWings titanium cranks, plus several other fades and colours that are pretty impressive.

Agave just added Why Cycles’ titanium handlebars to their online shop, available in either a Tie Dye Anodized finish or Satin Grey, priced at $279.99 and $259.99 USD, respectively. Both bars are made from high-end grade 9 titanium with a 780mm width, 31.8mm clamp, 9° sweep, and 4° rise. They weigh 295g and are finished by Peter in his shop in Durango.

Head over to AgaveFinishworks.com to see more.
