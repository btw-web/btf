PNW Components Releases The Loam Grip

Just announced, PNW Components has introduced the first grip in their line of bike components and accessories. The Loam Grips, named after the Loam Dropper Post Lever (as tested by Miles), are claimed to provide excellent grip, support for your hands, and a medium soft rubber compound that’s “soft enough to absorb vibration, yet firm enough to keep its shape under pressure,” as PNW puts it. The thinner grip pattern is designed for grip and feel, while the larger / thicker mountain patterns provide support and absorb vibration.

The grips feature an outside diameter of 30mm, a total width of 133.5mm, and use a single bolt-on clamp. As per PNW style, they are available in five different colours: blackout black, safety orange, seafoam teal, cement gray, and really red, meaning you can match your PNW Loam Lever if you’ve got one. The Loam Grip is available now from PNWComponents.com now and is listed at $19 USD.
