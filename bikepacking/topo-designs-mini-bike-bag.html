New Topo Designs Mini Bike Bag

The new Topo Designs Mini Bike Bag is a smaller version of their popular Bike Bag handlebar bag. It’s based around a weatherproof construction, complete with a weatherproof zipper and high-vis liner, and uses two velcro loops to attach to your bars. There’s one main interior compartment, a key clip, and the base of the bag is padded for some extra security. The smaller design means it could be great for those running narrow drop bars, or even younger riders looking for their first bikepacking bag. The Mini Bike Bag has a capacity of 1.5L and is made in Vietnam.

The Topo Designs Mini Bike Bag is available in three colour options: Turquoise, Red, or Black/White Ripstop. And as mentioned, it also comes in a standard 5.5L version for those looking for more storage. Find the complete specs below.

  • Capacity: 1.5L
  • Material: 210D nylon ripstop, 10oz vinyl tarp liner
  • Dimensions: 7″ x 3.3″ x 3.75″
  • Place of Manufacture: Vietnam
  • Price: $39 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: TopoDesigns.com

Find all the details and purchasing info at TopoDesigns.com.
