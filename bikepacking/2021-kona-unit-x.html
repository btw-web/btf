2021 Kona Unit X and Unit Announced

It’s hard to argue with the value offered by the Kona Unit and Unit X. They both come complete with Reynolds 520 Chromoly steel frames, 29 x 2.6″ tires, plenty of mounts, and a versatile geometry that’s practical for nearly any situation. The 2021 Unit X and Unit were just announced, and although they haven’t received any geometry updates, they both get new paint colors and updated build kits. Find the full build kits and pricing on both below.

2021 Kona Unit X

The geared Kona Unit X offers a lot for the price. Sliding dropouts, thru-axles, and big 29 x 2.6″ WTB Ranger tires are all stock for just $1,399 USD. Carried over from last year, the 2021 Kona Unit X is based around a SRAM SX-Eagle 1×12 drivetrain, but gets a new Gloss Metallic Rust with Dark Seafoam colorway.

2021 Kona Unit

If singlespeed is your thing, then the 2021 Kona Unit is definitely worth checking out. It shares all the same specs at the Unit X, minus the gears, and retails for just $1,199 for the complete build or as a frameset for $599 USD. For 2021, the Kona Unit comes with a Satin Fatgue Green w/ Gloss Yellow paint job, and we’re digging it.
