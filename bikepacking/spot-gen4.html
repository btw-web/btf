New SPOT Gen4 Satellite Messenger Released

The latest addition to SPOT’s line of satellite communication devices is the SPOT Gen4, building on the popular Gen3, a favourite amongst bikepackers worldwide. The Gen4 sees a complete redesign: the body itself has a complete new look and is now upgraded to IP68 for improved water resistance. It’s still powered by 4x AAA batteries, and is rated for 1,250 Check-In and Custom Messages on a single set. Find a breakdown of its features and specs, as well as SPOT’s product overview video, below.
