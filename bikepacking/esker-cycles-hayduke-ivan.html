Esker Cycles Hayduke Now Available in “Ivan”

Relaunched in 2018, the Esker Cycles Hayduke is a versatile hardtail mountain bike featuring a quadruple butted, heat treated 4130 ChroMoly frame, clearance for both 27.5 x 3.0″ and 29 x 2.3″ tires, and swing-arm swappable dropouts. Esker just announced a fresh new color option for those not quite digging Sublime, Caterpillar, or Ranchera — a clean pearl white frame with orange accents known as Ivan.

The Hayduke is available either as a frameset for $750 USD or as a complete for $2,200 or $3,000, depending on the build. Head over to EskerCycles.com to learn more.
