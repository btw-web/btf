Rapha + Snow Peak Announce Accessory Capsule Collection

On the heels of their new performance trailwear line, Rapha just announced its first collaboration with Snow Peak, the Japanese manufacturers of high-quality, lightweight outdoor equipment. The first collection consists of a co-branded bottle, mug, and spork, each featuring the line “seems appropriate,” which they say “conveys the contrasting environments of home and exploration.” Although there’s nothing truly new in this capsule, given the items already exist in Snow Peak’s catalog, the brands have suggested that this is just a first step toward collaborations to come. Find a quick peek at all three items below.

RAPHA + SNOW PEAK KANPAI BOTTLE

Made of vacuum-sealed, double-wall stainless steel, the Kanpai bottle keeps your drink at the desired temperature. The bottle comes with three different lids, two to keep your drinks hot or cold for hours at a time, and one to easily drink from on the bike. It has a capacity of 18.3 fl oz, weighs 12.5 oz, and comes with a hefty price tag of $135.

RAPHA + SNOW PEAK SINGLE WALLED MUG

This durable mug is made of ultralight Japanese titanium and features foldable handles for compact storage, making it easy to carry. The mug can be placed directly over a heat source, making it ideal for cooking while on overnight excursions. It’s dishwasher safe, holds 15.2 fl oz, weighs 2.4 oz, and is priced at $42.50.

RAPHA + SNOW PEAK SPORK

This versatile spork is super light, making it easy to carry along with the rest of your overnight essentials. Made from titanium, the spork is a durable utensil, ideal for cooking as well as eating. It’s dishwasher safe, measures 6.5″ long by 1.6″ wide, and costs $15.

As with other limited releases, the items in the Rapha x Snow Peak collection are currently only available to members of the Rapha Cycling Club but will be available for regular retail sale on June 12th. Find additional details over at Rapha.cc. Additionally, it’s worth noting that these same camp staples (without the special edition co-branding) can be purchased from your local Snow Peak retailer or at SnowPeak.com at a slightly reduced price.
