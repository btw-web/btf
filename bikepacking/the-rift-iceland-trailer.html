The Rift: Riding Iceland’s Fault Line (Video Trailer)

Photos by Rugile Kaladyte (@rugilekaladyte)

Released today, the latest documentary film from GCN+ follows ultra-endurance cyclists Jenny Graham and Lael Wilcox as they take on a challenging 200-kilometer bikepacking trip across the dramatic landscapes of Iceland and learn about how the Icelandic people have harnessed their unique natural resources to become a world leader in self-sustaining energy.

You can watch the two-minute trailer for The Rift: Riding Iceland’s Fault Line below (though note that you’ll need to purchase a membership to GCN+ to watch the full documentary), followed by a small gallery of images from trip photographer Rugile Kaladyte.
