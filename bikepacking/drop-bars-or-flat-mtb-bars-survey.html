Survey: Drop Bars or Flat/MTB bars?

Ah, the long-running debate: which is better for bikepacking and dirt-touring… drop bars or mountain bike style handlebars? We all have our own opinion, and there’s no right or wrong choice, but we’re curious to know who’s riding what. We also realize that different routes often require different bikes and equipment, so this question is framed for general mixed-surface long-distance bikepacking, if you had to choose just one. Please take this super quick survey and we’ll reveal the results in an upcoming YouTube video on this subject (make sure to subscribe to our channel to get a notification when it’s released).
