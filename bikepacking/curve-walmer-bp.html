Curve Announces “BP” Edition Walmer Bars

Originally intended for Curve’s GMX+ bikepacking bike, their Walmer Bars made a big splash when they were launched a couple of years ago. Not only were they the widest drop bars on the market, they also had a few details that resonated with riders, including shallow drops, a gentle backsweep, and extra width for bar-mounted bags.

Just announced, the new BP edition gets a wider central oversized section on either side of the clamping area. This provides even more space to mount lights, snack pouches, aero bars, or whatever else you could possibly think of mounting to a bar! The Curve Walmer BP comes in either 46 or 50cm sizes.

Additionally, Curve also announced narrow 40 and 43cm models, rounding out the lineup (below) to six sizes that range from 40-60cm, plus the two additional BP models. The Curve Walmer and Walmer BP retails for $145 AUD (~$109 USD). Find details at CurveCycling.com.
