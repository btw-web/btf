Collective Reward #083: ARCH Custom Frame Bag

We first introduced ARCH (@arch_ind) last summer during our five-part maker bundle giveaway series, through which we distributed several dozen handmade bikepacking bags from some of our favorite small brands, which included several pieces from Andrea Perici of ARCH, who’s been sewing custom bags and accessories since 2010. He has an incredible eye for design and has been creating some seriously impressive and original work from his small shop in Italy, including backpacks, seat packs, a variety of unique handlebar bag solutions, frame bags, and more.

For Collective Reward #083, one randomly selected Bikepacking Collective member in the European Union will be receiving a made-to-measure ARCH frame bag, custom built with their choice of colors, materials, and options. That’s around a 180€ value for our winner, who we’ll connect directly with Andrea to talk through the design process.

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, it isn’t too late to get signed up to be eligible for this giveaway. Join today and you’ll be automatically entered to win a custom frame bag from ARCH or one of many other prize packages. Support from our members makes BIKEPACKING.com possible, and membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world. Members also get first access to merchandise, receive two issues of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, can take advantage of exclusive industry discounts, and more.
