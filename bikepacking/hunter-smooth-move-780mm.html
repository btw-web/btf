Hunter Smooth Move Bars Now 780mm Wide

California-based Hunter Cycles is behind some of the most unique designs and handmade components we’ve ever seen, and those who know his work consider Rick Hunter, the proprietor of Hunter Cycles, a master tinkerer. His high-quality, small-batch products usually sell out quickly, so we thought we’d share the news that he just restocked his popular Smooth Move bars, and they’re now offered in a 780mm width—the widest offering yet!

The Hunter Smooth Move bars are designed by Hunter Cycles and made by Nitto in Japan. They are constructed from high-grade aluminum, use a standard 31.8mm clamp size, and have laser-etched logos that will last a lifetime. The dimensions and shape are custom-specced by Hunter. The low-rise version is perfect for modern mountain bikes while the high-rise version is better suited for commuters and town bikes. Both versions are offered with a silver or black finish and are available now (while they last) through Hunter Cycles’ webshop.

High-Rise Spec

  • Width: 780mm
  • Rise: 75mm
  • Backsweep: 15°
  • Upsweep: 5°
  • Weight: 405g
  • Price: $120-125 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: HunterCycles.BigCartel

Low-Rise Spec

  • Width: 780mm
  • Rise: 35mm
  • Backsweep: 15°
  • Upsweep: 5°
  • Weight: 380g
  • Price: $120-125 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: HunterCycles.BigCartel
