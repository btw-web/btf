Velo Orange is offering 20% off Gift Cards Through Monday

Velo Orange has historically avoided any Black Friday/Cyber Monday sales because of the stress it would put on employees. However, they just announced an option that gives you the ability to give a Velo Orange e-gift card or get a great deal on a future purchase. Starting now and continuing through the end of Monday, November 29th (11:59pm EST), you can get Velo Orange e-gift cards at 20% off. That’s $5 off a $25 gift card, $20 off a $100 gift card, $100 off a $500 gift card, etc. Read their terms below and find a link at the bottom of the post…

From Velo Orange:
Gift cards are a completely automated process for us, so no burden on our employees as we give everyone off Thursday and Friday, November 25th and 26th to spend time with their friends and families.

It allows you to give a gift without knowing the specific bike part your buddy needs, or push off your holiday shopping decision to whenever you want, while still getting 20% off. Or, treat yo’ self to a good deal!

These 20% off gift cards effectively turn any future purchase into a sale. Apply the 20% off gift card value to any order, including future purchases for items that are not currently in stock. In addition to being an employee friendly alternative to Black Friday/Cyber Monday, it spreads out demand over our supply chain. Wow, it’s a win-win!
