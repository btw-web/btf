Don’t Miss Another Issue of The Bikepacking Journal

The deadline to join our Bikepacking Collective and have your membership start with Issue 05 of The Bikepacking Journal is this Saturday, October 17th, at 11:59 PM EDT. If you join after that point, you won’t receive your first journal until we print Issue 06 in spring 2021.

Inside this issue you’ll find another 160 pages of the most engaging writing and inspiring photography from bikepacking trips all around the world. Our contributors bring us to Tanzania, Colombia, Sweden, Colorado, Mexico, and many other locations. And we’re shipping the new issue with a sticker sheet featuring seven different stickers, including our new coyote and hare drivetrain animals.

Becoming a Bikepacking Collective member is the best way to support our work at BIKEPACKING.com, where we’re committed to sharing in-depth articles, inspiring stories, original routes, and news on a daily basis. In addition to receiving The Bikepacking Journal, joining also means you’ll be entered to win one of more than 50 prizes in our upcoming giveaway drawing, gain access to exclusive discounts from some of our favorite companies, and much more.
