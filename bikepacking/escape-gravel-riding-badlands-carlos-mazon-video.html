The Escape: Gravel Riding in the Badlands with Carlos Mazon (Video)

Photos by Juan Barros (@juananfotografia)

The latest installment of The Escape from Scott Bikes features Carlos Mazón, founder of the Badlands gravel race, taking Scott’s marketing manager, Janosch Wintermantel, out for a ride along some of the remote and challenging 760-kilometer Badlands route through sunny southern Spain. As they pedal along through the stunning scenery, Carlos tells some stories about the track and its history and shares his passion for the regions it passes through. You can watch the four minute-video below, then scroll on for some images from their trip by photographer Juan Barros.
