Gear Up For Summer: New Bikepacking Collective Discounts

With the season of long days of riding coming up, we’re excited to announce three new discounts to help Bikepacking Collective members gear up for summer adventures. This trifecta of discounts will have your bikepacking kit dialed in, everything strapped down securely, and you sleeping comfortably. If you’re in the market for new gear, these special offers just might cover the costs of a Bikepacking Collective membership several times over!

Discounts include 10% off the full Revelate Designs catalog, 30% off Sea to Summit gear, and 15% off Voile straps (in addition to our ongoing 20-50% Gaia GPS discount). Find details below. Already a member? Look for the discount codes in your inbox in the next couple of days (or if you don’t want to wait, you can also find them in the new “Benefits” tab in your member account). Not yet a member? Join our Bikepacking Collective today and you’ll be eligible for all these discounts, in additional to several others that we’ll be adding soon, including a handful of companies based outside the US with whom we’re currently in talks.

Revelate Designs

Our friends at Revelate Designs are giving Bikepacking Collective members a special 10% discount on their entire catalog of durable, innovative bikepacking bags and accessories. It’s no secret that we love Revelate’s products, and we even awarded them the 2018 Bikepacking Gear of the Year Award.

Sea to Summit

Sea to Summit is offering Bikepacking Collective members a one-time discount of 30% off their range of products, from sleeping bags, inflatable pads, and more. We’ve reviewed and enjoyed several of their products, particularly their UL Sleeping Mat. Note: the Sea to Summit discount is only valid on items shipping to addresses in the US and Canada.

Voile Straps

We think Voile Straps are among the most useful bikepacking accessories out there, and now Bikepacking Collective members can receive 15% off all items at voilestraps.com. We’ve written a lot about these ultra-practical doodads, even including them in our lists of Bikepacking Hacks and Bikepacking Gear That Lasts.

Not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective? Join today and you’ll be eligible for all these discounts, along with several others that we’ll be adding soon. Additionally, you’ll also be in the running for our Collective Reward gear giveaways, you’ll receive two printed issues of The Bikepacking Journal each year, and lots more.
