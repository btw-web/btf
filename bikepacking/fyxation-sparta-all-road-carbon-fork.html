New Fyxation Sparta All Road Carbon Fork has Bottle Mounts.

UPDATE: make sure to see thiss fork in action in our 700c to 650b conversion article. And, find our full Gear Index of utility forks with bottle cage mounts here.

I am not sure why more companies aren’t making these, but here you go. The new Fyxation Sparta All Road Carbon Fork fits up to 42mm 700c tires or 650b x 50mm and sports a 12mm thru axle (included), 1 1/8″ aluminum straight steerer tube, and a flat mount disc brake mount. The utility-focused Sparta also has rack and fender mounts, and two pairs of fork eyelets for water bottles or a Blackburn Outpost Cargo Cage. The fork includes a 12mm thru axle skewer. A tapered version will be available in Fall 2017.

Specs

  • Length: 400mm axle to crown
  • Rake: 45mm
  • Width: 100mm
  • Steerer: 1 1/8″ alloy
  • Water Bottle/Fork Mounts: 2 eyelets per leg; works with Blackburn Outpost Cargo Cage
  • Brake Mount: Flat mount
  • Axle: 12mm thru axle (included)
  • Fork Weight: 747g (with uncut steerer tube)
  • Skewer Weight: 65g
