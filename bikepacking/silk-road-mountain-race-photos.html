2018 Silk Road Mountain Race Photo Sale

Jennifer Doohan, one of the photographers who traveled to Kyrgyzstan last summer to cover the inaugural Silk Road Mountain Race – and whose photos from the same race we featured in the first issue of The Bikepacking Journal – just opened up her entire catalog of images from the event for a limited-time sale.

Whether you competed in the first running of the event or just want to have a photo from the astonishingly beautiful Tian Shan Mountains to adorn your walls, we think Jennifer’s photos are well worth checking out. And with prices starting at around $4.00 per image, her photos make for an affordable way to do some armchair traveling. Want to support an independent photographer and and get a lovely desktop background in exchange? There are options for digital downloads, too.

You can browse through her Silk Road Mountain Race images and purchase prints online via SmugMug. To see more of Jennifer’s work, find her on Instagram @jdoohanphoto.
