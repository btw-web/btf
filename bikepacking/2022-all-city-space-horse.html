New All-City Space Horse Colors and Builds

Minneapolis-based All-City Cycles just shared news of two fresh colors and builds of their popular steel Space Horse, which they say is “ready to take you and your gear wherever adventure leads – from road riding to randonneuring to full-fledged touring.” It’s available in a wide range of sizes from 43cm up to 61cm to fit a variety of riders. The Space Horse features front and rear rack/fenders mounts, sports thru-axles and flat mount disc brakes, and the frame will clear 650B x 47mm tires on all sizes, and all but the smallest sizes (43/46cm) can also fit 700C x 45mm tires.

The 2022 Space Horse lineup includes two builds: a higher-end GRX build in “Champagne Shimmer” that retails for $2,599 MSRP, as well as a $1,950 Tiagra build in “Royal Mint.” Framesets are also available in both colors for $1,099.
