The 2021 Genesis Fugio Gets a Fresh Coat of Paint

The Fugio is UK-based Genesis Bikes’ adventure-gravel platform. The company just announced its 2021 lineup, including two steel models, the Fugio 20 and 30. There is also a more affordable alloy version, the Fugio 10. Here are a few details.

2021 Genesis Fugio 30

The 2021 Genesis Fugio 30 gets a mandrel butted and heat-treated Reynolds 725 frameset with a Shimano GRX 1×11 groupset, WTB i25 wheels, 560b x 47mm WTB Venture tires, and an interesting fade paint job. Find the build kit below.

  • FORK: GENESIS FULL CARBON GRAVEL DISC FORK
  • BOTTOM BRACKET: SHIMANO SM-BBR60
  • WHEEL SIZE: 650B
  • BRAKE LEVERS: SHIMANO GRX ST-RX810 11 SPEED
  • BRAKES: SHIMANO GRX BR-RX810 HYDRAULIC DISC
  • CASSETTE: SHIMANO CS-M8000 11-42T
  • CHAIN: KMC X11
  • GRIP TAPE: GENESIS
  • HANDLEBARS: GENESIS ALLOY 16 DEG FLARE XS 420mm, S/M 440mm, L 460mm, XL 480mm
  • HEADSET: F17A TAPER 1 1/8″ – 1 1/5″
  • REAR DERAILLEUR: SHIMANO GRX RD-RX812 11 SPEED
  • RIMS: WTB ST I25 TCS 2.0
  • SADDLE: GENESIS
  • SEAT POST: X-FUSION MANIC GRAVEL 50mm DROP
  • SHIFTERS: SHIMANO GRX ST-RX810 11 SPEED
  • SPOKES: STEEL 14 G
  • STEM: GENESIS ALLOY 31.8MM -6 DEG 100 MM
  • TIRES: WTB VENTURE 650 X 47C

The 2021 Genesis Fugio 30 will be available in four sizes (XS, S/M, L, XL) for £2,999.99. Find more details at GenesisBikes.co.uk.

2021 Genesis Fugio 20

Sporting a slightly less eye-catching fade paint scheme, the Genesis Fugio 20 gets the same steel frame and fork as the 30, with a slightly lower end build kit. Find details below.

  • FORK: GENESIS FULL CARBON GRAVEL DISC FORK
  • BOTTOM BRACKET: SRAM BB GXP
  • WHEEL SIZE: 650B
  • BRAKE LEVERS: SRAM APEX HYDRAULIC 11 SPEED
  • BRAKES: SRAM APEX HYDRAULIC DISC
  • CASSETTE FREEWHEEL: SRAM CS PG-1130 11-42T
  • CHAIN: KMC X11
  • GRIP TAPE: GENESIS
  • HANDLEBARS: GENESIS ALLOY 16 DEG FLARE XS 420mm, S/M 440mm, L 460mm, XL 480mm
  • HEADSET: F17A TAPER 1 1/8 – 1 1/5″
  • REAR DERAILLEUR: SRAM APEX 1 LONG CAGE 11 SPEED
  • RIMS: WTB ST I25 TCS 2.0
  • SADDLE: GENESIS
  • SEAT POST: GENESIS ALLOY 27.2 X 350mm
  • SHIFTERS: SRAM APEX HYDRAULIC 11 SPEED
  • SPOKES: STEEL 14 G
  • STEM: GENESIS ALLOY 31.8MM -6 DEG 100mm
  • TYRES: WTB VENTURE 650 X 47C

The 2021 Genesis Fugio 20 will be available in four sizes (XS, S/M, L, XL) for £2,299.99. Find more details at GenesisBikes.co.uk. Also, check out the Fugio 10 alloy model for £1,599.99
