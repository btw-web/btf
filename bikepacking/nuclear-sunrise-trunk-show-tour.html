Nuclear Sunrise Stitchworks Trunk Show Tour Dates Announced

Have you ever heard of Big Dave Wilson? Dave was hand making bikepacking gear as Nuclear Sunrise Stitchworks before most of us had even heard the term “bikepacking”. We had the chance to stay with Dave as we passed through El Paso a few years back and see his bomber custom work first hand.

This summer, Dave wants to meet up with a bunch of bikepackers. He’ll be hitting the road and setting up several trunk show socials to display his wares and meet and greet. Here are the dates. Stay tuned for more to be announced in Colorado, Idaho, and Nevada too:

June 12 – Albuquerque, NM – 6PM (learn more)
June 14 – Santa Fe, NM – 5:30PM (learn more)
July 20 (tentative) – Sun Valley, ID
July 22 (tentative) – Truckee, CA details to come
