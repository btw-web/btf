Collective Reward #015: Sawyer Micro Squeeze and Gravity Filter

While many water filters are somewhat bulky and heavy, the Sawyer Squeeze is anything but. We’ve relied on the Sawyer Squeeze Mini for years, on bikepacking outings such as the Trans Uganda, Tian Shan Traverse, and several others. Sawyer recently announced the Micro version, which has the same filter in a slightly smaller package. One Bikepacking Collective member will get a brand new Sawyer Micro Squeeze system, which includes a pouch and cleaning syringe, plus a Gravity System, which includes another Micro filter along with a 3.78 liter filtration bag and hose. Check out the gallery below, and if you’re not already a member of The Bikepacking Collective, get signed up for your chance to win.
