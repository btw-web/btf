2021 Salsa Fat Bikes Announced

Announced this morning, Salsa Cycles has launched their 2021 fat bike lineup, which includes the Beargrease, Mukluk, and Blackbarrow. Although the frames and features remain the same for all three, the Beargrease and Mukluk receive new colourways and build kits. There aren’t any drastic changes, besides the introduction of Shimano 12-Speed XT and SLX drivetrains and some different price points, depending on what you’re looking for.

2021 Salsa Beargrease

From Salsa: Beargrease is our fat bike built to go faster than you knew fat bikes could go. The carbon fiber frame and fork and 27.5 x 3.8” wheels and tires cover challenging terrain quickly. Cargo mounts let you carry everything you need in the backcountry. Beargrease is perfect for testing yourself in a snowy endurance race or embarking on a long-distance overnight bike trip.

Beargrease Overview:

  • High-modulus carbon fiber frame
  • Kingpin Deluxe, Kingpin, or Bearpaw fork depending on spec
  • 27.5 x 3.8” tires on 80 mm rims
  • 15 x 150mm (front), 12 x 197mm (rear)
  • Dropper post compatible with stealth routing
  • Available in 5 sizes: XS, S, M, L, XL
  • Beargrease Carbon XO1 Eagle: $5,999
  • Beargrease Carbon SLX: $3,199
  • Beargrease Carbon NX: $2,699
  • Beargrease Carbon Deore 11: $2,249
  • Beargrease Carbon Frameset: $1,799

Find complete details about the updated 2021 Salsa Beargrease at SalsaCycles.com.

2021 Salsa Mukluk

From Salsa: Mukluk is our most versatile fat bike, designed and built to excel in the wide variety of conditions that fat bikers face. With carbon and aluminum frame options, 26 x 4.6” tires for maximum float, and a multitude of cargo mounts, Mukluk is your ticket to all-terrain adventures.

Mukluk Overview:

  • High-modulus carbon fiber or 6066-T6 aluminum frame
  • Kingpin fork on carbon models, Bearpaw fork on aluminum models
  • 26 x 4.6” tires on 80 mm rims
  • 15 x 150mm (front), 12 x 197mm (rear)
  • Alternator 2.0 dropouts
  • Dropper post compatible with stealth routing
  • Compatible with Salsa Alternator 170/177 Rear Rack with Salsa Rack-Lock seat collar
  • Available in 5 sizes: XS, S, M, L, XL
  • MUKLUK CARBON XT: $3,699
  • MUKLUK CARBON NX EAGLE: $2,799
  • MUKLUK DEORE 11: $1,599
  • MUKLUK CARBON FRAMESET: $1,999

Find complete details about the updated 2021 Salsa Mukluk at SalsaCycles.com.
