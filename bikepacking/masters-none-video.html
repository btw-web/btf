Masters of None (Video)

Our friends from Overmountain Cycles and Fonta Flora star in Masters Of None, a short film that follows a group of Western North Carolina locals into the mountains surrounding Morganton. The film was supported by the 36 Hours in Kitsbow grant and was inspired by the camaraderie of a few good friends, each of whom brings their own skillset to the trip. The Route

The route these folks took follows a chunk of our Wilson’s Ramble. It starts in Morganton and finishes at Fonta Flora Whippoorwill Farm.
