Collective Reward #098: Search and State Bundles

Search and State’s rugged, made-in-NYC Field Shorts and comfy Japanese puckered cotton Field Shirts have long been on our list of favorite bike-camp apparel. Both have made it in our Gear of the Year Awards as well as a couple of Editor’s Dozen lists. Early this past summer, SAS released a short-sleeve version of the Field Shirt, too. Additionally, SAS expanded their line of women’s apparel, which is also made in New York, and they’ve been quietly adding a few other adventure-ready garments to their lineup, such as the new Free Solo shorts, which we’ve already heard good things about.

For our latest Collective Reward, we’re excited to give away two full SAS bundles to two lucky members. Winning members will get bundles one or two, or the women’s bundles. Find the details below…

Search And State Bundle One

One member will receive a Short-sleeve Field Shirt in a choice of Natural or Blue colors, and a pair of shorts with their choice of the technical Free Solo or the waxed-cotton Camo Field Shorts.

For those unfamiliar, the new Search And State Free Solo shorts (above) are made of a Blue-Sign Approved 4-Way stretch fabric that’s built to last. The fit is similar to the Field Shorts, which are made of a waxed cotton duck fabric that gives them more of a classic look. Find more details on the Field Shorts here, and the Free Solo here.

Search And State Bundle Two

The second bundle includes a long-sleeve Field Shirt in a choice of Black or Red and a pair of shorts with a member’s choice of the Free Solo or Camo Field Shorts.

Search And State Women’s Bundle

As an optional alternative to bundles one and two above, the women’s bundle includes a few of their more popular options from the newly expanded SAS women’s line. One member will receive a made-in-NYC SAS jersey of their choice—this includes multiple patterns and prints from their Print Shop, or a choice from the solid colors—plus the S4 Bib Short.

Find more of the SAS Women’s jersey’s here. Learn more about the S4 Bibs here.
