Velo Orange Releases the Seine Bar

The new Velo Orange Seine Bar is similar to their recently updated Crazy Bar but without the forward extensions. It’s designed for off-road riding and features a 40mm rise and 35° sweep that should help create a fairly upright riding position for long days on the bike. The Seine Bar is 780mm wide but has a generous grip area that can be trimmed shorter if need be, and it comes in a bead-blasted silver or noir finish.

Velo Orange Seine Bar Specs

  • Width: 780mm
  • Sweep: 35°
  • Grip Area: 205mm
  • Rise: 40mm
  • Clamp Size: 31.8mm
  • Weight: 443 grams
  • Price: $75 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Velo-Orange.com
