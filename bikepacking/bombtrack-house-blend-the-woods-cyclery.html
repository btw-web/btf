Bombtrack House Blend: The Woods Cyclery

For Bombtrack’s second House Blend video, through which they’re shining some light on and sharing their appreciation for the core shops that stock their bikes, we head to the village of Lyndhurst, England, for an introduction to The Woods Cyclery (@thewoodscyclery). Known for its carefully considered and beautifully photographed builds, this video provides a short introduction to the shop and its philosophy, as well as a look at the Bombtrack Arise they have in the workstand and some thoughts on why it’s one of their favorite models to build for customers. Watch the short video below, followed by a few photos of the Arise built up in different modes and out in the wild.
