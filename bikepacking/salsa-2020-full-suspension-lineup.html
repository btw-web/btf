Salsa Updates Full-Suspension Lineup for 2020

Announced this morning, Salsa Cycles has launched its 2020 lineup of full-suspension bikes, which includes the Spearfish, Horsethief, and Rustler. Though there aren’t any updates to the frames, there are several new colorways and specs. Notable changes include Fox suspension forks and shocks on the upper-end models, along with carbon wheels on the top-end models. Plus, for the first time, the Rustler is now available with a more affordable aluminum frame.

2020 Salsa Spearfish

The Spearfish is Salsa’s 29″ progressive XC bike that they say is, “built to crush stamina-testing terrain while thriving on technical climbs and descents.” It’s available as a carbon frame only or in five complete builds: Carbon XTR, Carbon XT, Carbon SLX, Aluminum SLX, and Aluminum SX Eagle.

Spearfish Overview:

  • 100mm split pivot travel, 120mm fork
  • 432mm chainstays
  • 67.8° headtube
  • Fits 29 x 2.2-2.6″ or 27.5 x 2.8-3.0″ tires
  • Flip chip for quick geometry adjustment
  • Available in sizes SM-XL
  • Spearfish Carbon XTR 1×12: $7,099
  • Spearfish Carbon XT 1×12: $5,399
  • Spearfish Carbon SLX 1×12: $4,499
  • Spearfish SLX 1×12: $3,199
  • Spearfish SX Eagle: $2,649
  • Spearfish Carbon Frame: $2,799

Find complete details about the updated 2020 Salsa Spearfish at SalsaCycles.com.

2020 Salsa Horsethief

The Horsethief is Salsa’s 29” all-around trail bike that they say is, “one bike for many trails.” It’s available as a carbon frame only or as one of five complete builds: Carbon XTR, Carbon XT, Carbon SLX, Aluminum SLX, and Aluminum SX Eagle

Horsethief Overview:

  • 432mm chainstays
  • 67° headtube
  • Fits 29 x 2.2-2.6″ or 27.5 x 2.8-3.0″ tires
  • Flip chip for quick geometry adjustment
  • Available in sizes SM-XL
  • Horsethief Carbon XTR 1×12: $7,099
  • Horsethief Carbon XT 1×12: $5,399
  • Horsethief Carbon SLX 1×12: $4,499
  • Horsethief SLX 1×12: $3,199

  • Horsethief Carbon Frame: $2,799

2020 Salsa Rustler

The Rustler is Salsa’s 27.5” playful trail bike that they say, “rewards riders who want to throw some body language in the way they tackle trails.” It’s available as a carbon frame only or in five complete builds: Carbon XTR, Carbon XT, Carbon SLX, Aluminum SLX, and Aluminum SX Eagle.

Rustler Overview:

  • 130mm split pivot travel, 150mm fork
  • 425mm chainstays
  • 65.8° headtube
  • Fits 27.5 x 2.3-2.8″ tires
  • Flip chip for quick geometry adjustment
  • Available in sizes XS-XL

  • Rustler Carbon SLX 1×12: $4,499
  • Rustler SLX 1×12: $3,199
