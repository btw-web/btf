New PNW Trail Apparel Collection

After two years in the making, PNW Components just launched a small apparel lineup that focuses on technical features, clean aesthetics, and what they call “affordable luxury” for each item, blending functionality and durability with a reasonable price point—descriptions that sound quite similar to their dropper post lineup, which has been a hit among some of us here. PNW’s Fall 2021 collection consists of a unisex jacket and jersey and a men’s riding short. Theirs is a small step into the apparel scene, but they’ve hinted at more to come.

PNW Lander Jacket

A lightweight trail companion designed for densely forested descents and foggy morning climbs. Designed with an anti-flop back pocket storage system, a helmet friendly hood, and an adventure-accommodating fabric to keep you focused on the trail ahead.

  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Colors: Super Nova (Orange) / Neutron (Green)
  • Place of Manufacture: China
  • Price: $149 USD

PNW Ozone Trail Jersey

Woven from a mix of 20% wool and 80% polyester, this jersey is soft, breathable, moisture-wicking, quick-drying, and anti-bacterial. Sleep easy knowing this jersey is Bluesign approved, guaranteeing the fabric meets the demanding requirements of the highest environmental standards for the textile industry.

  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Colors: Crater (Grey) / Helium (Blue)
  • Place of Manufacture: China
  • Price: $59 USD

PNW Shuttle Short

Focused on comfort, the Shuttle Short features a lightweight and mobile fabric that keeps the elements out and all your gear secure, including a cell phone pocket so good at securing your phone you’ll forget you even brought it.

  • Size Range: XS – XXL
  • Colors: Eclipse (Black)
  • Place of Manufacture: China
  • Price: $99 USD

All of of the collection is manufacture from Bluesign and OEKO-TEX certified fabrics and ships in compostable and biodegradable packaging. Like all PNW Components branded products, the Fall ’21 MTB Apparel is covered by a lifetime warranty. PNW is committed to covering any failures due to manufacturing defects and will repair or replace without charge to the original owner. For those wondering, PNW has a women’s line in the works as well, so stay tuned. Head over to PNWComponents.com to see more.
