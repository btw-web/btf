Curve GMX+ Carbon Fork Released

Building on their already impressive lineup of utility forks for mountain and gravel bikes, Melbourne-based Curve Cycling’s new GMX+ Carbon Fork is now available as a standalone optionâ€”separate from their GMX+ drop-bar monster-adventure bike. The GMX+ fork features the a relatively short 430mm axle-to-crown height, making it specific for modern drop-bar bikes, non-suspension corrected frames, and catering to frame builders looking to build rigid bikes without sacrificing tire clearance. The fork features a full carbon construction based around a 180mm post mount brake rotor with clearance for 3.0″ tires. The GMX+ Fork has a whopping six M5 mounts on each leg with a recommended load capacity of three kilograms across three mount points.

  • Tire Clearance: 29 x 3.0″
  • Material: Carbon
  • Offset: 55mm
  • Axle to Crown: 430mm
  • Steerer: 1.5″ Tapered
  • Axle: 15 x 110mm

Curve GMX+ Carbon Fork retails for $899 AUD (~$680 USD) and is available now through CurveCycling.com.
