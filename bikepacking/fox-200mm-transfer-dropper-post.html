Fox Introduces 200mm Transfer Dropper Post

Previously only offered in 100, 125, 150, and 175mm lengths, the Fox Transfer dropper post now comes in a longer 200mm travel version. We’ve been putting some miles on the 175mm version and are quite impressed, but it’s nice to see an even bigger option available to maximize travel for us long-legged folks. Find out what’s changed with the 2021 model, as well as complete specs on the 200mm Fox Transfer below.

The 2021 Transfer was completely overhauled to be lighter, more compact, and even easier to service than its predecessor. It’s available in a Factory Series version with the bronze Genuine Kashima Coating and Performance Elite with a shiny black anodized upper. To increase compatibility with the potential for more travel, the overall length and insertion depth has also been reduced across the board.

  • Travel: 200mm
  • Diameter: 30.9 and 31.6mm
  • Total Length: 530.7mm
  • Minimum Insertion: 150mm
  • Maximum Insertion: 320mm
  • Weight: 657 grams
