RAR Launches 2021 SJ Brooks Scholarship GoFundMe

Our friends at the Radical Adventure Riders (RAR)—whose mission is to support, celebrate, and connect communities who identify as non-binary, transgender, femme, and/or women who use their bicycles to explore—are currently trying to raise $23,000 to go towards this year’s 2021 SJ Brooks Scholarship. Their goal is to help lessen the financial and material barriers to travel by bicycle. Since 2018, RAR has granted twenty-seven SJ Brooks Scholarships with gear, cash, and bikes based on the recipient’s needs.

Today, they’ve launched a GoFundMe campaign to help grow the scholarship. All donations will go directly to financial stipends for the scholarship recipients to use to help pursue a spirited bicycle endeavor on their terms. You can find the campaign here, and we encourage everyone who is able to make a donation, no matter the value.
