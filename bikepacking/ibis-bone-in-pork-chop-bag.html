Ibis’ New Bone-in Pork Chop Bag

Ibis Cycles is now serving up vegan Bone-In Pork Chop frame bags. Made from ballistic nylon, these padded and reinforced bags feature a water resistant zipper and an easy to mount, strapless design using a plastic “bone” that locks into the linkage on various full-suspension Ibis bikes. The new Bone-In Pork Chop is available for select sizes of the Ripley, Ripmo, and Ripmo AF. Here’s the install video…

  • Patented design with a 7 year warranty
  • Unique and easy to install bracket tab designed to suspend a bag without the need for straps
  • Outside fabric is a padded and reinforced 210 ripstop nylon
  • Sealed with water resistant polyurethane reversible zipper tape
  • Simple design lets you access your damper valve adjustments
  • Padded side panels to add to durability
  • Internal storage and elastic webbing to keep CO2 cartridges and tools in place
  • Not available for small Ripmo, Ripmo AF or Ripley or the XL Ripmo AF

There are four sizes of the Ibis Bone-in Pork Chop bag for various sizes and models. Each sells for $79.99 and can be ordered at your local Ibis dealer or from Ibis directly. Learn more and see the compatibility chart at IbisCycles.com.
