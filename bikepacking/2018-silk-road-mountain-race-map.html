New Silk Road Mountain Race Map by Alex Hotchin

Our friend Alex Hotchin recently finished an illustrated map of the 2018 Silk Road Mountain Race, a route closely based on our own Tian Shan Traverse. You can buy an A2 or A3 print over at SilkRoadMountainRace.cc. Also, be sure to check out Alex’s illustrated map Bikepacking The World, which we commissioned for our inaugural Bikepacking Collective Reward giveaway.
