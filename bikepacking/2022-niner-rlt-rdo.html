2022 Niner RLT 9 RDO Announced

The RLT 9 RDO is Niner’s top-end carbon gravel bike. It was updated in 2019 alongside the entire Niner gravel lineup with a longer wheelbase, a lighter and stiffer frame, and full-sleeve internal cable routing, including dropper post routing. While there are no changes to the frame or fork for 2022, the RLT 9 RDO has been updated with some fresh paint and new build kits.

The new colour options are Avalanche Grey/Slate and Blood Red/Silver, both pictured below. As always, Niner is offering the RLT 9 RDO in a wide range of build options from the 2-Star Apex 1 for $3,299 USD all the way up to 5-Star SRAM Force 1 XPLR AXS for $6,399 USD. There are a total of eight build levels, including a frame-only option for $2,699 USD.

The RLT 9 RDO seems like a versatile option with its 26 mounting points, including extras for Niner’s bolt-on frame bag, clearance for 700c x 50mm or 650b x 2.0″ tires, thru-axles, and six frame sizes to choose from. Here’s a short overview video from Niner that outlines the RLT 9 RDO:
