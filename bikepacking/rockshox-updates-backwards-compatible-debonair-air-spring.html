Rockshox Updates (backwards compatible) Debonair Air Spring

One thing I love about the Rockshox Pike is the fact that it sits up high in the travel, then comes to life when you push it on the trail. These are characteristics not only beneficial on technical trail rides, but also while bikepacking. RockShox just upgraded this even more based on rider feedback. According to RockShox, the new DebonAir air spring provides even more initial support and maintains higher ride height than the previous model, which “adds more confidence on technical terrain.” They accomplished this by moving the air piston position (a whopping 10mm) relative to the dimple. “This means more initial support and the use of every millimeter of travel available to stay on top of the terrain.” According to some initial ride reports, it makes a significant difference. We look forward to trying it out…

The new Debonair spring comes in the new Pike, Revelation, Lyrik, or Yari, and is also available as an upgrade which is backwards compatible across previous generation DebonAir-equipped forks. Lear more over at SRAM.com.
