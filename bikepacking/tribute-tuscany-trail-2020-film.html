Tribute to Tuscany Trail 2020 (Film)

The organizers of The Tuscany Trail say it’s the worldâ€™s largest annual bikepacking event. Hundreds of cyclists from more than 30 countries around the world join every year, meeting in Tuscany to take on the self-supported route. The trail follows a unique 500-kilometer track that crosses enchanted forests and thousand-year-old towns, starting near Massa and heading south to finish at the oceanside town of Capalbio. The organizers put together a 13-minute film as a tribute to the 2020 Tuscany Trail, which you can watch below, followed by some photos from the event.

“These are postcard views for me, the feeling of experiencing these views on my bike on these beautiful white roads is a very difficult feeling to describe… The only way to find out is to come here and ride with us.”
