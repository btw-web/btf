All-new Hope Tech 4 E4 Brakes

I’ve become somewhat of an evangelist for Hope brakes over the last year. After putting their Tech 3 E4 brakes to a +3,000-mile long-term test spanning the East Coast, Baja Divide, and loads of riding in the steep and rowdy surrounds of Oaxaca, I’m sold. As we stated in our 2021 Gear of the Year awards, “These brakes are extremely impressive and are unmatched in the lever feel and modulation department… Their impressive machining, reliability, and superior performance make them my top pick for MTB brakes.”

The all-new Tech 4 E4 brakes build on this platform using new internal sealing for reduced friction and a new roller bearing pivot, allowing the spring rate to be reduced. According to Hope, “The lower spring rate in turn gives a lighter lever action, reducing rider fatigue and keeping our legendary feel and modulation.” I honestly can’t imagine it getting any better than the excellent feel of the Tech 3, so Hope certainly has my attention.

The E4 caliper was improved as well: “The updated E4 caliper utilizes hybrid style stainless steel pistons with a phenolic insert allowing for smoother movement and less maintenance, while still being able to manage high temperatures without heat transfer during extreme use.” Here are the full specs from Hope:

  • Increased power versus Tech 3
  • Lever shape updated to provide the best mechanical advantage during braking
  • Hinged clamp reducing weight and ergonomics
  • Shifter integration improved for more adjustability
  • Tool-free bite point and reach adjustments
  • Hybrid piston design
  • Rigid CNC’d one-piece caliper
  • Compatible with a wider range of current shifter options
  • Post mount options available
  • Choose either standard or braided hose (braided hose is an extra cost)

Tech 4 E4 brakes are available in Black, Purple, Red, Orange, Blue, and Silver with a braided hose for £205/€255/$260 or a black hose for £195/€245/$245. Hope also released the single-piston, XC-oriented Tech 4 X2 and the 4-piston downhill-specific Tech 4 V4. Details over at HopeTech.com.
