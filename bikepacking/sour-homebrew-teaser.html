Sour Homebrew Teaser

Our friends at Sour Bicycles (@sour.bicycles) in Dresden, Germany, recently sent over this teaser video called Homebrew, and although they haven’t spilled the beans or made a formal announcement yet, we’re pretty sure they have some exciting news in the pipeline. From what we can see in the video and the tagline “Coming Home,” it seems like they have plans to move at least some of their production closer to their home base in eastern Germany. At the time of publishing, there’s a countdown on their site with a little under six days remaining until the big reveal. We’ll be sure to share the news as soon as it’s made available.

Once the timer is expired, you’ll be able to find full details at Sour.Bike.
