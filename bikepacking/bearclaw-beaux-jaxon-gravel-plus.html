Bearclaw Unveils the Beaux Jaxon “Titanium Gravel Plus Bike”

Michigan-based Bearclaw Bicycle Co claims its recently announced Beaux Jaxon is “the first drop-bar bicycle in the history of drop-bar bikes to accept 27.5 x 3.0″ and 29 x 2.6″ tires while maintaining true all-road geometry.” They don’t think of it as a mountain bike with drop-bars; instead, it’s an all-road bike with mountain bike tires. Either way, we’ve added it to our big Gear Index of 29″ drop-bar Mountain Bikes. Here are a few pics, the specs, and geometry chart.

FEATURES

  • 148x12mm Boost thru-axle frame
  • 100x15mm RAMHORN fork
  • Fits up to 27.5×3 or 29×2.6 tires
  • 3 sets of three-pack bottle mounts on the frame
  • 2 sets of three-pack bottle mounts on the Ramhorn fork
  • Rack and fender mounts
  • Flat mount brake mounts
  • “Ultradistance Gravel Friendly Geometry. (UGFG)
  • 73mm BSA Standard Bottom Bracket
  • Full-external cable routing
  • Dropper post routing (external down tube – internal seat tube)
  • 3AL-2.5V double-butted titanium frame
  • 6AL-4V billet head tube, bb, dropouts, derailleur hanger, and chainstay plate

According to their website, the Bearclaw Beaux Jaxon will have two builds available starting at $3890. Both based around the Beaux Jaxon Ti 3AL-2.5V ALL-ROAD frame and the RAMHORN gravel+ fork. expect availability early February-ish.
