How to Beat the Kokopelli Trail’s Fastest Known Time (video)

Prior to attempting to beat one of mountain biking’s most-coveted Fastest Known Time (FKT) records—the 140-mile Kokopelli Trail running from Loma, Colorado to Moab, Utah—Peter Stetina chats with several elite cyclists who’ve ridden the route to determine what it will take to beat the record time of of 10 hours and 56 minutes set in 2020 by Kurt Refsnider

Fastest known times are meant to be broken. -Peter Stetina

Spoiler Alert: On board the Canyon Lux full-suspension cross-country mountain bike, Stetina applies his learnings to the trail and sets a new record of 10:24:43 to be the new FKT holder. Find more on this over at Canyon.com.
