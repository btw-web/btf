On the King’s Highway (Film)

Photos by David Styv (@styvdavid)

Back in March of last year, we shared Axel Carion’s story of his record-setting trip on the Jordan Bike Trail with fellow ultra-cyclist Jonas Deichmann, during which the pair of riders pedaled the 730 kilometers from Umm Qais in the north to Aqaba in the south in just 120 hours. As a follow-up to their trip, they recently released a 16-minute documentary-style film that provides an intimate look at what the two riders experienced as they crossed Jordan, engaging with local people and tackling tough terrain along the way.
