New Spurcycle Hip Pack

The new Spurcycle Hip Pack is made in the USA using lightweight Dyneema and X-Pac fabrics to create a simple and technical pack that weighs in at just 250 grams. According to Spurcycle, the Hip Pack was a year in the making before the first production run of 200 hip packs was released a few days ago.

The Spurcycle Hip Pack measures 28 x 10 x 14cm (11 x 4 x 5.5″) with four liters of packing volume and a zipper closure. It also has four interior pockets for stashing snacks or tools, and an internal zipper pocket to keep your phone or other valuables secure. Lastly, the pack has an external cord on the top to lash an extra layer.
