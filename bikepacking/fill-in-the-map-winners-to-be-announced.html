Fill in the Map Winners to be Announced…

Sorry for the delay, folks. I know many of you were waiting to hear whether your entry was selected, or just looking forward to seeing some great new overnighter routes. We’ve finally gotten through the heaps of submissions, voted, and are starting to contact and announce the winners that will be published on our Local Overnighter map throughout the next month. We’ve already contacted a couple winners, and will be reaching out to others over the next week or two. The first winner we selected and contacted is Emily Cheng (@alpinemily), whose North Bay Overnighter: Tour of Microclimates was published as a feature today! Don’t miss it and the incredible gallery of photos Emily captured. And, stay tuned for more…
