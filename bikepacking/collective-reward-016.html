Collective Reward #016: Swagstravaganza

For Collective Reward #016, we’ve curated a ridiculous collection of swag. The hoard includes 16 patches, two bandanas, three water bottles, two T-shirts (Jones Bikes and Blackburn, both size Medium), a WTF totebag, and more stickers than you can count (many not shown). One lucky Bikepacking Collective member will win it all. Winner will be drawn December 31st. If you’re not already a member of The Bikepacking Collective, get signed up for your chance to win.
