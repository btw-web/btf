Collective Reward #032: Widefoot CargoMount and LiterCage

Nebraska-based Widefoot designs and builds a small but growing collection of practical, durable, innovative products, and we’re excited to be able to share a couple of them with our supporters. For Collective Reward #032, two lucky Bikepacking Collective members will win a prize package from Widefoot that includes one LiterCage, one CargoMount, and two Voile straps, all in their choice of colors and lengths.

CargoMount

As the latest product from Widefoot, the CargoMount offers a beautiful and practical way to attach some extra gear to your bikepacking rig. It’s made of a lightweight aluminum and is available in four different powder coated colors. Widefoot’s clever 3×3 mounting configuration enables users to fine tune the CargoMount’s position on frames/forks with three-pack bosses. Best of all, it weights just 71g.

LiterCage

Widefoot’s LiterCage securely hold oversized (3.5″ diameter) bottles that are too large to fit in standard bottle cages. This includes most bottles from Klean Kanteen, Hydro Flask, and Nalgene (there’s a complete list of Widefoot’s site). Their unique five-hole mounting bracket lets users find the perfect location for the LiterCage using standard two-bolt bosses, or with three-pack bosses, or even using hose clamps. The LiterCage is made from durable stainless steel, and like the CargoMount it’s designed and manufactured in Nebraska, USA.

If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, join today and you’ll be eligible for this Widefoot prize package (and the growing list of other Collective Rewards), and you’ll also receive two issue of The Bikepacking Journal in the mail each year, along with several other great member benefits. If you’ve already joined, you’re entered to win. Good luck!
