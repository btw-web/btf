Swift Industries’ New Every Day Caddy

Not every ride ends with setting up camp in some remote location, and sometimes you just need enough room to carry the essentials. That’s exactly where the new Swift Industries Every Day Caddy (or EDC for short) comes in. At first glance, it might look like your standard saddlebag, but it’s actually based around a two-piece design that allows for easier, quicker access—which is often the downfall of tiny under-saddle bags.

The Swift Every Day Caddy uses an external VX21 harness with an HDPE insert for stiffness and attaches to your saddle rails using an included 15” Voile Strap and bungee toggle to your seatpost. A removable 1L rolltop Dyneema Ripstop stuff sack fits inside the harness and clips to each side of the harness. The Every Day Caddy is currently offered in Black, Coyote, and Dark Green, as well as a limited edition Swift Campout purple version. Check out Swift’s launch video below.

  • Capacity: 1L
  • Material: VX21 X-Pac / Dyneema Ripstop
  • Weight: 106 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Washington, USA
  • Price: $80 USD

Check out the Every Day Caddy bag over at BuiltBySwift.com.
