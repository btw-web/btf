Zpacks New Made in USA Goose Down Jacket

Florida-based Zpacks just launched a new Goose Down Jacket that they say strikes the perfect balance between weight and warmth. The ZPaks Goose Down Jacket is constructed with responsibly sourced and Responsible Down Standard certified DownTek water-resistant 950 fill power goose down, which stays dry 90% longer than untreated down. The liner and shell are both treated with a PFOA and PFO free C0 durable water repellent to resist light rain and body moisture.

Features and add-ons are kept to a minimum to keep the jacket lightweight, but there are still two hem pockets for your hands, a fitted/adjustable hood, an adjustable hem elastic, plus elastic at the wrists to keep the warmth in. There’s also a size-specific internal chest pocket that can be flipped inside out to store the jacket when not in use or to be used as a pillow.

  • Material: 51 oz/sqyd Ventum Ripstop Nylon
  • Insulation: DownTek 950 Fill Power Water Repellent Goose Down
  • Fill Weight: 85g (small) to 130g (xxl)
  • Total Weight: 174g (small) to 249g (xxl)
  • Place of Manufacture: Florida, USA
  • Price: $375 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Zpacks.com
