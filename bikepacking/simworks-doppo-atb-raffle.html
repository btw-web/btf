SimWorks Social Reform Benefit Raffle

Words by SimWorks

In light of recent events, and in the wake of a groundswell of inspiring actions and initiatives taken by numerous businesses and individuals, SimWorks is hosting a Social Reform Benefit Raffle. We wish to join with so many others in the fight to do our part to make an impact for greater equality, social justice, and the dismantling of institutions and policies of systemic racism and oppression here in this country. We ask that you join with us in making a contribution to several organizations that are doing the hard work of providing resources and leadership in the fight to secure social, educational, political, and economic equality, with a goal of eliminating discrimination and ensuring the health and well-being of all persons.

Prizes

Simworks is giving away one Doppo ATB complete bike (57cm) with SimWorks original parts (So Bar, Anna Lee Stem, Beatnik Seatpost, Turtle 58 fender, Volummy 27.5×1.9″, SimRoll, Nissen Stainless Outer Cable, SimDrops) and the following:

  • Paul Components x SimWorks Chocolate Edition (Klamper, QR Skewers, QR Seat Collar)
  • Chris King (Headset, Bottom Bracket, Front & Rear Hub)
  • Velocity Cliffhanger Wheels
  • White Industries crankset
  • Shimano XTR, 11-speed drivetrain
  • Selle Anatomica X1 Series Saddle Vintage

There will also be second prize to include a SimWorks cockpit (stem/handlebar/seatpost/bar-ends) and a third prize of any pair of Simworks tires.

How to Enter

SimWorks donation raffle can be entered a couple of ways. You can elect to either purchase a raffle ticket for $25, with your donation (100% of proceeds) going to an organization among a list of options including Black Lives Matter, Don’t Shoot PDX, and more; or you can spend $100 or more in the Simworks web store and your name will be added to our raffle participants list. For all customers spending $100 or more, you can provide indication of an organization of your choice amongst a list of options, and Simworks will donate 10% of your merchandise total. This 10% pledge is honored for both our retail customers as well as our fantastic dealers and builders.

Additionally, for those individuals electing to purchase a raffle ticket for $25, in addition to your raffle entrance, you will receive a discount code for 20% off a future transaction in our web store. These codes will be issued and e-mailed to recipients each evening during the course of this raffle. Of course we welcome people to combine these options as well. Multiple entries in to the raffle provide you with more chances to collect prizes.
