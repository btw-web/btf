Bikepacking La Siberia: The GR247 in Winter (Video)

The GR247 is an incredible route that loops through southern Spain’s Sierras de Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Natural Park. Sergio Luna Naranjo (@viajarandroll) tackled this challenging ride in the dead of winter, crossing the fields of Hernán Perea with a blanket of snow, and he did so completely alone. Watch Sergio’s documentary about the trip below, then scroll down for more on the route, including a link to our full route guide to the GR247.

The GR247 is a wonderful loop that we’ve ridden on a couple of occasions, the latest being on the Altravesur bikepacking route. Find details about the GR247 below and check out the related content grid for more routes and stories from southern Spain.

The GR247

This 200-mile (309-kilometer) loop through Andalusia’s Sierras De Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Natural Park offers a nice mix of fantastically rugged riding, Spanish small-town amenities, and rural Andalusian culture… all within what are arguably the most beautiful mountains south of Madrid.
