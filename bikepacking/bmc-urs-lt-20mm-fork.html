The BMC URS LT Features a 20mm Micro-suspension Fork

We covered the interesting BMC URS when it was released in 2019. With a rather progressive geometry and 10mm of built-in travel on the rear stays, it’s one of the more forward-thinking gravel bikes that’s come to market recently.

The company just announced the next iteration of the URS, the BMC URS LT, which features a 20mm “Micro Travel Technology Suspension Fork.” According to BMC, it’s optimized for gravel riding by dampening more than 46% of the typical impacts. The fork uses a coil-sprung and oil-damped design that’s completely concealed in the steerer with the hydraulic damper sitting above the crown. It runs with 25% sag (5mm) and has a lockout control that’s mounted directly on top of the steerer. Find specs and photos below.

The BMC URS LT is offered in sizes S-XL and comes in two builds. The URS LT One retails for CHF8,499.00 (about $9,186 USD) with yellow paint and a Force AXS Eagle drivetrain. The gray BMC URS LT Two has a price tag of CHF6,499.00 ($7,025 USD) and comes with a Rival AXS Eagle drivetrain. Find the full specs and details over at BMC-Switzerland.com.
