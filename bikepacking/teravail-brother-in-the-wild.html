Teravail × Brother in the Wild Giveaway

Photos by Tom Farrel (@fomtaz)

To celebrate Teravail coming on as a partner for the 2021 Brother in the Wild UK event, Brother Cycles just announced a new giveaway! All you have to do is sign up for the Brother Cycles newsletter and you’ll be entered into the drawing. The winner will receive a pair of Teravail tires of their choice, plus two tickets to the sold-out Brother in the Wild UK event, which is taking place from September 10th to 12th in Dorset.

For those unfamiliar, the Brother in the Wild series is a meetup event based around gravel riding and good times. Although we haven’t had the opportunity to attend one of their events ourselves, we’re looking forward to making that happen one of these days. You can get a better idea of what it’s all about by checking out our recap of the 2020 Brother in the Wild UK event.

Find all the details and fine print over at BrotherCycles.com.
