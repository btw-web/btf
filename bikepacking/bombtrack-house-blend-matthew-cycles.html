Bombtrack House Blend: Matthew Cycles

We head to Tokyo for Bombtrack’s latest House Blend video shop tour, where we meet the small crew behind Matthew Cycle, learn a little about what makes them special, and watch as they put together quite an unexpected interpretation of Bombtrack’s popular Hook EXT frameset. Check out the five-minute video below, along with a few photos of the finished build and a list of selected components.

Build Highlights

  • Frame/Fork Bombtrack Hook EXT
  • Rims Velocity Aileron
  • Tires Teravail Ehline
  • Handlebars Nitto Hunter
  • Headset White Industries
  • Crankset White Industries
  • Pedals MKS GAMMA
  • Brakes Magura MT4
  • Saddle Sella Italia Flite
  • Stem Veno Hi Stack

You can find more from Matthew Cycle on Instagram @matthewcycle.
