The 2017 Swift Campout is on!

For the third year in a row, Swift Industries is putting out a global call for a bikepacking overnighter to take place on Saturday night closest to the Summer Solstice — June, 24th, 2017. Given that this is one of the longest days of the year — for folks in the Northern Hemisphere — that means more time for riding, swimming, fishing, cooking, rock climbing, or whatever other fun times you want to weave into your solstice overnight trip. For a few ideas on how to plan your 2017 Swift Campout, check out our bikepacking routes map. Otherwise, plan your own. Here’s the official press release from Swift with more information:

Seattle, WA — Swift Industries announces Swift Campout, its 3rd annual bike-camping campaign. Nominated “Best New Event” in BIKEPACKING.com’s 2015 Bikepacking Awards, the event calls adventure cyclists worldwide to go on a bike-camping overnight on June 24th, 2017. Leading up to the event, the Swift Campout campaign serves as a twelve-week digital skillshare for the adventure community and includes assignments and prizes issued every two weeks for generating bike-camping content for the Swift Campout community.

Swift Campout is hosted at builtbyswift.com/swiftcampout, where bicycle-campers register their Campout to gain access the digital skillshare dispatch for challenges and assignments to earn high quality outdoor and cycling gear from our presenting partners.

There’s also patches and other goods at their online store for the event…

About Swift Industries

Swift Industries is a bicycle bag manufacturing company in Seattle, WA. All Swift Industries products are designed and manufactured in-house by a team that is committed to high-quality purpose-built products that defy mainstream gear aesthetics. The Swift Industries brand is a pre-eminent generator of culture and trend in the cycling and outdoor industries. For more company information please see our full Press Kit.

Seeking Press Participation

Let’s rally the bicycle and outdoor community to go bike-camping on June 24th and 25th, 2017. Help us spread the word through your publications, social media avenues, and by participating with your own Swift Campout! Please direct your audiences to builtbyswift.com/swiftcampout to register.
