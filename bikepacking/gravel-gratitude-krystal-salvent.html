Gravel & Gratitude: Krystal Salvent and Her Salsa Warbird

Photos by Adam Andres Pawlikiewicz Mesa

The first installment of Salsa’s powerful new storysite, Gravel & Gratitude, is now live. Part one of three features Krystal Salvent (@krystalsalvent), co-leader of Black Girls Do Bike’s Denver chapter, who shares the story of how she first got into cycling and how riding has helped her find joy, community, and more.

Learn more about Krystal (and her Salsa Warbird) over at SalsaCycles.com, featuring a beautiful gallery of images by Adam Andres Pawlikiewicz Mesa and a short video from Evan Green. And stay tuned, as we’ll be sharing parts two and three as they’re released in the coming weeks.
