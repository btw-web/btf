Blackburn’s Hitchhiker Bag can be used with a handlebar roll or without

Blckburn’s Outpost Elite Hitchhiker bag is designed to add 2.5 liters of storage to a handlebar roll bikepacking system or on day riders or commutes as a standalone bar bag. In addition to its unique box shape, the Hitchhiker features a water-resistant welded seam design and flip top opening, internal organizer pockets, and an external bungee to strap an extra layer or carry out trash from the backcountry. Using the included longer straps, the Hitchhiker works with the Outpost Elite Handlebar Roll as well as other brands’ bar rolls. It also comes with two short straps for attaching directly to the handlebars.

  • Weight: 250 grams
  • Installation: 2 Short Bar Straps, 2 Long Bar Straps W/G-Hooks
  • Capacity: 2.5L
  • Place of Manufacture: China
  • Price: $80
  • Manufacturer’s Details: BlackburnDessign.com
