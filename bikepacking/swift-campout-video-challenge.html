The Swift Campout Video Challenge

In collaboration with Skid Lizards, Swift Industries just announced the Swift Campout Video Challenge. They’re looking to get a glimpse into your 2020 Campout experience: the good, the bad, the ugly, the hilarious.

First, participate in the official Swift Campout on September 26-27th. While doing so, capture your Campout on vid with an iPhone or pro gear, it really doesn’t matter. They’re looking for creativity, entertainment, hilarity, and meditative scenic inspiration. One talented winner will have their project shared on the Swift Instagram and YouTube channels, and will receive a giant prize package stacked with gear from Topo Designs, Kitsbow, Bedrock Sandals, Dangle Supply, Camp And Go Slow, Sixmoon Designs, Fat Tire, MiiR, Black Coffee Roasting, and Hey Hi Hello Magazine!

To enter, follow these guidelines:

  • Edit and share your footage to IGTV the week of Sep 28th. Must meet October 4th deadline!
  • Must tag @swiftindustries @skidlizards #swiftcampout #swiftcampoutvidchallenge in post!
  • Video must be no longer than 5-minutes in length!
  • Bonus points for sharing to Stories, Reels, and main feed
