“I Just Want to Ride” Film Screenings Announced

“I Just Want to Ride,” a film by Rugile Kaladyte, follows Lael Wilcox as she races the 2019 Tour Divide, a 2,750 mile self-supported mountain bike race from the Canada to Mexico along the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route. Having set the women’s record in 2015, she went back to attempt to win the overall race. An all-star race field, an unprecedented summer solstice snowstorm, and online noise fueled her race. Ultimately, Lael just wanted to ride.

The film will be presented at several screenings prior to launch in mid-November. Find it here:

October 15th — Anchorage, Alaska (The Bicycle Shop at Dimond, 7:00 PM)
October 17th — Louisville, Colorado (PEARL iZUMi HQ, 6:30 PM)
October 21st — Leadville, Colorado (Leadvelo Bicicasa, 6:00 PM)
October 23rd — Salida, Colorado (A Church, 7:00 PM)
November 5th — Atlanta, Georgia (Location & Time TBD)
November 15th — Tucson, Arizona (Campfire Cycling HQ, 6:00 PM)
