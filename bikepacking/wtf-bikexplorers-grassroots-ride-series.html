WTF Bikexplorers Grassroots Ride Series

Just announced on the coattails of the WTF Bikexplorers Summit, the Grassroots Ride Series is an effort to create a stronger, more connected WTF Bikexploring community. WTF Bikexplorers is seeking ride leaders to organize multi-day, self-supported adventure rides within their local WTF communities.

In addition to the call for ride leaders, WTF also unveiled the map below, which features registered rides all over the country. When you register your ride it will be shared on the WTF Bikexplorers website, where you can connect with riders all over the map! The window to register rides for the duration of 2019 is open through May 31st.
