To the End of the Universe (Video)

Reflecting on adventures of a lifetime, Crystal Haggard says hers is being a mother to Forrest. The team at Wahoo Fitness tagged along with the pair to document a snapshot of their life together for this moving piece on the joys and challenges of motherhood.

“This video is for all the rad moms out there that are leading by example, challenging the status quo, and raising the next generation of adventurers and rippers,” says Wahoo. You can watch the full seven-minute film below, which features Cyrstal reading a heartfelt letter to Forrest set against footage of the two of them riding through a variety of cinematic landscapes around the American Southwest.

“You taught me that riding bikes doesn’t need to be about how far or fast you go. It can be about making up a new game, telling a story, or just being together in a new place.”
