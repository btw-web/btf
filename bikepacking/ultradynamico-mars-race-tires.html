Ultradynamico Launches New 2.2″ MARS Race Tires

The Ultradynamico Rubber Corporation (@ultradynamico) just announced their latest tire, the 2.2″ MARS Race, which is currently available in a 26 x 2.2″ version with red casing, and a grey 27.5″ x 2.2″ version expected to be announced next week. The new MARS Race tires are priced at $85/piece and come in at around 600g per tire. As Ultradynamico describes their newest release, “Sometimes you veer slightly off course and end up in a place like MARS. If you are running our rider-designed, ATB performance tuned rubber, you’ll not only be styling for the NASA livestream, but you’ll also be in for a great ride.”
