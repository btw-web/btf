PAUL Announces Limited Edition Purple

Not sure if it’s age, or the fact that I’m colorblind, but purple has crept in as my new favorite color over the last couple years. Just in time, PAUL cooked up a limited edition batch! They offered purple way back in the early 90’s when it was all the rage, and they launched a throwback purple run a couple years ago to kick off PAULcamp. And, apparently, people haven’t stopped asking for it since. So, for a limited time, they are offering Boxcar Stems, seat posts, brake levers, Klampers, and more in a juicy electric purple anodized finish.

Parts are available for pre-order now (orders ready in 4-6 weeks). And, if you’d like something else they make in purple, you can email them a request. Head over to PAULcomp.com to get in on the pre-order.
