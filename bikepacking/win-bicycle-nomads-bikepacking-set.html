Win Bicycle Nomad’s Bikepacking Set Up

State Bicycle, Blackburn Design, and Bicycle Nomad have teamed up to give away what they’re calling the “Bicycle Nomad ultimate bikepacking set up” to one lucky winner, which includes a limited edition State Bicycle Co. x Bicycle Nomad frameset and a full suite of bags and accessories from Blackburn—the same bikepacking setup that Erick Cedeño of Bicycle Nomad used during his recent ride on the Underground Railroad Route. On top of that, the winner is also entitled to an hour-long bike camping Zoom clinic with Erick. Taken together, it’s a prize package worth more than $1,000 in parts and gear.

Full Prize Package:

  • State Bicycle Co. x Bicycle Nomad Limited Edition Frameset ($459.99)
  • Blackburn Outpost Elite Seat Pack ($170)
  • Blackburn Outpost Elite Handlebar Bag ($150)
  • Blackburn Outpost Frame Pack ($140)
  • Blackburn Core pump ($40)
  • Blackburn Big Switch Tool ($30)
  • 2x Blackburn Outpost Cages ($30/pc)
  • Blackburn Outpost Bike And Camp Headlamp ($60)
  • One-hour private bike camping clinic provided by Bicycle Nomad via Zoom (Priceless)

Note: This giveaway is only open to residents of the United States who are 18+ years old.

Find complete details and entry instructions over at StateBicycle.com. Act fast, as winners will be announced on January 1st, 2021! Want to know more about Bicycle Nomad’s partnership with State Bicycle? Find our interview here.
