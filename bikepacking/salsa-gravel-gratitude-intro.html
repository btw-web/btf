Gravel & Gratitude from Salsa Cycles: Introduction

Photos by Adam Andres Pawlikiewicz Mesa

Salsa’s latest digital project is a four-part storysite called Gravel & Gratitude that celebrates gravel and highlights the stories of three people who love to ride it: Krystal Salvent, Leonardo Brasil, and Brooke Goudy. Over the next several weeks, Gravel & Gratitude will explore the relationship between riding and how the three riders feel physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Dreamed up and realized by an incredibly talented team of friends of the site—with photography by Adam Andres Pawlikiewicz Mesa (@adamonthego), videography by Evan Green (@thegreenevan), and production by Jalen Bazile (@jalenbazile)—we’re eagerly awaiting the next three parts of this series.

Head over to SalsaCycles.com to check out an introduction to the riders in the upcoming installments (premiering April 20th, April 27th, and May 11th) and the creative team behind the project.
