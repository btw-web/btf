Apidura’s New City Handlebar Pack

Designed to carry commuting essentials such as tools, a phone, or extra layers, the new Apidura City Handlebar Pack is made from welded waterproof PU-coated nylon and can attach to the handlebars on any bike. The Handlebar Pack features a full-width velcro opening, a large storage chamber with an internal mesh pocket and back slip pocket, quick-release buckles, subtle reflective details, and Hypalon-reinforced straps for durability.

The Apidura City Handlebar Pack has two liters of storage and measures 15cm tall by 10cm deep by 20cm wide. According to their website it weighs 200 grams and sells for £52.50. Find details over at Apidura.com.
