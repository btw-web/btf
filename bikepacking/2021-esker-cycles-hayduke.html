Esker Cycles Hayduke in Stock with new Build Specs

The Hayduke is Esker Cycles’ versatile and longstanding Chromoly steel mountain bike. It has clearance for both 27.5 x 3.0″ and 29 x 2.3″ tires, swing-arm swappable dropouts that allow for multiple drivetrain setups, and is Esker’s vision of the perfect hardtail. Until now, the Hayduke has always been equipped with a 120mm 29” fork built with 27.5”+ wheels and tires to allow riders swap between the 27.5”+ and 29” wheel sizes. Although the same versatility will exists, this new crop of Haydukes will be built up with a 27.5″ 140mm travel fork to focus on the 27.5″ platform—making way for a soon-to-be-announced 29″ Esker model. The Hayduke is now offered in three different builds and as a frameset, which are all outlined below.

Hayduke H1

  • Fork Marzocchi Z2 140mm
  • Brakes Shimano Deore 4-piston
  • Dropper SDG Tellis / SDG Lever
  • Wheels Stans Flow S2 Comp / Shimano MT620
  • Drivetrain Shimano Deore 12-Speed, 10-51T
  • Tires Terrene McFly 27.5 x 2.8″
  • Price $2,000 USD

Hayduke H2

  • Fork MRP Raven 140mm
  • Brakes SRAM G2 RS
  • Dropper SDG Tellis / Wolf Tooth Remote
  • Wheels Industry Nine 1/1 Enduro S
  • Drivetrain SRAM GX 12-Speed, 10-52T
  • Tires Terrene McFly 27.5 x 2.8″
  • Price $2,950 USD

Hayduke H3

  • Fork MRP Ribbon 140mm
  • Brakes Shimano XT 4-piston
  • Dropper SDG Tellis / Wolf Tooth Remote
  • Wheels Industry Nine 1/1 Enduro S
  • Drivetrain Shimano XT 12-Speed, 10-51T
  • Tires Terrene McFly 27.5 x 2.8″
  • Price $3,250 USD

The Hayduke is also available as a frameset, complete with Portage dropouts, an axle, seat collar, and a Wolf Tooth Components headset for $750. It’s offered in five different frame sizes and in four different colours: Sublime (green), Caterpillar (yellow), Ranchera (red), and Ivan (white). Complete builds are available in limited quantities exclusively through Esker Cycles.

Find complete builds kits and ordering info at EskerCycles.com.
