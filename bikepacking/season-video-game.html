Season: A Bicycle Adventure Video Game

Season is a third-person adventure video game where you play as a solo woman traveller whose mission is to document, photograph, draw, and record life. It’s part bicycle trip, part cultural exploration, set in a surreal version of the mid-twentieth century, where thousands of years have passed without any progress. Besides the premise of a bicycle adventure video game, the amazing artwork and serene stylizing caught our attention as something truly unique.

Writer and creative director Kevin Sullivan said he’s excited to present the long-gestating project. “We’re dizzily excited to share the world of Season with the world of the real world. Just as the character in the game makes a cultural collection, the game itself has been that for us. It has absorbed our worries about the state of the planet, our joy at living on it, things we’ve seen, people we’ve loved and lost, all tied together into something, hopefully, beautiful and strange,” he said.

Season isn’t available to play just yet, but will be available on Steam for PC and Playstation soon. Head over to Play-Season.com to learn more.
