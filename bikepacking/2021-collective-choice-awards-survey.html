2021 Collective Choice Awards Survey

For the third year in a row, we just sent out our annual Collective Choice survey asking our Bikepacking Collective members for their picks for the best films, routes, and bikes of 2021. Members, be sure to check your email inboxes, and if you don’t see it there, check your spam folder. You can also find a link to the survey under the “Member Benefits” tab when you’re logged in on the website (where you can also find the current list of 15 exclusive discounts from some of our favorite bike and outdoor brands). Thanks for sharing your opinions! We look forward to kicking off our annual Bikepacking Awards soon…
