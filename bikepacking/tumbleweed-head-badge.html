Tumbleweed’s New Head Badge and Waterproof Frame Bag

The Tumbleweed Bicycle Company just released a new head badge, adding to the swagger of the Prospector, a made to go anywhere bikepacking bike that’s been featured on several posts here. Designed by Tom Lane, AKA @gingermonkey, the new sculptural head badges will be included on all new Prospector frames and will also be available for anyone who has already purchased a Prospector.

In addition, Tumbleweed has collaborated with bag maker Porcelain Roacket to create Prospector-specific 52Hz frame packs. These bags are patterned specifically for all Prospector frame sizes in a custom brick/rust/terra cotta fabric selected to work with either of the Prospector colors, Sandglow or Turquoise. Learn more at the Tumbleweed web store. There are only a handful of each size.
