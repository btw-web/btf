Introducing the Swift Industries Dovetail Collection

In partnership with the Appleton-Whittell Research Ranch (AWRR), an 8,000-acre expanse of grassland prairies, oak woodlands, and ephemeral riparian habitat in southern Arizona, Swift Industries will be leveraging the Dovetail Collection to raise awareness and contribute 5% of revenues toward the organization’s conservation, research, and outreach programming. AWRR’s liaison to the cycling community, professional cyclist Sarah Swallow, will join the campaign to speak on behalf of the Research Ranch’s mission.

The Swift Industries Dovetail Collection includes Swift’s large-capacity bag, the Zeitgeist Pack ($228 USD), Kestrel Handlebar Bag ($150 USD), the Sidekick Stem Pouch ($60 USD), and the Seeker Travel Kit ($65 USD). Rounding out the collection is the Standard Issue 8×25 binocular from NOCS Provisions ($95 USD), a letterpress field notebook from Mordecai Book Building ($14 USD), and a Dovetail commemorative cotton twill “dad cap” ($28 USD). Each item in the collection features Wyatt Hersey’s stunning Kestrel patch, and for those who want more, the Dovetail Patch Kit ($16 USD) adds three more feathered friends: the great blue heron (Ardea), the peregrine falcon, and the Cassin’s vireo.

The entire Dovetail Collection is live now on BuiltBySwift.com.
