Swift × Crust Zeitgeist Pack Collab

I don’t want to brag, but I think my 2021 bikepacking prediction of more maker collaborations is ringing true so far. The latest comes from Swift Industries and Crust Bikes, who partnered on a limited edition Zeitgeist Pack that is available to purchase directly through Crust’s website. The Zeitgeist Pack has two sets of mounting slots that are spaced to fit both handlebars and saddle loops (including Brooks saddles). It also comes with two Voilé Nano Straps, foam spacers, and an internal HDPE stiffener to help it hold its shape.

The Swift × Crust Zeitgeist Pack comes in either black or teal with the Crust Unicorn embroidered onto the flap. The bag is constructed from VX21 X-Pac by Swift in Seattle, Washington and provides up to 12L of storage—the perfect size for most wide drop bars.

  • Capacity: 12L
  • Material: VX21 X-Pac
  • Weight: 626 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Washington, USA
  • Price: $195 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: CrustBikes.com
