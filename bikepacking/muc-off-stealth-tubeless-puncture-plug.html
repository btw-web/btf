Muc Off Stealth Tubeless Puncture Plug

Stealth Tubeless Puncture Plugs are CNC machined from 6061 aluminum and feature an expanding silica plug that fits most mountain bike and gravel/road handlebars with a 17mm or larger internal diameter. Each pair of plugs includes swappable 17mm, 19mm and 21mm silica plugs to fit with different handlebar diameters. The plugs are compatible with all open-end grips and drops bars, and secure into the handlebar ends using an expansion screw that tightens using a 4mm Allen key. Each pair of Mic Off Stealth Tubeless Puncture Plugs include:

  • 1 x Stainless steel 2-in-1 puncture plug/reamer tool
  • 1 x knife trimming tool
  • 5 x thin tire plugs 5 x medium tire plugs & 5 x thick tire plugs
  • Dimensions 89 x 27 x 27mm
  • Weight: 33g per side (excluding tire plugs)
  • Colors: Available in 10 anodized colors
  • Price: £30 / 35€
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Muc-Off.com
