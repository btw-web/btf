Two New Arrivals from Cascada

Cascada’s apparel lineup aims for the perfect mix of performance fabric and rider-friendly features, all with timeless colours and fun graphics. Most of their products are made in Italy and look like great options for bikepacking as well as time off the bike. I’m a huge fan of their button-up Land Wool Shirt, which accompanied me on lots of rides this winter and a trip into the South Chilcotins of British Columbia last year. Casacada just announced two new products: the Raw Lightweight Merino Jersey and Howling Button Up Shirt.

Cascada Raw Lightweight Merino Jersey

The Cascada Raw Lightweight Merino Jersey is made from a smooth, lightweight blend of merino wool and recycled polyester. It’s designed to be quick-drying, moisture-wicking, resistant to odour, and ideal for year-round outdoor pursuits. It has a relaxed fit, is made in Italy, and comes in either a rust or sage colour.

  • Material: 50% Merino Wool, 50% Recycled Polyester
  • Size Range: XS – XL
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €80 (~$95 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Cascada.cc

Cascada Howling Button Up Shirt

We’re firm believers that a good collared shirt can help avoid, and potentially get you out of, certain sticky situations while out bikepacking. Their new wolf-covered Howling Button Up Shirt is made from a stretchy blend of polyester and elastane to keep it lightweight and breathable but still casual enough to not look completely out of place around town. The shirt has a button-up closure, a longer rounded back hem, two chest pockets, a sunglasses loop on the front, and is also made in Italy.

  • Material: 91% Polyester, 9% Elastane
  • Size Range: XS – XL
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €100 (~$119 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Cascada.cc

You can browse Cascada’s entire range of cycling apparel over at Cascada.cc.
