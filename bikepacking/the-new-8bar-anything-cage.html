The New 8bar Anything Cage

Following the launch of the TFLSBERG bikepacking bike, the new 8bar Anything Cage is the latest cargo cage to hit the market, and looks like more of a lightweight rack system than a cage. The Anything Cage (which has the same name as Salsa’s original Anything Cage, for the record) is a black powder coated aluminum cage featuring six mounting holes, two cargo straps, and a rather large platform that has more of a flat profile than traditional cages. Find details and specs below…

8bar Anything Cage

  • Height: 24cm (9.5″)
  • Width: 13cm (5.1″)
  • Depth: 10cm (3.9″)
  • Weight: 280 grams (9.9oz)
  • Max load diameter: 20cm (7.9″)

The 8bar Anything Cage is available now for 29.90€. Learn more over at 8bar-bikes.com.
