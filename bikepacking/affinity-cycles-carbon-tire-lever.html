Affinity Cycles Launches US-made Carbon Tire Lever

Although carbon fiber tire levers might not be the most popular upgrade, it’s no secret that cheaply made, plastic tire levers break and get thrown outâ€”and that’s no good. New York City-based Affinity Cycles has designed a US-made carbon fiber tire lever that is apparently much stronger and durable than traditional levers, while also being rim-friendly whether you’re riding alloy or carbon rims.

The levers are constructed from both unidirectional and 3k woven carbon fiber, feature dual-sided ergonomics, and are designed and manufactured in-house at Affinity HQ in Brooklyn, New York. The are priced at $25/pair and are available online through AffinityCycles.com and from your local bike shop via J&B importers. Affinity doesn’t provide an exact weight, but we’ve reached out to find out and will update this post when we know.

Due to demand, levers are being sold on a made to order basis as Affinity ramps up production.
