Redshift Dream Gravel Bike Giveaway

Redshift Sports – makers of the Shockstop Stem and Seatpost, among other innovative products – is giving away a complete Pivot Vault, built up as what they’re calling the Dream Gravel Bike. All in, it’s valued at over $8,000. They’ve partnered with several leading component manufacturers to create something special. One winner will receive a Vault frameset in their size, built up as shown below.

Build Highlights Include:

  • Pivot Vault Frameset
  • SRAM 1x Group
  • Stages Power Meter
  • BOYD Carbon Wheels
  • Redshift ShockStop Stem + Seatpost
  • IRC Tires
  • Form Saddle
  • Apidura Bags
  • Wolf Tooth Accessories

To enter this giveaway, simply submit your name and email address at RedShiftSports.com/DreamBike. And you can earn additional entries by watching a short video or sharing the link. Even if you don’t take home the grand prize, five additional winners will receive their choice of Shockstop Stem and Seatpost!

Don’t delay, as the deadline is just a few days away on Wednesday, June 5th! Enter here.
