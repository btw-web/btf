Introducing the Rockgeist Dr. Jones Handlebar Bag and System

Made specifically for the Jones Loop H-Bar or the Surly Moloko Bar, Dr. Jones is a modular system that includes a removable cargo net, wallet-lid, and the main bag. The cargo net was created to carry bulky items such as jackets, wet tents, tarps, or ground sheets. Or, you could fill it up with a few bottles, food, or whatever other random overflow items you happen to bring along.

The wallet-lid is a reversible top that can expand up and down, depending on the contents of the main bag. One side is a clear-view vinyl to allow you to see your phone (fits plus-sized smart phones) the other side is made of X-Pac with a zipper for internal access. To attach the wallet-lid to the bars, the system uses a simple girth-hitch with the included elastic cords. In addition, the main body has a daisy chain running around the entire circumference for easy attachment along your Jones Loop or Moloko Bar.

Materials and Specs

  • Net Tubular Climbing webbing for the cargo net
  • Bag X-Pac for the main bag and lid
  • Weight 282 grams (8.9 oz)
  • Capacity 8 liters max
  • Place of Manufacture Asheville, USA
  • Price $128.95
  • Manufacturer’s Details Rockgeist.com
