Weighing in at around 2.3 pounds, the Soul 1P is a double-walled, freestanding tent that’ll comfortably fit one person and their gear inside. It’s available in a bikepacking-specific model with short 12″ tent pole segments and is built using materials that strike an ideal balance between durability and weight. It even earned a place in our 2021 Bikepacking Gear of the Year awards. As Miles concluded in his review, the Soul is “well-designed, reasonably durable, and almost perfect for solo bikepacking trips.”
