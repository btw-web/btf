New PEdALED Kyoto Gravel Collection

Not everyone is comfortable riding around in skin-tight bib shorts and cycling jerseys, and PEdALED’s new Kyoto Gravel Collection, which consists of the Kyoto Gravel T-Shirt and Kyoto Gravel Shorts, is designed to address this. Both the shirt and shorts were designed with gravel and trail riding in mind, providing enough on-bike performance with a slightly more casual look that we’re liking the look of. Find details on both below.

PEdALED Kyoto Gravel T-Shirt

The Kyoto Gravel T-Shirt is made from merino wool, making it ideal for all-day adventures. Merino wool’s natural breathability, moisture wicking properties, and odour control are second to none, and a top choice among bikepackers. The Kyoto Gravel T-shirt has a slim, athletic fit, and a lengthened hem for better on-bike coverage. The chest pocket is made from 100% Japanese cotton, the same found in PEdALED’s cycling caps, and features a designated sunglasses sleeve for secure storage. It’s available in seven sizes, from XXS to XXL, and three colours; black, navy, and burgundy.

  • Material: 67% Merino Wool 22% Polyamide 11% Elastan
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $96 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: PEdALED.com

PEdALED Kyoto Gravel Shorts

Sharing similar qualities with the Gravel T-Shirt, the Kyoto Gravel Shorts are meant to be comfortable both on and off-bike. They’re roomy enough to layer over your favourite bib shorts, and provide a little more protection for when things get hectic. They also feature a slim cut, and the 100% polyester fabric is quick-drying, breathable, and stretchy. There are two zippered pockets and a drawcord closure. The Kyoto Gravel Shorts come in black only, in sizes XS to XXL.

  • Material: 100% Polyester
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: $156 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: PEdALED.com
