AGU Releases Venture Bikepacking Bag Collection

AGU has been in the cycling industry for over 50 years and was at the forefront of cycling apparel and accessories throughout the 1970s and 1980s. From designing the best cycling “rain suit” in 1976, to printing a full-color product catalogue just a couple years later, AGU has been a key player of European cycling. Keeping up with the trends, AGU just announced a full line of bikepacking bags, all made of recycled polyester and available in either khaki brown or black. Here’s a quick breakdown of the new AGU Venture bag collection.

AGU Venture Handlebar Pack

The Venture Handlebar Pack is a water-resistant, all-in-one handlebar pack with roll-top closures on both ends. Webbing straps and foam spacers secure the pack on your bar and head tube, and multiple daisy chain loops can be used to lash down small items. Extra visibility in low light conditions is provided by 3M reflective details on key areas of the bag.

  • Made of Recycled water-repellent Polyester
  • Standout Features Foam spacers and straps with quick release buckles
  • Capacity 17L
  • Price €59.99 ($67 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details Link

AGU Venture Frame Pack

The Venture Frame Pack is a partial frame bag that features several easily adjustable straps thanks to the exterior daisy chain. A water repellent YKK zipper keeps the contents dry and an integrated hydration port is ideal for those looking for the ability to carry a small water bladder on longer rides. The Frame Pack is available in three different sizes; small (3 liters), medium (4 liters), and large (5.5 liters).

  • Made of Recycled water-repellent Polyester
  • Standout Features YKK (water-repellent) zipper closure
  • Capacity 3L-5.5L
  • Price €34.99-54.99 ($39-$61 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details Link

AGU Venture Seat Pack

The Venture Seat Pack is the only bag in the collection that features a waterproof liner, ensuring its contents stay dry in all conditions. The seat pack uses a fairly standard design: two large velcro straps around the seat post, a roll-top closure, and compression straps on each side. A shock cord and daisy chain on the top of the seat pack provide some camp mug dangle opportunities.

  • Made of Waterproof through Dryliner Technology
  • Standout Features Roll-top opening and 3M reflective details
  • Capacity 20L
  • Price €69.99 ($78 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details Link

AGU Venture Snack Pack

The Venture Snack Pack is AGU’s take on the feed bag, providing a great place for snacks, water, or even a small camera. The Snack Pack features adjustable straps, a drainage hole, two exterior pockets, one-handed access, and reflective details.

  • Made of Recycled water-repellent polyester
  • Standout Features Mounts at either side of stem
  • Capacity 1L
  • Price €19.99 ($22 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details Link

AGU Venture Top Tube Bag

Like the Snack Pack, the Venture Top Tube Bag offers easily accessible storage. A water-repellent YKK zipper promises to keep most of the weather out, and the adjustable straps ensure no interference with other bags or cables. The interior mesh pocket and horizontal divider offer some organization for the 0.7L top tube bag.

  • Made of Recycled water-repellent polyester
  • Standout Features One handed zipper opening
  • Capacity 0.7L
  • Price €24.99
  • Manufacturer’s Details Link

For more information, or to purchase one of these bags, head over to AGU.com.
