Cairngorms Winter Bikepacking (Video)

Our friends Annie Le (@a_girl_outside) and Huw Oliver (@topofests) set out from their home for a snowy overnighter in Scotland’s beautiful Cairngorms National Park for their Good Night 2020 Campout. Along the way, Annie made this video to bring us along for the ride, complete with time-lapse footage and loads of beautiful winter scenery from the Cairngorms. Watch it below, alongside a gallery of photos from their outing.

To find more videos from Annie Le, check out her YouTube channel, Annie Le Outside. And for more snowy escapades from these two, be sure to check out their recent story, Slow and Precious from Greenland. Also, for more on Scotland, dig back into Mar Sin Leat from The Bikepacking Journal Issue 02, and find Huw’s Complete Guide to Bikepacking Scotland here.
