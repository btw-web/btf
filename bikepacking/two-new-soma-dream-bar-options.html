Two New Soma Dream Bar Options

Previously only offered in one size with a 50mm rise and 25° backsweep, the Soma Dream Bar was just updated with two options to help dial in your cockpit. It’s a roomy riser bar with a healthy amount of sweep for a natural wrist position, and Soma recommends it for bikepacking and touring.

Soma just introduced the new Dream Lowdown Riser with a 25mm rise and 20° backsweep, which is available in both black and silver. There’s also a new Hi-Rise Riser with big 70mm rise and 30° backsweep, which is also available in black and silver. The Dream Bar is made from 6061 T6 Aluminum and all are 780mm wide and use a standard 31.8mm stem clamp size.

  • Material: 6061 T6 Aluminum
  • Weight: 425g (Lowdown) / 470g (Hi-Rise)
  • Place of Manufacture: Taiwan
  • Price: $99.99 USD

You can find more details at SomaFabShop.com.
