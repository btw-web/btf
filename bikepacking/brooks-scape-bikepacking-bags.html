Brooks England Releases Scape Line of Bike Touring and Bikepacking Bags

Known for their touring-friendly saddles and traditional adventure cycling aesthetic, it was only a matter of time before Brooks developed its own line of bikepacking and touring bags. While we’ve had this forthcoming lineup on our radar for a few months, Brooks accelerated the launch ahead of schedule. With that, here’s a quick rundown of the new Scape range and some of the product highlights. Stay tuned for more details down the road.

The all-new Brooks Scape Travel Bag range offers a fairly unique lineup of 10 bags made for all disciplines of adventure cycling, from touring to bikepacking, and from day rides to gravel racing. Innovative features in the lineup include a fabric that blends a classic look with modern performance, including a 100% waterproof construction. Bags in the Scape series are made from a combination of waterproof 600D polyester and PVC-free nylon to resist rips, tears, abrasions, and UV damage. To keep them waterproof, Brooks employed a high-frequency welding technique for their construction. Other details include removable dry bags, roll-top closures, sealed zips, and flaps above zippers to ensure a high standard of waterproofing in heavy weather conditions.

Here are some details on a few of the bags that caught our eye in the Scape range:

Brooks Scape Frame Bag

The Brooks Scape Frame Bag is designed to fit most standard-sized frames. Like other bags in the lineup, it’s made with welded PU-coated nylon and is 100% waterproof, according to Brooks. The bag features two full-length zips and is made for bikepacking, gravel, and day rides.

  • Capacity 3L
  • Dimensions W 460 x H 120 x D 60mm
  • Weight 200g
  • Max Load 3kg
  • Price $110.00
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com

Brooks Scape Seatpost Bag

Similar to bags like the Revelate Terapin and Porcelain Rocket’s Mr. Fusion, the Scape Seatpost Bag is a saddle pack designed around a holster attachment and its own waterproof dry sack.

  • Capacity 8-10L
  • Dimensions W 400-600 x H 180 x D 140mm
  • Weight 500g
  • Max Load 3kg
  • Price $150
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com

Brooks Scape Handlebar Bag

Built specifically for off-road bikepacking, the Scape Handlebar Roll is a two-piece harness system made to securely hold its own adjustable, waterproof dry bag. The bag has two internal compartments.

  • Capacity 10-12 L
  • Dimensions W 160 x H 160 x D 350-400mm
  • Weight 670g
  • Max Load 5kg
  • Price $140
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com

Brooks Scape Small Pannier

The Brooks Scape Small Pannier is a waterproof pannier made with a roll-top closure and welded, weatherproof details. The Small Pannier is designed to be used on a front or rear rack.

  • Capacity 10-13L
  • Dimensions W 260 x H 300-470 x D 150mm
  • Weight 630g
  • Max Load Not Specified
  • Price $135
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com

Brooks Scape Top Tube Bag

The Brooks Scape Top Tube Bag is welded from the same waterproof material as the other bags in the Scape line, making it ideal for electronics and other personal items you want readily available. According to Brooks, it’s equipped with a 100% waterproof KCC zipper to keep contents dry.

  • Capacity 0.9L
  • Dimensions W 210 x H 110 x D 40mm
  • Weight 100g
  • Max Load 1kg
  • Price $60
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com

Brooks Scape Saddle Pocket Bag

The Scape Saddle Pocket Bag is a small waterproof accessory bag the can be used as a classic saddle bag or as a pocket bag to attach to the other Scape bags to provide additional storage.

  • Capacity 0.7L
  • Dimensions W 150 x H 55 x D 80mm
  • Weight 70g
  • Max Load Not Specified
  • Price $50
  • Manufacturer’s Details BrooksEngland.com
