Surly Bridge Club Now in Loo Azul Blue

The Surly Bridge Club lineup has been expanded to include another 700c build, complete with a fresh new Loo Azul paint job and a wide range 11-speed drivetrain. While not much else has changed, and the Bridge Club remains an incredibly versatile and affordable option for those on the hunt for a multi-purpose dirt road touring or commuting machine. Cass reviewed the Bridge Club last year and it’s been a favourite of his ever since. Find photos and the complete build kit below.

Build Kit

  • Frame Surly Bridge Club, Chromoly Steel
  • Fork Surly Bridge Club, Chromoly Steel
  • Headset Cane Creek 40
  • Crankset Shimano Deore M5100 32t
  • Bottom Bracket Shimano BSA
  • Rear Derailleur Shimano Deore M5100 Shadow Plus, 11-speed
  • Cassette Shimano Deore CSM5100 11-51t
  • Shifter Shimano Deore M5100 Rapid Fire
  • Chain KMC X11 EPT
  • Brakes Tektro HD-M275
  • Rotors Tektro 160/160mm
  • Front Hub Novatec 9×100 QR
  • Rear Hub Novatec 9×141 QR 11-spd
  • Rims WTB ST i21 TCS
  • Tires Surly Extraterrestrial 700×41, 60tpi Tubeless Ready
  • Stem Promax, 31.8mm
  • Handlebar Surly Terminal Bar
  • Grips Velo Black
  • Saddle WTB Volt 142
  • Seatpost Promax, 27.2mm

The new Loo Azul Bridge Club is available as a complete build for $1,099 or as a frameset for $525 USD. The same build outlined above also comes in black if blue isn’t doing it for you.
