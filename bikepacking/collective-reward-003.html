Packable Expedition Jacket

The PJ-1 is Search and State’s Hooded Packable Expedition Jacket, conceived after weeks of riding across the US on the Search Brigade. It’s packable, completely waterproof, windproof, and breathable. As a medium-weight jacket, it was designed to be comfortable for long rides in a variety of conditions, as well as off the bike in casual conditions. It features a drawstring hood that stows into the collar when not in use, and the whole jacket packs into its own pocket, down to about the size of a softball. One winner will receive a black PJ-1 in the size of their choice.

Field Shorts

The Search and State Field Shorts have been tested on the bike all over the world, from Cuba, to Tasmania, and all the way across the US. They’re made from durable waxed cotton and treated with machine-washable wax, making them resistant to water and stains, while also remaining plenty breathable. One winner will also receive a pair of olive Field Shorts in the size of their choice.
