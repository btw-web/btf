Bikepacking The Mid South

In the final days of 2020, The Mid South’s Josh McCullock (@midsouthgravel) went out on a short, solo bicycle campout under the Cold Moon to clear the mind and move the body. This video is set in Logan County, Oklahoma, on lands of the Wichita, Kiowa, Osage, and Kickapoo Nations.

“With this, we bid a peaceful goodnight to 2020.”
