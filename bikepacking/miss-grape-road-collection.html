Miss Grape’s New Road Collection

Although we’re big fans of sticking to trails and gravel roads, the fact is lots of cyclists prefer paved roads and can also benefit from lighter bags for carrying gear on long rides and multi-day road tours. Italy-based Miss Grape’s new Road Collection caters to those folks and includes a seat bag, burrito-style handlebar bag, handlebar roll, two partial frame bags, and a top tube bag. Check them all out below.

Miss Grape Cluster 7

The Cluster 7 is a lightweight seat bag that’s optimized for gravel and road bikes with a narrow Q-factor. Miss Grape claims that it’s 100% waterproof. It attaches via a large velcro strap around the seatpost and two buckles under the saddle rails, and has a max capacity of 7L.

  • Capacity: 7L
  • Material: 420D Nylon / 300D Polyester
  • Weight: 249 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €145 (~$172 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: MissGrape.net

Miss Grape Internode 2 and 3

The Internode is a partial frame bag with a slim, lightweight design. It is offered in two sizes (2 and 3) that provide up to 2L or 3L of storage, respectively. It is also touted as 100% waterproof, made in Italy, and constructed from a blend of Nylon and Polyester.

  • Capacity: 2L / 3L
  • Material: 420D Nylon / 300D Polyester
  • Weight: 139 / 179 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €99 / €105 (~$118 / $125 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: MissGrape.net

Miss Grape Node Road and Node 2H

The Node Road is Miss Grape’s narrowest top tube bag yet. At 4.8cm wide, it’s the narrowest in its category, and makes for a rub-free design that’s perfect for quick-access items and snacks on the go. It is offered in a standard version and bolt-on version for bikes with top tube mounts.

  • Material: 420D Nylon / 300D Polyester
  • Weight: 98 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: €65 (~$77 USD)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: MissGrape.net

Miss Grape Tendril 6

The Tendril 6 uses the same design as their Tendril 4.1 handlebar roll, but increases it’s capacity to 6L. It uses a double-ended roll top design, waterproof construction, and attaches to the bars via two adjustable webbing straps and foam spacers. Keep an eye on the Miss Grape website for more details, as it is not yet available.

  • Capacity: 6L
  • Material: 420D Nylon / 300D Polyester
  • Weight: 240 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Italy
  • Price: TBD
