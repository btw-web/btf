700c WTB ByWay Now Available!

We never would have guessed we’d be excited about a road-ish tire. However, the WTB ByWay is something special; we gave it a Gear of The Year award last year based on use during the Irreverent Road Ride in Vermont, D2R2, on tour in Japan, and on the Appalachian Gravel Growler. Itâ€™s fantastic on gravel, both in grip and durability. So, we’re happy to see that WTB just made it available in 700c x 44mm (as well as 34 and 40mm) with either black or tan sidewalls.

The new 700c WTB ByWays fall in with a new pricing structure where all tubeless drop bar tires are $59.95, regardless of diameter or width. This change results in a price decrease of $24 for all WTB adventure-road tires as well as a $11 decrease for all 650b Road Plus tires. All these tires will be available from WTB’s California warehouse by the middle to end of September.
