For Sale: 100 Copies of The Bikepacking Journal Issue 03

If you haven’t yet become a supporter of the site by joining our Bikepacking Collective, you’ve likely never had the chance to hold a copy our biannually printed, members-only publication, The Bikepacking Journal. We print one copy for each of our members, and not many beyond that. Our third issue of The Bikepacking Journal spans 160 color pages and features pedal-powered essays and stories from Bosnia, Spain, Oman, Minnesota, Patagonia, the Great Divide, Romania, the Andes, Timor-Leste, the Southwestern U.S., and Peru. In it, we wanted to continue pushing beyond simply sharing inspiring stories and photography. As much as we love reading about life-altering trips to dream destinations, our goal was to broaden the scope of themes and diversity of voices we published in print. And we hoped to engage our audience in a way that provided opportunities for learning and reflecting.

If you visit the site regularly and enjoy the content, we hope you’ll support our work by joining the Bikepacking Collective, through which you’ll receive two copies of The Bikepacking Journal every year, in addition to lots of other great benefits. If you’re not quite ready to back us, though, picking up one of these single issues is a great way to see the kinds of storytelling and photography you’ve been missing out on. We have just 100 copies for sale at a cost of $20 + $5 shipping anywhere in the world. We anticipate these selling out quickly, so pick up a copy here if we’ve captured your interest. When they’re gone, they’re gone!
