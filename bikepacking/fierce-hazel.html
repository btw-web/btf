Fierce Hazel Pouches are Made from Leftover Material

Founded by Frankie Holt in Los Angeles, California, Fierce Hazel is a female-owned, sustainable cycling accessory company. The brand name comes from Frankie’s alter ego, which was born when she found a pack of clothing tags that read Specially Fashioned by Hazel. The company’s flagship products are two ultra-lightweight bags made entirely from unused material left over at the factory by bigger brands that overestimated the amount required for production. Hazel states that each of their bags are ethically made by hand at a factory in Vietnam that pays a living wage and has reasonable working hours and a good work environment. Here are the details…

Echelon All-Conditions Ride Pouch

The Echelon All-Conditions Ride Pouch is designed to be a simple, weatherproof pocket organizer for cards, a phone, and other small personal items. The Echelon comes in two sizes, with the larger one measuring 4.15″ W x 7″ H (10.5 x 17.8cm). The pouch is constructed from weatherproof 30D PU and silicone-coated nylon with a YKK waterproof zipper and retails for $27. You can also get it in Ballistics Black for $29. Find a feature list below, or more at FierceHazel.com.

  • Sleek one-compartment design fits just the essentials
  • Front slip pocket for fast access to a handy wipe or drink tablet
  • Back flat pocket for a phone or notebook
  • Hidden pocket fits tampon, pen or lip balm
  • Easy-open YKK® nylon zipper
  • Mini-tabs facilitate quick opening
  • Designed to make your ride prep quicker and easier
  • 3 multifunction pockets
  • Offers quick access to ride basics
  • Laser-cut fabric is seam sealed
  • Phones up to 3.25″ x 5.75″ fit inside without an added protective case

Tour de Fierce Ultralight Cycling Wallet

The Tour de Fierce Ultralight Cycling Wallet measures 4″ H x 6.75″ W x .75″ D (10.2 x 17.1 x 1.9cm) and is designed to carry a phone, passport, credit cards, and other personal items in a very lightweight package (only 40 grams). It could also fit a multitool, tire levers, patch kit, and other such tools. The wallet is constructed from weatherproof 30D PU and silicone-coated nylon with a YKK waterproof zipper and retails for $42. Find a feature list below, or more at FierceHazel.com.

  • Divided main compartment keeps everything organized
  • Front slender pocket for fast access to a tampon, tire lever, lip balm or whatever
  • Back flat pocket for a phone or notebook
  • Waterproof YKK® zipper keeps your valuables dry
  • Additional zip compartment keeps your cash, credit card, ID safe
  • Internal key clip so you never fear losing your keys
  • Oversize zipper pull for easy access even when you’re wearing full-fingered gloves
  • Sized to be used as a wallet or as a tool sack
  • No questions asked, 90-day return policy
