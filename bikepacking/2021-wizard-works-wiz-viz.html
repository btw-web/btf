Wizard Works Rolls Out the New Wiz Viz Collection

While last year’s release coincided with daylight saving time, this year, London-based Wizard Works decided to celebrate the launch of their latest Wiz Viz bags on Halloween. As Veronica Lowe, co-founder of Wizard Works put it, “We love the idea that our seasonal collection can be winter focused, why should summer get all the fun?!” Similar to last year’s collection, the latest Wiz Viz lineup is designed to reflect light at night and stay visible when its miserable—we need do all we can to keep the winter party rolling, after all.

Made with a combination of ultra-visible orange X-Pac and black Cordura with a punctual reflective pattern, this is the second iteration of the Wiz Viz collection, continuing the tradition of celebrating safety and winter riding. Available as a Lil Presto barrel bag, Go-Go top tube bag, and Teeny Houdini Saddle bag, Wiz Viz is designed to provide 360° of visibility and help carry those extra snacks folks need to keep morale high on a cold day’s ride.

Wiz Viz is available until daylight savings returns in March 2022, at which point it will disappear for the summer months, returning the following autumn with a brand-new style. Find details over at WizardWorks.com.
