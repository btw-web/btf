New Brooks Cambium Colors for C17 and Bar Tape

For those unfamiliar, Cambium is Brooks’ innovative saddle and bar tape lineup. The All Weather C17 saddle features a vulcanised natural rubber and organic cotton top designed for long-ride comfort and durability. Cass has put it to long term use, and it’s become Virginia’s favorite saddle. Brooks just announced two new colors to the collection, Octave and Bronze. These two colors are available in the C17 and matching rubber bar tape.

The Brooks C17 is available for $120 in both colors and the bar tape sells for $40.
