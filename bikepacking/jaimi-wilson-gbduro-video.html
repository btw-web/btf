Introducing GBDURO rider and Lyon Cycle Athlete Jaimi Wilson (Video)

Lyon Cycle supported athlete Jaimi Wilson is currently riding the GBduro on a Salsa Warbird GRX 810 with Ortlieb bikepacking bags. As you may have seen in our Stage 2 recap earlier, Jaimi was the first woman to roll into checkpoint two, and is currently readying herself for Stage 3 as she sits in 3rd place overall (92h:05m) in the 4-stage bikepacking race behind Angus Young and Mark Beaumont. Watch the video below and follow her on the Tracker. Go Jaimi!

“If Lachlan Morton is going to cry over Great Dunn Fell, like, I can do that. I’ve been over Great Dunn Fell before…”
