Kitsbow Icon Now in Caprock Red

While the Kitsbow Icon shirt is a bit of an investment, it’s a pretty good one in our opinion. It’s a tough shirt that’s built to last, and it’s handmade to order in North Carolina, fits like a glove, and constructed using excellent materials, including US-sourced Pendleton wool. It’s pretty much the only shirt I wear when it’s cold outside. Kitsbow just released a new pattern called Caprock Red, perhaps named after the dramatic caprock waterfalls that are in the area, characterized by a top layer of rock—often granite—that’s harder than the underlying stone. With the addition of the new Caprock pattern, the Icon is now available in seven colors…

The Caprock Red Icon is available in both women’s and men’s versions, with the men’s cut in either relaxed or Signature fit (my preference). Find it over at Kitsbow.com: Men’s / Women’s. Also, be sure to learn more about Kitsbow in our site visit post below.
