The Brooks Scape Collection is now Available in Black

Following last week’s introduction of three new bags into their Scape range, Brooks England just announced that the collection has been expanded to include a number of all-black bags alongside the original mud green colorway. The Scape bags that are now available in black include the top tube bag, frame bag, large and small panniers, saddlebag, and handlebar pouch.

Only the colors have been changed in this welcome update, and all six bags retain the focus on waterproof materials, modular functionality, and hardwearing fabrics found across the entire line. For complete details on the Brooks Scape range, which now includes 13 bags, head over to BrooksEngland.com.
