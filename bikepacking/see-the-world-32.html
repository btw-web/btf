See The World, Episode 32

Iohan Gueorguiev just released the 32nd episode of his self-filmed See The World series documenting his bikepacking trip from Alaska to Argentina. This one picks up within the lunar landscape shown at the end of episode 31 and explores what caused this entire valley to be covered in rocks, sand, and ash…
