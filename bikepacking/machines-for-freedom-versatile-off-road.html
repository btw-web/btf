Machines for Freedom Versatile Off Road Collection

Machines for Freedom is a women-founded and led cycling apparel brand on a mission to further representation and body positivity through thoughtful product design and storytelling. Their new Versatile Off Road Collection features several technical garments that are designed to be as comfortable for urban commuting as they are for touring and mountain biking. As always, the line is available in sizes XS – XXXL, and in a variety of different colours and fits. Check it out below.

The Key Short

They describe The Key Short as “the perfect cycling overshort that combines off-the-bike styling with superior performance on the bike.” They can be paired with a liner short, or not, are made from a super-stretchy polyester, and are moisture-wicking and quick drying. The Key Short is offered in two inseam lengths, 5.5″ (regular) and 11″ (long), and in sizes 24 to 38. There are five different pockets and several different colours to choose from.

  • Material: 100% Polyester
  • Size Range: 24-38
  • Inseam: 5.5″ / 11″
  • Price: $108 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: MachinesforFreedom.com

Technical Crew

The Technical Crew is like your favourite cotton sweatshirt, but with the moisture-resistant and quick-drying properties you want from a technical shirt. It features contoured sleeves, a secure rear zippered pocket, and is made from a blend of polyester, spandex, and rayon.

  • Material: 72% Polyester / 23% Rayon / 5% Spandex
  • Size Range: XXS-XXXL
  • Colours: MFF Black, Citronelle
  • Price: $98 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: MachinesforFreedom.com

Technical Tee

Available in both a short-sleeve and long-sleeve version, the Technical Tee lineup is soft, sustainable, and designed to go anywhere. They are made from an eco-friendly MicroModal fabric, which is made from beech trees and is quick-drying and silky soft. They have a boxy, relaxed fit that drapes over any body type and is resistant to wrinkles.

  • Material: 90% Micro Model / 10% Elastane
  • Size Range: XXS-XXXL
  • Colours: Himalayan Salt, Cobalt, MFF Black
  • Price: $78 USD (short sleeve) / 88$ USD (long sleeve)
  • Manufacturer’s Details: MachinesforFreedom.com
