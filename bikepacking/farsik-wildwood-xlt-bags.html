Farsik x Wildwood XLT Bags

Handmade by Farsik Supply on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, the new XLT Bags are designed specifically for Wildwood Cycles’ (also based on Vancouver Island) XLT Basket cargo cage. Although there is a chance the bags could work with other cages, the XLT Basket has a large platform that perfectly matches up with the bags. They’re made from VX21 X-Pac with a bright interior liner, use a roll-top closure with cam buckles to secure the load, and have a velcro attachment for attaching to the cage.

Both the bags and cages are handmade in Canada, and having met both Jesse of Wildwood and Mike of Farsik, I’m excited to see these two continue to work together on projects like this. The bags are sold as a pair, don’t include the XLT cages ($64.99 each), and should be shipping out by February 20th.

  • Capacity: 5L/each
  • Material: X-Pac
  • Weight: 200 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: British Columbia, Canada
  • Price: $214.99 CAD/pair

You can learn more or order a set over at WildwoodCycles.ca.
