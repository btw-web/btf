2021 Devinci Hatchet Lineup Released

The Hatchet Open Road G-Spec series includes Devinci’s most capable gravel bikes. They describe it as a “fun-igniting platform that merges off-road DNA with a moonshot mindset that shreds Strava gravel, brings the party to Grinduro and even plays nice on pure pavement.” The Hatchet has clearance for 700c x 45mm / 650b x 2.1″ tires, a MTB-inspired long top tube and short stem, and is offered in both aluminum and carbon builds. The frame has four bottle mounts in total, as well as internal cable routing, hidden fender and rack mounts, and a press-fit bottom bracket.

The 2021 Devinci Hatchet G-Spec is offered in four different builds: two carbon and two aluminum. Prices range from $1,599 to $4,199 CAD (~$1,213 to $3,186 USD), starting with the aluminum Sora 2×9 build all the way to the top-end carbon GRX 1×11 option. Find a breakdown of the different builds below.
