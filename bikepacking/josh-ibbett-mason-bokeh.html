Josh Ibbett Runs Through His Mason Bokeh Gravel Bikepacking Rig

In his latest YouTube video, our friend Josh Ibbett talks through his Mason Cycles Bokeh set up for racing and multi-surface bikepacking. The bike features a GRX drivetrain and a unique custom painted Lauf suspension fork. Watch the video and scroll down for a full bike build kit.

  • Frame Mason Bokeh Aluminum frame
  • Fork Lauf Grit SL
  • Groupset Shimano GRX
  • Wheels Hunt Carbon Dynamo Disc
  • Tyres WTB Riddler 45mm
  • Bars Ritchey Venture Max Carbon
  • Stem Ritchey C220
  • GPS Wahoo Element
  • Saddle WTB High Tail
  • Frame Bag MissGrape
  • Bottle cages Wolf Tooth Morse Cage
  • Bottles RawVelo
  • Light Exposure Revo
