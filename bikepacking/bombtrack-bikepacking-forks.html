Bombtrack Releases Three Bikepacking-Specific Aftermarket Forks

Prior to today’s news, Bombtrack had just one aftermarket fork option, the EXT V1. Now, the German bike manufacturer has three aftermarket fork options available. Here are photos and details about each…

Bombtrack BPC fork

The Bombtrack BPC (stands for Bike Packing Carbon) features a 500mm axle-to-crown for mountain bikes and clearance for 27.5 x 3.0″ tires or full 29 x 3.0. The BPC will sell for $380USD/360GBP/350€. Here are the specs:

  • FULL CARBON FIBER
  • Steerer: Tapered, 1-1/8″-1-1/2″, 300mm
  • Offset: 51mm
  • AC Height: 500mm
  • Hub spacing: 15 x 110mm
  • Brakes: Postmount, Max 203mm Rotor
  • Internal Routing: Brake Hose, Dynamo Cable
  • Weight: 650g (Including mounting bolts, uncut steerer)
  • Tire clearance: 27.5″ x 3.0″ / 29″ x 3.0″
  • Max load per triple cage mount: 4.5kg
  • Details at Bombtrack.com

Bombtrack BPS Fork

The Bombtrack BPS (stands for Bike Packing Steel) features a 500mm axle-to-crown for mountain bikes and clearance for 27.5 x 3.0″ tires or full 29 x 3.0. The BPS will sell for $260USD/250GBP/240€. Here are the specs:

  • CRMO STEEL
  • Steerer: Tapered, 1-1/8″-1-1/2″, 300mm
  • Offset: 51mm
  • AC Height: 500mm
  • Hub spacing: 15 x 110mm
  • Brakes: Postmount, Max 203mm Rotor
  • Internal Routing: Brake Hose, Dynamo Cable
  • Weight: 1590 (Including mounting bolts, uncut steerer)
  • Tire clearance: 27.5″ x 3.0″ / 29″ x 3.0″
  • Triple Cage Mounts, Through-Hole Lowrider Mounts, Drilled Crown, Max load per triple cage mount: 4.5kg
  • Details at Bombtrack.com

Bombtrack EXT (v2) Fork

The Bombtrack EXT features a 398mm axle-to-crown (for gravel bikes) and clearance for 650B x 52mm tires or 700C x 45mm. The EXT will sell for $360USD/340GBP/330€. Here are the specs:

  • FULL CARBON FIBER
  • Steerer: Tapered, 1-1/8″-1-1/2″, 300mm
  • Integrated Race, 40mm, 36°
  • Offset: 48mm
  • AC Height: 398mm
  • Hub spacing: 12 x 100mm
  • Brakes: Flatmount, Max 160mm Rotor
  • Internal Routing: Brake Hose, Dynamo Cable
  • Weight: 544g (Including mounting bolts, uncut steerer)
  • Tire clearance: 650B x 52mm / 700C x 45mm
  • Triple Cage Mounts, Fender Mounts, Max load per cage mount: 4.5kg
  • Details at Bombtrack.com

Contact your Bombtrack Bicycles dealer for more information or to order one of your own…
