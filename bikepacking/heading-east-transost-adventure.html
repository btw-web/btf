Heading East (Transost Adventure) Movie Released for Streaming

Filmed in 2019, Heading East follows Jochem, Carolin, and Dennis along the Transost Challenge, a self-supported bikepacking race from Bayreuth in Germany to the Black Sea in Romania. This was the first edition of this event and led the riders along a 2,900km route with 56,000m of climbing over more than 27 mountain ranges in Eastern Europe, including the Ore Mountains, Giant Mountains, the High Tatras, the Beskid, and the Carpathians.
