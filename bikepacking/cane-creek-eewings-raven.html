Cane Creek eeWings Raven

If you haven’t lusted after Cane Creek’s titanium eeWings cranks enough, now there’s another version to want. The new eeWings Raven are constructed of the same titanium as normal ti eeWings, but painted deep black with a premium automotive-style paint, affixed with a water-transfer decal “W” logo, and clear coated with a high-gloss protective finish. 

Cane Creek claims the new finish on the eeWings Raven is very durable, but the paint can be expected to chip, scratch and wear over time with use. The eeWings Raven cranks also include a protective film that you can apply to ensure that the paint lasts as long as possible. eeWings Raven are available for mountain at 170mm and 175mm arm lengths and for all-road at 170mm arm length. Both road and mountain cranks retail for $1049.

In addition to the eeWings Raven, Cane Creek also announced the eeWings Mountain cranks are now available in a 165mm arm length and that the Cane Creek preloader rings are now available in a variety of anodized color options. 165mm eeWings Mountain cranks retail for $999 and are only available in the original brushed-titanium finish. The Cane Creek Preloader is currently available in orange, red, green, blue and the original black and retails for $29.

All three products are available now through Cane Creek distributors and retailers or rider-direct in North America through canecreek.com. Find more info on the new Cane Creek sewings Raven here.
