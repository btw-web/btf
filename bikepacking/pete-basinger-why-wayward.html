Pete Basinger goes places on his Wayward (lots of places!)

You might recognize Pete’s name from his Baja Divide record last year; he completed the 1,547 mile (2490km) ride from Tecate, Mexico to San Jose del Cabo, Mexico in 11 days, 11 hours, and 20 minutes (11:11:20), setting the Fastest Known Time (FKT) for the Baja Divide bikepacking route. Pete just put together this video showcasing a lot of riding on that same bike, the award winning Why Cycles Wayward.
