Trade in Your Old Cycling Computer for a Hammerhead Karoo 2 Discount

We’ve been testing the new Hammerhead Karoo 2 and are pretty impressed so far (stay tuned for that review). We just heard that Hammerhead is running a limited-time offer where you can trade in your old cycling computer and receive a significant discount toward a new Karoo 2. Trade-in values range from $60 to $170 depending on your device, and there are dozens of devices that qualify, including computers from Garmin, Lezyne, Wahoo, and more.

Photoset above by Joe Cruz

This seems like a pretty good deal if you’re already thinking about the Karoo 2. But, it won’t last long. The two-week program kicked off yesterday, November 17th, and runs through the 30th. Learn more over at Hammerhead.io.
