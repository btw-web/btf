The Dynaplug / Wheelie Wrench Pro from Fix Manufacturing

Fix Manufacturing specializes in small multi-tools for surfers, skateboarders, snowboarders, and cyclists. With Hans Rey’s design insight and a tubeless tire plugger from Dynaplug, the latest Wheelie Wrench Pro looks like it could be pretty useful for bikepacking. The Dynaplug / Wheelie Wrench Pro is made from aluminum and steel-alloy and includes seven different hex keys, a T25, 8mm and 10mm open ended wrenches, a chain breaker, and more. Find photos, pricing info, and the complete function list below.

  • 2, 2.5, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8mm hex key
  • T25
  • Tire lever
  • Phillips screwdriver doubles as slotted screwdriver
  • 8mm and 10mm open ended wrench
  • Rotor straightener for minor bends
  • Includes 11-speed quick links (SRAM or Shimano compatible)
  • Accepts other quick links 10-speed or greater
  • Presta valve core remover
  • Stuck presta valve stem opener
  • Chain breaker for 10-speed and greater
  • #0 and #2 spoke wrenches
  • Tool splits in two and transforms into a long wrench handle
  • Bottle opener

The Dynaplug / Wheelie Wrench Pro has a claimed weight of 5 oz (141g) and is available now directly from FixMfg.com, but as we learned from the first version, it’ll likely sell out quickly!
