2020 Salsa Warroad Lineup: New Specs and Fresh Paint

Originally released last year, the Salsa Warroad is the newest bike in their all-road lineup, designed as a road bike that can comfortably veer off pavement. For 2020, Salsa has added several new build kits to the mix, including everything from Force AXS to a more affordable Carbon Tiagra build. With prices ranging from $5,899 to $1,999, there’s a Warroad for nearly everyone out there. For more information on the Warroad and how it differs from the Warbird, check out our original article.

The 2020 Salsa Warroad comes in eight complete builds, as well as a frameset only option. All builds are still designed around a carbon fiber frame and fork, offering multiple bottle mounts, fender mounts, and clearance for up to 700c x 35mm or 650b x 47mm tires. See a detailed breakdown of the highest end Force AXS 700, our personal favourite (and slightly beefier) Apex 1 650 build, and images of the other builds down below.
