Svein Tuft: Reconnecting with the Cascades (Video)

Aside from being a stage winner at the Tour de France and Giro d’Italia, as well as a 13-time Canadian national champion, Svein Tuft (@svein.tuft) has tackled some seriously epic backcountry adventures by bike. After retiring from pro road riding, making the move from Europe back to British Columbia, and becoming a 7mesh ambassador, he set out on a series of local adventures in an effort to reconnect with the wilderness of the West Coast and the areas he first discovered as a young man. Watch the first episode, Reconnecting With The Cascades, below.

“It’s the mountains, it’s the open wildness. It’s the fact that behind that next valley there’s nothing there.”—Svein Tuft
