2020 Kona Sutra LTD

The 2020 Kona Sutra LTD offers a few improvements over last year’s model, and 2018 LTD, which we already thought was near perfect (read our review). The most visible update is a new paint scheme. It features a “Gloss Earth Grey” color with grey and “Mustard” decals. Kona also made a couple smart changes to the frame and the complete build kit…

The most significant update to the Sutra LTD (or so it appears) can be found on the fork. It looks like Kona corrected the one major flaw we found in past models—they added a third mount to each blade making each set a complete three-pack suited for Anything style cargo cages. Perfection! We’ve reached out to Kona for verification, so hopefully the photographic evidence doesn’t lie! Verified!

Another excellent upgrade can be found in the wheels specced on the complete. Kona increased the internal rim width from 23mm to 25mm on a WTB KOM Light rim. We’re big fans of wider rims, so this is a welcome addition as well. This slightly wider rim is better dialed for gravel tires ranging from 42-50mm and 29er MTB rubber in widths from 2.0-2.2″. Kona also switched to tanwall WTB Venture 50mm tires on the complete, up from 45mm on past LTDs.

Like past models, the 2020 Kona Sutra LTD offers plenty of room for bigger tires, too. Folks have reported squeezing in 29 × 2.35″ tires as well as 27.5 x 2.8″ rubber, although both of those sizes might be pretty tight. 29 x 2.25″ and 27.5 x 2.6″ are more realistic max clearances.

Build Kit

Kona specced the 2020 Sutra LTD with a build kit that’s fairly consistent with past models. A SRAM Rival 1 11-speed shifter and derailleur, 10-42t cassette, and direct mount race Face crankset make up a versatile drivetrain. Otherwise, there’s not much to complain about; Kona did a pretty good job, in my opinion, properly appointing this bike for both ‘gravel plus’ riding and bikepacking.
