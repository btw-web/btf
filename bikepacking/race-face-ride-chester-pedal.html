Race Face Announces new Ride Pedal and Updated Chester

It’s no secret that Race Face makes some impressive bike components. And their growing line of flat pedals is no different, offering great performance at competitive prices. Logan put his set of Atlas pedals through the wringer during his 1,500-mile Trans-Uganda trip, and had many positive things to say about them. Read more about those and other flat pedals for bikepacking in our guide here.

Race Face just announced a new pedal to their lineup, the Ride pedal, which looks to be a cost-effective option to those riding flats who aren’t in need of metal traction pins. The Chester pedal, which shares similar characteristics to the Ride but offers replaceable pins, has been updated as well. Check out some of the specs below, including the rainbow of colours that might just be the finishing touch to your latest build…

Race Face Ride Pedal

The Ride pedal features a slim nylon composite platform, available in a set of nine vibrant colour options and offers a lightweight concave design for serious performance. Molded pins give you all the grip you need without the risk of damaging your shoes or shins. Its durable Cro-Mo steel axle features a standard 9/16‚Äù pedal thread and serviceable internal parts. Race Face also offers a two-year warranty on the Ride pedal, and from our experience, will go above and beyond to keep their customers happy.

Specs

  • Colours: Black, Blue, Red, Green, Orange, Purple, Yellow, Turquoise, Pink
  • Bearings: Cartridge bearings and DU bushings
  • Platform Size: 110mm x 101mm
  • Axle Material: Chromoly Steel
  • Body Material: Nylon Composite
  • Weight: 330g/pair
  • Number of Pins: 18 Molded Pins
  • Price: $34.99 USD

Race Face Chester Pedal

Not wanting to be left out of the party, Chester got dressed up with some new tread traction on its slim concave platform to give additional grip to the eight replaceable hex traction pins per side. The nylon composite body turns on a fully sealed Cro-Mo axle with a 100% serviceable bearing and bushing system. And its available in nine colours to match your bike, including hot pink! The same two-year warranty applies to the Chester.

Specs

  • Colours: Black, Blue, Red, Green, Orange, Purple, Yellow, Turquoise, Pink
  • Bearings: Cartridge bearings and DU bushings
  • Platform Size: 110mm x 101mm
  • Axle Material: Chromoly Steel
  • Body Material: Nylon Composite
  • Weight: 360g/pair
  • Number of Pins: 16 SHCS hex traction pins per pedal
  • Price: $49.99 USD

Both the new Ride and updated Chester pedals are available from Race Face now, so head over to RaceFace.com or ask your local bike shop.
