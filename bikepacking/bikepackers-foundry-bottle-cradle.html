Bikepackers Foundry Bottle Cradle: Made in Alberta

Owned and operated by Sara Stuart in Canmore, Alberta, Bikepackers Foundry is a relatively new bag maker that focuses on unique storage solutions for bikepacking. Their Bottle Cradle caught our eye as a simple solution for mounting an extra bottle on your top tube, requiring no tools, dedicated mounting points, or hardware.

The Bottle Cradle is constructed from VX-21 X-pac, polyester webbing, and plastic stiffeners. A replaceable 2.5mm elastic cord holds its contents in place, while also making any bottle easily accessible while on the go. There are webbing attachment points on both ends of the bag, allowing the Cradle to be mounted by your stem or against your seatpost.

  • Material: X-Pac
  • Weight: 80 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Canmore, Alberta
  • Price: $70 CAD

Check out the Bottle Cradle and their other bag offerings over at BikepackersFoundry.com.
