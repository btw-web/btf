Collective Reward #041: PackNW Ridgeline Roll-Top Hip Packs

You may have noticed Pack Northwest’s stylish Ridgeline in our complete index of handmade hip packs for bikepacking earlier this summer, and now we’ve teamed up with the crew at PackNW to give away a custom Ridgeline to two members of our Bikepacking Collective. Winners can customize the colors on their pack to make it one of a kind.

PackNW Ridgeline Features

Despite being super simple, the Ridgeline comes packed with features. These include:

  • Roll-top closure
  • Expandable main compartment
  • Exterior zippered pocket
  • Two interior pockets
  • Two interior elastic straps
  • Side compression straps
  • Lightly padded waist gussets

Measuring 12″ x 3″ x 8″ when rolled down, the Ridgeline lets you carry only what you need, but can also be expanded up to 12″ tall for those occasions when you need to cram in an extra burrito or beverage. And it’s available in either X-Pac VX21 or 1000d Cordura, depending on your preferences.
