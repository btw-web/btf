Collective Reward Roundup: Another 40 Chances to Win

We’ll be drawing the winners for our latest batch of Collective Reward gear giveaways tomorrow, and this time around there are 40 great prizes up for grabs, including a $1,375 Mason Bokeh all-road adventure frameset, headsets from Wolf Tooth Components, several pieces of beautiful bicycle-themed artwork, and more.

Join our Bikepacking Collective before 11:59 pm EDT tomorrow, October 17th, and you’ll be eligible to win one of the prizes you see below. Membership is open to anyone, anywhere in the world, for the same price. By joining, you’ll also receive Issue 05 of The Bikepacking Journal, will have access to discounts from several bike and outdoor companies, and lots of other great benefits.

Great Divide Mountain Bike Route Maps

Ten members who joined after the deadline to receive Issue 03 last fall will be receiving our final copies of the beautiful and highly sought after Great Divide Mountain Bike Route map that we had hand-illustrated by Alex Hotchin. View reward.

Wolf Tooth Components Premium Headsets

Three Bikepacking Collective members will be receiving Premium Headsets from our friends at Wolf Tooth Components in Minnesota. Winners can choose from nine colorful options and a variety of standards to fit their bike. View reward.

Custom Cedaero Frame Bag

One lucky winner will get a made-to-measure Cedaero frame bag in their choice of color and materials. Cedaero’s photo fitting system will ensure a perfect match for our winner’s bike. View reward.

Tumbleweed Prints by Sean Hipkin

Four of our members can take their pick between the “Starry Night” or “Craggy Peaks” prints that illustrator Sean Hipkin created for Tumbleweed Bicycle Co. These gorgeous prints measure an impressive 18×24″ and and were printed in the USA on a heavy archival-grade paper stock. View reward.

Rene Herse Tires

We’re giving a set of Rene Herse tires to one randomly selected Bikepacking Collective member, who can choose any two tires from the wide range of Rene Herse tires for road, gravel, all-road, and adventure. View reward.

Mason Bokeh Frameset

One especially lucky winner will receive a $1,375 Mason Bokeh all-road frameset in their choice of size (50-62cm) and color (Element Grey, Flare Orange, of Filter Green), a beautiful rig that’s optimized for bikepacking adventures. View reward.

Copies of The Bikepacking Journal 04

Missed the cutoff for Issue 04 earlier this year? Twenty of our members who joined after April 17th will be receiving a copy of The Bikepacking Journal 04 along with their copy of the upcoming fifth issue. View reward.

Help Us Grow – We Need Your Support!

Join the Bikepacking Collective and your contributions will help us build a better, more sustainable BIKEPACKING.com, and create original bikepacking routes, authentic stories, and quality guides. A Bikepacking Collective membership includes a subscription to our printed publication, The Bikepacking Journal, as well as access to exclusive merchandise, industry discounts, prizes like these, and a voice in the future of the website.
