Aventura de a Dos: Atacama 01 (Video)

In partnership with Aventura de a Dos, AcrossAndes.cc, Specialized, and Wahoo, Atacama is a documentary series that highlights the beauty of Chile’s remote gravel roads, parks, cultures, and more. Join Paulina Pinto (@paulinapintof) from Across Andes as she shares and reflects on her experience riding through the The Atacama Desert, the driest nonpolar place on Earth. Watch the full nine-minute video below, and don’t forget to put on subtitles if you’re not a Spanish speaker.
