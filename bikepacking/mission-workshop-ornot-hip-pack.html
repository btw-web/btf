Limited Edition Mission Workshop / Ornot Axis Hip Pack

Based on demand, it was only a matter of time before Ornot released a hip pack of their own, and they jumped at the opportunity to collaborate with Mission Workshop when the opportunity arose. The result is a limited-edition version of Mission Workshop’s standard Axis bag, but in new orange, burgundy, or navy colour ways.

The Mission Workshop / Ornot Axis Hip Pack is made from beefy 500d nylon, with a TPU coated nylon interior. Inside there’s a zippered pocket, key clip, and a U-lock holder is built into the rear panel. The bag isn’t fully waterproof, but they claim it has great water resistance thanks to the water-resistant fabrics and large zipper storm flaps used in its construction. The total weight of the bag is 227g (8oz) and the capacity is 2.5L.

As mentioned above, the hip pack is available as a limited run in three colour options, and is available now from OrnotBike.com for $130 USD.
