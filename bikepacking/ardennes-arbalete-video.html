Ardennes Arbalete (video)

Earlier this month, Jeanne Lepoix and Xavier Tipa cycled the Ardennes Arbalète bikepacking route in Belgium. They completed the 365-kilometer loop in four days and Jeanne made this film along the way using a Sony Alpha 7III and a GoPro 7. Watch it below, then scroll down to find a few photos and learn more about the route.

The Ardennes Arbalète is a long-weekend bikepacking loop that starts and ends in Liège, Belgium. The route offers a little something for everyone: fast forest gravel, beat up singletrack, cruisey bike paths through castle-dotted valleys, quaint encounters with cheery locals, humbling reminders of the area’s poignant history, and the promise of fantastic beer around nearly every corner.
