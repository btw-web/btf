SimWorks Adds New Finishes to Fun 3 Handlebar

SimWorks says their Fun 3 Handlebar is one of their most popular bars because it works well on so many different styles of bikes. Plus, it’s bent three times, making it half riser bar and half promenade bar, with a 20mm rise and 35° sweep. The Sim Works Fun 3 Bar is made from heat-treated aluminum by Nitto in Japan. Find details and specs below…

SimWorks just introduced two new finishes to the Fun 3 lineup: Shot Silver and Black, providing some more traditional colour options for anyone piecing together a special build. For now, they are only available in the 750mm width option, but a narrower 680mm version will follow.

  • Width: 750mm/680mm
  • Bar Clamp: 31.8mm
  • Rise: 20mm
  • Sweep: 35°
  • Material: Heat Treated Aluminum
  • Weight: 359g (680mm), 393g (750mm)
  • Place of Manufacture: Japan
  • Price: $125 USD

For more details on the Fun 3 Bar and the new finishes, head over to Sim-Works.com.
