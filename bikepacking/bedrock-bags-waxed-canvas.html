Bedrock Bags Waxed Canvas Lineup

In addition to their Fresh Bag Of The Month series, Durango, Colorado’s Bedrock Bags also creates a unique product for each season. This spring? Waxed canvas: a timeless material that’s been used in traditional bike saddlebags and other outdoor gear for generations. Bedrock paired their modern bag designs with a heavyweight, US-made waxed canvas, giving their lineup a whole new aesthetic.

Products available in waxed canvas include the Black Dragon Dropper Seat Bag, the Coconino Seat Bag, Dakota Tank Bag, Entrada Handlebar Bag, Honaker Nalgene Bag, Sinbad Stash Pack, and Tapeats Handlebar Bag.

While waxed canvas has proven its durability over many years and countless miles, it does require a bit more care and upkeep than technical fabrics such as X-Pac or Cordura, and it won’t be as lightweight. But, with use, waxed canvas bags acquire a beautiful patina, are also quite weather resistant.

Bedrock’s Waxed Canvas lineup is limited edition and it’s not as readily available as their normal line. Once the season is over, it’s gone. So, if you are interested, better act now.
