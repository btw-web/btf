The New Teravail Warwick

The Teravail Warwick is the company’s latest “progressive trail” mountain bike tire, designed to be versatile enough to handle chunky XC terrain or more aggressive enduro-style trails. The Warwick features a dual-compound tread design with firm stepped center lugs to reduce rolling resistance and carry speed. Softer and larger multi-faceted side lugs with an open transition area provide corner traction and bite into loose terrain.

The Warwick is offered in three sizes: 27.5 x 2.5″, 29 x 2.3″, and 29 x 2.5″. Each comes in three casing options: Light and Supple ($70), Durable ($85), and Ultra-Durable ($90) in black or tan sidewalls. The Warwick should be available to order in the next couple of weeks. Learn more over at Teravail.com.
