Second Time is the Charm: Kokopelli Trail (Video)

If anyone was watching the Rodeo Labs Instagram feed last week, you would have seen a group of friends, all riding drop-bar Rodeo Labs bikes, tackling the Kokopelli Trail from Fruita, Colorado to Moab, Utah. The Kokopelli is a classic bikepacking route, but is usually ridden on a mountain bike, so we were eagerly following along to see how the riders fared. The group was made up of Stephen Fitzgerald (@stevetheintern) from Rodeo Labs, alongside his friends David Hornick, Nicholas Gilroy, Peder Horner, Travis Palmer, and Bryan Derstine. Stephen compiled all of his cell phone videos into a 34-minute video that provides a raw look at what the Kokopelli Trail is all about. Watch it below, then scroll down for some beautiful photos he captured during the trip.
