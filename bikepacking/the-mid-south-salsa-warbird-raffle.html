The Mid South Salsa Warbird Raffle

The Mid South Gravel event takes off this Thursday in Stillwater, Oklahoma, and will bring in some of the fastest gravel riders around. The organizers have collaborated with Tulsa artist Yatika Starr Fields on a stunning one-of-a-kind Salsa Warbird, and they’re raffling it off in support of the The Grey Snow Eagle House. Operated by the Iowa Tribe of Oklahoma, the facility gives long-term care and rehabilitation for permanently injured bald and golden eagles, offers on and off-site educational opportunities, and participates in nationwide research endeavors.

Digital raffle tickets are being sold online during Mid South weekend, and three winners will be chosen on Sunday, March 13th. The first-place winner takes home the complete Salsa Warbird build (size 56), second place gets a new KUAT NV 2.0 rack, and third place gets a Mid South x Eliel cycling kit. The 2022 Salsa Warbird that’s up for grabs is sporting an incredible build, featuring a SRAM XPLR/AXS drivetrain, carbon ZIPP components, and a carbon ZIPP wheelset.

Raffle tickets are $10 each, with no limit on how many you can purchase. Get yours at MidSouthGravel.com.
