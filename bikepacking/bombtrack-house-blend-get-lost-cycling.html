Bombtrack House Blend: Get Lost Cycling

Bombtrack’s newest House Blend shop tour brings us down to beautiful Wellington, New Zealand, to check out Get Lost Cycling (@getlostcycling) and meet its owners, Koen and Stu. They talk through an interesting rigid Cale AL build (check out our recent first ride review) and share a little about their values as a shop, which focuses on adventure and building up a local community of riders. Watch the full four-minute short below, followed by photos and build specs of the Cale AL featured in the video.

Bombtrack Cale AL Build

  • Frame 2021 Bombtrack Cale AL (size L)
  • Fork ENVE Mountain fork
  • Rims WeAreOne Union carbon
  • Hubs DT Swiss 350 hubs
  • Tires Teravail Sparwood 29 x 2.2″
  • Handlebars Soma Dream Riser
  • Headset Cane Creek 40
  • Crankset Shimano SLX M7100 170mm
  • Derailleur Shimano SLX
  • Brakes Shimano SLX
  • Shifter(s) Shimano SLX
  • Saddle Brooks Cambium C17 All Weather Carved
  • Seatpost BikeYoke Revive 31.6 180mm
  • Stem Thomson X4 50mm
  • Dropper Lever OneUp Components
  • Grips ODI Longneck lock on
  • Frame bag Stealth bike bags, partial bolt on

Learn more about Get Lost Cycling at GetLostCycling.co.nz.
