Collective Reward #049: Outdoor Provisions Energy Bars

For Collective Reward #049, three of our Bikepacking Collective members in Europe will be receiving a variety pack of 18 bars from our friends at UK-based Outdoor Provisions. There are a lot of energy bars on the market these days, but we like Outdoor Provisions bars because they don’t contain any refined sugars, are vegan, and most importantly, all of their wrappers are made entirely from compostable materialsâ€”something we hope more companies will embrace in the future. Winners will also receive an enamel raptor pin, part of Outdoor Provisions’ efforts to raise awareness about raptor persecution.

Outdoor Provisions founders Christian Smith and Luke Douglas are actively involved in the bikepacking community, too. They launched the Second City Divide route earlier this year, linking Manchester and Glasgow, and you may remember Luke’s story of his Pamir Highway crossing that we shared back in 2018.

Outdoor Provisions Variety Packs contain:

  • 5 x Cherry Bakewell
  • 5 x Choc Kendal Mint Cake
  • 4 x Bara Brith
  • 4 x Parkin
