State Bicycle x Grateful Dead Klunker Collaboration

Both State Bicycle Co. and The Grateful Dead have built their respective followings around awareness, self-expression, and freedom. So it’s no surprise to see these two brands collaborating on a full line of bicycles, cycling apparel, and accessories. Each piece is adorned with the most treasured symbols associated with the Dead: Dancing Bears, a Steal Your Face skull, and the Lightning Bolt.

The collaboration includes two limited edition versions of State’s Klunker bike—Lightning Bolt Klunker—decorated with Dead-themed paint and decals. The State Klunker is a retro-inspired, combination of a beach cruiser, mountain bike, and BMX bike, optimized for warm days with wind in your hair.

“Having teamed up with the Grateful Dead, we are combining environmental consciousness with the free spirit that has characterized multiple generations of Dead Heads around the world. The music and the vibe of the Grateful Dead is a perfect match for the expansive feelings we all gain through bicycling!” – State Bicycle Co.’s Co-Founder, Mehdi Farsi

The collection includes the single speed Klunkers, several jerseys, saddles, bar tape, and more. Head over to StateBicycle.com to check it out.
