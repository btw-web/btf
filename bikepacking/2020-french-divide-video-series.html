2020 French Divide Video Series

Photos by Milo_pix (@milo_pix), Aubin Berthe (@aubinberthe), and Louis Lambin (@louislambinphotography)

The French Divide is a challenging 2,200-kilometre route that links the Franco-Belgian border to the Franco-Spanish border, following gravel roads and trails using the GR footpaths of Santiago de Compostela. The 2021 grand depart is just a few days away, so we’ve gathered up the official recap videos from last year’s event (episodes one to four) and some photos to give everyone a sense of what to expect the event. Note that non-French speakers will need to use the “auto-translate” option on the third and fourth videos, which is handy, but not perfect! Check it all out below.
