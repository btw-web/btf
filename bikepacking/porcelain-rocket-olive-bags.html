Porcelain Rocket Goes OliveIt!

Usually offered in just LightBlack, several of Porcelain Rocket’s bags are now available for a limited time in a new olive drab waterproof fabric, OliveIt! Bags offered in OliveIt include the modular Microwave Panniers, handy Nigel handlebar bag, and the cavernous Meanwhile basket bag. If you want one, you better act quick. As with all other Porcelain Rocket bags, they are made in small runs and only available when they’re available!

Make sure to check out our original review of the Nigel here. The latest version got a few really nice updates (a few photos below), including integrated Voile Nano handlebar straps and an improved backing structure. We’ve also been enjoying the Microwave Panniers, so stay tuned for a review on those, too.
