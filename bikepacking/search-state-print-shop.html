Search and State Announces “Print Shop”

SAS’ prints have always been their most coveted pieces, with most disappearing within just a few hours of release. So in order to offer a wider, more diverse print collection each season, they just announced the Print Shop, a dedicated page on SearchAndState.com featuring a revolving selection of limited edition signature prints. The Print Shop opened today with three brand new florals: The Black Palm, Pretty Lights 2, and SAS’ first pink jersey, Buena Vista.

Like other SAS apparel, the Print Shop floral jerseys are made in NYC. Find more at SearchAndState.com.
