New Garbaruk Cage For GRX Rear Derailleur Allows a 50T Cassette

Just after we released our big, year-and-a-half-long mullet drivetrains test, Garbaruk expanded their cage lineup with a version made specifically for Shimano GRX 11-speed rear derailleurs. The new cage replaces the standard cage on either a RD-RX812 or RD-RX817 (Di2) derailleur to expand its capability to a 50T wide-range cassette, such as Garbaruk’s own 10-50, 11-50, or the Sunrace CSMX80 11-50t cassette.

The 47 gram, 7075-T651 aluminum Garbaruk GRX cage is available in eight anodized colors (Black or Silver or Blue or Gold or Green or Orange or Red or Violet) for $65. You can also get anodized 11 and 16T pulleys in the same colors for an additional $67 ($132 total). Fid out more over at Garbaruk.com.
