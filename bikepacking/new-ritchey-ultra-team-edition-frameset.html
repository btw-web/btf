New Ritchey Ultra Team Edition Frameset

The latest Ultra Team Edition frameset from Ritchey is a looker! Decked out in Ritchey’s iconic red, white, and blue team colours, the Ultra Team Edition frameset is what Ritchey describes as a “modern, versatile steel hardtail that caters those of us that like to get rowdy, go big and dip into no-holds-barred shredding.” The frame is designed around a 120mm travel fork, boost hub spacing, and can accommodate tires up to 27 x 2.8″ or 29 x 2.4″. The heat-treated, triple-butted Ritchey Logic steel frame has a replaceable stainless-steel derailleur hanger, internal dropper post routing, and a 73mm threaded bottom bracket.

It appears not much has changed on the geometry side of things. It’s still based around a 68.5° head tube angle, 439mm chain stays, a 73.5° seat tube angle. At first glance it looks like it would make a great all-rounder, for those looking to build up a steel bikepacking rig that will handle technical singletrack riding as well. Find additional specs below.

  • Headset: Integrated – 1.5” tapered
  • Rear Hub: Boost 12x148mm
  • Seatpost: 30.9mm seat tube with replaceable seat collar
  • Front Derailleur: Front derailleur clamp size: 34.9mm
  • Drivetrain: Designed for 1X or 2X chainrings
  • Max Chainring Size: 38T single – 26/36T double
  • Max Tire Size: 27.5 x 2.8” or 29 x 2.4”
  • Rear Brake: Post Mount 160mm, adaptors for 180mm
  • Sizes: S (15″) / M (17″) / L (19″) / XL (20.5″)
  • Weight: 2.57kg (L/19″)

The Ritchey Ultra Team Edition Frameset retails for $999 USD and is available through RitcheyLogic.com or your local dealer.
