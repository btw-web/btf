The Adventure Dispatch Documentary Series

Today Specialized unveiled The Adventure Dispatch documentary series. The series features the newly appointed Specialized Adventure team, composed of four genuine, talented and inspiring individuals: Ty Hathaway, Sarah Swallow, Steve Fassbinder, and Benedict Wheeler (aka Ultraromance). The Adventure team was assembled to share unique two wheeled perspectives and passions, while also breaking down the barriers to getting outdoors.

… it’s pretty mind-blowing that not very many people take advantage of what they have right in their backyard.”

Over the next few months the team will be releasing short documentary films dedicated to each athlete, as well as how-to videos to teach riders what they need to know, from fixing flats to choosing routes through the wild. Watch the preview to get a little stoke, then share the love and go ride your bike.
