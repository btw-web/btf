New Rattlesnake bartape by Camp and Go Slow

I’m digging this new diamondback rattlesnake themed bartape by our friends over at Camp and Go Slow. The 3300mm long custom patterned tape is designed to cover ultra wide bars such as Crust’s Towel Rack. The tape sells for $42 and comes packaged in a canvas drawstring sack modeled after a reptile transport bag. Find this, a wool bandana, and several other new goodies over at Campandgoslow.com.
