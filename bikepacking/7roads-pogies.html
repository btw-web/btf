Check out the 7Roads Pogies This Winter

Elena and Stanislav, the duo behind Ukraine-based 7Roads Bags (@7r_bags), have caught our attention on a few occasions with their clever and creative bags, racks, and other handmade goods. Most recently, we couldn’t help but notice some beautiful new colors of their pogies, which are designed to work with drop bars or flat bars and were tested in temperatures down to -23°C (-9.4°F) with just thin gloves on underneath.

The 7Roads Pogies are offered with a Cordura or X-Pac construction and feature a velcro/elastic cinch closure and optional inner pockets for storing small items like a wallet and keys. All pogies are made in Ukraine, and they range in price from €68 ($77) for black Cordura pogies without inner pockets to €86 ($98) for X-Pac pogies in your choice of colors and with pockets inside.

Learn more about these pogies and 7Roads’ broader range of unique bags and accessories over at 7rbags.com.
