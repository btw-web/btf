Updated Rockgeist Dr. Jones Bag Released

Originally released in 2019, the Dr. Jones Bag from Rockgeist is designed specifically for the front loop found on Jones H-Bars (both standard and the riser versions) and the Surly Mokolo bar. It’s a modular system that includes a removable cargo net, wallet-lid, and main body. The cargo net, which is now made from Hypalon, is meant to carry bulky items such as jackets, wet tents, tarps, groundsheets, and the like.

The wallet-lid is a reversible lid that can expand up and down depending on the contents of the main bag. One side is a thick vinyl to see your phone (fits plus-sized phones) and the other side has the X-Pac zipper access. The main body has a daisy chain running around the entire circumference for easy attachment along your Jones Loop or Moloko Bar. It’s currently available in four colors.

  • Capacity: 9-10L
  • Material: Hypalon cargo net, X-Pac body and lid
  • Weight: 283 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: North Carolina, USA
  • Price: $128.95 USD

Find additional specs and photos and buy one for yourself over at Rockgeist.com.
