Have you seen Pedro’s Micro Levers?

We’re longtime fans of the iconic, yellow Pedro’s plastic tire levers. They have the perfect shape and are super strong. Pedro’s extended the same lever end shape to its (relatively) new Micro Levers. At just 20 grams, the lever pair snaps together and packs a few extra tricks, including a storage for a pair of master links, a valve core tool, and more…

The Pedro’s Micro Levers are priced at $4.25 per pair/system and are available in yellow, pink, green, orange, and black. Learn more over at Pedros.com.
