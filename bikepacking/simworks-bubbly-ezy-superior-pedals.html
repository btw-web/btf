New Quick-Release SimWorks Bubbly EZY Superior Pedals

Released back in January as the first pedal collaboration between SimWorks and MKS, the SimWorks by MKS Bubbly Pedals are elegant and durable, and after using them for almost a year, we can report that they’ve proven to be functional, wonderfully smooth spinning, and easy on the eyes, too.

SimWorks just announced that the Bubbly Pedals are now available using MKS’ unique quick-release mechanism, a design they’ve been using for more than 20 years. Users now have the option to remove and reinstall their favorite Simworks pedals on the flyâ€”with no tools required. The EZY superior system can help address storage limitations, security concerns, and those looking to swap pedals with bikes with ease. The pedals use a connection inspired by a gas coupler mechanism that’s a breeze to use, strong, and has “no discernible play,” according to SimWorks.

The Bubbly Pedal Ezy Superior pedals come in three colors: polished, black, or bronze. They have a wide concave platform, a triple-sealed bearing assembly, and a chrome-plated forged spindle.

  • Size: 97 x 108 x 30mm
  • Body Material: Aluminum Alloy
  • Plate Material: Heat-treated Aluminum + Anodized
  • Weight: 392g (pair)
  • Place of Manufacture: Japan
  • Price: $148 USD

You can learn more or order a set over at Sim.Works.
