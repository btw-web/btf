State Bicycle Company Updates 6061 Black Label All-Road

First announced back in October, State Bicycle Company’s 6061 Black Label All-Road offers an affordable point of entry for riders looking to pick up a drop-bar rig for gravel riding, commuting, or light-duty bikepacking. It’s built around an aluminum frame and carbon fork with clearance for 700c x 45mm or 650b x 50mm tires and features two sets of bottle cages, fender mounts, internal cable routing, and thru-axles. Buyers can choose between 700c or 650b wheelsets with complete builds, or both sets of wheels for a reasonable upcharge.

The State 6061 Black Label All-Road is now available in two fresh colors, Dark Woodland and Deep Pacific, and comes equipped with a State-branded 1×11 wide-range drivetrain. Pricing remains the same as when it was originally announced, $1,399.99 for a complete bike with your choice of wheels or $679.99 for a frameset only. State will include both 700c and 650b wheels for an additional $349.99 and buyers can swap to State’s Monster Fork for an extra $179.99.

6061 Black Label Spec Highlights:

  • Frame: 6061 Alloy, Tapered Head Tube, Rear Thru-Axle, Internal Cable Routing, 2x Bottle cage mounts and fender mounts
  • Fork: Full carbon with fender and mid-blade rack mounts, 12×100 thru-axle and internal brake routing
  • Drivetrain: State All-Road 1×11 speed with 40×11-42 gearing
  • Brakes: Tektro mechanical
  • Wheels: Tubeless ready with Vittoria Terreno 700 x 38mm or 650 x 47mm tubeless-ready tires

State just opened sales for the updated 6061 Black Label All-Road, with delivery expected around mid-February. They’ll likely sell through these very quickly, so we recommend ordering sooner rather than later if you’re looking for a bike for the year ahead and this one ticks all your boxes.

Head over to State’s website to find complete build details, geometry charts, and customer reviews for the 6061 Black Label All-Road in Dark Woodland or Deep Pacific.
