2021 Salsa Cutthroat

For those unfamiliar, Cutthroat is Salsaâ€™s Tour Divide-inspired drop bar bike. The Cutthroat was conceived as a svelte carbon bikepacking and endurance race machine designed for the endless ribbons of gravel, doubletrack, and smooth singletrack that the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route is known for. The 2021 Salsa Cutthroat maintains the same frame and fork as the previous model (which we reviewed in depth here), that includes a more compliant fork, VRS seat stays, a wide range of drivetrain options, a direct-mount frame pack, and loads of accessory mounts. You can find all the details on that review, and scroll down for details on each of the 2021 Salsa Cutthroat builds.

2021 Salsa Cutthroat AXS Eagle

The high end 2021 Salsa Cutthroat comes with a full SRAM Eagle AXS group, including the Rainbow color chain and cassette. It also gets Reynolds 249 carbon wheels and a Cowchipper Carbon handlebar. Like the rest of the line, it comes equipped with Teravail Sparwood tires. The 2021 Salsa Cutthroat AXS Eagle comes in black with an iridescent blue-green trim for $7199. Find the full AXS Eagle build over at SalsaCycles.com. Also, find a few more details on the paint over at Salsa’s blog.

2021 Salsa Cutthroat GRX 810

The 2021 Salsa Cutthroat GRX 810 gets a Shimano GRX 810 2x drivetrain with a 46/30T Easton chainring equipped Raceface Aeffect R crankest. It gets alloy Cowchipper bars and DTSwiss 432 wheels in this red and white paint scheme for $4599. Find the full GRX 810 build over at SalsaCycles.com.

2021 Salsa Cutthroat GRX 600

2021 Salsa Cutthroat GRX 600 gets relatively the same drivetrain, swapping out a different chain and Raceface Ride crankset. The 600 model gets WTB MT400 wheels and a snazzy paint job for $3499. Find the full GRX 600 build over at SalsaCycles.com.

2021 Salsa Cutthroat Apex 1

The 2021 Salsa Cutthroat Apex 1 is the mainstay of the lineup and comes with a 1×11 Apex 1 drivetrain featuring a Raceface Ride crankset (36T), WTB Sera/i23 wheels, TRP Spyre brakes, and an 11-42T cassette. The Apex 1 model comes in your choice of three colorways for $2899. Find the full Apex 1 build over at SalsaCycles.com.

2021 Salsa Cutthroat Frameset

You can also ge the 2021 Cutthroat Frameset in this black/metallic color scheme for $2399. Find details on the 2021 Salsa Cutthroat Frameset over at SalsaCycles.com.
