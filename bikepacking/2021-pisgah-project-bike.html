Support Pisgah Trails and Win this $9,000 Gravel Bike

For the fifth consecutive year, Cane Creek has put together a special bike to raffle off in support of the Pisgah National Forest, which needs our help more than ever. Over the last year, tropical storms and the resulting landslides and wind damage have devastated the Pisgah Ranger District. Just this past summer, many trails and bridges were badly damaged, destroyed, or closed from the 14″ of rain that fell and the 100-year flood that ensued after tropical storm Fred rolled through. Find the full press release below with build details about the bike and how you can get raffle tickets and potentially win it.

Press Release
In conjunction with The Pisgah Conservancy (TPC), several regional businesses and a generous group of supporting sponsors, Cane Creek was able to build a very special bicycle with a noble purpose. This year’s Gravel specific bike will be raffled with all proceeds directly benefiting the Pisgah Conservancy. Their mission is to support the conservation and sustainability in the Ranger District of the Pisgah National Forest, representing all of the user groups that love and advocate for the district. Over the past 4 years The Pisgah Project Raffle has been able to raise over $130,000 – With these funds the TPC has been able to continue their work to support recreation, maintain and develop trail systems, protect the watershed, improve the habitat of native species and continue to educate users on conservation principles.

This year’s Pisgah Bike is made possible by donations from Cane Creek Cycling Components, Industry Nine, Thomson Bike Products, Continental Tires, fizik and Crank Brothers.

New for 2021 – A one of a kind Gravel bike, built up to be the perfect bike for exploring the vast gravel network found within the Pisgah National Forest.

The 2021 Pisgah Project raffle will run from November 1st, through December 10th, 2021. Riders will receive one entry for every $25 raffle ticket purchase at https://www.pisgahconservancy.org/how-you-can-help/the-pisgah-project/the-bike.html All Raffle Tickets Must Be Purchased Online. The drawing will be held and a winner will be chosen at random on December 11th, 2021 on Cane Creek Cycling Components Facebook livestream.

To purchase a ticket and enter the raffle, visit PisgahConservancy.org

2021 Pisgah Project bike

This year’s Pisgah Project bike is based around a Thomson titanium frame and has a retail value of more than $9,000 featuring top of the line components from Cane Creek Cycling Components Thomson, Industry Nine, Fi’zi:k, Crank Brothers, and Continental Tires. Here’s the full build kit.

  • Frame: Thomson Titanium Piedmont gravel frame set
  • Handlebars: Alloy Drop Bars (Dirt) 440mm / 31.8mm
  • Stem: Thomson Elite 31.8mm X4 100mm / 31.8mm
  • Seatpost Collar: Thomson
  • Spacers: Thomson Elite Alloy Spacer kit
  • Cranks: Cane Creek eeWings All-Road Titanium Crankset – 170mm
  • Bottom Bracket: Cane Creek Hellbender 110 Bottom Bracket
  • Seatpost: Cane Creek eeSilk + Carbon Suspension Seatpost
  • Headset: Cane Creek 110-Series – ZS44 | EC44 Tapered headset
  • Parts: Cane Creek eeNut Preload Assembly, eeBarKeep – Blue
  • Wheelset: Industry Nine TRAIL 270 TRA Level 3 Wheelset
  • Tires: Continental Terra Trail 650 x 40B
  • Pedals: Crankbrothers Candy 11 Ti pedals
  • Saddle: fizik TERRA Bondcush
  • Bar Tape: fizik 3mm Tacky Bar Tape
  • Chainrings: Praxis 2x Buzz 48/32 Chainrings
  • Drivetrain: Shimano GRX RX810 2 x Mechanical Groupset

To purchase a raffle ticket for your chance to win, head to PisgahConservancy.org.
