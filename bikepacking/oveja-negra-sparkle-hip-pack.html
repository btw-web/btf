Tamar’s Sparkle Motion Royale Hip Pack

Oveja Negra is letting their staff’s creativity run wild with their ongoing Ideas From The Flock bag series, which are fun, limited edition versions of some of their classic bike bags. Their most recent design comes from Oveja’s sewing specialist Tamar Madrigal (@tamar.noir), and is inspired by her “love for rockabilly, vintage and Dia de los Muertos, and a lifetime of always being that weird one.”

Tamar’s Sparkle Motion Royale Hip Pack comes in a classic, roll-top Royale hip pack design featuring lined black retro glitter vinyl, rainbow leopard print accents, and a sparkly sugar skull patch. The bag has a simple roll-top closure, a zippered interior pocket, side compression straps, and high density closed cell foam reinforced hip wings. They are also offering extra different spokes for different folks stickers and patches for those that want them.

  • Capacity: 4L +
  • Material: Cordura and Ottertex waterproof fabrics
  • Weight: 396 grams
  • Place of Manufacture: Colorado, USA
  • Price: $105 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: OvejaNegraBikepacking.com
