Stridsland Now Available from Wizard Works

Owned and operated by Matias Stridsland, Stridsland is responsible for some seriously cool bike builds and as well as designing and manufacturing some equally nice bike components. Matias grew up on Canada’s west coast but now lives in Copenhagen, Denmark with his 2-year-old son, and is behind the catchy tagline (and stickers) Ride Slow, Die Whenever. Due to Brexit it has become impossible for Stridsland to sell to customers in the UK but Wizard Works has stepped in as the exclusive UK dealer. At 5:00pm Copenhagen time (GMT+1) Stridsland’s web shop will also open up, so head over there if Wizard Works’ sells out and you’re not from the UK.
