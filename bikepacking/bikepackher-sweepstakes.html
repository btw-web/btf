#BikepackHer Sweepstakes… One Day Left!

#BikepackHer is an ongoing mission created by Revelate Designs to celebrate, recognize, and encourage more women to participate in cycling and bikepacking. The prize package includes a frameset from Otso, bags from Revelate, and tons of other useful stuff for bikepacking.
