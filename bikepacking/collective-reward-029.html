Collective Reward #029: Nomad Nutrition Plant-Based Meals Giga Pack

Last year, Miles had the chance to taste several of Nomad Nutrition‘s adventure-ready meals, and he was thoroughly impressed (see his full review here). And for Collective Reward #029, we’re giving one Bikepacking Collective member a Giga Pack of Nomad meals – that’s 12 packs in total, six large (meal) and six small (snack). That’s about an $85 value, and will have one lucky winner enjoying delicious and healthy meals on several trips to come.

This Giga Pack includes:

  • 1x Kathmandu Curry 50 gram (1.76oz) pack
  • 1x Irish Shepherd’s Pie 50 gram (1.76oz) pack
  • 1x Indian Red Lentil Stew 50 gram (1.76oz) pack
  • 1x Hungarian Goulash 50 gram (1.76oz) pack
  • 1x Caribbean Curry 50 gram (1.76oz) pack
  • 1x Ukranian Borscht 50 gram (1.76oz) pack
  • 1x Kathmandu Curry 100 gram (3.5oz) pack
  • 1x Irish Shepherd’s Pie 100 gram (3.5oz) pack
  • 1x Indian Red Lentil Stew 100 gram (3.5oz) pack
  • 1x Hungarian Goulash 100 gram (3.5oz) pack
  • 1x Caribbean Curry 100 gram (3.5oz) pack
  • 1x Ukranian Borscht 100 gram (3.5oz) pack

From Miles’ review:

“All of the meals are plant-based, gluten free, non-gmo, dairy free, soy free, and palm oil free. Nomad Nutrition utilizes a unique drying technique called REVdry that not only eliminates the need for preservatives, but maintains flavour, texture, and nutritional value – something that can’t be said for traditional freeze dried or dehydrated food.

So, how do they taste? Pretty darn good. I’ve had my fair share of prepackaged camp meals over the years. At best, only a few of those were actually good. It’s obvious that there are real ingredients in there that smell like real food – an experience I can’t say I’ve enjoyed with the others out there. You’d think that you should be able to see some lentils down in that bag of Indian Red Lentil Stew, and you can.”
