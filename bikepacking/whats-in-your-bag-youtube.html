What’s in your bag? YouTube Livestream Tonight!

What’s in your bag? To kick off this new chat series, each week for the next month, Jess Daddio, Joe Cruz, Miles Arbour, and Neil Beltchenko will be unpacking their bikepacking bags live on YouTube. This informal chat will showcase what bags each individual is using, what they are carrying, and why they carry that specific gear in that spot. Today we will be starting with the seat pack and rear rack position. Tune in below, and be sure to subscribe to our YouTube channel to get notifications for upcoming live events and videos.
