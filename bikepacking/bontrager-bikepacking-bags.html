Bontrager Introduces a line of Bikepacking Bags

Waterloo, Wisconsin-based Bontrager released a new line of bikepacking Adventure Bags today. The all-new Adventure Top Tube Bag and Adventure Frame Bag are “inspired by Trek and Bontragerâ€™s legacy of adventure” and designed with durability in mind, according to the company. Each bag is constructed from what appears to be black X-Pac, although Bontrager doesn’t specify materials or where they’re made. Other features include hi-vis interior fabric and straps with a soft rubber coating to reduce abrasion.

The Bontrager half-frame bag features a single zippered main pocket, and one smaller compartment pocket on the non-drive side.
