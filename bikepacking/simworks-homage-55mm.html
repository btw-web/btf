SimWorks Homage Tire Now Available in 55mm Width

Originally only offered in a 43mm width in both 700c and 650b platforms, SimWorks is now offering their Homage tire in a wider 55mm version for those seeking a larger footprint for mixed-terrain rides. The Homage is SimWorks’ nod to the early days of mountain biking, combining a capable tread pattern, comfortable riding characteristics, and durability with a tire that’s designed to be surefooted in all conditions.

The 55mm Homage tire will initially only be offered in the 650b size, carrying over all the same characteristics of the narrower 43mm version, making a great option for a do-mostly-everything tire for your dirt-touring rig. The Homage uses an Aramid bead, their ZSG Natural Compound rubber, and weighs in at 625g. The Homage tire is offered with a green tread with tan sidewall or as a classic black with tan option.

  • Wheel Size: 650b
  • Tire Width: 55mm
  • Recommended Pressure: Max 350kPa / 50psi
  • Weight: 625 grams
  • Price: $62 USD
  • Manufacturer’s Details: Sim.Works
