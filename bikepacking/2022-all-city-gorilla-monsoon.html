2022 All-City Gorilla Monsoon Colors Are Here

The 2022 All-City Gorilla Monsoon is here, offering all the same features as the previous version, but with fresh graphics, paint, and new build kits. It’s built up around a 612 Select steel frame with geometry adapted from their cyclocross lineup that’s designed for carrying heavier loads. It has stealth dropper post routing, a 73mm threaded bottom bracket, mounts for racks, fenders, and more, and comes on a 27.5 x 2.4” platform with clearance for 700 x 42mm tires. We’re seeing price increases throughout the industry, and the Gorilla Monsoon is no different, with both models increasing by about $400 USD, and the frameset by $150 for 2022. Find photos and complete build details on the 2022 All-City Gorilla Monsoon below.

2022 All-City Gorilla Monsoon APEX

The 2022 Gorilla Monsoon APEX comes in a funky “Charred Berry” fade color scheme and features mechanical disc brakes and a dropper post actuated from a small lever just below the left brake hood. The complete APEX model will retail for $2,499 or you can get a frameset in this paint scheme for $1,050. See more at AllCityCycles.com.

  • Fork 612 Select Chromoly Steel, TIG Welded and Brazed
  • Headset Cane Creek 40 Series
  • Stem AL6061, 3D forged, 4-bolt stem, 31.8mm
  • Handlebar AL6061, 31.8mm, 12° Flair
  • Bar Tape All-City Embossed Black Tape
  • Shifter/Brake Lever SRAM Apex, 1×11
  • Rear Derailleur SRAM Apex 1, Long Cage
  • Brakeset Tektro MD-C550, 160mm f/r, 6-Bolt
  • Crankset FSA Comet 38t, MegaEVO
  • Bottom Bracket FSA MegaEVO
  • Seatpost TranzX JD-TSI01, 27.2, 350mm L, 50mm drop, DL-24 Lever
  • Saddle All-City Gonzo or DDK-5255
  • Cassette SunRace 11-speed, 11/42t
  • Chain KMC X11 EPT
  • Hubs Novatec 32h
  • Rims WTB ST i25 TCS 2.0, 32h
  • Tires WTB Riddler, 27.5 x 2.4″, TCS Light/Fast

2022 All-City Gorilla Monsoon GRX

The 2022 Gorilla Monsoon GRX comes in a “Aqua Seafoam” fade color scheme and features hydraulic disc brakes and a dropper post actuated from the left shift lever. The complete GRX model will retail for $2,950 or you can get a frameset in this paint scheme for $1,050. Find the full build kit below and see more at AllCityCycles.com.

  • Shifter/Brake Lever Shimano GRX RX810, 1×11
  • Rear Derailleur Shimano GRX RX810
  • Brakeset Shimano GRX RX810, Hydraulic
  • Crankset FSA V-Drive 38T, MegaEVO
  • Bottom Bracket FSA MegaEVO
  • Seatpost TranzX JD-TSI01, 27.2, 350mm L, 50mm drop, DL-24 Lever
  • Saddle All-City Gonzo or DDK-5255
  • Cassette SunRace 11-speed, 11/42t
  • Chain KMC X11 EPT
  • Hubs All-City Go-Devil 32h

  • Tires WTB Riddler, 27.5 x 2.4″, TCS Light/Fast

Both builds are live now on AllCityCycles.com, so head there for more info or drop into your local All-City dealer to get one for yourself.
