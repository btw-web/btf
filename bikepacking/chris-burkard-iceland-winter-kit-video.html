Chris Burkard’s Iceland Winter Pack List (Video)

Following his recent winter crossing of Iceland with Angus Morton and Rebecca Rusch, which you can read about in our interview with the whole team here, friend of the site Chris Burkard put together this post-ride video gear breakdown, in which he walks through his entire kit and how he packed it on his bike for the six-day, 300-mile journey. Watch it below, followed by a written list of what he brought with him.
