New Shimano XT, Saint, and SLX Composite Flat Pedals

Shimano just quietly released updates to their flat pedal lineup, notably the XT, SLX, and Saint pedals. Each have a few new interesting features and were redesigned to be lighter and a little more svelte than their chunky predecessors. If they are as durable as their SPD brethren, they could be a fantastic option for bikepacking and dirt-touring. Unfortunately, there aren’t details on pricing or availability quite yet, but you can find all the other details below…

Shimano XT flat pedal (PD-M8141)

The new DEORE XT flat pedal is perhaps the most compelling out of the three. Positioned for enduro/trail riding the PD-M8141 is significantly lighter than its predecessor (by 78 grams). The new XT flat pedal has a wide 109mm, concave aluminum platform and adjustable pins. Find the full specs from Shimano below.

  • Dual concave platform: Fore-Aft: 17.5 – 13.3 – 17.5 mm; side: 17.2 – 13.3 – 17.2 mm
  • 10 pins, +4 optional inner positions; 6mm and 3mm pins included
  • Platform size: 112 x 109mm
  • Durable chrome-moly axle and thin aluminum pedal body
  • New axle and bushing construction
  • Center thickness: 13.3mm
  • 8mm hexagon wrench mount
  • Weight: 428g (78g lighter than PD-M8140-ML)

Learn more about the PD-M8141 over at bike.shimano.com.

Shimano SLX flat pedal (PD-GR400)

The SLX version of the flat pedal is also quite interesting as it has a full resin composite pedal body, although it’s not quite as wide as the XT. The PD-GR400 is 139g lighter than the previous SLX-range pedal (PD-GR500), and comes in black, blue, or red.

  • Center thickness: 16mm
  • 9 removable pins per side
  • Platform size: 101 x 96mm
  • 8mm hexagon wrench mount
  • Bushing construction axle
  • Weight: 394 g

Learn more about the PD-GR400 over at bike.shimano.com.

Shimano Saint flat pedal (PD-M829)

The Saint flat pedal was designed for downhill and aggressive trail riding and features a wide platform with a thin pedal body and an interesting skid plate made from “carbon composite resin.” The Saint pedal has a chromemoly axle with a redesigned bushing construction that adds up to a 156 gram weight savings over the previous PD-MX80 pedal.

  • Dual concave platform: Fore-Aft: 16.1 – 11.6 – 16.1mm; Side: 13.9 – 11.6 – 13.9mm
  • 10 pins, +4 optional inner positions; 6mm and 3mm pins included
  • Platform size: 121 x 110mm
  • Aluminum + Carbon composite resin
  • Center thickness: 11.6mm
  • New axle and bushing construction
  • 8mm hexagon wrench mount
  • Weight: 397g

Learn more about the PD-M829 over at bike.shimano.com.
