Leaving Time Behind: Bikepacking the Arizona Trail (film)

Photos by Riley Seebeck (courtesy of Freehub Mag)

One of the great things about bikepacking is how time becomes irrelevant. Without agendas, expectations, and obligations, you can ignore the clock and be fully absorbed by the landscape. Jackie Paaso and Stan Jorgensen did just that as they set off on a quick, three-day, 90-mile mission on the Arizona Trail. The two embraced the moment and abided only by the rhythm of mother nature. Watch the film about their trip below with a few photos courtesy of Freehub Magazine.

When you come back to civilization, you leave a little bit of yourself out on the trail.
