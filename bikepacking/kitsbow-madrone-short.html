New Women’s Kitsbow Madrone Short

We’ve been waiting for a new Kitsbow women’s short to follow the award-winning Haskell, and we just got an email with the announcement. Designed by women for women, the new Kitsbow Madrone Short features classic tailoring, two choices of inseam (7″ or 11″), and waist sizes from 26 to 36. They have subtle styling that won’t look out of place in town or commuting to work, a dual snap front closure, belt loops, two back pockets with a security zipper, and subtle reflective hits for visibility in low light. The Kitsbow Madrone Shorts are made by hand, one at a time, at their shop in North Carolina, and are offered in three colors: Black Olive, Gunmetal Grey, and Black.

  • Size Range: 26 to 36
  • Material: 93% nylon and 7% elastane
  • Place of Manufacture: North Carolina, USA
  • Price: $169 USD

The new Madrone short is available to order now at Kitsbow.com. There’s currently is an 8-week wait for the shorts to be made and shipped.
