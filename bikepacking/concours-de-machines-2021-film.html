Concours de Machines 2021: The Film

We’ve covered in this year’s Concours de Machines in detail – you can see some of the posts below. This film, beautifully produced by CreaLens for the Association des Artisans du Cycle, really helps put it all in context.

Note that the organisers are working on a subtitled version, too. If you can offer any help, do get in touch.

But in the meantime, enjoy a bike ‘show’ that’s unlike anything else that we know of, one whose bikepacking theme has revealed all manner of innovative and out-of-the-box ideas for those who love to camp out with their bicycles.

With thanks to Magali Paulin for studio and outdoor imagery from the 2021 Concours de Machines.
