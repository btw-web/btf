EVOC Announces New RIDE Backpacks

The RIDE series is the latest addition to EVOC’s massive selection of backpacks and bags that are designed to be multifunctional and affordable, making them a good fit for mountain biking, commuting, and everyday life. The RIDE backpacks are offered in three sizes—8, 12 and 16 litres—and come fully equipped with a large main compartment, hydration bladder sleeve, tool compartment, and an exterior helmet sleeve. They also have a compartment for carrying glasses, a rear light clip, and an integrated whistle in the chest strap. The 12 and 16 litre versions have additional mesh pockets on the belt for anything you need to have quick access to while riding. All models utilize EVOC’s Air Pad system for better cushioning and air circulation through the integrated ventilation channels.

The RIDE 8 comes in black or loam-carbon grey, the RIDE 12 in carbon grey-black or red-carbon grey, and the RIDE 16 in black or light olive. All packs are available with or without a 2L bladder. See a pricing breakdown below.

MSRP with 2L Bladder

  • Evoc Ride 8L $120
  • Evoc Ride 12L $130
  • Evoc Ride 16L N/A

MSRP without Bladder

  • Evoc Ride 8L (500g)$90
  • Evoc Ride 12L (590g)$100
  • Evoc Ride 16L (600g)$110

The EVOC RIDE backpacks are planned to be available in stores from autumn 2020. Head over to EVOCSports.com to learn more.
