Announcing the 2021 Bombtrack Hook lineup

Photos by Jason Sellers

Bombtrack just released its 2021 Hook lineup. Instead of having multiple complete models delineated by numbers and suffixes, there are just three bikes within the gravel-adventure range: the base model Hook, the Hook EXT, and the carbon Hook EXT C. Each get a few frame tweaks, new colors, and refined build kits. Here are the details on each.

2021 Bombtrack Hook

Fitted with 700C x 40mm or 650B x 42mm tires, the versatile steel base model Hook still has plenty of mounts for gear options on the frame, including direct mounts for a top tube bag, three sets of triple cage mounts, and rack and fender mounts. Also, the Hook’s full carbon fork has two additional sets of three-pack bosses for further cargo duties.

New for 2021, the Hook specced in size-matched 650b (XS and S) or 700c wheels to standardize the bike’s geometry. In addition, it gets a revised Columbus Cromor double butted frame with a new head tube and 86.5mm wide T47 bottom bracket to increase tire clearance. Find the frame specs below and scroll down for the build kit.

  • BB Standard: T47 – 86.5mm shell width
  • Headset Standard (S.H.I.S.): EC34 / 28.6 | EC44 / 40
  • Fork Max Tire Clearance: XS-S 650B x 52mm / M-XL 700C x 45mm
  • Frame Max Tire Clearance: XS-S 650B x 42mm / M-XL 700C x 42mm
  • Maximum chainring clearance 1x: 40T with 45mm chainline
  • Maximum chainring clearance 2x: 48/32T with 46.9mm chainline
  • Seat Post Diameter: 27.2mm
  • Brake mount front/rear: Flat Mount
  • Maximum rotor clearance front/rear: 160mm
  • Front through axle: 12mm x 100mm
  • Rear through axle: 12mm x 142mm
  • Crankarm Length: XS-S 165mm / M 170mm / L-XL 175mm
  • Spoke count (front/rear): 32 / 32
  • Handlebar Width (drops): XS-S 400mm / M 420mm / L-XL 440mm
  • Stem Length: XS-S 80mm / M 90mm / L-XL 100mm
  • Stem angle: -7°

  • Stem BOMBTRACK ROAM forged aluminium stem, -7°

The 2021 Bombtrack Hook is available in Glossy Metallic Blue at Bombtrack dealers for 2,599.99 USD / 2299,99 EUR / 3.199,99 CAD / 2.200,00 GBP / 278,000 ¥ / 3.500,00 AUD. Currently, the Hook is not yet available as frameset only option. Stay tuned for more details.

2021 Bombtrack Hook EXT

With a quality Colombus Cromor frame, a high-end carbon fork, loads of mounting options, and dirt-focused 27.5 x 2.0″ tires, the Hook EXT grabbed a slot in our Gear of The Year awards last year. The 2021 model stays relatively the same with the addition of a revised head tube (40g lighter), a rear light mount, and integrated rear light cable routing. They also added the version 2 model of the full-carbon EXT fork with triple cage mounts and internal dynamo cable routing into the steerer. Find the frame specs below and scroll down for the build kit.

  • Headset Standard (S.H.I.S.): EC34 / 28.6 | EC44 / 40

  • Frame Max Tire Clearance w/o fender: 650B x 52mm / 700C x 40mm
  • Maximum chainring clearance 1x: 44T with 45mm chainline
  • Maximum chainring clearance 2x: 50/36 T with 45mm chainline
  • Seat Post Diameter: 27.2mm
  • Brake mount front/rear: Flat Mount
  • Maximum rotor clearance front/rear: 160mm
  • Front through axle: 12mm x 100mm
  • Rear through axle: 12mm x 142mm

  • Spoke count (front/rear): 32/32
  • Handlebar Width (drops): XS 400mm / S-M 420mm / L-XL 440mm

2021 Bombtrack Hook EXT C

For 2021, the carbon fiber Hook EXT C gets a new frame and fork with a tapered head tube, thru-axles, rack and fender mounts, stealth dropper post routing, and a T47 bottom bracket. Along with some minimal changes to the geometry, Bombtrack revised the stays and yoke for added chainring clearance. They also added the version 2 model of the full-carbon EXT fork with triple cage mounts and internal dynamo cable routing into the steerer.

Geometry tweaks to the frame include a 5mm longer top tube, 2mm of additional fork offset, and a very slightly longer reach and taller stack. Find the frame specs below and scroll down for the build kit.

  • BB Standard: T47 – 68mm shell width
  • Headset Standard (S.H.I.S.): IS42 / 28.6 | IS52 / 40
  • Fork Max Tire Clearance: 650B x 52mm / 700C x 45mm
  • Frame Max Tire Clearance: 650B x 52mm / 700C x 45mm
  • Maximum chainring clearance 1x: 46T with 45mm chainline
  • Maximum chainring clearance 2x: 50/34T with 43.5mm chainline
  • Seat Post Diameter: 27.2mm
  • Brake mount front/rear: Flat Mount
  • Maximum rotor clearance front/rear: 160mm
  • Front through axle: 12mm x 100mm
  • Rear through axle: 12mm x 142mm
  • Crankarm Length: XS 165mm / S-M 170mm / L-XL 175mm
  • Spoke count (front/rear): 28/28
  • Handlebar Width (drops): XS 400mm / S-M 420mm / L-XL 440mm
  • Stem Length: XS-S 80mm / M 90mm / L-XL 100mm
  • Stem angle: -7°
