Has the Oyster Bar Gone Too Far?

Last week it was the Surly Truck Stop Bar, and the week before that it was the RideFarr Aero Gravel bar. Now there’s the Oyster Bar handlebar, “A Bicycle Handlebar for Long Rides.” This new steering apparatus claims to be ready for gravel/bikepacking/whatever and blends flat bars with drops for even more hand positions. There’s no doubt that we’re in massive state of influx with new drop handlebars. But are they going to far?
