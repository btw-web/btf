New All-City Cosmic Stallion Titanium

Expanding beyond the steel bikes they’ve become known for, All-City has entered the space metal game with their first-ever titanium frameset. The Cosmic Stallion Ti is their all-road endurance bike with enough capability to handle rough gravel roads without sacrificing precise handling and speed on tarmac. Constructed from All-City’s proprietary Oberon Titanium, the Cosmic Stallion Ti is three-quarters of a pound lighter than an A.C.E. steel frame equivalent.

Cosmic Stallion Ti Frameset Specs

  • Oberon Titanium Tubing: Our proprietary titanium tubing was custom designed to be light as a feather for all-road riding, precise handling, and aggressive speed as long as you both shall live
  • Columbus Futura Cross Carbon Fork: Di2 Routing Options, Rear Rack and Fender Mounts, Premium Corrosion Resistance
  • All-City Signature Details: Custom Ti dropouts, All-City Shot Collar
  • Tire Clearance: Built with versatility in mind, it accommodates tire sizes 700c x 45mm or 650b x 42mm (with fenders)

The All-City Cosmic Stallion Ti will be available in mid-February in a small, exclusive run of frames. The frameset retails for $3,199 USD. More information can be found at AllCityCycles.com or through your local All-City dealer.
