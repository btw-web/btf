Velocio’s 2021 UNITY Project: Fighting for Climate Action

Continuing a mission they started back in 2017, the team at Velocio recently announced this year’s UNITY jersey and the organizations they’ll be supporting through its sales. For 2021, they’re focusing on climate change by donating 100% of UNITY jersey sales to three climate-focused non-profit groups: The Environmental Justice Foundation, 350.org, and Protect Our Winters.

Customers can choose which of the three groups they’d like to support at checkout. Individual jersey sales add up to a meaningful contribution that supports organizations doing important work. Last year, UNITY jersey sales raised more than $100,000 to help out communities affected by the COVID-19 crisis.

This year’s UNITY jersey is priced at $169 and is available in men’s sizes XS to XXXXL and women’s sizes XXS to XXXL. Pre-orders are open through May 31st, and orders are expected to ship around the middle of August. You can learn more the non-profit groups Velocio is supporting this year and purchase a jersey over at Velocio.cc.
