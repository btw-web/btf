Interview with Dusty Eroh of Revelate Designs

In the latest video on our YouTube channel, Neil chats with the longest-tenured employee at Revelate Designs, Dusty Eroh. Owner Eric Parsons hired Dusty near 10 years ago to help him fulfill shipments and other odd jobs. Today, Dusty is one of only three full-time employees at Revelate Designs, where he wears many hats. In this interview, Neil and Dusty chat about the process of designing a new bikepacking bag from the ground up, the time and effort it takes, and the process beyond. They also touch on Dusty’s long lost love for alpine climbing, his new guiding business (Alaska Bike Adventures), and his love for trail work.
