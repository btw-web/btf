New Discounts for Bikepacking Collective Members

Although most of us are holding off on taking major bikepacking trips this season, even day rides and backyard adventures can take their toll on gear. And lots have bikepackers have started dreaming up builds or planning routes for when it’s safe to hit the trails again. With that in mind, we’ve put together a fresh batch of exclusive discounts to help our Bikepacking Collective members get rolling with deals on tires, apparel, bags, saddles, navigation apps, and more.

If you’re in the market for new gear, these special offers just might offset the costs of a Bikepacking Collective membership several times over. If you’re not yet a member of our Bikepacking Collective, it isn’t too late to get signed up to take advantage of these offers.
