Wolf Tooth Announces Five New Grips

Minneapolis-based Wolf Tooth Components announced yesterday that they’ve acquired RedMonkey Sports, absorbing RedMonkey’s line of silicone grips into their own.

This means Wolf Tooth is now offering five new grips in a wide variety of colors and styles, with more on the way in the coming months. All are made from a proprietary dual-density extruded silicone foam and feature a soft, tacky feel. And they’re deceptively comfortable given their relative simplicity.

Wolf Tooth’s New grip Lineup:

  • Razer Grip: 5mm foam wall thickness (30mm installed diameter), available in 10 colors, lightweight, ideal for riders who enjoy sensitive bike handling and control.
  • Karv Grip: 6.5mm foam wall thickness (32mm installed diameter), available in 10 colors, thicker foam makes it perfect for long rides.
  • Karv Cam Grip: 6.5mm foam wall thickness (32mm installed diameter), available in two colors, two facets carved along grip allow you to choose an ergonomic position that best supports your hand position and riding style.
  • Fat Paw Grip: 9.5mm foam wall thickness is the thickest silicone foam grip on the market (36mm installed diameter), available in 10 colors, ideal for riders with large or gloved hands.
  • Fat Paw Cam Grip: 9.5mm foam wall thickness (36mm installed diameter), available in two colors, two facets carved along grip allows you to choose an ergonomic position that best supports your hand position and riding style.

All five of these grips are available directly via WolfToothComponents.com now, and will be available wherever Wolf Tooth is sold shortly. And they’re promising even more new grips and accessories soon. Stay tuned!
