Introducing the 2021 MTBGB

The Los Angeles Field Guide, a vanguard in bike fashion, just introduced it’s latest breakthrough in bicycle trendiness: the 2021 Mountain Bike Marketed as a Gravel Bike (MTBGB). According to The LA Field Guide, their design team “pulled inspiration for the MTBGB’s geometry from the entire pantheon of bicycle history. Road, BMX, downhill, cross country, time trial, motocross, tandems.” But that’s not all. The 2021 MTBGB has a record-setting 75 bottle bosses and an all new 1×18 drivetrain developed by Formula 1 and NASA, featuring Fully Actuated Range for Climbing Excellence (F.A.R.C.E.) technology with a wide-range 11-112t cassette. Find the full specs and a lot more interesting photos over at LAFieldGuide.com.
