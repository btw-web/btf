Touring Talk: the Alaska Highway and Beyond

 

About the Interview
The Alaska Highway is a mythical place for many.  Peer talks about cycling desolate stretches of roads under difficult conditions, wildlife encounters and what it takes to tough out a blizzard at the Arctic Circle.

Touring Talk episode 2: The Alaska Highway all the way up to the Arctic by worldbiking

Name:

Peer Anselstetter

Country:

Germany

Route:

The Arctic Circle to Vancouver with lots of detours to experience cycling in Alaska.

Opinion on riding in Alaska:

Not exactly a cyclist’s paradise…it’s tough going but ultimately worth the suffering.

Toughest Day:

A non-stop 8 hour full-on snowstorm in the month of May.
