Touring Talk: Biking Boomers

 

Touring Talk episode 8: biking boomers by worldbiking

 

Name:

Chris and Heather Hartridge

Country:

Canada

Touring Experience:

Long trips in Canada and the USA with plans for an extended multi-year tour in the future.

Comfort touring or Camping?

CAMPING!  And that is comfort for these two.  A thermarest and fresh air beats the Travel Lodge any day.

Their opinion on bike touring:

They love the freedom of touring and the incredible level of fitness that goes along with a pedal-powered holiday.

Advice for first-timers to bicycle touring:

Ease in to it.  Listen to your body and don’t get obsessed about distances or reaching a particular destination.
